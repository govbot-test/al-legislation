4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, The Department of Human
6 Resources receives funds for the administration of the
7 food stamp program in an amount equal to 5% of the
8 value of food stamp benefits issued statewide. In order
9 to comply with the provisions set forth in the One Big
10 Beautiful Act, this bill would increase that amount of
11 7.5%.
12 Under existing law, the Children's Health
13 Insurance Program (CHIP)is funded as a first charge
14 against the use tax allocated to the General Fund. This
15 bill would amend that provision to allow for other
16 sources of revenue to be used to fund CHIP.
