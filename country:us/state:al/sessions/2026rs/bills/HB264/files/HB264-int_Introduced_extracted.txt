4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, the administration of an
6 estate or a will contest may be removed to the circuit
7 court in the county where the proceeding is pending
8 unless the probate court or judge has concurrent
9 equitable jurisdiction with the circuit court.
10 This bill would allow removal of estate
11 administration and will contests to the circuit court
12 without assigning any special equity, provided the
13 probate court and circuit court do not have concurrent
14 jurisdiction.
15 This bill would also make nonsubstantive,
16 technical revisions to update the existing code
17 language to current style.
