5 RETIREMENT AS TALLAPOOSA COUNTY 911 DIRECTOR.
6
7 WHEREAS, with great pride and special pleasure,
8 highest commendations are conveyed to Anita Haggerty on the
9 occasion of her retirement as the Tallapoosa County 911
10 Director after 34 years of dedicated service; and
11 WHEREAS, Anita Haggerty began her career with the
12 county's emergency 911 system on October 1, 1991, before it
13 was up and running; she had been working as a paramedic with
14 East Alabama Medical Center EMS when the 911 board recruited
15 her; her first task was to ensure that every road in the
16 county was named and each resident's address was accurate
17 before the system could go online in 1994, a process that
18 took three years; and
19 WHEREAS, during her tenure, Anita trained hundreds of
20 dispatchers in Public Safety Telecommunications, Emergency
21 Medical Dispatch standards, and CPR; as technology improved
22 over the years, she ensured that Tallapoosa County 911
23 stayed up to date, implementing the Tallapoosa County 911
24 GIS office, as well as the establishment of Enhanced 911
25 (E911), which increased the ease in getting to emergency
26 locations based on cell phone location among other essential
27 improvements; and
28 WHEREAS, noting how difficult it is to depart from a

30 Anita will return as a part-time employee in February 2026;
