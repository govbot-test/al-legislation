9 Relating to Class 1 municipalities; to authorize the
10 city council to set the salary of the mayor; to amend Sections
11 11-43-80 and 11-43-86, Code of Alabama 1975, to further
12 provide for the mayor's compensation; to make conforming
13 changes; and to repeal Act 84-618 of the 1984 Regular Session
14 (Acts 1984, p. 1256), providing for an expense allowance for
15 the mayor of a Class 1 municipality; Section 11-43-86.1, Code
16 of Alabama 1975, providing for an increase in the salary of
17 the mayor in a Class 1 municipality; and Section 45-37A-52.93,
18 Code of Alabama 1975, providing for the salary of the Mayor of
19 Birmingham.
20 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
21 Section 1. (a) The annual salary of the mayor of a
22 Class 1 municipality for the duration of his or her term shall
23 be set by the city council in an amount that is no less than
24 two hundred twenty thousand dollars ($220,000) per year. The
25 salary of the mayor shall not increase or decrease during his
26 or her term.
27 (b) The mayor shall receive the annual salary payable
