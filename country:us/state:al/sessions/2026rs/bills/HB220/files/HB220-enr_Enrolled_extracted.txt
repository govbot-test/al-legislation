HB220 Enrolled
2
3 Relating to multi-member boards; to authorize the
4 Governor, the Lieutenant Governor, the Speaker of the House of
5 Representatives, the President Pro Tempore of the Senate, the
6 Minority Leader of the House of Representatives, and the
7 Minority Leader of the Senate to remove and replace at any
8 time any board member over which he or she has appointing
9 authority in certain circumstances; and to provide exceptions.
10 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
11 Section 1. (a) As used in this section, "board" means
12 any board, authority, or commission comprised of individual
13 members, of which some or all members are appointed, as
14 established under state law.
15 (b) Any individual appointed to a board by the
16 Governor, the Lieutenant Governor, the Speaker of the House of
17 Representatives, the President Pro Tempore of the Senate, the
18 Minority Leader of the House of Representatives, or the
19 Minority Leader of the Senate pursuant to a state law that
20 authorizes the appointment shall serve at the pleasure of the
21 Governor, the Speaker of the House, the President Pro Tempore
22 of the Senate, the House Minority Leader, or the Senate
23 Minority Leader, as the case may be.
24 (c)(1) Notwithstanding any law to the contrary,
25 including any law providing a specific removal process, the
26 Governor, the Lieutenant Governor, the Speaker of the House of
27 Representatives, the President Pro Tempore of the Senate, the
28 Minority Leader of the House of Representatives, and the
Page 1
Minority Leader of the House of Representatives, and the
HB220 Enrolled
29 Minority Leader of the Senate, at any time, may remove any
30 board member over which he or she has appointing authority.
31 (2) Any board member removed pursuant to subdivision
32 (1) shall be replaced in accordance with existing law by the
33 respective appointing authority, provided the new member meets
34 the applicable qualifications to serve on the board, as set
35 forth in the relevant state law.
36 (d) This section does not authorize the Governor to
37 remove any individual whom the Governor previously appointed
38 to fill a vacancy of an elected office.
39 (e) This section does not apply to the Alabama State
40 Port Authority, the State Ethics Commission, or to the
41 governing board of any two-year or four-year public
42 institution of higher education.
43 Section 2. This act shall become effective on October
44 1, 2026.
