4 HJR___ RECOGNIZING AND SUPPORTING THE OBSERVANCE OF PROFOUND
5 AUTISM DAY.
6
7 WHEREAS, the definition of autism has evolved over
8 time, resulting in an increasingly heterogeneous spectrum of
9 abilities, needs, and life experiences; and
10 WHEREAS, while the entire autism spectrum deserves
11 recognition, understanding, and support, the term profound
12 autism is intended to provide clarity regarding individuals
13 who require 24-hour care from an adult caregiver throughout
14 their lives; and
15 WHEREAS, individuals with profound autism may also
16 have a severe intellectual disability and may have minimal
17 or no functional language, creating distinct and complex
18 support needs; and
19 WHEREAS, due to these unique challenges, individuals
20 with profound autism are frequently excluded from research
21 initiatives, services, and support programs that are not
22 adequately designed to meet their level of need; and
23 WHEREAS, in April 2023, the Centers for Disease
24 Control and Prevention released its first prevalence study
25 on profound autism, concluding that 26.7 percent of children
26 eight years of age with autism in the United States meet the
27 criteria for profound autism, meaning that approximately one
28 in four children with autism are profoundly affected; and

29 WHEREAS, the study further found that, compared to
30 children with non-profound autism, children with profound
31 autism were more likely to be female; to come from racial
32 and ethnic minority groups; to experience low socioeconomic
33 status; to be born prematurely or with low birth weight; to
34 exhibit self-injurious behaviors; to have seizure disorders;
35 and to have significantly lower adaptive functioning scores;
36 and
37 WHEREAS, individuals with profound autism and the
38 families and caregivers who support them live in our
39 communities, often in isolation, and require meaningful
40 inclusion, understanding, and sustained support; and
41 WHEREAS, specific recognition of the unique
42 challenges faced by individuals with profound autism is
43 critical to ensuring equitable access to care, research,
44 education, and community-based services; now therefore,
45 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA, BOTH
46 HOUSES THEREOF CONCURRING, That the Profound Autism Alliance
47 is honored and commended on the observance of Profound
48 Autism Day on March 17, 2026, and this resolution is offered
49 in highest tribute to those whose dedicated efforts promote
50 dignity, inclusion, and meaningful support for individuals
51 living with profound autism.
