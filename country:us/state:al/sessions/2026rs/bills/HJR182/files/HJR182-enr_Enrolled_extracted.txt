7 WHEREAS, with great pride and special pleasure, we
8 highly honor Montgomery County Sheriff Derrick Cunningham
9 for being named the 2026 Ferris E. Lucas Sheriff of the Year
10 by the National Sheriffs' Association; and
11 WHEREAS, Derrick Cunningham was born in Tacoma,
12 Washington, and moved to Tuscaloosa, Alabama, with his
13 family when he was 12 years old; prior to graduating from
14 Tuscaloosa County High School, he joined the Alabama
15 National Guard in 1984, proudly serving until he retired
16 with 24 years of service; and
17 WHEREAS, he graduated from the police academy in
18 1989; within 10 years, he had worked his way up to the
19 Detective Division investigating homicides; he later became
20 a member of the International Homicide Investigators
21 Association; in 1999, Montgomery County Sheriff Steve
22 Marshall appointed him as Chief Deputy, and he served in
23 that capacity for 15 years; and
