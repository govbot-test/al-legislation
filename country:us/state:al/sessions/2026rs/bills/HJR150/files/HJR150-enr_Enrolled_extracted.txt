9 WHEREAS, Kappa Alpha Psi Fraternity®, Incorporated,
10 was founded on January 5, 1911, on the campus of Indiana
11 University in Bloomington, Indiana; and
12 WHEREAS, guided by its noble fundamental purpose,
13 "Achievement in Every Field of Human Endeavor," the
14 fraternity has, for more than a century, cultivated
15 leadership, scholarship, and service, a legacy first
16 exemplified by its 10 distinguished founders, the majority
17 of whom earned collegiate degrees and achieved notable
18 success in their respective professions; and
19 WHEREAS, Kappa Alpha Psi Fraternity®, Incorporated,
20 has grown to include more than 250,000 members worldwide,
21 with over 700 undergraduate, alumni, and international
22 chapters, forming a powerful global network committed to
23 excellence and brotherhood; and
24 WHEREAS, through the Kappa Alpha Psi Foundation, the
25 fraternity sponsors impactful programs that promote
26 community service, social welfare, youth mentorship, and
27 academic scholarship, thereby strengthening communities
28 across the nation and abroad; and
