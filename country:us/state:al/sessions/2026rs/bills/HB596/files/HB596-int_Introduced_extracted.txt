4 SYNOPSIS:
5 This bill would create the Main Street Revival
6 Act to revitalize small municipal downtowns and
7 commercial districts by providing redevelopment grant
8 programs to support business startups, stimulate local
9 entrepreneurship, reuse vacant or underutilized
10 properties, preserve historic buildings, support mixed
11 retail use and mentorship programs, and support local
12 governments by providing technical and financial
13 support to update planning, zoning, and land use
14 regulation.
15 This bill would require the Alabama Department
16 of Economic and Community Affairs to administer the
17 program.
18 This bill would also create a rehabilitation tax
19 credit for renovations of certain
20 properties.
