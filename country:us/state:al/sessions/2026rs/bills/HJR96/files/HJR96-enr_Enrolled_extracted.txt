4 RECOGNIZING ALABAMA ARMY NATIONAL GUARD STAFF SERGEANT
5 RONALD WAYNE HURST UPON HIS RETIREMENT.
6
7 WHEREAS, it is with profound appreciation and deepest
8 admiration that we recognize Staff Sergeant (SSG) Ronald
9 Wayne Hurst for more than 20 years of faithful military
10 service, first as a member of the United States Navy and
11 more recently as a distinguished noncommissioned officer in
12 the Alabama Army National Guard; and
13 WHEREAS, throughout his noble career, SSG Hurst has
14 demonstrated unwavering dedication to duty, mission
15 accomplishment, and the welfare of sailors, soldiers, and
16 their families; and
17 WHEREAS, his military journey began in the U.S. Navy,
18 as a three-year term from June 1987 to June 1990; Hurst
19 served in the Naval Presidential Ceremonial Honor Guard and
20 was stationed in Washington, D.C., where his duties included
21 funerals in Arlington Cemetery; Hurst also stood guard at
22 the door of then-Department of Defense Secretary Dick
23 Cheney, as well as participated in Presidential galas and
24 inaugurations; and
25 WHEREAS, following a 15-year break in service, SSG
26 Hurst reenlisted in the Alabama Army National Guard in
27 December 2005 as a Horizontal Construction Engineer; he has
28 held key assignments with the 1312th Engineer Detachment,
