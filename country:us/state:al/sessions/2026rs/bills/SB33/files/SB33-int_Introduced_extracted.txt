4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, veterans who were exposed to
6 dangerous levels of radiation due to the atomic bomb
7 and weapon testing from 1944 to 1962 may be issued a
8 distinctive license tag for a motor vehicle.
9 This bill would expand the list of veterans
10 eligible to receive a distinctive license tag based on
11 exposure to dangerous levels of radiation.
