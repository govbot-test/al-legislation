4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Current law provides for a vessel registration
6 fee in an amount based on length of the vessel, as well
7 as an additional fixed registration fee of $5 per
8 vessel.
9 This bill would consolidate these fees and
10 provide for the distribution of these fees.
11 This bill would also make nonsubstantive,
12 technical revisions to update the existing code
13 language to current style
