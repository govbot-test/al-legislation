4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, an individual who qualifies
6 for a military, veteran, or gold star family
7 distinctive license plate may be required to recertify
8 his or her eligibility every five years.
9 This bill would exempt individuals whose
10 qualifying status is permanent from being required to
11 recertify their eligibility every five years for a
12 military, veteran, or gold star family distinctive
13 license plate.
