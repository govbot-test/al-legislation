6 TO BE ENTITLED
7 AN ACT
8
9 Related to legal tender; to authorize the rounding of
10 the total amount of any in-person cash transaction to the
11 nearest five cents.
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
13 Section 1. (a) The amount of an in-person cash
14 transaction may be rounded to the nearest five cents using the
15 following methodology:
16 (1) If the final digit of the amount in the transaction
17 is one or two, the final digit must be rounded down to zero.
18 (2) If the final digit is three or four, the final
19 digit must be rounded up to five.
20 (3) If the final digit is six or seven, the final digit
21 must be rounded down to five.
22 (4) If the final digit is eight or nine, the final
23 digit must be rounded up to zero.
24 (5) If the final digit is five or zero, the final digit
25 must not be rounded.
26 (b) Subsection (a) does not alter the sales price, the
27 amount of tax collected under Section 40-23-26, Code of
28 Alabama 1975, or any other authority providing for the

29 collection of sales tax, or any surcharges, assessments, or
30 fees imposed on the sale.
31 (c)(1) Subsection (a) may apply to the amount of the
32 transaction or to the amount of change tendered to the
33 purchaser.
34 (2) Subsection (a) shall not apply to any transaction
35 for which payment is made by a method other than cash,
36 including electronic funds transfer, check, gift card, money
37 order, or credit card. If the transaction is made using both
38 cash and another form of payment, subsection (a) shall apply
39 to the portion of the transaction amount that is paid for
40 using cash.
41 (3) Subsection (a) shall not alter or affect any
42 payment amount authorized, cleared, or settled through any
43 payment system that does not use cash.
44 (4) Subsection (a) shall not apply to any transaction
45 where payment is made to a governmental entity. For purposes
46 of this subdivision, "governmental entity" means any state or
47 local agency or instrumentality thereof located in the state.
48 (d) The Department of Revenue shall post a notice on
49 the department's website stating the authorization to begin
50 rounding transactions pursuant to this section.
51 Section 2. This act shall become effective immediately.
