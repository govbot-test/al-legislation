4 HR____ SUPPORTING THE CITY OF SPANISH FORT AND BALDWIN
5 COUNTY FOR NAMING A PORTION OF STATE ROUTE 225 "AMERICAN
6 PATRIOTS WAY" IN HONOR OF UNITED STATES SERVICE MEMBERS AND
7 VETERANS.
8
9 WHEREAS, the founding of the United States of America
10 and the service and sacrifice of those men and women who
11 have worked diligently to claim, preserve, protect, and
12 defend the rights and freedoms enjoyed by the people of this
13 great nation since its formation have come at a great cost;
14 and
15 WHEREAS, the City of Spanish Fort and Baldwin County
16 enjoy a rich history related to the American experience
17 which shapes our state's values, culture, and community; and
18 WHEREAS, there are many American Patriots who have
19 made great sacrifices in their support and defense of this
20 nation, and the landscape of Spanish Fort and Baldwin County
21 give evidence of their lives and sacrifice from the nation's
22 beginning to the present; now therefore,
23 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE
24 LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA, That this body offers its support to
25 the City Council of the City of Spanish Fort and Baldwin
26 County for celebrating the 250th Anniversary of the signing
27 of the Declaration of Independence and honoring those
28 American Patriots who have worked diligently to claim,
Page 1

29 preserve, protect, and defend the rights and freedoms
