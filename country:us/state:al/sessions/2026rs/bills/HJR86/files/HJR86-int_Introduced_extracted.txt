5 INTERNATIONAL PRESIDENT OF THETA CHI FRATERNITY.

7 WHEREAS, Tobin "Toby" Roth was installed as
8 International President of Theta Chi Fraternity on August 5,
9 2024, during the 168th Anniversary Convention in New
10 Orleans, Louisiana; and
11 WHEREAS, Mr. Roth has faithfully served on Theta
12 Chi's Grand Chapter since 2018 in several key leadership
13 roles, including International Vice President, International
14 Chaplain, and International Marshal; and
15 WHEREAS, Theta Chi Fraternity, one of the oldest
16 men's collegiate fraternities in North America, was founded
17 in 1856 and has initiated more than 200,000 members and
18 established 245 chapters with the mission of developing
19 college-age men into successful students, good citizens,
20 lifelong brothers, and resolute leaders; and
21 WHEREAS, Mr. Roth was initiated into the Alpha Phi
22 Chapter of Theta Chi Fraternity at The University of
23 Alabama, where the bonds of brotherhood, personal
24 development, and leadership were first instilled in him; and
25 WHEREAS, prior to joining the Grand Chapter, Mr. Roth
26 served as House Corporation President for the Alpha Phi
27 Chapter from 2014 to 2018 and was an alumni delegate at the
28 158th Anniversary Convention in 2014; and
Page 1
158th Anniversary Convention in 2014; and
HJR86 INTRODUCED
29 WHEREAS, as an undergraduate, Mr. Roth held the
30 positions of Vice President and Scholarship Chair of the
31 Alpha Phi Chapter and served as a collegiate delegate to the
32 132nd Anniversary Convention in 1988, where he received the
33 prestigious Key Man Award; in 1990, he was honored as "Greek
34 Man of the Year" by the Order of Omega as the outstanding
35 senior fraternity man at The University of Alabama; and
36 WHEREAS, in his role as International President, Mr.
37 Roth continues to uphold and embody the ideals and values of
38 Theta Chi Fraternity by dedicating his time, energy, and
39 talents to supporting its members and chapters nationwide;
40 now therefore,
41 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA, BOTH
42 HOUSES THEREOF CONCURRING, That Tobin "Toby" Roth be
43 formally and enthusiastically commended for his lifelong
44 dedication to Theta Chi Fraternity and congratulated on his
45 installation as International President, with deep
46 appreciation for his outstanding leadership, character, and
47 unwavering commitment to the brotherhood.
