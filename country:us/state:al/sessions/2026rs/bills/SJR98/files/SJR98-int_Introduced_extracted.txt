4 SJR___ NAMING THE PORTION OF ALABAMA HIGHWAY 9 FROM MT.
5 HEBRON ROAD TO BOSWELL ROAD IN ELMORE COUNTY THE LAMAR S.
6 "BUBBA" WOODHAM, JR. HIGHWAY.
7
8 WHEREAS, it is with the highest accolade that this
9 body honors Lamar S. "Bubba" Woodham, Jr. by requesting the
10 State Department of Transportation designate the portion of
11 Alabama Highway 9 from Mt. Hebron Road to Boswell Road in
12 Elmore County, Alabama, the Lamar S. "Bubba" Woodham, Jr.
13 Highway; and
14 WHEREAS, Bubba Woodham was born in Montgomery in
15 1953; he graduated from Sidney Lanier High School and
16 matriculated to Auburn University where he earned a Bachelor
17 of Science in Civil Engineering; and
18 WHEREAS, Bubba Woodham continued a generational
19 legacy when he began his career with the Alabama Department
20 of Transportation (ALDOT) as a laborer in a survey party,
21 working summers while attending high school and college; he
22 progressed through multiple ALDOT bureaus, including
23 Materials and Tests, Maintenance, Design, Engineering
24 Services, Office Engineer, Right-of-Way, Assistant Chief
25 Engineer for Administration, and Deputy Director of
26 Administration, retiring after more than 47 years of
27 dedicated service to the State of Alabama; and
28 WHEREAS, Bubba Woodham is a Registered Professional
Page 1
WHEREAS, Bubba Woodham is a Registered Professional
SJR98 INTRODUCED
29 Civil Engineer and Professional Land Surveyor in the state;
30 he is also a retired Commander in the United States Navy
31 Civil Engineer Corps; he and his wife of nearly 50 years,
32 Carol, make their home on Lake Martin; together they share
33 three children and nine grandchildren; now therefore,
34 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA, BOTH
35 HOUSES THEREOF CONCURRING, That to honor Lamar S. "Bubba"
36 Woodham, Jr. for his years of service to the State of
37 Alabama, the State Department of Transportation is
38 encouraged to coordinate with local governing officials to
39 consider designating the portion of Alabama Highway 9 from
40 Mt. Hebron Road to Boswell Road in Elmore County the Lamar
41 S. "Bubba" Woodham, Jr. Highway and to erect appropriate
42 signs or markers, consistent with applicable requirements
43 and laws, in recognition of this designation.
