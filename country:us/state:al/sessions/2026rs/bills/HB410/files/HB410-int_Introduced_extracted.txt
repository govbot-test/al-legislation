9 Relating to Limestone County; to amend Section
10 45-42-230, Code of Alabama 1975; and to further provide for
11 the compensation of the sheriff.
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
13 Section 1. Section 45-42-230, Code of Alabama 1975, is
14 amended to read as follows:
15 "§45-42-230
16 (a) Beginning with the next term of office, the Sheriff
17 of Limestone County shall receive an annual salary that is 85
18 percent of the annual salary of the highest paid Thirty-ninth
19 Judicial Circuit Court Judge.
20 (b) Beginning January 18, 2027, the Sheriff of
21 Limestone County shall receive a base annual salary of one
22 hundred forty-nine thousand dollars ($149,000) per annum. This
23 salary shall be in lieu of the salary provided in subsection
24 (a) which shall be void.
25 (c) The sheriff shall continue to receive the same
26 cost-of-living increases and other uniform increases as
27 granted to all county employees by the county commission.
28 (b)(d) The sheriff shall not personally financially
