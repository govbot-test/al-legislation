HB273 INTRODUCED
29 Section 1. Section 11-43-12, Code of Alabama 1975, is
30 amended to read as follows:
31 "§11-43-12
32 (a) No alderman or officer or employee of the
33 municipality shall be have, directly or indirectly,
34 interesteda financial interest in any work, business, or
35 contract, the expense, price, or consideration of which is
36 paid from the treasury, nor shall any member of the council or
37 officer of the municipality be surety for any person having a
38 contract, work, or business with such the municipality for the
39 performance of which a surety may be required.
40 (b) Notwithstanding subsection (a), an employee, if
41 otherwise qualified, is not prohibited from accepting or
42 receiving funds from a community development block grant or
43 other grant program, where the funds flow through the
44 municipal treasury, provided:
45 (1) The benefit to the employee is similar and in a
46 like manner to other recipients of the grant program; and
47 (2) The employee does not play any decision-making role
48 in eligibility for receiving funds from the grant program.
49 (c) The exception in subsection (b) is not available to
50 any municipal elected official.
51 (b)(d) Any person individual who violates any of the
52 provisions of this section shall be guilty of a misdemeanor
53 and, on conviction thereof, shall be fined not less than
54 $50.00 fifty dollars ($50) nor more than $1,000.00one thousand
55 dollars ($1,000), and may also be sentenced to hard labor for
56 the county for not more than six months."
