4 Relating to the City of Jemison; to further provide for the
5 sale of draft beer in the city.
6 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
7 Section 1. The Jemison City Council, by resolution, may
8 authorize the sale or distribution of draft beer by retail
9 licensees of the Alcoholic Beverage Control Board in the City
10 of Jemison. The authorization shall include the sale of draft
11 beer for off-premises consumption in a growler, keg, or other
12 similar container by persons licensed by the board to make
13 retail sales for off-premises consumption.
14 Section 2. This act shall become effective immediately.
