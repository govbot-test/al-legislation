1 Enrolled, An Act,

3
4 Relating to retirement benefits; to add Section
5 12–18–166 to the Code of Alabama 1975, to authorize qualifying
6 circuit clerks to participate in both a supernumerary circuit
7 clerk program and the Employees' Retirement System.
8 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
9 Section 1. Section 12-18-166 is added to the Code of
10 Alabama 1975, to read as follows:
11 §12-18-166
12 (a) No individual may participate in both a
13 supernumerary circuit clerk program and the Employees’
14 Retirement System based on the same service.
15 (b) An individual who independently qualifies for a
16 supernumerary circuit clerk program and the Employees'
17 Retirement System based on separate years of service may
18 participate in both programs.
19 Section 2. This act shall become effective on July 1,
20 2026.
