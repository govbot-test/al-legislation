15
16 Related to sales and use tax; to exempt the Cook Museum
17 of Natural Science from the payment of state sales and use
18 taxes; and to allow municipalities and counties to exempt this
19 entity from local sales and use taxes.
20 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
21 Section 1. (a) The Cook Museum of Natural Science is
22 exempt from paying any state sales and use taxes.
23 (b) Any county or municipality may exempt the Cook
24 Museum of Natural Science from the payment of county or
25 municipal sales and use taxes in accordance with Section
26 40-23-4.01, Code of Alabama 1975.
27 Section 2. This act shall become effective on September
28 1, 2026.
