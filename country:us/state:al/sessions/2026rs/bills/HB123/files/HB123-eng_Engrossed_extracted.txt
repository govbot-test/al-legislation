6 TO BE ENTITLED
7 AN ACT
8
9 Related to sales and use tax; to exempt the Cook Museum
10 of Natural Science and the McWane Science Center from the
11 payment of state sales and use taxes; and to allow
12 municipalities and counties to exempt this entity from local
13 sales and use taxes.
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
15 Section 1. (a) The Cook Museum of Natural Science and
16 the McWane Science Center are exempt from paying any state
17 sales and use taxes.
18 (b) Any county or municipality may exempt the Cook
19 Museum of Natural Science or the McWane Science Center from
20 the payment of county or municipal sales and use taxes in
21 accordance with Section 40-23-4.01, Code of Alabama 1975.
22 Section 2. This act shall become effective on September
23 1, 2026.
