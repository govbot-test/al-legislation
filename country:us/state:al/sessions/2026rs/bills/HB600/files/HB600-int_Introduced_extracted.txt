4 SYNOPSIS:
5 This bill would create the Skilled Trades
6 Workforce Recovery Program Act to provide full tuition
7 for rural residents enrolled in eligible trade
8 programs; provide grants to employers in rural areas
9 who sponsor registered apprenticeships in high-demand
10 fields; offer stipends to students and trainees for
11 purchasing required tools or commuting to job sites and
12 training facilities; fund a statewide campaign to
13 promote trade careers; and to recognize prior work
14 experience and military training toward trade
15 certifications.
16 This bill would require the Alabama Department
17 of Workforce to administer the programs.

35 Section 1. This act shall be known and may be cited as
36 the Skilled Trades Workforce Recovery Program Act.
37 Section 2. The purpose of this act is to increase
38 workforce participation in high-demand skilled trades by
39 expanding access to affordable training and incentivizing
40 employer-based apprenticeships, especially in rural and
41 economically distressed areas.
42 Section 3. For purposes of this act, the following
43 terms have the following meanings:
44 (1) DEPARTMENT. The Department of Workforce.
45 (2) ELIGIBLE PROGRAM. A trade certification program in
46 fields identified as high-need by the department.
47 (3) RURAL AREA. A jurisdiction designated by the
48 department as rural based on population thresholds and
49 distance from major urban centers.
