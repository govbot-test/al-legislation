6 WHEREAS, with great pride and special pleasure, Drake
7 State Community and Technical College (Drake State) is
8 highly commended for providing accessible, high-quality
9 education to students in North Alabama for 65 years; and
10 WHEREAS, Drake State was established in 1961 in
11 Huntsville, Alabama, with 27 students enrolled in four
12 technical programs designed to meet regional workforce
13 needs; originally named Huntsville State Vocational
14 Technical School, the institution was renamed in 1966 to
15 honor Joseph Fanning Drake, a distinguished educational
16 leader and long-time President of Alabama Agricultural and
17 Mechanical University; and
18 WHEREAS, in 1973, Drake State achieved technical
19 college status and began awarding the Associate in Applied
20 Technology degree; in 2013, the institution became J.F.
21 Drake State Community and Technical College, uniquely
22 combining comprehensive community college academic transfer
23 programs with advanced technical and workforce training; and
24 WHEREAS, for 65 years, Drake State has served as a
25 critical workforce development partner for Huntsville and
26 North Alabama; the college offers Associate in Applied
27 Science, Associate in Arts, and Associate in Science
28 degrees, as well as short-term certificates and
