5 COLLEGE ON ITS 65TH ANNIVERSARY.
6
7 WHEREAS, with great pride and special pleasure, Drake
8 State Community and Technical College (Drake State) is
9 highly commended for providing accessible, high-quality
10 education to students in North Alabama for 65 years; and
11 WHEREAS, Drake State was established in 1961 in
12 Huntsville, Alabama, with 27 students enrolled in four
13 technical programs designed to meet regional workforce
14 needs; originally named Huntsville State Vocational
15 Technical School, the institution was renamed in 1966 to
16 honor Joseph Fanning Drake, a distinguished educational
17 leader and long-time President of Alabama Agricultural and
18 Mechanical University; and
19 WHEREAS, in 1973, Drake State achieved technical
20 college status and began awarding the Associate in Applied
21 Technology degree; in 2013, the institution became J.F.
22 Drake State Community and Technical College, uniquely
23 combining comprehensive community college academic transfer
24 programs with advanced technical and workforce training; and
25 WHEREAS, for 65 years, Drake State has served as a
26 critical workforce development partner for Huntsville and
27 North Alabama; the college offers Associate in Applied
28 Science, Associate in Arts, and Associate in Science
