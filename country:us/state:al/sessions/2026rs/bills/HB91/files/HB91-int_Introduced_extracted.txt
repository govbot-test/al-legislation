4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, there is an additional
6 docket fee assigned in all circuit, district, and
7 municipal court filings. The fee allocated to the
8 sheriff's office may only be expended for the operation
9 of the jail.
10 This bill would allow the funds allocated to the
11 sheriff's office to be expended to support the
12 functions of the office of the sheriff.
