5 Regular Session (Acts 1957, p. 240), Act 865, 1978 Regular
6 Session (Acts 1978, p. 1294), Act 93-565, 1993 Regular Session
7 (Acts 1993, p. 944), Act 98-477, 1998 Regular Session (Acts

10 Act 2004-435, 2004 Regular Session (Acts 2004, p. 435), Act
11 2009-374, 2009 Regular Session (Acts 2009, p. 698), and Act
12 2021-466, 2021 Regular Session, relating to the compensation
13 of the sheriff.
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
15 Section 1. (a) Until January 18, 2027, the Sheriff of
16 Talladega County shall continue to receive the same base
17 annual salary the sheriff is receiving on May 31, 2025.
18 (b) Beginning on the effective date of this act, in
19 addition to the base annual salary authorized in subsection
20 (a), the sheriff shall receive an expense allowance of fifteen
21 thousand two hundred dollars and thirty-two cents ($15,200.32)
22 per annum, payable in equal monthly installments from the
23 county general fund.
24 (c) Beginning January 18, 2027, the sheriff shall
25 receive a base annual salary of one hundred twelve thousand
26 dollars ($112,000) per annum, payable in equal monthly
27 installments from the county general fund, and the salary and
28 expense allowance authorized in subsections (a) and (b) are
