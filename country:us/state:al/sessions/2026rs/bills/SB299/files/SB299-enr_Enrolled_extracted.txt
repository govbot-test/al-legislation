SB299 Enrolled
1 Enrolled, An Act,
2
3 Relating to Barbour County; to amend Section 45-3-60,
4 Code of Alabama 1975, to further provide for the coroner's
5 mileage rate.
6 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
7 Section 1. Section 45-3-60, Code of Alabama 1975, is
8 amended to read as follows:
9 "§45-3-60
10 In addition to any compensation or expense allowance
11 now provided by Section 12-19-192, the Coroner of Barbour
12 County shall receive an expense allowance and mileage to be
13 set by the county commission by resolution not to exceed four
14 hundred dollars ($400) a month expense allowance and fifty
15 cents ($.50) per mile a mileage reimbursement rate equal to
16 the Internal Revenue Service's business use rate, to be paid
17 from the county general fund."
18 Section 2. This act shall become effective on October
19 1, 2026.
