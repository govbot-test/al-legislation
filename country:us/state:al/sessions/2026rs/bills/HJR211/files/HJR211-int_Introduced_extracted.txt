5 FRATERNITY, INCORPORATED, DAY IN THE STATE OF ALABAMA.
6
7 WHEREAS, we are pleased to recognize March 17, 2026,
8 as Omega Psi Phi Fraternity, Incorporated, Day in the State
9 of Alabama; and
10 WHEREAS, a nonprofit social and service-based
11 organization, Omega Psi Phi Fraternity, Incorporated, was
12 founded on November 17, 1911, on the campus of Howard
13 University in Washington, D.C.; there are now more than 700
14 chapters located in the United States and around the world
15 with Alabama having 33 of those said chapters with 18
16 graduate and 15 undergraduate; and
17 WHEREAS, Mr. Darryl Sinkfield of Montgomery, Alabama,
18 serves as the Omega Day Chair; Mr. Barrington L. Dames of
19 Huntsville, Alabama, serves as the 21st State of Alabama
20 Representative of Omega Psi Phi Fraternity, Incorporated;
21 Mr. Christopher A. Brooks of Birmingham, Alabama, serves as
22 the 25th – 7th District Representative of Omega Psi Phi
23 Fraternity, Incorporated, and Mr. Ricky L. Lewis of Los
24 Angeles, California, serves as the 42nd Grand Basileus of
25 Omega Psi Phi Fraternity, Incorporated; and
26 WHEREAS, Omega Psi Phi Fraternity, Incorporated, has
27 continuously played a progressive, cooperative, and
28 constructive role in the civic and political life of our
Page 1
constructive role in the civic and political life of our

29 communities, the nation, and worldwide for more than 100
30 years; now therefore,
31 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA, BOTH
32 HOUSES THEREOF CONCURRING, That we take great pleasure in
33 recognizing March 17, 2026, as Omega Psi Phi Fraternity,
34 Incorporated, Day in the State of Alabama.
35 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That a copy of this
36 resolution be presented to Brother Barrington L. Dames, who
37 serves as State Representative of the organization, with the
38 highest praise and sincere best wishes for all future
39 service endeavors.
