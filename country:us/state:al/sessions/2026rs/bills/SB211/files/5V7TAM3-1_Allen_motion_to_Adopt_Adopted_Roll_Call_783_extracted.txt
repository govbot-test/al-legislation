4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, certain state public
6 educational authorities may implement a sick leave bank
7 to facilitate the transfer of sick leave or
8 catastrophic leave from one employee to another,
9 subject to certain limitations.
10 This bill would require the State Board of
11 Education to adopt a model sick leave policy providing
12 for the use of sick leave banks, catastrophic leave,
13 and the donation of sick leave.
14 This bill would remove the cap limiting how much
15 catastrophic sick leave an employee may donate to
16 another employee.
17 This bill would require the sick leave policy to
18 allow employees to donate sick leave directly to
19 another employee, subject to certain limitations, even
20 if the donor and receiving employee work for different
21 education authorities.
22 This bill would require each educational
23 authority to implement a sick leave bank policy that
24 does not contradict the requirements of this bill.
25 This bill would also revise the duties of sick
26 leave bank committees and require policies developed by
27 local sick leave bank committees to be approved by the
28 educational authority.
