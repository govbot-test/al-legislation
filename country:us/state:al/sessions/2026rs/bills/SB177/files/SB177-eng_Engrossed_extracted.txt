9 Relating to health insurance; to require that health
10 benefit plans offered in this state which pay or reimburse for
11 breast examinations, including mammography, provide the
12 coverage without imposing a deductible, copayment, or
13 coinsurance charge on the beneficiary.
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
15 Section 1. (a) For purposes of this section, the
16 following terms have the following meanings:
17 (1) COST-SHARING REQUIREMENT. A deductible, copayment,
18 coinsurance, or similar expense borne by the insured for a
19 covered benefit under a health benefit plan.
20 (2) DIAGNOSTIC BREAST EXAMINATION. A medically
21 necessary and appropriate examination of the breast by
22 technology that includes, but is not limited to,
23 contrast-enhanced mammography, diagnostic mammography, breast
24 magnetic resonance imaging, breast ultrasound, or molecular
25 breast imaging that is used to:
26 a. Evaluate an abnormality seen or suspected from a
27 screening examination for breast cancer; or
28 b. Evaluate an abnormality detected by another means of
