4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, owners of certain dams and
6 reservoirs may elect to have their dams and reservoirs
7 inspected and file with the local emergency management
8 agency an emergency action plan if the dam or reservoir
9 has a high or significant hazard potential.
10 This bill would require the Alabama Emergency
11 Management Agency to administer the Alabama Dam Safety
12 Program and replace all duties of local emergency
13 management agencies under the program with the Alabama
14 Emergency Management Agency.
15 Also under existing law, participating owners
16 must notify the local emergency management agency of
17 the construction, modification, or removal of a dam or
18 reservoir.
19 This bill would require participating owners to
20 submit plans for constructing, modifying, or removing a
21 dam or reservoir to the Alabama Emergency Management
22 Agency for approval.
23 This bill would authorize the agency to adopt
24 rules to administer the program, including approval
25 procedures and fees.
26 This bill would require the agency submit to the
27 Governor and Legislature a program implementation plan
28 and any operational and financial needs of the agency
