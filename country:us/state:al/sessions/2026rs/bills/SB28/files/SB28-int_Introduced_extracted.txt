4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, individuals who are retired
7 to work in certain capacities and at certain levels of
8 compensation without suspension of his or her
9 retirement allowance.
10 This bill would authorize any individual who is
11 retired under the Employees' Retirement System or the
12 Teachers' Retirement System, has the applicable Alabama
13 Peace Officers' Standards and Training certification,
14 and was classified as either a law enforcement officer
15 or a state policeman prior to retirement to perform
16 duties as a sheriff's deputy or municipal police
17 officer without suspension of his or her retirement
18 allowance, provided the compensation earned does not
19 exceed $52,000 per year.
20 This bill would also authorize any individual
21 who is retired under the Employees' Retirement System
22 or the Teachers' Retirement System and is licensed to
23 practice law in this state to perform duties as an
24 assistant district attorney without suspension of his
25 or her retirement allowance, provided the compensation
26 earned does not exceed $52,000 per year.
