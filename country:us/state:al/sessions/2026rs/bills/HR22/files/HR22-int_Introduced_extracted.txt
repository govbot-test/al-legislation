5 ON THE OCCASION OF HIS RETIREMENT.

7 WHEREAS, we extend our sincerest commendation and
8 gratitude to Robertsdale, Alabama, Police Chief Bradley
9 Kendrick, who faithfully upheld the responsibilities he was
10 given to serve and protect the citizens of Robertsdale with
11 more than 36 years of law enforcement experience; and
12 WHEREAS, Chief Kendrick served 23 of those years as
13 Chief of Police for the City of Robertsdale Police
14 Department; Mayor Ruthie Campbell describes Chief Kendrick
15 as a staple in the community who will be sorely missed; and
16 WHEREAS, a native of Robertsdale, Chief Kendrick grew
17 up in the community and is a graduate of Robertsdale High
18 School; he became a leader known for honor, integrity, and
19 dedication; and
20 WHEREAS, Chief Kendrick was a founding member of the
21 Robertsdale Major Crimes Unit, held top leadership positions
22 with the Alabama Association of Chiefs of Police, and was a
23 contributing member of the Alabama Peace Officers' Standards
24 and Training Commission's education committee; and
25 WHEREAS, known by colleagues for his humble nature,
26 unique sense of humor, and ability to balance numerous
27 duties at once, Chief Kendrick led the Robertsdale Police
