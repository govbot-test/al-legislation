HJR315 Enrolled
1 Enrolled, An Act,
2
3 EXPRESSING GRATITUDE TO THE ALABAMA CONGRESSIONAL
4 DELEGATION.
5
6 WHEREAS, Alabama's hospitals have provided
7 exceptional, quality care in communities across Alabama for
8 over 100 years; and
9 WHEREAS, Alabama's hospitals not only provide care to
10 patients 24 hours a day, seven days a week, but also
11 generate over $25 billion economic impact in Alabama each
12 year, directly employing 77,000 people; and
13 WHEREAS, each hospital job supports another job in
14 the community and rural hospitals are often the largest
15 employer in its area; and
16 WHEREAS, hospitals in Alabama are facing significant
17 challenges with high rates of uninsured patients, a
18 population with chronic conditions, and the lowest Medicare
19 reimbursement rate in the country, forcing eight rural
20 hospitals to close since 2011; and
21 WHEREAS, the formula for calculating the Medicare
22 hospital wage index is seriously flawed, heavily distorted
23 by exceptions, and creates a self-perpetuating disadvantage
24 for low-wage and rural states, resulting in Alabama
25 hospitals being paid substantially less for the same care;
26 and
27 WHEREAS, reforming the wage index is essential to
28 ensure access to care for every American, especially those
Page 1
ensure access to care for every American, especially those
HJR315 Enrolled
29 in rural states; and
30 WHEREAS, the Alabama Congressional Delegation has
31 encouraged the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services to
32 address the wage index disparity by implementing a rule
33 change to reflect modern patterns in labor commuting for
34 rural health care workers; now therefore,
35 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA, BOTH
36 HOUSES THEREOF CONCURRING, That the members of this body
37 express our gratitude to the Alabama Congressional
38 Delegation for their commitment to finding relief to this
39 decades old problem that has crippled hospitals.
40 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That we encourage the Centers
41 for Medicare and Medicaid Services to consider an adjustment
42 to the wage index through rulemaking to ensure an equitable
43 Medicare payment formula for all hospitals to protect access
44 to care for all Americans.
