4 HJR___ CELEBRATING THE LIFE AND LEGACY OF GEORGE W. BARBER,
5 JR.
6
7 WHEREAS, it is with profound sorrow and deep respect
8 that we record the passing of George W. Barber, Jr. of
9 Birmingham, Alabama, on February 15, 2026, at the age of 85;
10 and
11 WHEREAS, an extraordinary citizen of this state,
12 George Barber was a lifelong entrepreneur whose keen
13 business acumen and competitive drive were equaled only by
14 his generosity of spirit and steadfast humility; he
15 consistently chose to place others in the spotlight rather
16 than himself, and he will be remembered for his generous
17 spirit and enduring influence on all who were privileged to
18 know him; he leaves behind a world made better by his
19 presence and a legacy that will long endure the passage of
20 time; and
21 WHEREAS, George Barber was born on September 11,
22 1940, to George W. Barber and Julia W. Barber; after
23 graduating from Auburn University, where he was a member of
24 Sigma Alpha Epsilon, he enlisted in the Alabama Air National
25 Guard and faithfully served six years with the 117th
26 Tactical Reconnaissance Wing; and
27 WHEREAS, his business leadership was rooted in a
28 century of family tradition; born into his family's dairy
