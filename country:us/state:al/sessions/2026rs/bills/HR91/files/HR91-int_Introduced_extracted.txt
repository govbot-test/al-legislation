5 ALABAMA WOMEN'S COMMISSION.

7 WHEREAS, we take great pride and pleasure in
8 commending Cathy Waller McGowin for eight years of exemplary
9 service as a member of the Alabama Women's Commission; and
10 WHEREAS, Cathy Waller McGowin is a native of
11 Birmingham, Alabama, where she attended Huffman High School,
12 and in 1984 relocated to Mobile, Alabama, with her husband,
13 Dr. Jeff McGowin, as he began his career in orthopedic
14 surgery; and
15 WHEREAS, Mrs. McGowin earned her nursing degree from
16 the Capstone College of Nursing at The University of Alabama
17 in 1980 and was inducted into the Sigma Theta Tau
18 International Honor Society for Nursing; and
19 WHEREAS, she has devoted her professional career to
20 nursing and health care, serving patients in both Jefferson
21 and Mobile Counties in a variety of clinical settings,
22 including Emergency Care, Critical Care, Post-Surgical Care,
23 and Cardiac Rehabilitation; and
24 WHEREAS, in addition to her professional service,
25 Mrs. McGowin has demonstrated a longstanding commitment to
26 community involvement and volunteerism in Mobile County,
27 including service on the Board of Directors for the Junior
28 League of Mobile and the Medical Alliance for Mobile County;

League of Mobile and the Medical Alliance for Mobile County;

30 WHEREAS, her charitable work includes volunteering

32 programs and summer camp experiences for children ages seven

34 4th grade students in mathematics through Partners in

37 WHEREAS, as an active community volunteer, Mrs.
38 McGowin has chaired fundraising efforts for numerous

43 WHEREAS, in recognition of her leadership and
44 commitment to advancing the status of women, Mrs. McGowin
45 was appointed by Governor Kay Ivey in 2017 to serve as a
46 Commissioner on the Alabama Women's Commission and has
47 served as Chair of the Commission since October 2020; and
48 WHEREAS, during her tenure with the Alabama Women's

51 program, a collaborative study with The University of
52 Alabama examining the effects of parental cell phone use on

54 designed for agency leaders across the State of Alabama; and
55 WHEREAS, Mrs. McGowin is also an active member of St.
56 Ignatius Catholic Church, where she has volunteered her time
