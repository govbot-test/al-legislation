29 corporation that is not incorporated pursuant to Title 11,

32 of Examiners of Public Accounts in the same manner as provided
33 in Chapter 5A of Title 41. The department may charge a

35 (b) After June 14, 2023, beginning after the close of

42 the department suspects fraud or mismanagement of funds by the

44 fee for the audits or examinations.
45 (c) This section does not apply to or affect any public
46 water supply system, waterworks plant, or water distribution
47 system owned or operated by a municipality or county, or by a
48 public corporation, board, or other entity organized, created,
49 or incorporated pursuant to Title 11."
