9 Relating to counties and municipalities; to authorize
10 Class 1 municipalities to establish and regulate a nonprofit
11 community land trust for the purpose of creating affordable
12 housing alternatives; to provide for qualifications and
13 appointment of members of the board of directors; to authorize
14 an annual audit of the trust by the Department of Examiners of
15 Public Accounts; to require the community land trust to
16 publish eligibility selection criteria; to allow a community
17 land trust to acquire and lease real property to qualified
18 low-income and moderate-income lessees through 99-year ground
19 leases; to require all leases to include an option for the
20 trust or organizing municipality to buy back the property
21 interest; to authorize ad valorem property tax exemptions for
22 certain community land trust property; and to establish
23 standards for ad valorem assessment.
24 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:

26 as the Alabama Community Land Trust Act of 2026.
