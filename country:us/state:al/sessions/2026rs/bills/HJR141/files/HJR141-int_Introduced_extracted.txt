4 HJR___ CONGRATULATING THE ALBERTVILLE HIGH SCHOOL CHEER TEAM
5 FOR WINNING THE NATIONAL CHAMPIONSHIP.
6
7 WHEREAS, the Alabama Legislature takes great pride in
8 recognizing outstanding accomplishments by our state's
9 students and athletes; and
10 WHEREAS, the Albertville High School Cheer Team has
11 achieved remarkable success by winning the 2026 National
12 High School Cheerleading Championship on February 1, 2026,
13 in the Large Gameday Division, bringing home a team trophy,
14 individual medals, and white champion jackets; and
15 WHEREAS, the national championship follows
16 Albertville's Class 7A state championship on November 10,
17 2025; it was the team's first Alabama High School Athletic
18 Association sports title in school history, and now the
19 Albertville Aggies can add National winnings to their
20 impressive repertoire; and
21 WHEREAS, the team has demonstrated a consistent level
22 of excellence by also winning the Alabama 7A North Regional
23 Gameday, the Universal Cheerleaders Association (UCA) Magic
24 City Regional Large Varsity Gameday, the UCA Space Center
25 Regional Large Varsity Gameday, the American Spirit League
26 Gameday Championship, the American Spirit League Grand
27 Championship, and the American Spirit League Best
28 Choreography of the Year Award; and
