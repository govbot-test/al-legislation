3 CONGRATULATING THE ALBERTVILLE HIGH SCHOOL CHEER TEAM FOR

6 WHEREAS, the Alabama Legislature takes great pride in
7 recognizing outstanding accomplishments by our state's
8 students and athletes; and
9 WHEREAS, the Albertville High School Cheer Team has
10 achieved remarkable success by winning the 2026 National
11 High School Cheerleading Championship on February 1, 2026,
12 in the Large Gameday Division, bringing home a team trophy,
13 individual medals, and white champion jackets; and
14 WHEREAS, the national championship follows
15 Albertville's Class 7A state championship on November 10,
16 2025; it was the team's first Alabama High School Athletic
17 Association sports title in school history, and now the
18 Albertville Aggies can add National winnings to their
19 impressive repertoire; and

22 Gameday, the Universal Cheerleaders Association (UCA) Magic
23 City Regional Large Varsity Gameday, the UCA Space Center
24 Regional Large Varsity Gameday, the American Spirit League
25 Gameday Championship, the American Spirit League Grand
26 Championship, and the American Spirit League Best
27 Choreography of the Year Award; and
