4 Relating to insurance; to amend Sections 27-58-1 and
5 27-58-4, Code of Alabama 1975; to recognize that a higher risk
6 of prostate cancer exists in certain groups of men; and to
7 require health insurance plans to cover screening of both
8 younger high-risk men and all older men, free of out-of-pocket
9 costs.
10 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
11 Section 1. This act shall be known and may be cited as
12 the David "Mac" McElhaney and Roy S. Johnson Prostate Cancer
13 Prevention Act.
