4 SYNOPSIS:
5 This bill would require cryptocurrency kiosk
6 operators to provide certain disclosures to a consumer
7 during a transaction.
8 This bill would require kiosk operators to
9 provide all receipts of transactions to the consumer
10 and digital receipts to the Alabama Securities
11 Commission.
12 This bill would provide fraud prevention
13 measures for cryptocurrency transactions using kiosks
14 and require operators to refund transactions that were
15 fraudulently induced.
16 This bill would require operators to establish a
17 consumer service line and a direct line of
18 communication with local law enforcement agencies and
19 the commission.
20 This bill would also provide civil and criminal
21 penalties for certain violations of this section.
