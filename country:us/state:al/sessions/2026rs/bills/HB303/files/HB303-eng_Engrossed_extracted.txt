6 TO BE ENTITLED
7 AN ACT
8
9 Relating to cryptocurrency; to add Section 8-7A-28 to
10 the Code of Alabama 1975; to require operators of
11 cryptocurrency kiosks to provide certain disclosures during a
12 transaction; to require that kiosk operators provide a receipt
13 for a cryptocurrency transaction; to implement certain fraud
14 protection measures for cryptocurrency kiosk transactions and
15 require refunds for fraudulently induced transactions; and to
16 provide for certain civil and criminal penalties.
17 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
