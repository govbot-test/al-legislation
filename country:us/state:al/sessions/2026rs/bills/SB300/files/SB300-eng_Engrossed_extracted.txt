6 TO BE ENTITLED
7 AN ACT
8
9 Relating to distinctive license plates; to amend
10 Section 32-6-300, Code of Alabama 1975, to further provide for
11 the design requirements for the "Helping Schools" license
12 plates; and to authorize certain individuals to receive the
13 new design of the license plate at no cost.
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
15 Section 1. This act shall be known and may be cited as
16 the Senator Michael A. Figures Act.
17 Section 2. Section 32-6-300, Code of Alabama 1975, is
18 amended to read as follows:
19 "§32-6-300
20 (a) Owners of all motor vehicles who are residents of
21 Alabama, upon application to the judge of probate or
22 commissioner of licenses, complying with the state motor
23 vehicle laws relating to registration and licensing of motor
24 vehicles and payment of the regular license fee for plates as
25 provided by law for the motor vehicle, and the payment of an
26 additional annual fee of twenty dollars ($20), shall be issued
27 license plates which shall consist of a white background; bear
28 the words "Helping Schools." in red lettering; and contain a
Page 1
the words "Helping Schools." in red lettering; and contain a
SB300 Engrossed
29 drawing of a red apple, green book, and yellow ruler.
30 (b) The plates shall be issued, printed, and processed
31 in the same manner as other personalized plates are in this
32 chapter. The plates shall be valid for five years and shall be
33 replaced at the end of the period with either conventional
34 plates or other personalized plates, or with new "Helping
35 Schools" plates. Payment of the required motor vehicle license
36 fees and taxes for the years during which a new vehicle
37 license plate is not issued shall be evidenced as provided for
38 in Section 32-6-63. The State Department of Revenue shall
39 design, or have designed, design of the "Helping Schools"
40 plates shall be made in compliance with all laws and
41 regulations.
42 (c) For the registration year beginning January 1,
43 2027, only, an individual in possession of a "Helping Schools"
44 plate that was bought or renewed during the period of March 1,
45 2025, to December 31, 2026, the Department of Revenue shall
46 issue a new "Helping Schools" plate that meets the design
47 requirements of this section, and shall not charge a fee for
48 the replacement plate."
49 Section 3. This act shall become effective on January
50 1, 2027.
