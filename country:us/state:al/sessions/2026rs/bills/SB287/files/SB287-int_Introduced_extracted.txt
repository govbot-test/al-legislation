4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Existing law allows a licensed importer to bring
7 manufactured outside of the United States for
8 distribution to the Alcoholic Beverage Control Board
9 (ABC) or a wholesaler. There is currently no statutory
10 recognition that a licensed importer may make the same
11 distribution with respect to alcoholic beverages
12 manufactured domestically, but outside the State of
13 Alabama.
14 This bill would recognize that a licensed
15 importer may import alcoholic beverages into the state
16 which are manufactured in the United States.
17 This bill would change the beginning of the
18 licensure year for importers from January 1 to October
19 1 to conform with the licensure year for other ABC
20 license categories.
21 This bill would also delete duplicative language
22 and make nonsubstantive, technical revisions to update
23 the existing code language to current style.
