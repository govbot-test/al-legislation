Section 1:
WAYS AND MEANS EDUCATION SUBSTITUTE TO HB411
OFFERED BY REPRESENTATIVE BAKER
1
2
3
4 SYNOPSIS:
5 This bill would exempt the Auxiliary of Big Oak
6 Ranch, Incorporated, from payment or collection of
7 state sales and use taxes.
8 This bill would also allow municipalities and
9 counties to exempt this entity from local sales and use
10 taxes.
11
12
13 A BILL
14 TO BE ENTITLED
15 AN ACT
16
17 Related to sales and use tax; to exempt the Auxiliary
18 of Big Oak Ranch, Incorporated, from the payment or collection
19 of state sales and use taxes; and to allow municipalities and
20 counties to exempt this entity from local sales and use
21 taxes.
22 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
23 Section 1. (a) The Auxiliary of Big Oak Ranch,
24 Incorporated, is exempt from paying or collecting any state
25 sales and use taxes.
26 (b) Any county or municipality may exempt the entity
27 from the payment or collection of county or municipal sales
28 and use taxes in accordance with Section 40-23-4.01, Code of
Page 1
and use taxes in accordance with Section 40-23-4.01, Code of
29 Alabama 1975.
30 Section 2. This act shall become effective on September
31 1, 2026.
