5 Under existing law, the Growing Alabama Act
7 cash contributions to an economic development
8 organization approved by the Renewal of Alabama
9 Commission to fund improvements to sites owned by local
10 economic development organizations and intended to be
11 used by an industry or business.

13 local economic development organizations to qualify for
14 Growing Alabama tax credits funding if all parties

16 This bill would also make nonsubstantive,
17 technical revisions to update existing code language to
18 current style.
