5 HUNTSVILLE WOMEN'S BASKETBALL TEAM FOR ITS HISTORIC SEASON.
6
7 WHEREAS, highest commendations and congratulations
8 are herein proudly presented to The University of Alabama in
9 Huntsville (UAH) Women's Basketball Team for its historic
10 2025-26 season, culminating in competing in the National
11 Collegiate Athletic Association (NCAA) Division II Final
12 Four; and
13 WHEREAS, through teamwork, diligence, and a
14 commitment to excellence, the UAH Chargers finished with a
15 program-record 32 wins, including 21 conference victories,
16 while also setting records for home wins with 20 and NCAA
17 Tournament wins with four; and
18 WHEREAS, the Chargers claimed their first-ever Gulf
19 South Conference (GSC) Regular Season Championship, earned a
20 Top 25 national ranking for the first time in program
21 history, and hosted the NCAA South Regional for the first
22 time; and
23 WHEREAS, the UAH Women's Basketball Team won the NCAA
24 South Regional Championship against Eckerd 66-46 to advance
25 to the Elite Eight; subsequently, the team defeated Cal Poly
26 Pomona 61-53 and advanced to the NCAA Division II Final
27 Four; despite the team's loss in the Final Four, the
28 Chargers capped off the most successful season in program
