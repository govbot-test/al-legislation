4 SJR___ COMMENDING THE ALABAMA STATE CHIROPRACTIC ASSOCIATION
5 ON THE OCCASION OF ITS 100TH ANNIVERSARY.
6
7 WHEREAS, the Alabama State Chiropractic Association
8 (ASCA) proudly celebrates its 100th Anniversary in 2026,
9 marking a century of dedication to advancing the
10 chiropractic profession and improving the health and
11 well-being of the residents of the State of Alabama; and
12 WHEREAS, established in 1926, ASCA was founded to
13 unite and represent doctors of chiropractic across the state
14 and to promote the highest standards of professional
15 excellence, ethics, and patient care; and
16 WHEREAS, for 100 years ASCA has played an essential
17 role in advocating for patients' access to safe, effective,
18 and noninvasive chiropractic care while working
19 collaboratively with policymakers and health professionals
20 to advance public health initiatives throughout Alabama; and
21 WHEREAS, chiropractic physicians throughout Alabama
22 have provided conservative, non-pharmaceutical care for
23 musculoskeletal and neuromuscular conditions, helping
24 countless residents manage pain, restore function, and
25 improve quality of life; and
26 WHEREAS, the members of ASCA have demonstrated
27 exemplary dedication to their communities through patient
28 care, public service, continuing education, and leadership
