4 HJR___ COMMENDING PRAY VESTAVIA FOR HOSTING A COMMUNITY
5 PRAYER EVENT TO CELEBRATE HOLY WEEK.
6
7 WHEREAS, with great pride and special pleasure, Pray
8 Vestavia is highly commended for hosting "A Community Event
9 for God's Kingdom: Covering Our City in Prayer" to celebrate
10 Holy Week; and
11 WHEREAS, the event was created and organized by
12 Martha Dazzio, Ashley Stockard, Kym Prewitt, and Jill
13 Kimbrough; these ladies of profound faith are encouraging
14 their neighbors to participate in this street-to-street
15 community prayer event; and
16 WHEREAS, Holy Week is a sacred week for Christianity,
17 celebrating and commemorating Christ's passion, death, and
18 resurrection; Holy Week begins on Palm Sunday and concludes
19 with Easter Sunday; each day carries distinct liturgical
20 significance, revealing deeper layers of Christ's paschal
21 mystery; now therefore,
22 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA, BOTH
23 HOUSES THEREOF CONCURRING, That Pray Vestavia and its
24 organizers are highly commended for celebrating this
25 foundational Christian tradition and encouraging others in
26 the community to participate in the Holy Week prayer event.
