5 TO BE ENTITLED
6 AN ACT
7
8
9 Relating to Cleburne County; to provide for the
10 electronic filing of business personal property tax returns;
11 and to authorize the revenue commissioner to establish
12 procedures.
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
14 Section 1. (a) The Cleburne County Revenue Commissioner
15 may establish procedures for electronic filing for the
16 reporting, assessment, and payment of business personal
17 property taxes pursuant to Section 40-7-59, Code of Alabama
18 1975. A complete business personal property tax return filed
19 electronically shall be in the format prescribed by the
20 revenue commissioner and shall contain the same information as
21 a business personal property tax return filed on paper. The
22 timely filing and electronic signature requirements shall be
23 as provided by the revenue commissioner generally in
24 conformance with existing procedures for electronic filing of
25 other electronic tax returns.
26 (b) Effective for any business personal property tax
27 return filed on or after October 1, 2026, the Cleburne County
28 Revenue Commissioner may require any of the following business
Page 1
Revenue Commissioner may require any of the following business
HB625 INTRODUCED
29 personal property tax returns to be filed electronically:
30 (1) A return filed by any business engaged in the
31 leasing of personal property.
32 (2) A return filed by any business with personal
33 property assets of ten thousand dollars ($10,000) or more.
34 (3) A return prepared for filing by any third-party tax
35 preparer.
36 (c) The Cleburne County Revenue Commissioner shall
37 conduct training sessions and otherwise assist any taxpayer in
38 the procedures for electronic filing pursuant to this section.
39 Section 2. This act shall become effective on October
40 1, 2026.
