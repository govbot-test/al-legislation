6 TO BE ENTITLED
7 AN ACT
8
9 Relating to counties and municipalities; to require the
10 governing bodies of each county and municipality to publish
11 online all financial statements, audits, monthly expenditures,
12 and budgets; and to authorize the Chief Examiner of the
13 Department of Examiners of Public Accounts to adopt rules.
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
15 Section 1. (a) All county commissions and municipal
16 governing bodies shall annually publish all financial
17 statements relating to the financial status of the county or
18 municipality, for the 12-month period ending on the last day
19 of the fiscal year as established by the county or
20 municipality. The fiscal statements shall include, but not be
21 limited to, audits, monthly expenditures, and budgets which
22 shall be published in a prominent location on the official
23 website of the county or municipality.
24 (b) If the county or municipality does not maintain an
25 official website, the information required in subsection (a)
26 shall be posted on the website of the Association of County
27 Commissions of Alabama or the Alabama League of
28 Municipalities, as appropriate.
Page 1
Municipalities, as appropriate.
SB105 Engrossed
29 (c) Any rules related to publishing information
30 described in subsection (a) shall be prescribed by the Chief
31 Examiner of the Department of Examiners of Public Accounts as
32 authorized in Section 41-5A-5, Code of Alabama 1975.
33 (d) Notwithstanding the requirements of subsection (a),
34 a county commission or municipal governing body may redact
35 from published monthly expenditures specific line-item details
36 of expenditures that relate directly to the safety and
37 security of critical infrastructure as defined by Section
38 13A-7-4.3, Code of Alabama 1975, or any otherwise protected or
39 confidential information.
40 Section 2. This act shall become effective on October
41 1, 2028.
