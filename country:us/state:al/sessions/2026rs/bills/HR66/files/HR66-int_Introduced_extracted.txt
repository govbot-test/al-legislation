7 WHEREAS, Richard Dayton has served the residents of
8 Foley, Alabama, with distinction and dedication as a member
9 of the city council from 2020 until his unexpected passing
10 on August 21, 2025; and
11 WHEREAS, during his tenure, he worked tirelessly to
12 help lead the City of Foley in planning several major
13 construction projects and infrastructure upgrades to address
14 the needs of the city as one of the fastest growing in
15 Baldwin County; and
16 WHEREAS, Richard helped invest in continued quality
17 of life for residents through upgrades at the library,
18 public works, civic center, and continued investments in the
19 Main Street program to enhance public safety, education, and
20 infrastructure; and
21 WHEREAS, he served with integrity, compassion, and a
22 selfless devotion to public service, earning the respect and
23 admiration of fellow public servants and residents alike;
24 and
