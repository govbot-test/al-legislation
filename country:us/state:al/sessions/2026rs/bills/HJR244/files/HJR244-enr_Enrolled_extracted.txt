3 HONORING THE YOUNG LADIES OF CLASS MENTORSHIP AND DEBUTANTE
4 PROGRAM AND ITS FOUNDER SHEYANN WEBB-CHRISTBURG.
5
6 WHEREAS, the Legislature of Alabama takes great pride
7 in recognizing Sheyann Webb-Christburg, the founder and
8 Executive Director of the Young Ladies of Class (YLOC)
9 Mentorship and Debutante Program; her life's work and
10 dedication as a youth advocate have inspired and impacted
11 generations of young people throughout the state and abroad;
12 and
13 WHEREAS, through her 45-year journey as a youth
14 advocate and humanitarian, she founded YLOC in January 2024
15 with a vision to provide young girls with transformative
16 experiences that foster personal growth, career growth,
17 leadership development, civic engagement, community
18 services, and etiquette training; and
19 WHEREAS, the YLOC Program is well-structured to
20 provide various workshops, cultural activities, and special
21 events to instill in young girls the values of character,
22 class, poise, grace, sophistication, and purpose; and
23 WHEREAS, YLOC's focus is to equip young girls with
24 the essential skills, knowledge, and confidence to lead
25 productive and successful lives; these concentrated efforts
26 are provided through mentorship, motivation, and
27 inspiration, as they strive to help them unlock their full
28 potential and navigate life's challenges with grace and
