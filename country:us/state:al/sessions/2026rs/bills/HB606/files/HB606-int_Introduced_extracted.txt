9 Relating to Baldwin County; to amend Section
10 45-2-120.12, Code of Alabama 1975; to establish that
11 appointment of certain contract employees by the county
12 commission is permissive; and to remove certain positions from
13 the list of contract employees that are appointed by the
14 county commission.
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
16 Section 1. Section 45-2-120.12, Code of Alabama 1975,
17 is amended to read as follows:
18 "§45-2-120.12
19 (a)(1) One appointed employee position may be filled by
20 the judge of probate which positionThe judge of probate may
21 appoint one employee who shall be designatedserve as chief
22 clerk.
23 (2) Two appointed employee positions may be filled by
24 the revenue commissioner which positionsThe revenue
25 commissioner may appoint two employees who shall be
26 designatedserve as chief clerk of collections and chief
27 appraiser.
28 (3) Three appointed employee positions may be filled by

29 the sheriff which positionsThe sheriff may appoint three
30 employees who shall be designatedserve as chief deputy, chief
31 jailer, and chief of the civil division.
32 (b) The county commission may appoint the following
33 shall be appointed contract employees: County administrator,
34 emergency management director, juvenile detention director,
35 personnel director, building official, clerk/treasurer, county
36 engineer, development and environmental director, budget
37 director, and communication and information systems director.
38 (c) Each of the appointed contract employees shall be
39 employed under contract with the commission. Each contract
40 shall include at a minimum the following provisions:
41 (1) A provision for termination and severance.
42 (2) A job description for the position.
43 (3) An annual written evaluation to be reviewed with
44 the employee and retained by the commission in the employee's
45 personnel file.
46 (4) A provision that the contract will be nonbinding on
47 the successor commission after each four-year election;
48 however, the contracts must be cancelledcanceled or continued
49 within 90 days of the successor commission taking office.
50 (5) A provision that any appointed contract employee
51 employed by the county commission may not be subject to any
52 action to cancel or not to renew the contract or in any other
53 manner dismiss or terminate the contract employee except by a
54 three-fourths vote of the elected members of the county
55 commission."
