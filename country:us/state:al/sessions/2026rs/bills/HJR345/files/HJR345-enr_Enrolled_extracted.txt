4 COMMENDING TANNER TESSMANN AND RECOGNIZING JUNE 19, 2026, AS
5 TANNER TESSMANN DAY IN ALABAMA.
6
7 WHEREAS, Tanner Tessmann, named the 2024 Young Male
8 Player of the Year by the United States Soccer Federation,
9 is one of two Alabamians positioned to be on the U.S. roster
10 for this summer's World Cup; and
11 WHEREAS, a native of Hoover, Tanner Tessmann was
12 captain of the 2024 U.S. Olympic soccer team in Paris; the
13 24-year-old defensive midfielder has played professionally
14 for Olympique Lyonnais in France for the last two years; and
15 WHEREAS, both a talented soccer player and football
16 kicker, Tanner Tessmann had planned to play both sports at
17 Clemson University; before enrolling at Clemson, however, he
18 signed a contract with FC Dallas; his soccer path then took
19 him to the Italian club Venezia for three years; and
20 WHEREAS, Tanner Tessmann has now played for two of
21 Europe's top five leagues, Italy's Serie A in 2021-22 before
22 relegation and France's Ligue 1 the past two seasons, while
23 simultaneously climbing the U.S. ladder; since playing every
24 minute of four Olympic matches in France, he has integrated
25 into the senior squad of the United States Men's National
26 Soccer Team; last fall, he started twice and came off the
27 bench twice, scoring in the 5-1 disastrous defeat of Uruguay
28 in Tampa; with World Cup veteran Tyler Adams recovering from
