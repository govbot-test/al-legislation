6 TO BE ENTITLED
7 AN ACT
8
9 Relating to solar power; to prohibit the construction
10 and operation of new solar power facilities for one year.
11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
12 Section 1. (a) For purposes of this section, the term
13 "solar power facility" means a large-scale, ground-mounted
14 installation of photovoltaic panels that are designed to
15 generate electricity using solar power for use off-site or
16 sale to a third party, including all equipment and facilities
17 required to operate the facility and transmit generated
18 electricity.
19 (b) In order to protect the coastline and watershed,
20 for one year after the effective date of this act, a solar
21 power facility not operating or under construction on the
22 effective date of this act may not be constructed or operated
23 in any county that borders the Gulf of America.
24 Section 2. This act shall become effective immediately.
