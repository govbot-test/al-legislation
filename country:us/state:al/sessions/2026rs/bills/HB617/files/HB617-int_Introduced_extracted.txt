4 SYNOPSIS:
5 This bill would prohibit the construction and
6 operation of new solar power facilities for one year.

9 A BILL
10 TO BE ENTITLED
11 AN ACT
12
13 Relating to solar power; to prohibit the construction
14 and operation of new solar power facilities for one year.
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
16 Section 1. (a) For purposes of this section, the term
17 "solar power facility" means a large-scale, ground-mounted
18 installation of photovoltaic panels that are designed to
19 generate electricity using solar power for use off-site or
20 sale to a third party, including all equipment and facilities
21 required to operate the facility and transmit generated
22 electricity.
23 (b) For one year after the effective date of this act,
24 a solar power facility not operating or under construction on
25 the effective date of this act may not be constructed or
26 operated.
27 Section 2. This act shall become effective immediately.
