4 EXTENDING THE JOINT INTERIM STUDY COMMISSION ON EXPANDING
5 ACCESS TO HIGH-QUALITY OUT-OF-SCHOOL EDUCATION PROGRAMS.
6
7 WHEREAS, House Joint Resolution 100, enacted as Act
8 2025-131 in the 2025 Regular Session, created the Joint
9 Interim Study Commission on Expanding Access to High-Quality
10 Out-of-School Education Programs to provide recommendations
11 for enhancing after school and summer educational
12 programming; and
13 WHEREAS, Act 2025-131 provided that the study
14 commission would submit findings, conclusions, and
15 recommendations to the Legislature no later than the
16 fifteenth day of the 2026 Regular Session, at which time the
17 study commission would be dissolved; and
18 WHEREAS, the study commission needs additional time
19 in order to continue to develop recommendations relating to
20 enhancing after school and summer educational programming;
21 now therefore,
22 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA, BOTH
23 HOUSES THEREOF CONCURRING, That the Joint Interim Study
24 Commission on Expanding Access to High-Quality Out-of-School
25 Education Programs, created pursuant to Act 2025-131, 2025
26 Regular Session, is extended.
