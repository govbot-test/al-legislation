4 HJR___ EXTENDING THE JOINT INTERIM STUDY COMMISSION ON
5 EXPANDING ACCESS TO HIGH-QUALITY OUT-OF-SCHOOL EDUCATION
6 PROGRAMS.
7
8 WHEREAS, House Joint Resolution 100, enacted as Act
9 2025-131 in the 2025 Regular Session, created the Joint
10 Interim Study Commission on Expanding Access to High-Quality
11 Out-of-School Education Programs to provide recommendations
12 for enhancing after school and summer educational
13 programming; and
14 WHEREAS, Act 2025-131 provided that the study
15 commission would submit findings, conclusions, and
16 recommendations to the Legislature no later than the
17 fifteenth day of the 2026 Regular Session, at which time the
18 study commission would be dissolved; and
19 WHEREAS, the study commission needs additional time
20 in order to continue to develop recommendations relating to
21 enhancing after school and summer educational programming;
22 now therefore,
23 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA, BOTH
24 HOUSES THEREOF CONCURRING, That the Joint Interim Study
25 Commission on Expanding Access to High-Quality Out-of-School
26 Education Programs, created pursuant to Act 2025-131, 2025
27 Regular Session, is extended.
