4 SYNOPSIS:
5 This bill would increase the fee for the child
6 labor certificates from $15 to $50 per child.
7 This bill would provide for the distribution of
8 penalty fees collected for violating child labor laws.
9 This bill would remove certain boiler and
10 pressure vessels from being exempt from certain boiler
11 and pressure vessel standards.
12 This bill would further provide for the fee for
13 pressure vessel certificates of inspection and the
14 distribution of those fees.
15 This bill would require the Elevator Safety
16 Review Board to review the latest editions of elevator
17 safety standards within a certain amount of time and
18 have any adopted standards become effective within six
19 months of the board's review date.
20 This bill would require new elevator
21 installations to meet the state elevator code that was
22 in effect when the building construction permit was
23 issued or at the time of final approval from the
24 Division of Construction Management, while
25 modifications or alterations must meet the standards in
26 effect at the time the elevator permit was issued.
27 This would extend the renewal period for certain
28 elevator-related licenses.
