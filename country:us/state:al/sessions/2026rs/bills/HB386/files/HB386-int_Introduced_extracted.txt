9 Relating to Elmore County; to amend Section 45-26-233,
10 Code of Alabama 1975, to further provide for pistol permit
11 fees.
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
13 Section 1. Section 45-26-233, Code of Alabama 1975, is
14 amended to read as follows:
15 "§45-26-233
16 (a) In Elmore County, in addition to any fee or fees
17 provided for by law there shall be an additional the issuance
18 fee for a pistol permit or license as provided for in Section
19 13A-1l-75 in the amount of shall be twenty dollars ($20).
20 (b) One dollar ($1) of each fee collected under
21 subsection (a) shall be paid into the county general fund and
22 the remaining nineteen dollars ($19) of each fee shall be
23 deposited by the sheriff of the county in any bank located in
24 the county, into a fund known as the Sheriffs' Law Enforcement
25 Fund. The fund shall be drawn upon by the sheriff or duly
26 authorized agent and shall be used exclusively for law
27 enforcement purposes.
28 (c) The establishment of the Sheriffs' Law Enforcement
Page 1
(c) The establishment of the Sheriffs' Law Enforcement

29 Fund as provided in this section and the use of such the fund
30 shall in no way diminish or take the place of any other
31 imbursement or other source of income established for the
32 sheriff or the operation of his or her office."
33 Section 2. This act shall become effective on October
34 1, 2026.
