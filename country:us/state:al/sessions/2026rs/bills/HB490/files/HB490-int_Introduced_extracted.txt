5 TO BE ENTITLED
6 AN ACT
7
8
9 Relating to Tallapoosa County; to provide for the
10 salary of the county revenue commissioner; and to provide for
11 the salary of the judge of probate.
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
13 Section 1. (a) Until the next term of office, the
14 Revenue Commissioner of Tallapoosa County shall continue to
15 receive the same base annual salary that the revenue
16 commissioner is receiving on May 31, 2026.
17 (b) Beginning on June 1, 2026, in addition to the base
18 annual salary authorized in subsection (a), the revenue
19 commissioner shall receive an expense allowance in such amount
20 as necessary so that the total annual compensation received
21 equals one hundred thousand dollars ($100,000) per annum,
22 payable in equal monthly installments from the general fund of
23 the county.
24 (c)(1) Effective beginning with the next term of
25 office, the revenue commissioner shall receive a base annual
26 salary of one hundred thousand dollars ($100,000) per annum,
27 payable in equal monthly installments, and subsections (a) and
28 (b) are void.

29 (2) The salary provided by this subsection shall be in
30 lieu of any salary or other compensation provided by law and
31 shall be paid from the same funding sources as the salary of
32 the revenue commissioner is paid on June 1, 2026.
33 Section 2. (a) Until the next term of office, the Judge
34 of Probate of Tallapoosa County shall continue to receive the
35 same base annual salary that the judge of probate is receiving
36 on May 31, 2026.
37 (b) Beginning on June 1, 2026, in addition to the base
38 annual salary authorized in subsection (a), the judge of
39 probate shall receive an expense allowance in such amount as
40 necessary so that the total annual compensation received
41 equals one hundred and six thousand dollars ($106,000) per
42 annum, payable in equal monthly installments from the general
43 fund of the county.
44 (c)(1) Effective beginning with the next term of
45 office, the judge of probate shall receive a base annual
46 salary of one hundred and six thousand dollars ($106,000) per
47 annum, payable in equal monthly installments from the same
48 funding sources as the salary of the judge of probate is paid
49 on June 1, 2026, and subsections (a) and (b) are void.
50 (2) The salary provided by this subsection shall be in
51 lieu of any salary provided by law and shall supersede the
52 compensation provided to the judge of probate in Act 275, 1969
53 Regular Session (Acts 1969, p. 607-608), as amended by Act
54 437, 1978 Regular Session (Acts 1978, p. 449-450), and the
55 specific provisions of that act are void.
