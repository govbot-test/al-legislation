3 Relating to Tallapoosa County; to provide for the
4 salary of the county revenue commissioner; and to provide for

6 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
7 Section 1. (a) Until the next term of office, the
8 Revenue Commissioner of Tallapoosa County shall continue to
9 receive the same base annual salary that the revenue
10 commissioner is receiving on May 31, 2026.
11 (b) Beginning on June 1, 2026, in addition to the base
12 annual salary authorized in subsection (a), the revenue
13 commissioner shall receive an expense allowance in such amount
14 as necessary so that the total annual compensation received
15 equals one hundred thousand dollars ($100,000) per annum,
16 payable in equal monthly installments from the general fund of
17 the county.
18 (c)(1) Effective beginning with the next term of
19 office, the revenue commissioner shall receive a base annual
20 salary of one hundred thousand dollars ($100,000) per annum,
21 payable in equal monthly installments, and subsections (a) and
22 (b) are void.
23 (2) The salary provided by this subsection shall be in
24 lieu of any salary or other compensation provided by law and
25 shall be paid from the same funding sources as the salary of
26 the revenue commissioner is paid on June 1, 2026.
27 Section 2. (a) Until the next term of office, the Judge
28 of Probate of Tallapoosa County shall continue to receive the
