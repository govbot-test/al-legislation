4 SYNOPSIS:
5 This bill would provide for two exempt personnel
6 positions within the Pharmacy Benefits Manager Division
7 of the Department of Insurance.
8
9
10 A BILL
11 TO BE ENTITLED
12 AN ACT
13
14 Relating to the Department of Insurance; to provide for
15 two exempt personnel positions within the Pharmacy Benefits
16 Manager Division.
17 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
18 Section 1. The Commissioner of Insurance, in
19 conformance with Section 36-26-10, Code of Alabama 1975, may
20 employ personnel to operate the Pharmacy Benefits Manager
21 Division within the Department of Insurance, including two
22 employees who are in the exempt service and not subject to the
23 state Merit System. These positions are in addition to any
24 other exempt position otherwise authorized by law for the
25 department. The compensation of these employees shall be set
26 by the commissioner.
27 Section 2. This act shall become effective on June 1,
28 2026.
