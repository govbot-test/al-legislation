4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, an individual who holds a
6 municipal office may not serve on a municipal planning
7 commission.
8 This bill would remove the prohibition for
9 municipalities with less than 7,500 residents.
10 Under existing law, an individual who is a
11 municipal officer may not serve on a board of directors
12 of a local water, sewer, or fire protection authority.
13 This bill would remove the prohibition.
14 Under existing law, an individual who is a
15 county or municipal officer may not serve on a board of
16 directors for a solid waste disposal district.
17 This bill would also remove this prohibition for
18 individuals who do not serve on the governing
19 authority.
