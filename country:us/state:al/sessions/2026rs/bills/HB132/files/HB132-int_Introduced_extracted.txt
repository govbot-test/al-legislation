4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, a person commits the crime
6 of assault in the third degree if, with intent to cause
7 physical injury to another person, he or she causes
8 physical injury to any person.
9 This bill would provide that a person commits
10 the crime of assault in the second degree if, with
11 intent to cause physical injury to a child in an
12 educational environment, he or she causes physical
13 injury to any person.
