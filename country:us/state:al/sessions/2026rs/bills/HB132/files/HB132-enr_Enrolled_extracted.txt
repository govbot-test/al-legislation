5 13A-6-21, Code of Alabama 1975; to revise the crime of assault
6 in the second degree.
7 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
8 Section 1. Section 13A-6-21, Code of Alabama 1975, is
9 amended to read as follows:
10 "§13A-6-21
11 (a) A person commits the crime of assault in the second
12 degree if the person does any of the following:
13 (1) With intent to cause serious physical injury to
14 another person, he or she causes serious physical injury to
15 any person.
16 (2) With intent to cause physical injury to another
17 person, he or she causes physical injury to any person by
18 means of a deadly weapon or a dangerous instrument.
19 (3) He or she recklessly causes serious physical injury
20 to another person by means of a deadly weapon or a dangerous
21 instrument.
22 (4)a. With intent to prevent a peace officer, as
23 defined in Section 36-21-60, a detention or correctional
24 officer at any municipal or county jail or state penitentiary,
25 emergency medical personnel, a utility worker, or a
26 firefighter from performing a lawful duty, he or she intends
27 to cause physical injury and he or she causes physical injury
28 to any person.

31 duty by a private or public entity is a peace officer
32 performing a lawful duty when the person is working in his or
33 her approved uniform while off duty with the approval of his
34 or her employing law enforcement agency. Provided, however,
35 that nothing Nothing contained in this subdivision shall be
36 deemed or construed as amending, modifying, or extending the
37 classification of a peace officer as off-duty off duty for

41 this subdivision shall be deemed or construed as amending,

43 as a result of any action or inaction on the part of an

90 (c) For the purposes of this section, "utility worker"
