8 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
9 Section 1. Section 9-11-54.1 is added to the Code of
10 Alabama 1975, to read as follows:
11 §9-11-54.1
12 (a) Beginning with the 2026 license year, an individual
13 eligible to be issued an annual license under Section 9-11-54
14 may purchase a lifetime license in lieu of an annual license
15 upon application and payment of a fee to the Department of
16 Conservation and Natural Resources as follows:
17 (1)a. Lifetime Disabled Resident Hunting License.
18 1. Individuals who are less than 50 years of age shall
19 be charged fifty dollars ($50) for a lifetime disabled
20 resident hunting license.
21 2. Individuals who are 50 years of age or older shall
22 be charged thirty dollars ($30) for a lifetime disabled
23 resident hunting license.
24 b. Fees collected pursuant to this subdivision shall be
25 deposited into the Alabama Game and Fish Endowment Fund.
26 (2)a. Lifetime Disabled Freshwater Fishing License.
27 1. Individuals who are less than 50 years of age shall
28 be charged fifty dollars ($50) for a lifetime disabled
