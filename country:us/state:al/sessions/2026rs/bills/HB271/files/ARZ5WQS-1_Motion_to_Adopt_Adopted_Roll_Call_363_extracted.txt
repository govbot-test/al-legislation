9 Relating to the Department of Conservation and Natural
10 Resources; to add Section 9-11-54.1 to the Code of Alabama
11 1975, to establish lifetime disabled state resident special

13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
14 Section 1. Section 9-11-54.1 is added to the Code of
15 Alabama 1975, to read as follows:
16 §9-11-54.1
17 (a) Beginning with the 2026 license year, an individual
18 eligible to be issued an annual license under Section 9-11-54
19 may purchase a lifetime license in lieu of an annual license
20 upon application and payment of a fee to the Department of
21 Conservation and Natural Resources as follows:
22 (1)a. Lifetime Disabled Resident Hunting License.
23 1. Individuals who are less than 50 years of age shall
24 be charged fifty dollars ($50) for a lifetime disabled
25 resident hunting license.
26 2. Individuals who are 50 years of age or older shall
27 be charged thirty dollars ($30) for a lifetime disabled
28 resident hunting license.
