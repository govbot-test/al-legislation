4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, law enforcement officers may
6 issue citations for certain violations, traffic
7 offenses, and misdemeanors in lieu of performing a
8 custodial arrest.
9 This bill would authorize a warrant for such a
10 violation to be sworn and issued by a judge or
11 magistrate remotely, digitally, via video link, or by
12 telephone and would specify that the physical presence
13 of the affiant before the judge or magistrate is not
14 required.
15 This bill would allow law enforcement officers
16 to swear to the citations they issue for certain
17 violations, traffic offenses, and misdemeanors in the
18 presence of a judge or magistrate remotely, digitally,
19 via video link, or by telephone.
20 This bill would also authorize law enforcement
21 officers to act as a magistrate in the limited scope of
22 administering oaths to other law enforcement officers
23 to swear to the facts contained in a citation.
