4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, makeup services are
6 considered a practice of esthetics and subject to the
7 licensing and training requirements of the Alabama
8 Board of Cosmetology and Barbering.
9 This bill would remove makeup services from the
10 definition of esthetics.
11 This bill would exempt makeup application
12 services from regulation by the Alabama Board of
13 Cosmetology and Barbering.
14 This bill would also define blow-dry styling and
15 exempt those services from regulation by the Alabama
16 Board of Cosmetology and Barbering.
