29 $460,000 from the Education Trust Fund to Southern Preparatory
30 Academy in Camp Hill, Alabama for the support and maintenance
31 of the educational program of the institution, for the fiscal
32 year ending September 30, 2027.
33 Section 2. The above appropriation is made for the
34 support of public education in Alabama and for the support and
35 maintenance of the above program. The appropriation shall be
36 subject to the provisions, terms, conditions, and limitations
37 of the Budget and Financial Control Act, Code of Alabama 1975,
38 Section 41-4-80 et seq.; the provisions of the Budget
39 Management Act of 1976, Code of Alabama 1975, Sections 41-19-1
40 et seq.; and any other provisions of this act.
41 Section 3. (a) Prior to the release of any funds
42 appropriated under this act, the following reports shall be
43 submitted:
44 (1) An operations plan for the fiscal year 2027,
45 including goals and measurable performance indicators, shall
46 be submitted to and approved by the Director of Finance, as
47 provided for state agencies in Code of Alabama 1975, Section
48 41-19-10.
49 (2) An audited financial statement for all operations
50 during the fiscal year 2025.
51 (b) It is the intent that funds appropriated for fiscal
52 year 2027 shall be released by the Director of Finance
53 following receipt of the above reports.
54 (c) In addition, quarterly reports shall be made to the
55 Director of Finance relating actual expenditures and
56 accomplishments to planned expenditures and accomplishments.
