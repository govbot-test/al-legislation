HB242 Enrolled
2
3 To make an appropriation of $460,000 from the Education
4 Trust Fund to Southern Preparatory Academy in Camp Hill,
5 Alabama for the support and maintenance of the educational
6 program of the institution, for the fiscal year ending
7 September 30, 2027; to provide that the appropriation is
8 subject to certain provisions of the Code of Alabama 1975; to
9 require an operations plan and an audited financial statement
10 prior to the release of any funds; and to require an end of
11 year report.
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
13 Section 1. There is hereby appropriated the sum of
14 $460,000 from the Education Trust Fund to Southern Preparatory
15 Academy in Camp Hill, Alabama for the support and maintenance
16 of the educational program of the institution, for the fiscal
17 year ending September 30, 2027.
18 Section 2. The above appropriation is made for the
19 support of public education in Alabama and for the support and
20 maintenance of the above program. The appropriation shall be
21 subject to the provisions, terms, conditions, and limitations
22 of the Budget and Financial Control Act, Code of Alabama 1975,
23 Section 41-4-80 et seq.; the provisions of the Budget
24 Management Act of 1976, Code of Alabama 1975, Sections 41-19-1
25 et seq.; and any other provisions of this act.
26 Section 3. (a) Prior to the release of any funds
27 appropriated under this act, the following reports shall be
28 submitted:
Page 1
submitted:
HB242 Enrolled
29 (1) An operations plan for the fiscal year 2027,
30 including goals and measurable performance indicators, shall
31 be submitted to and approved by the Director of Finance, as
32 provided for state agencies in Code of Alabama 1975, Section
33 41-19-10.
34 (2) An audited financial statement for all operations
35 during the fiscal year 2025.
36 (b) It is the intent that funds appropriated for fiscal
37 year 2027 shall be released by the Director of Finance
38 following receipt of the above reports.
39 (c) In addition, quarterly reports shall be made to the
40 Director of Finance relating actual expenditures and
41 accomplishments to planned expenditures and accomplishments.
42 An end of year performance report for the fiscal year 2027
43 shall be made to the Director of Finance stating the work
44 accomplished and the services provided and the costs of
45 accomplishing the work and providing the services, citing
46 meaningful measures of program effectiveness and costs, as is
47 required for state agencies in Code of Alabama 1975, Section
48 41-19-11.
49 (d) The Director of Finance shall forward a copy of all
50 required reports to the Legislative Council in a timely
51 manner.
52 Section 4. This act shall become effective October 1, 2026.
