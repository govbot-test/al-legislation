4 HR____ CELEBRATING THE 80TH ANNIVERSARY OF CAMPBELL HARDWARE
5 AND SPORTING GOODS.
6
7 WHEREAS, we proudly congratulate the owners and
8 employees of Campbell Hardware and Sporting Goods in
9 Robertsdale, Alabama, for celebrating their 80th year of
10 family-style service, small-town charm, and big-city
11 selection that have kept customers happy since February
12 1946; and
13 WHEREAS, the original business partners, William
14 Leslie Wingo and his cousin Leonard Campbell, thought they
15 were filling a need in their community by providing a place
16 for locals to find nuts and bolts, cast-iron skillets, and
17 electrical supplies; the store quickly became a place to not
18 only find hardware and housewares, but good conversation as
19 well; 80 years later, that aspect of the business has not
20 changed; and
21 WHEREAS, John Nelson Wingo took over operations from
22 his father in 1974, and now his daughter, Ashley Kucera, is
23 on board and taking the store in new directions while still
24 respecting its past; they and their staff have a combined
25 100-plus years of experience in hardware, sporting goods,
26 and customer service; and
27 WHEREAS, choosing to keep the store in its original
28 downtown Robertsdale location, Ashley has updated and
