4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Existing law does not address faculty senates or
6 tenure policies in public institutions of higher
7 education.
8 This bill would provide that faculty senates may
9 only be established by the governing board of a public
10 institution of higher education, and provide that the
11 faculty senate may be only advisory in nature.
12 This bill would require each governing board
13 that establishes a faculty senate to adopt a policy
14 regarding the membership, duties, and leadership of the
15 faculty senate.
16 This bill would prohibit faculty senates from
17 being delegated final decision-making authority on any
18 matter or issuing statements unrelated to the faculty
19 senate's duties.
20 This bill would require the governing board of
21 each public institution of higher education to adopt a
22 tenure policy.
23 This bill would require each tenure policy to
24 require periodic post-tenure reviews and authorize the
25 dismissal of a tenured faculty member in certain
26 circumstances.
27 This bill would allow governing boards to adopt
28 procedures for the dismissal of tenured faculty

32 This bill would provide governing boards with

36 This bill would also prohibit accrediting
