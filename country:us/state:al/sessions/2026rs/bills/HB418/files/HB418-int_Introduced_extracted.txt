19 remove the personal property of occupants who are in
20 default and place the items in a suitable storage space
21 pending its sale or other disposition.
22 This bill would authorize operators of storage
23 facilities to include a provision in the rental
24 agreement notifying the occupant of the operator's
25 right to dispose of an occupant's personal property
26 left at the self-service storage facility for a certain
27 amount of time after the end of the rental agreement.

33 occupants to remove personal property from a
34 self-service storage facility following the termination

38 of the self-service storage facility after the rental
