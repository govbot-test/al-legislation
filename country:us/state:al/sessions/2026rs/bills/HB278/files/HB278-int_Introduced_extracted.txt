4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under current law, an annual state income tax
6 credit is offered to certain firefighters and rescue
7 squad members who complete certain approved training
8 each year.
9 This bill makes that income tax credit permanent
10 and expands it to include volunteer firefighters who
11 are certified as a Firefighter I, Emergency Medical
12 Responders, and fire support persons.
13
14
