3 To alter, rearrange, and extend the boundary lines and

5 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
6 Section 1. The boundary lines and corporate limits of
7 the City of Monroeville in Monroe County are altered and
8 rearranged to include within the corporate limits of the
9 municipality, in addition to the lands now included, all of
10 the following territory:
11 State Highway No. 21 Description
12 BEGINNING AT THE INTERSECTION OF THE SOUTH LINE OF
13 SECTION 22 TOWNSHIP 6 NORTH, RANGE 7 EAST, MONROE COUNTY,

15 STATE HIGHWAY No. 21, BEING MORE OR LESS AT THE INTERSECTION
16 OF OAK GROVE ROAD WITH SAID STATE HIGHWAY, THENCE
17 NORTHEASTERLY ALONG SAID STATE HIGHWAY RIGHT OF WAY LINES 9800
18 FEET MORE OR LESS TO THE INTERSECTION OF SAID STATE HIGHWAY
19 RIGHT OF WAY LINES WITH THE NORTH LINE OF THE SOUTHEAST ¼ OF
20 THE SOUTHEAST ¼ OF SECTION 14 TOWNSHIP 6 NORTH, RANGE 7 EAST,
21 MONROE COUNTY ALABAMA, BEING MORE OR LESS AT THE INTERSECTION
22 OF TIGER DRIVE WITH SAID STATE HIGHWAY, INCLUDING ANY AND ALL
23 RIGHT OF WAY FLAIRS AND CHANGES ALONG SAID RIGHT OF WAY OF
24 ALABAMA STATE HIGHWAY No. 21 IN THE ABOVE DESCRIBED PORTION.
25 U.S. Highway No. 84 Description
26 BEGINNING AT THE INTERSECTION OF THE WEST LINE OF
27 SECTION 15 TOWNSHIP 6 NORTH, RANGE 7 EAST, MONROE COUNTY,
28 ALABAMA, WITH THE NORTH AND SOUTH RIGHT OF WAY LINES OF U.S.

45 RIGHT OF WAY FLAIRS AND CHANGES ALONG SAID RIGHT OF WAY OF
46 ALABAMA STATE HIGHWAY No. 21 IN THE ABOVE DESCRIBED PORTION.
47 Section 2. In accordance with Section 11-42-6 of the
48 Code of Alabama 1975, a map showing the proposed territory to
49 be altered, rearranged, and extended from the City of
50 Monroeville is on file and open to public inspection in the
51 office of the Judge of Probate in Monroe County, Alabama.
52 Section 3. This act shall become effective on October
53 1, 2026.
