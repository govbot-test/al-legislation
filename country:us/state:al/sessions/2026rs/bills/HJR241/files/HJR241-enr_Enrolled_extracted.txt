4 RECOMMENDING STATE GOVERNMENT ENTITIES TO AVOID USING THE
5 TERM, "WEST BANK."
6
7 WHEREAS, the State of Alabama has long supported the
8 creation of a Jewish state and homeland, evidenced by the
9 unanimous passage of a 1943 joint resolution, which became
10 Act 144; and
11 WHEREAS, the names "Judea" and "Samaria" are the
12 historically and biblically accurate terms for the ancestral
13 heartland of the Jewish people, comprising the historical,
14 spiritual, and geographical core of the Land of Israel; and
15 WHEREAS, the renaming of the territory to "West Bank"
16 was a political and administrative process designed to
17 formalize Jordan's control over the areas of Judea and
18 Samaria that it occupied during the 1948 Arab-Israeli War;
19 and
20 WHEREAS, Israel liberated the land from Jordan during
21 the 1967 Six-Day War and reinstated its historical name,
22 with the land south of Jerusalem being considered Judea and
23 the land north of Jerusalem being considered Samaria; the
24 ongoing use of "West Bank" expunges the Jewish identity of
25 Judea and Samaria and obscures the deep historical,
26 religious, and legal connections of the Jewish people to the
27 land; now therefore,
