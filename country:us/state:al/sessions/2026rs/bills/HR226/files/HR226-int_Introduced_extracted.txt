5 JAMES FOUNDATION'S 20TH ANNUAL "HONORING OUR ELDERS DAY"
6 GALA.
7
8 WHEREAS, with great joy and special pleasure, we
9 honor Mrs. Linda Casselman Akenhead as the special guest at
10 the Rosetta James Foundation's 20th Annual "Honoring Our
11 Elders Day" Gala at the Jackson Center in Huntsville,
12 Alabama, on Saturday, March 14, 2026; and
13 WHEREAS, Mrs. Akenhead was born in San Diego and
14 raised in Bakersfield; she attended California State
15 University for a time and decided to move east and enroll at
16 The University of Alabama, graduating with a Bachelor of
17 Science in Foods and Nutrition; after passing the National
18 Dietetics exam, she began her career as a Clinical Dietitian
19 at the UAB Hospital in Birmingham, where she met her
20 husband, Dr. Akenhead; she completed her master's degree at
21 The University of Alabama at Birmingham and, although the
22 couple moved to Montgomery and back to Birmingham, they
23 settled in Huntsville, where they raised their two sons and
24 became embedded in the growth of the community; and
25 WHEREAS, Mrs. Akenhead has long been devoted to the
26 arts and community service; a lifelong musician, she plays
27 flute and piano, often performing for weddings and church
28 events; she became deeply involved with the Huntsville
Page 1
events; she became deeply involved with the Huntsville
HR226 INTRODUCED
29 Symphony Orchestra and the Huntsville Museum of Art; she has
30 also served Huntsville in numerous leadership roles,
31 including Trustee for Randolph School, Chair of Vive Le
32 Livre for the Library Foundation, board member of Huntsville
33 Hospital Foundation and Millennium Society Founder,
34 Christian Women's Job Corps, Blount Hospitality House, and
35 First United Methodist Church, where she has served in
36 multiple ministries; and
37 WHEREAS, Mrs. Akenhead's meaningful contributions to
38 community service have had a profound impact on the arts,
39 health care, and scores of women with mentorship and
40 compassion through the job corps; she holds the philosophy
41 that giving back is both a privilege and a responsibility,
42 ensuring future generations enjoy the same rich
43 opportunities they have cherished; and
44 WHEREAS, the mission of the Rosetta James Foundation
45 is to honor the wisdom, service, and dedication of our
46 cherished senior citizens; the "Honoring Our Elders Day"
47 Gala is a heartfelt tribute to community and civic
48 volunteers 70 years of age and above who have dedicated
49 their lives to uplifting others and fostering positive
50 change; these remarkable individuals exemplify the very
51 spirit of compassion, leadership, and service that inspires
52 us all; and
53 WHEREAS, this annual celebration not only shines a
54 spotlight on these distinguished elders but also serves as a
55 platform to invest in the next generation of agents for
56 change; through the generous support of its attendees and

change; through the generous support of its attendees and

57 donors, the Gala raises vital funds to provide scholarships
58 for deserving students who embody the principles of
59 volunteerism, academic excellence, and civic engagement;
60 these outstanding students will also be recognized during
61 the event, connecting their bright futures to the enduring
62 legacies of those who came before them; and
63 WHEREAS, this year's Gala will be held at noon on
64 Saturday, March 14, at the Jackson Center; the extraordinary
65 contributions of our elders will be honored while future
66 leaders will be empowered to continue building a legacy of
67 service and excellence; now therefore,
68 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE
69 LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA, That Mrs. Linda Casselman Akenhead
70 will be highly honored on an unforgettable afternoon filled
71 with inspiration, celebration, and community, and we
72 endeavor to continue uplifting and investing in those who
73 make a difference, both today and tomorrow.
