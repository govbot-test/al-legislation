4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Existing law provides compensation for paid
6 firefighters who contract cancer on the job but makes
7 no similar provision for volunteer firefighters.
8 This bill would provide compensation to the
9 survivors of a volunteer firefighter who dies of
10 work-related cancer by making them eligible for the
11 death benefit awarded by the State Board of Adjustment
12 for first responders who die in the line of duty.
