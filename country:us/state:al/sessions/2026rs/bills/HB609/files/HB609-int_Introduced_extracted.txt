4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, the Department of Economic
6 and Community Affairs is required to disburse monies
7 from the Neighbors Helping Neighbors Fund to eligible
8 recipients for weatherization assistance. To qualify
9 for assistance, a household income must be at or below
10 125 percent of the poverty level or meet other
11 qualifications.
12 This bill would change the threshold for
13 eligibility under the weatherization assistance program
14 to households having an income level at or below 200
15 percent of the poverty level.
16 This bill would authorize the department to
17 contract with certain nonprofit corporations to
18 administer the weatherization assistance program in
19 lieu of requiring that funds must be provided to
20 designated community action agencies or county
21 governments to administer the program.
22 This bill would also expand the source of monies
23 that may be deposited into the Neighbors Helping
24 Neighbors Fund.
