4 SYNOPSIS:
5 This bill would require all persons to obtain a
6 permit from the Alabama Department of Environmental
7 Management to operate a utility-scale solar energy
8 project.
9 This bill would provide for a permit application
10 process, establish filing fees, and require the
11 department to issue a decision in writing within 30
12 days of receiving a permit application.
13 This bill would require operators of
14 utility-scale solar energy projects to decommission the
15 projects within one year of ceasing operation and
16 restore the land within three years of ceasing
17 operation.
18 This bill would require operators of
19 utility-scale solar energy projects to establish a
20 bond, security deposit, or other irrevocable financial
21 assurances with the department in amounts sufficient to
22 decommission the project and restore the affected
23 lands.
24 This bill would authorize the department to
25 undertake decommissioning and restoration activities of
26 utility-scale solar energy projects that the department
27 determines are abandoned.
28 This bill would establish the Abandoned
