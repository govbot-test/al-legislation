HB636 Enrolled
2
3 Relating to Washington County; to amend Section 3 of
4 Act 84-549, 1984 Regular Session (Acts 1984, p. 1174), to
5 further provide for the compensation of the circuit clerk.
6 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
7 Section 1. Section 3 of Act 1984-549, 1984 Regular
8 Session (Acts 1984, p. 1174), is amended to read as follows:
9 "Section 3. The provisions of this act are severable.
10 If any part of this act is declared invalid or
11 unconstitutional, such declaration shall not affect the part
12 which remains When the person holding the office of circuit
13 clerk on January 1, 2026, vacates such office, Section 1 of
14 this act shall be repealed immediately and shall have no
15 effect on future office holders."
16 Section 2. This act shall become effective on October
17 1, 2026.
