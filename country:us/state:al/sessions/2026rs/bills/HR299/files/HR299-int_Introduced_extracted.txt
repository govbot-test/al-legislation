5 TRAVELERS AWARD FOR TEACHING EXCELLENCE.
6
7 WHEREAS, highest commendations and congratulations
8 are proudly presented to Kristi Bridges Smith, a dedicated
9 educator from Rainbow Middle School in Etowah County, for
10 receiving the coveted Travelers Insurance Award for Teaching
11 Excellence; and
12 WHEREAS, Mrs. Smith proudly represented Etowah County
13 and the State of Alabama at the Salute to Excellence in
14 Education Awards Gala held in Washington, D.C., on February
15 13, 2026; and
16 WHEREAS, the Gala celebrated and honored 48
17 outstanding educators from across the United States and is
18 often referred to as the "Academy Awards of Education"; the
19 Travelers Award recognizes educators who embody excellence
20 in their practice, commitment, and leadership to the
21 education profession; and
22 WHEREAS, Mrs. Smith is completing her 32nd year as an
23 educator; since 2014, she has taught sixth grade history and
24 math, served as a peer helper coordinator, and a Beta Club
25 sponsor at Rainbow Middle School; in her current role, she
26 ignites passion for mathematics as a Math Bee sponsor,
27 guiding the team to 10 straight championships; she also
28 works as an off-campus tutor for K-12 and college students;

30 WHEREAS, prior to Rainbow Middle, Mrs. Smith taught

32 sixth grade for six years at Hewitt-Trussville Middle

45 now therefore,

51 success in her future endeavors.
