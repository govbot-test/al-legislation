4 HR____ COMMENDING THE LEGACY AND SERVICE OF CLINTON ADAMS.
5
6 WHEREAS, it is a great honor to recognize Clinton
7 Adams for his legacy of empowerment and leadership; he has
8 committed over 20 years to enriching the ballroom culture
9 and supporting the LGBTQ+ community, particularly in
10 Alabama; and
11 WHEREAS, his journey began in the late 1990s when, as
12 a teenager, he immersed himself in the vibrant world of
13 ballroom; by 2004, he began formally teaching others,
14 launching an impactful movement that continues to thrive
15 today; and
16 WHEREAS, he dedicated himself to teaching ballroom
17 culture, drawing upon his extensive self-directed learning
18 and practice; he founded the first series of ballroom
19 mini-balls at Club 21 in Birmingham, which rapidly became a
20 vital hub for local youth; his ability to create a safe and
21 supportive environment within this chosen family structure
22 provided many individuals the mentorship they desperately
23 sought; and
24 WHEREAS, Clinton Adams has become a leading advocate
25 for health and wellness within the LGBTQ+ community; he
26 provided awareness of sexually transmitted diseases (STDs)
27 by organizing over 100 testing events and utilized ballroom
28 culture to encourage youth to seek necessary health care;
