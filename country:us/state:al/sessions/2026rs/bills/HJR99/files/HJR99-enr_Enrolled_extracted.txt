7 WHEREAS, it is with the highest commendation and
8 esteem that the Alabama Legislature recognizes the 43rd
9 Alabama State Games Opening Ceremony as an America 250

11 WHEREAS, the Alabama State Games, originally known as
12 the Alabama Sports Festival (ASF), operating as a 501(c)(3)
13 nonprofit organization, was established at the request of
14 the United States Olympic Committee and incorporated in
15 1982; it is a member of the National Congress of State Games
16 and the second-oldest State Games program in the United
17 States; and
18 WHEREAS, the Alabama State Games touches the lives of
19 thousands of Alabamians each year by creating an

22 $466,000 to participating athletes, and hosting additional
23 programs to encourage academics, healthy lifestyles, and
24 good citizenship; and
