6 WHEREAS, it is with great pride and distinct pleasure
7 that we recognize and commend Elaine Sessions on being named
8 the 2025 Citizen of the Year by the Southwest Mobile County
9 Chamber of Commerce, an honor reflecting her extraordinary
10 dedication to service and her steadfast commitment to
11 improving the lives of others; and
12 WHEREAS, Elaine Sessions exemplifies the highest
13 ideals of citizenship; as a respected professional,
14 compassionate servant leader, and tireless advocate for her
15 community, she has consistently gone above and beyond to be
16 a source of light, leadership, and encouragement throughout
17 Mobile County; and
18 WHEREAS, this distinguished award recognizes not only
19 her decades of professional excellence but also her lifelong
20 devotion to community service; an associate broker with
21 Roberts Brothers Realtors, Elaine Sessions has devoted 48
22 years to the real estate profession; she began her career
23 while teaching high school in Grand Bay and Theodore and
24 later taught real estate classes for more than 35 years,
25 shaping and mentoring countless professionals; today, she
26 continues to lead and inspire a successful team of agents,
27 generously sharing her knowledge and experience with others
28 in the field; and
