4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, an individual who parks in a
6 designated disability access parking place without
7 proper authorization will face varying penalties.
8 This bill would provide that an individual who
9 is not an individual with a disability or who is not
10 transporting an individual with a disability and uses a
11 distinctive disability license plate or placard to park
12 in a designated disability access parking place shall
13 be guilty of a violation and face a fine and community
14 service in certain circumstances.
15 This bill would require certain individuals to
16 notify the Department of Revenue within a certain
17 period of time of the death of a disabled individual
18 who was issued a windshield placard.
19 This bill would also authorize the Department of
20 Revenue to create an electronic scanning system for
21 disability placards.
