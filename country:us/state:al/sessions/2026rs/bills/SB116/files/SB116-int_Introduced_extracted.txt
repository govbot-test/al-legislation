4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, circuit clerks are not
6 authorized to participate in both a supernumerary
7 program and the Employees' Retirement System.
8 This bill would authorize circuit clerks who
9 independently qualify for a supernumerary program and
10 the Employees' Retirement System based on separate
11 years of service to participate in both programs.
