HB255 Enrolled
1 Enrolled, An Act,
2
3
4 Relating to the Alabama Crime Victims Compensation
5 Commission; to amend Section 15-23-12, Code of Alabama 1975,
6 to further provide for the filing of claims.
7 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
8 Section 1. Section 15-23-12, Code of Alabama 1975, is
9 amended to read as follows:
10 "§15-23-12
11 (a) Compensation shall not be awarded in any of the
12 following circumstances:
13 (1) A claim has been filed with the commission later
14 than one year two years after the injury or death upon which
15 the claim is based, unless the commission finds there was good
16 cause for the failure to file within that time.
17 (2) To a claimant who was the offender, or an
18 accomplice of the offender, or who encouraged or in any way
19 participated in the criminally injurious conduct.
20 (3) If the award would unjustly benefit the offender or
21 accomplice of the offender.
22 (4) The criminally injurious conduct resulting in
23 injury or death was reported to a law enforcement officer
24 later than 72 hours after its occurrence, unless the
25 commission finds there was good cause for the failure to
26 report within that time.
27 (b) Compensation otherwise payable to a claimant may be
28 diminished or denied to the extent that the economic loss is
Page 1
diminished or denied to the extent that the economic loss is
HB255 Enrolled
29 recouped from collateral sources;, or to the extent that the
30 degree of responsibility for the cause of the injury or death
31 is attributable to the victim as determined by the commission.
32 (c) The commission, upon finding that the claimant or
33 victim has not fully cooperated with appropriate law
34 enforcement agencies, may deny, withdraw, or reduce an award
35 of compensation.
36 (d) The commission, on its own motion or on request of
37 the claimant, may reconsider a decision granting or denying an
38 award or determining its an award amount. An order on
39 reconsideration of an award shall not require a refund of
40 amounts previously paid, unless the award was obtained by
41 fraud. The right of reconsideration does not affect the
42 finality of a commission decision for the purpose of judicial
43 review."
44 Section 2. This act shall become effective on October
45 1, 2026.
