5 A BILL
6 TO BE ENTITLED
7 AN ACT
8
9 Relating to government ethics; to add Section 36-25-1.4
10 to the Code of Alabama 1975, to provide that the ethics laws
11 do not limit certain research and development activities
12 conducted by public institutions of higher education.
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
14 Section 1. Section 36-25-1.4 is added to the Code of
15 Alabama 1975, to read as follows:
16 §36-25-1.4
17 Nothing in this chapter shall be deemed to limit or
18 restrict the ability of public institutions of higher
19 education, along with the public officials and public
20 employees within the institutions, to:
21 (1) Accept and award grants and contracts, conduct
22 research, collaborate with persons both within and outside the
23 institution, enter into technology transfer agreements, and
24 otherwise commercialize, protect, and share intellectual
25 property by agreement in accordance with institution policy;
26 and
27 (2) Establish one or more entities organized under
28 Title 10A to promote innovation and commercialization
Page 1
Title 10A to promote innovation and commercialization
SB237 Engrossed
29 consistent with the institution's mission.
30 Section 2. This act shall become effective on June 1,
31 2026.
