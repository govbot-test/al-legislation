41 Section 2. Section 22-20-5.5 is added to the Code of
42 Alabama 1975, to read as follows:
43 §22-20-5.5
44 (a) A food service establishment as defined in the
45 sanitation rules adopted by the Alabama Department of Public
46 Health shall include any cafeteria, canteen, commissary, or
47 other location that purchases or receives delivery of food for
48 storage, and prepares, stores, serves, or dispenses food in
49 individual portion size to inmates, which is operated by the
50 Department of Corrections or any county or municipality.
51 (b)(1) The Alabama Department of Public Health may
52 enforce existing rules that govern food service establishments
53 which may also be applied to facilities operated by the
54 Department of Corrections or a county or municipality as
55 described in subsection (a).
56 (2) The Alabama Department of Health shall adopt rules
Page 2
(2) The Alabama Department of Health shall adopt rules
SB84 INTRODUCED
57 that are appropriate for food service establishments operated
58 by the Department of Corrections and county and municipal
59 jails, to ensure the sanitary conditions of food storage,
60 preparation, and service, the enforcement of which shall be
61 under the jurisdiction of the health officer in the county
62 where the facility is located.
63 (3) Pursuant to subdivision (1), the Alabama Department
64 of Public Health shall consider the unique requirements and
65 limitations of correctional facilities and jails, and may
66 inspect food service establishments operated therein, identify
67 sanitary violations, issue scores, and prescribe corrective
68 action, but may not order the suspension or termination of any
69 food service establishment that is:
70 a. Operated by the Department of Corrections without
71 the agreement of the Commissioner of Corrections; or
72 b. Operated by a county or municipal jail without the
73 agreement of the governing body of the county or municipality.
74 (4) In any case in which the Department of Corrections,
75 or a county or municipality, contracts with a private
76 individual or entity to serve or dispense food in individual
77 portion size to inmates, the individual or entity shall remain
78 subject as a food service establishment to existing,
79 applicable sanitation rules of the Alabama Department of
80 Public Health.
81 Section 3. This act shall become effective on October
82 1, 2026.
