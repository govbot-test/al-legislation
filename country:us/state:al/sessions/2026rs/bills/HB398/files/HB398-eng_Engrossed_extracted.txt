6 TO BE ENTITLED
7 AN ACT
8
9 Relating to sales and use taxes; to exempt High Socks
10 for Hope, from the payment of state sales and use taxes; and
11 to allow municipalities and counties to exempt this entity
12 from local sales and use taxes.
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
14 Section 1. (a) High Socks for Hope is exempt from
15 paying state sales and use taxes.
16 (b) Any county or municipality may exempt this entity
17 from the payment of county or municipal sales and use taxes in
18 accordance with Section 40-23-4.01, Code of Alabama 1975.
19 Section 2. This act shall become effective on September
20 1, 2026.
