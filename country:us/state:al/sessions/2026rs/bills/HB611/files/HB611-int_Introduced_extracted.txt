9 Relating to Winston County; to authorize the Winston
10 County Commission to levy a lodging tax; to provide for the
11 collection and distribution of the proceeds of the tax; to
12 impose a fire protection fee; to provide for the collection
13 and distribution of the fire protection fee; and to provide
14 for a referendum.
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
16 Section 1. (a) In addition to all other taxes now
17 imposed by law, the Winston County Commission may levy a
18 privilege or license tax upon the occupancy of a room or
19 rooms, lodging, or accommodation furnished to a transient in
20 any hotel, motel, inn, tourist camp, tourist cabin, short-term
21 rental, or any other place in which accommodations are
22 regularly furnished to transients for consideration.
23 (b) The tax shall be paid by the transient guest
24 occupying the accommodations and shall be collected by the
25 person, firm, or corporation furnishing the accommodations at
26 the time payment for occupancy is made.
27 (c) The amount of the tax shall be equal to eight
28 percent of the charge for the rooms, lodgings, or
Page 1
percent of the charge for the rooms, lodgings, or
HB611 INTRODUCED
29 accommodations, including the charge for use or rental of
30 personal property and services furnished in the room.
31 (d) Rentals or services taxed under Article 1, Chapter
32 23 of Title 40, Code of Alabama 1975, shall be exempt from the
33 tax levied under this act.
34 Section 2. (a) The tax authorized to be levied by this
35 act shall be collected and enforced in the same manner as the
36 state lodgings tax as provided in Chapter 26 of Title 40, Code
37 of Alabama 1975, and subject to the same exemptions as the
38 state lodging tax as provided in Chapter 26 of Title 40, Code
39 of Alabama 1975. Nonprofit organizations qualifying as tax
40 exempt under 26 U.S.C. § 501(c)(3) shall also be exempt from
41 the tax levied by this act.
42 (b) The tax herein levied shall constitute a debt due
43 to the county. The tax, together with any interest and
44 penalties, shall constitute and be secured by a lien upon the
45 property of any person from whom the tax is due or who is
46 required to collect the tax.
47 (c) The county shall receive the tax, is authorized to
48 enforce this act, and shall have and exercise all rights and
49 remedies applicable or which may be provided for in the future
50 for the collection of the state lodging tax by the Department
51 of Revenue.
52 Section 3. The proceeds from the tax collected pursuant
53 to this act shall be distributed as follows:
54 (1) Fifty-five percent to road and bridge improvements
55 within Winston County, distributed among county commission
56 districts based upon the percentage of lodging facilities or
Page 2
districts based upon the percentage of lodging facilities or
HB611 INTRODUCED
57 short-term rental properties located within each county
58 commission district, as evaluated annually by the Winston
59 County Revenue Commissioner.
60 (2) Twenty percent to the Sheriff of Winston County to
61 be expended at the discretion of the sheriff for law
62 enforcement purposes.
63 (3) Fifteen percent to the Winston County General Fund
64 to promote tourism development in Winston County, including,
65 but not limited to, marketing, visitor attraction, and
66 recreational development.
67 (4) Ten percent to the Winston County General Fund.
68 These funds shall first be used exclusively to retire any
69 outstanding debts or loans associated with the construction of
70 the Winston County judicial building and jail existing at the
71 time this act is passed. After these loans and debts have been
72 fully satisfied, the funds may be used for other lawful county
73 purposes.
74 Section 4. (a) In addition to the lodging tax
75 authorized to be levied under this act, the Winston County
76 Commission shall impose and collect a fire protection fee in
77 the amount of five dollars ($5) per night for each transient
78 accommodation rented or furnished within Winston County.
79 (b) The fee shall be paid by the transient guest
80 occupying the accommodations and shall be collected by the
81 person, firm, or corporation furnishing the accommodations at
82 the same time and in the same manner as the lodging tax and
83 shall be remitted to the county.
84 (c) The proceeds from the fire protection fee shall be
Page 3
(c) The proceeds from the fire protection fee shall be

85 distributed equally among volunteer fire departments operating
86 in Winston County.
87 Section 5. This act shall become operative only if
88 approved by a majority of the qualified electors of Winston
89 County who vote in an election to be held on the day
90 designated by the Judge of Probate of Winston County, at a
91 referendum held pursuant to Section 67-1.00 of the
92 Constitution of Alabama of 2022. The notice of the election
93 shall be given by the judge of probate, and the election shall
94 be held, conducted, and the results canvassed in the same
95 manner as other county elections. The election shall be held
96 in conjunction with the next regularly scheduled state or
97 local election to be held in the county. The question shall
98 be, "Do you favor the adoption of Act ___, of the 2026 Session
99 of the Alabama Legislature, establishing an eight percent tax
100 on lodging charges and a five dollar ($5) fire protection fee
101 per night on lodging accommodations? Yes ( ) No ( )." The
102 county shall pay any costs and expenses not otherwise
103 reimbursed by a governmental agency which are incidental to
104 the election. If a majority of the votes cast in the election
105 are "Yes," this act shall become effective immediately. If the
106 majority of the votes are "No," this act shall be repealed and
107 shall have no further effect. The judge of probate shall
108 certify the results of the election to the Secretary of State.
109 Section 6. This act shall become effective on October
110 1, 2026.
