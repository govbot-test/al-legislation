9 Relating to Montgomery County; to amend Section
10 45-51-231.01, Code of Alabama 1975, to further provide for the
11 expense allowance and salary of the sheriff if the sheriff has
12 served two or more full consecutive terms as sheriff.
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
14 Section 1. Section 45-51-231.01 of the Code of Alabama
15 1975, is amended to read as follows:
16 "§45-51-231.01
17 (a)(1) Any Sheriff of Montgomery County who has served
18 two consecutive full terms shall receive an additional expense
19 allowance of twenty seventy thousand dollars ($20,000)
20 ($70,000) per year, payable in equal monthly installments from
21 the general fund of the county. This expense allowance shall
22 be in addition to all other compensation or benefits granted
23 to the sheriff and may be treated as compensation for
24 retirement contributions.
25 (2) This subsection shall be void at the earlier of the
26 end of the term of the sheriff serving on August 1, 2023, or
27 on upon a vacancy in the office of sheriff for any reason.
28 (b) Effective beginning the next term of office of the
Page 1
(b) Effective beginning the next term of office of the

29 Sheriff of Montgomery County, and thereafter, any sheriff who
30 has served two or more consecutive full terms as sheriff,
31 shall receive an annual salary in the amount of one hundred
32 seventy-five thousand dollars ($175,000) two hundred
33 twenty-five thousand dollars ($225,000). The salary shall be
34 paid from the general fund of the county.
35 Section 2. This act shall become effective on the first
36 day of the first month following its passage.
