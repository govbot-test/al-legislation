4 HR____ CELEBRATING MRS. KATHLEEN PEEVY UPON THE OCCASION OF
5 HER 100TH BIRTHDAY.
6
7 WHEREAS, highest commendations and congratulations
8 are offered to Mrs. Kathleen Peevy of Brewton, Alabama, on
9 the momentous occasion of her 100th Birthday; and
10 WHEREAS, Kathleen Peevy was born on March 15, 1926,
11 to Martin and Julia Still of Brewton; she graduated from
12 W.S. Neal High School in 1944 and then attended business
13 school in Montgomery; and
14 WHEREAS, following her graduation from business
15 school, Kathleen started working for Bell South as a
16 telephone operator; this career took her to Portland,
17 Oregon; Milton, Florida; and Pensacola, Florida; during her
18 time in Milton, she went to school to learn to become a test
19 desk man for Bell South; this was unique for her as she was
20 the first female test desk man in northwest Florida; and
21 WHEREAS, over the years, Kathleen has been involved
22 in Ladies' Aid with the Methodist Church, and The Telephone
23 Employees Activities Association, where she enjoyed playing
24 golf, sewing, cooking, and gardening; a few years ago,
25 Kathleen was honored to serve as Grand Marshal of the W.S.
26 Neal High School Homecoming Parade; and
27 WHEREAS, among her accomplishments, she has enjoyed
28 being a wife, mother, grandmother, great-grandmother, and
