5 A BILL
6 TO BE ENTITLED
7 AN ACT
8
9 Relating to vessels; to prohibit law enforcement
10 officers from stopping or boarding vessels for safety or
11 marine sanitation equipment inspections.
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
13 Section 1. (a) A law enforcement officer may not stop
14 or board a vessel for the sole purpose of performing a safety
15 inspection or marine sanitation equipment inspection.
16 (b) For the purposes of this section, the term "vessel"
17 has the meaning as defined in Section 33-5-3, Code of Alabama
18 1975.
19 Section 2. This act shall become effective on June 1,
20 2026.
