4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, the Alabama State Law
6 Enforcement Agency is responsible for enforcing the
7 laws regarding the operation of vessels on waters under
8 the jurisdiction of this state.
9 This bill would prohibit a law enforcement
10 officer from stopping or boarding a vessel for the
11 purpose of performing a safety or marine sanitation
12 equipment inspection.
