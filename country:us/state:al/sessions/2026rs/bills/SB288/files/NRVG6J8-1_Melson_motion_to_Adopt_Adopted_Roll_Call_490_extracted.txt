4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, the directors of an
6 agriculture authority's board of directors are
7 appointed by a combination of the county commission and
8 legislative delegation where the agriculture authority
9 is located.
10 This bill would require the board of directors
11 to appoint a successor upon the expiration of each
12 director's term five years after an agriculture
13 authority is established.
14 Under existing law, an agriculture authority may
15 only purchase items on the statewide public contract
16 list.
17 This bill would authorize an agriculture
18 authority to make purchases through a national or
19 regional governmental cooperative purchasing program.
20 Under existing law, county and municipal sales
21 and use tax proceeds collected by an agriculture
22 authority are rebated to the agriculture authority.
23 This bill would provide that county and
24 municipal tax proceeds from the sale of alcoholic
25 beverages collected by an agriculture authority are
26 rebated to the agriculture authority.
27 This bill would also make nonsubstantive,
28 technical revisions to update existing code language to
Page 1
technical revisions to update existing code language to
29 current style.
