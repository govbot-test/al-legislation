4 SYNOPSIS:
5 The Fair Campaign Practices Act requires
6 political action committees and principal campaign
7 committees to deposit and hold campaign contributions
8 in and make expenditures from a banking account. The
9 law also states that contributions, along with any
10 proceeds made from investing the contributions, may be
11 used to pay expenditures. The law is otherwise silent
12 with regard to investing campaign contributions.
13 This bill would require all contributions to a
14 political action committee or principal campaign
15 committee to be held in a federally insured financial
16 institution.
