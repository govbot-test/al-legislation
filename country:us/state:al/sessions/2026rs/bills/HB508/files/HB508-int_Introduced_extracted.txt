4 SYNOPSIS:
5 This bill would propose an amendment to the
6 Constitution of Alabama of 2022, to repeal Section
7 28-11.20, relating to Etowah County and requiring voter
8 approval to annex any territory within the county into
9 any municipality not located wholly within the
10 boundaries of the county before January 1, 1986.

14 TO BE ENTITLED
15 AN ACT
16
17 To propose an amendment to the Constitution of Alabama
18 of 2022, relating to Etowah County; to repeal Section 28-11.20
19 of the Constitution of Alabama of 2022, requiring voter
20 approval to annex any territory within the county into any
21 municipality not located wholly within the boundaries of the
22 county before January 1, 1986, with certain exceptions.
23 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
24 Section 1. The following amendment to the Constitution
25 of Alabama of 2022, is proposed:
26 PROPOSED AMENDMENT
27 Section 28-11.20 of the Constitution of Alabama of
28 2022, relating to Etowah County and requiring voter approval

29 to annex any territory within the county into any municipality
30 not located wholly within the boundaries of the county before
31 January 1, 1986, is repealed.
32 Upon ratification of this constitutional amendment, the
33 Code Commissioner shall number and place this amendment as
34 appropriate in the constitution omitting this instructional
35 paragraph and may make the following nonsubstantive revisions:
36 change capitalization, hierarchy, spelling, and punctuation
37 for purposes of style and uniformity; correct manifest
38 grammatical, clerical, and typographical errors; revise
39 internal or external citations and cross-references; harmonize
40 language; and translate effective dates.
41 END PROPOSED AMENDMENT

43 shall be held in accordance with Sections 284, 284.01, and 285
44 of the Constitution of Alabama of 2022, and the election laws
45 of this state. The appropriate election official shall assign
46 a ballot number for the proposed constitutional amendment on
47 the election ballot and shall set forth the following
48 description of the substance or subject matter of the proposed
49 constitutional amendment:

51 the Constitution of Alabama of 2022, to repeal Section
52 28-11.20 of the Constitution of Alabama of 2022, requiring
53 voter approval before annexing any territory within the county
54 into a municipality not located wholly within the boundaries
