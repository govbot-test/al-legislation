3 To propose an amendment to the Constitution of Alabama

9 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
10 Section 1. The following amendment to the Constitution
11 of Alabama of 2022, is proposed:
12 PROPOSED AMENDMENT
13 Section 28-11.20 of the Constitution of Alabama of
14 2022, relating to Etowah County and requiring voter approval
15 to annex any territory within the county into any municipality
16 not located wholly within the boundaries of the county before
17 January 1, 1986, is repealed.
18 Upon ratification of this constitutional amendment, the
19 Code Commissioner shall number and place this amendment as
20 appropriate in the constitution omitting this instructional

23 for purposes of style and uniformity; correct manifest
24 grammatical, clerical, and typographical errors; revise
25 internal or external citations and cross-references; harmonize
26 language; and translate effective dates.
27 END PROPOSED AMENDMENT
