1 Enrolled, An Act,
2
3 Relating to Henry County; to further provide for the
4 disposition of special transaction fees collected by the Henry
5 County Judge of Probate.
6 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
7 Section 1. Section 45-34-84.20, Code of Alabama 1975,
8 is amended to read as follows:
9 "§45-34-84.20
10 (a) In addition to all other fees and costs provided by
11 law, a special transaction fee in an amount determined by the
12 Henry County Commission, but not exceeding five dollars ($5),
13 shall be paid to the Henry County Judge of Probate when any
14 public business is transacted in his or her office.
15 (b) The special additional transaction fees shall be
16 collected by the judge of probate and deposited no later than
17 the 20th day of the month following collection as follows:
18 (1) The first two dollars ($2) into a special fund in
19 the Henry County Judge of Probate office to be used at the
20 discretion of the judge of probate for the costs of
21 maintaining, preserving, and upgrading the records and
22 recording equipment and other services necessary for the
23 improvement of the office of the judge of probate.
24 (2) Any amount in excess of two dollars ($2) into the
25 Henry County General Fund.
26 (c) The judge of probate may transfer funds from the
27 special fund in the Henry County Judge of Probate office to
28 the Henry County General Fund at his or her discretion."
