6 AN ACT

9 Relating to Henry County; to further provide for the
10 disposition of special transaction fees collected by the Henry
11 County Judge of Probate.
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
13 Section 1. Section 45-34-84.20, Code of Alabama 1975,
14 is amended to read as follows:
15 "§45-34-84.20
16 (a) In addition to all other fees and costs provided by
17 law, a special transaction fee in an amount determined by the
18 Henry County Commission, but not exceeding five dollars ($5),
19 shall be paid to the Henry County Judge of Probate when any
20 public business is transacted in his or her office.
21 (b) The special additional transaction fees shall be
22 collected by the judge of probate and deposited no later than
23 the 20th day of the month following collection as follows:
24 (1) The first two dollars ($2) into a special fund in
25 the Henry County Judge of Probate office to be used at the
26 discretion of the judge of probate for the costs of
27 maintaining, preserving, and upgrading the records and
28 recording equipment and other services necessary for the
Page 1
recording equipment and other services necessary for the
HB459 INTRODUCED
29 improvement of the office of the judge of probate.
30 (2) Any amount in excess of two dollars ($2) into the
31 Henry County General Fund.
32 (c) The judge of probate may transfer funds from the
33 special fund in the Henry County Judge of Probate office to
34 the Henry County General Fund at his or her discretion."
35 Section 2. This act shall become effective immediately.
