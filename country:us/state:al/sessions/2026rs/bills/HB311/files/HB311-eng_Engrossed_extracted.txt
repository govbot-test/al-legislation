6 TO BE ENTITLED
7 AN ACT
8
9 Relating to retirement benefits; to amend Section
10 36-22-64, Code of Alabama 1975, to authorize qualifying
11 sheriffs to participate in both a supernumerary sheriff
12 program and the Employees' Retirement System.
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
14 Section 1. Section 36-22-64, Code of Alabama 1975, is
15 amended to read as follows:
16 "§36-22-64
17 (a) No individual may participate in both a
18 supernumerary sheriff program and the Employees' Retirement
19 System based on the same service.
20 (b) An individual who independently qualifies for a
21 supernumerary sheriff program and the Employees' Retirement
22 System based on separate years of service may participate in
23 both programs.
24 (c) If any sheriff is eligible for retirement benefits
25 under any other county, state, or municipal retirement plan or
26 act, then, in that event, he or she shall elect the plan plans
27 or act acts in which he or she desires to participate and
28 shall so notify the proper authority within not later than 60
Page 1

29 days after he or she becomes eligible for any payments under
30 this article. It is further; provided, however that such
31 election shall not affect any such person's individual's
32 entitlement to benefits under the provisions of Article 4 of
33 Chapter 21 of this title."
34 Section 2. This act shall become effective on October
35 1, 2026.
