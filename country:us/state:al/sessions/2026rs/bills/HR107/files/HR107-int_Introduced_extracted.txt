4 HR____ MOURNING THE DEATH AND CELEBRATING THE LIFE OF
5 RICHARD ALLEN BENDER.
6
7 WHEREAS, it is with profound sorrow that we note the
8 death of Richard Allen "Rich" Bender of Citronelle, Alabama,
9 on January 28, 2026, at the age of 71; and
10 WHEREAS, his departure leaves a void in the lives of
11 his loving family, many friends, and the community where he
12 was highly regarded; and
13 WHEREAS, Rich Bender was born in Altoona,
14 Pennsylvania, on February 25, 1954, and began his career
15 there as a carpenter and general contractor; he also worked
16 as an Emergency Medical Technician; and
17 WHEREAS, he moved to Citronelle in 2000 and worked at
18 RDA, Crowne Health Care of Citronelle as Maintenance
19 Supervisor, South Alabama Utilities, and Andrews Hardware;
20 and
21 WHEREAS, he loved taking on projects and was never
22 satisfied until they were completed perfectly, often using
23 his skills to help others; he was a dedicated husband to his
24 wife, Tina, who frequently helped him with his projects; and
25 he also enjoyed relaxing by working on jigsaw puzzles and
26 watching Gunsmoke reruns; and
27 WHEREAS, Rich made many friends in Citronelle and was
28 a valued member of the community; he served on the
