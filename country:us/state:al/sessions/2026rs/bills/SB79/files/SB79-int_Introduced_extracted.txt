4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under current law, any contributions made by an
6 employer to a Trump Account on behalf of an employee,
7 or dependent of an employee pursuant to 26 U.S.C. §
8 530A, shall be included in gross income of the
9 employee.
10 Under expiring law, an individual taxpayer is
11 allowed an exclusion from gross income for amounts paid
12 by their employer on any qualified education loan
13 pursuant to 26 U.S.C. § 127(c)(1)(B).
14 This bill would exclude contributions by the
15 employer to Trump Accounts from the gross income of an
16 individual taxpayer and make the exclusion for amounts
17 paid by an employer on qualified education loans
18 permanent for individual taxpayers.
