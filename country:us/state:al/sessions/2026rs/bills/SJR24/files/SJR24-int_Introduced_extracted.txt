4 SJR___ MOURNING THE DEATH AND CELEBRATING THE LIFE OF JOHN
5 WALLER GILES.
6
7 WHEREAS, it is with profound sorrow that we note the
8 passing of Mr. John Waller Giles at the age of 71 in
9 Luverne, Alabama, while we also celebrate his compassion,
10 advocacy, and public service that aided many during his
11 lifetime; and
12 WHEREAS, his departure on January 17, 2026, leaves a
13 void in the lives of his loving family, many friends, and
14 the community where he was highly regarded; and
15 WHEREAS, born on September 27, 1954, in Orlando,
16 Florida, John was the second son of William Ollie Giles, Sr.
17 and Mary Ella Waller Giles; and
18 WHEREAS, John was a lifelong resident of Montgomery
19 and Honoraville, Alabama; he graduated from Robert E. Lee
20 High School in Montgomery and joined the family business,
21 Giles Enterprises, Inc., for the first part of his career;
22 he then founded Peaches'n Clean, a carpet-cleaning business
23 still in operation today; and
24 WHEREAS, he also held a variety of public positions;
25 he served as the Small Business Advocate for the State of
26 Alabama and Chief of Existing Business Industry Services
27 under Governor Guy Hunt and served as the Appointment
28 Secretary for Governor Fob James; he then served as
