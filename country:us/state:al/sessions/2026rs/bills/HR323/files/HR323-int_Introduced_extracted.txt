6 WHEREAS, Elder Robert Smith, Sr. devoted his life to
7 the ministry of the Gospel, answering the call to preach in
8 1973 and faithfully proclaiming the Word of God for more
9 than five decades; and
10 WHEREAS, in 1980, he began his pastoral leadership at
11 the Church of the Living God in Coffeeville, Alabama, where
12 he served with steadfast dedication for 45 years; and
13 WHEREAS, throughout his ministry, Elder Smith was
14 known as a faithful shepherd, a powerful preacher, and a
15 source of spiritual strength, ministering to countless
16 individuals and leaving an enduring imprint on the lives he
17 touched; and
18 WHEREAS, he stood firmly on the principles of faith
19 and truth, preaching with integrity and a deep sense of
20 purpose, embodying a life committed not only to God's Word,
21 but to the people he was called to serve; and
22 WHEREAS, even after transitioning from active
23 pastoring due to health, his legacy of service, leadership,
24 and devotion continues to inspire, reflecting a life well
25 lived, a race well run, and a servant who remained faithful
26 to his calling; and
27 WHEREAS, his life's work stands as a testament to
28 enduring faith and a profound commitment to uplifting

33 celebrate the life of Elder Robert Smith, Sr., recognizing
34 his extraordinary contributions to ministry and the lasting
35 influence he has had on his congregation, community, and all
36 who were blessed to know him; it is an honor to dedicate
37 this resolution to him with utmost gratitude, admiration,
