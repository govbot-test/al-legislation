4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing administrative rule of the State
6 Oil and Gas Board, carbon dioxide storage facility
7 operators are charged certain statutorily authorized
8 fees adopted by the board.
9 This bill would codify and modify certain
10 existing fees and add an additional fee.
11 This bill would prohibit carbon dioxide being
12 stored in a storage facility that is more than 50 miles
13 from where the carbon dioxide was generated.
14 This bill would also prohibit carbon dioxide
15 generated outside of the state from being stored in in
16 the state.
