5 TO BE ENTITLED
6 AN ACT
7
8
9 Relating to Limestone County; amend Section
10 45-42-110.01, Code of Alabama 1975; to remove the limit placed
11 on the additional expense allowance provided to members of the
12 board of registrars; to authorize the county commission to set
13 the amount of the additional expense allowance by resolution.
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
15 Section 1. Section 45-42-110.01, Code of Alabama 1975,
16 is amended to read as follows:
17 "§45-42-110.01
18 (a) This section shall apply only to Limestone County.
19 (b)(a)(1) Each member of the Limestone County Board of
20 Registrars shall be entitled to receive an additional expense
21 allowance in the amount of fifteen dollars ($15) per day of an
22 amount established by the county commission through
23 resolution, for each day when the board is authorized to meet
24 and conduct business. The county commission may change or
25 amend the amount of the allowance through passing a subsequent
26 resolution. The expense allowance provided for in this
27 sectionsubsection shall be in addition to any and all other
28 compensation, salary, and expense allowance provided by law
Page 1
compensation, salary, and expense allowance provided by law
HB412 INTRODUCED
29 and shall be paid out of the county general fund.
30 (c)(2) The operation of this sectionsubsection shall be
31 retroactive to October 1, 2001, and all actions and payments
32 made to the board on and after that date are hereby ratified
33 and confirmed.
34 (b) Beginning on the effective date of this act, the
35 Limestone County Commission shall establish, by resolution, an
36 expense allowance for members of the board of registrars in an
37 amount determined by the commission. This subsection
38 supersedes subsection (a), and on the effective date of this
39 act, the expense allowance in subsection (a) shall be void.
40 The expense allowance provided for in this subsection shall be
41 in addition to any and all other compensation, salary, and
42 expense allowance provided by law and shall be paid out of the
43 county general fund."
44 Section 2. This act shall become effective on October
45 1, 2026.
