4 SJR___ MOURNING THE DEATH AND CELEBRATING THE LIFE OF THE
5 HONORABLE SAMUEL ALAN HARPER.
6
7 WHEREAS, it is with profound sorrow that we note the
8 passing of our former colleague and friend, The Honorable
9 Samuel Alan Harper, of Alabaster, Alabama, on December 18,
10 2025, at the age of 68; and
11 WHEREAS, born and reared in Northport, Alabama, Alan
12 Harper earned his baccalaureate degree from The University
13 of Alabama in 1986; the university honored him as the School
14 of Communications Alumnus of the Year in 2001; among many
15 other accolades and awards, he was selected as Man of the
16 Year by the Aliceville Area Chamber of Commerce in 2009 and
17 Legislator of the Year by the Economic Development
18 Association of Alabama in 2012; and
19 WHEREAS, Mr. Harper's career evidenced his commitment
20 to public service; during his decade-long career with the
21 Tuscaloosa County Industrial Development Authority, his work
22 was pivotal in bringing numerous companies, more than 4,000
23 jobs, and more than $500,000,000 in capital investment to
24 the area, including the development and launch of the
25 Tuscaloosa County Mercedes facility; and
26 WHEREAS, Mr. Harper also served as the City Manager
27 of Aliceville and as Retail Development Director for the
28 City of Northport; his public service continued as State
