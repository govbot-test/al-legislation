4 Relating to crimes and offenses; to amend Section
5 13A-6-24, Code of Alabama 1975; to further provide for the
6 crime of reckless endangerment; and to provide conditions for
7 increased criminal penalties for a violation.
8 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
9 Section 1. This act shall be known and may be cited as
10 the Amy Dicks Act.
11 Section 2. Section 13A-6-24, Code of Alabama 1975, is
12 amended to read as follows:
13 "§13A-6-24
14 (a) A person commits the crime of reckless endangerment
15 if he or she does either of the following:
16 (1) recklessly Recklessly engages in conduct which that
17 creates a substantial risk of serious physical injury to
18 another personindividual.
19 (2) Recklessly engages in conduct that creates a
20 substantial risk of serious physical injury to multiple
21 individuals.
22 (b)(1) Reckless endangerment A violation of subdivision
23 (a)(1) is a Class A misdemeanor.
24 (2)a. Except as provided in paragraph b., a violation
25 of subdivision (a)(2) is a Class C felony.
26 b. A violation of subdivision (a)(2) is a Class B
27 felony if the person uses a deadly weapon or dangerous
28 instrument in the commission of the offense."
