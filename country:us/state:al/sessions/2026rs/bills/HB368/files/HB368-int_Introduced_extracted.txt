5 TO BE ENTITLED
6 AN ACT
7
8
9 Relating to Randolph County; to provide for staggered
10 terms for members of the county commission.
11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
12 Section 1. Effective for the general election in 2028,
13 the members of the Randolph County Commission representing
14 Districts 1, 3, and 5 shall be elected for terms of two years
15 and the other members shall be elected for terms of four
16 years. Thereafter, at the general election preceding the
17 expiration of the term of any member of the commission, any
18 successor shall be elected for a term of four years.
19 Section 2. This act shall become effective on October
20 1, 2026.
