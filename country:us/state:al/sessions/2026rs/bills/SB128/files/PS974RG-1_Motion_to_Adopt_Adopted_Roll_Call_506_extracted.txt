10 Relating to the Alabama Sunset Law; to continue the
11 existence and functioning of the Alabama Sickle Cell Oversight
12 and Regulatory Commission until October 1, 2030, with certain
13 modifications; to amend Sections 22-10B-1 and 22-10B-2, Code
14 of Alabama 1975, to place the commission as a division within
15 the Alabama Department of Public Health; to require the
16 department to provide all necessary administrative services
17 for the commission; to authorize the department to provide for
18 an executive director and outside experts for the commission;
19 to transfer all property of the commission to the department;

21 confirmation by the Senate to serving upon appointment unless
22 and until rejected by the Senate; and to repeal Section
23 22-10B-7, Code of Alabama 1975, relating to the Alabama Sickle
24 Cell Oversight and Regulatory Commission.
