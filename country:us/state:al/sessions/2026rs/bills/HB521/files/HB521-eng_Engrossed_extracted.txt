9 Relating to boards of registrars; to amend Sections
10 17-3-2, 17-3-5, and 17-3-8 as last amended by Act 2025-22 of
11 the 2025 Regular Session, and Section 17-4-33, Code of Alabama
12 1975; to require candidates for a board of registrars to
13 submit to a criminal background check; to require that the
14 state pays the salaries of the boards of registrars when the
15 boards' working days are affected by county, state, or
16 national emergencies; to require registrars to be considered
17 as employees for purposes of receiving natural disaster,
18 pandemic relief, or other emergency funds; and to permit
19 members of the board of registrars to omit certain information
20 about themselves from the voter registration list.
