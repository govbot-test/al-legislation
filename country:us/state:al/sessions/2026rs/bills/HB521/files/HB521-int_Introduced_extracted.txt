4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, the boards of registrars are
6 charged with overseeing the registration of voters in
7 the counties of Alabama and are subject to certain
8 qualifications.
9 This bill would require candidates for
10 appointment to a board of registrars to submit to a
11 criminal background check and report the findings to
12 the board that appoints registrars.
13 Under existing law, each board is allocated a
14 certain number of days that it may meet.
15 This bill would require that the state pay
16 registrars when a scheduled working day is affected by
17 a declared county, state, or federal emergency or
18 similar order.
19 Under existing law, a member of a board of
20 registrars is considered an employee for purposes of
21 Social Security.
22 This bill would require that registrars be
23 considered state employees for purposes of receiving
24 natural disaster, pandemic, or other emergency relief
25 funding.
26 Under existing law, certain public officials are
27 entitled to omit all required information except their
28 name from the generally available voter registration

29 list.
30 This bill would allow registrars to omit all
31 information about themselves except their name from the
