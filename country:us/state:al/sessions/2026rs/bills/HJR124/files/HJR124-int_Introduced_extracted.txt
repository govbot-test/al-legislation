7 WHEREAS, it is with great pride and pleasure that we
8 recognize and commend the exemplary service of Mountain
9 Brook Police Officer Richard Knecht, who serves as the
10 School Resource Officer (SRO) for Mountain Brook High
11 School, for receiving the Safe Schools Leadership Award and
12 for being named the 2025 City of Mountain Brook Employee of
13 the Year; and
14 WHEREAS, Officer Knecht began his law enforcement
15 career in the City of Bessemer in 2000 and transferred to
16 the Mountain Brook Police Department (MBPD) in 2003; he has
17 served as the Mountain Brook High School (MBHS) SRO for more
18 than eight years, during which time he has earned the
19 respect, trust, and admiration of students, faculty,
20 administrators, and parents alike; and
21 WHEREAS, he was nominated for the Safe Schools
22 Leadership Award in recognition of his dedication to school
23 safety, exceptional leadership, and unwavering commitment to
24 the well-being of students, qualities that distinguish him
25 among his peers across the state; and
26 WHEREAS, the Safe Schools Leadership Award was
27 presented to Officer Knecht at The Alabama Association of
28 School Resource Officers (TAASRO) Conference held June 2–6,
Page 1
School Resource Officers (TAASRO) Conference held June 2–6,

29 2025, which was attended by approximately 1,200 school
30 resource officers and school administrators from Alabama and
31 neighboring states; and
32 WHEREAS, in addition to this honor, Officer Knecht
33 was recognized as a TAASRO School Safety Specialist, an
34 elite designation held by only 15 officers statewide; and
35 WHEREAS, on January 21, 2026, at the Mountain Brook
36 Chamber of Commerce Annual Luncheon, Officer Knecht was
37 honored for his exceptional service, leadership, and
38 positive impact on the community by being named the 2025
39 City of Mountain Brook Employee of the Year, a tribute that
40 reflects not only his professional excellence but also the
41 lasting influence he has made on the lives of students,
42 families, and colleagues through his dedication to public
43 service; now therefore,
44 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA, BOTH
45 HOUSES THEREOF CONCURRING, That we most highly commend
46 Officer Richard Knecht for his faithful and exemplary
47 service, unwavering commitment to school safety, and
48 dedication to the City of Mountain Brook and the State of
49 Alabama, and we extend to him our heartfelt gratitude,
50 respect, and sincere best wishes for continued health,
51 happiness, and fulfillment in all the days to come.
