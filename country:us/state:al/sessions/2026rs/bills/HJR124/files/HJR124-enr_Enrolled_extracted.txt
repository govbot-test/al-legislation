4 COMMENDING MOUNTAIN BROOK POLICE OFFICER RICHARD KNECHT.
5
6 WHEREAS, it is with great pride and pleasure that we
7 recognize and commend the exemplary service of Mountain
8 Brook Police Officer Richard Knecht, who serves as the
9 School Resource Officer (SRO) for Mountain Brook High
10 School, for receiving the Safe Schools Leadership Award and
11 for being named the 2025 City of Mountain Brook Employee of
12 the Year; and
13 WHEREAS, Officer Knecht began his law enforcement
14 career in the City of Bessemer in 2000 and transferred to
15 the Mountain Brook Police Department (MBPD) in 2003; he has
16 served as the Mountain Brook High School (MBHS) SRO for more
17 than eight years, during which time he has earned the
18 respect, trust, and admiration of students, faculty,
19 administrators, and parents alike; and
20 WHEREAS, he was nominated for the Safe Schools
21 Leadership Award in recognition of his dedication to school
22 safety, exceptional leadership, and unwavering commitment to
23 the well-being of students, qualities that distinguish him
24 among his peers across the state; and

28 2025, which was attended by approximately 1,200 school
