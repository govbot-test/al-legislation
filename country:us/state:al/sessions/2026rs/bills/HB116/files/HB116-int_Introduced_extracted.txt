4 SYNOPSIS:
5 The Volunteer Rescue Squad Tuition Reimbursement
6 Program pays tuition on behalf of students who are
7 studying to qualify for emergency medical services
8 personnel licensure from the Alabama Department of
9 Public Health. In exchange for tuition, the students
10 contract with the program to work for at least two
11 years for an ambulance service, a volunteer rescue
12 squad, or a volunteer fire department.
13 Current law authorizes the member schools of the
14 Alabama Community College System, the Alabama Fire
15 College, and the University of South Alabama to provide
16 the necessary educational program to students and
17 receive tuition reimbursement. This bill would add the
18 Alabama Department of Public Health through its
19 regional agencies of the Office of Emergency Medical
20 Services to the group of participating educational
21 institutions.
22 This bill would also make nonsubstantive,
23 technical revisions to update the existing code
24 language to current style.
