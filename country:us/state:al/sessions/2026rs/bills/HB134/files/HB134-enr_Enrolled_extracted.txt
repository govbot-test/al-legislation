3 Relating to Lamar County; to amend Section 45-38-71.01,
4 Code of Alabama 1975; to revise the process for personal
5 property auctions by the county commission; to remove any
6 publication requirement for the disposition of county property
7 via online auction; and to make nonsubstantive, technical
8 revisions to update existing code to current language style.
9 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
10 Section 1. Section 45-38-71.01, Code of Alabama 1975,
11 is amended to read as follows:
12 "§45-38-71.01
13 (a) This section shall only apply in Lamar County.
14 (b)(1) All contracts for the sale or disposal of real
15 property, or tangible personal property, equipment, or other
16 items owned by or under the control of the county commission
17 shall be let by free and open competitive sealed bids. The
18 chair of the county commission shall certify to the
19 description and condition of the property, shall give
20 jurisdiction in writing for the disposal of the property,
21 shall estimate the value of the property, and shall keep the
22 certification as a part of the permanent record of the
23 commission. Tangible personal property, equipment, or other
24 items owned by or under the control of the county commission
25 which have has trade-in value may be offered as a credit
26 against the cost of property purchased in accordance with the
27 Alabama Competitive Bid Law Article 3 of Chapter 16 of Title
28 41.
