4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, initial applicants and
6 certain individuals seeking a renewal for a driver
7 license are subject to an examination created by the
8 Alabama State Law Enforcement Agency (ALEA).
9 This bill would require ALEA to administer all
10 examinations for securing a driver license exclusively
11 in the English language.
12 This bill would also make nonsubstantive,
13 technical revisions to update the existing code
14 language to current style.
