9 Relating to Class 3 municipalities; to mandate minimum
10 staffing requirements for municipal law enforcement agencies;
11 to authorize the State of Alabama to assume operational
12 oversight of a municipal police department under certain
13 conditions; to authorize the Alabama State Law Enforcement
14 Agency to establish a corrective action plan and take other
15 remedial measures for municipalities in default; and to
16 authorize the Alabama State Law Enforcement Agency to adopt
17 rules.
18 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
19 Section 1. (a) Each Class 3 municipality shall maintain
20 a minimum ratio of 1.9 full-time law enforcement officers for
21 every 1,000 residents of the municipality, as determined by
22 the 2020 federal decennial census.
23 (b) A Class 3 municipality that does not meet the
24 requirements of subsection (a) on October 1, 2026, shall be
25 granted an initial grace period of five years to achieve full
26 compliance. The municipality shall be exempt from satisfying
27 the requirements of subsection (a) during the grace period,
28 provided the municipality complies with the requirements of
