11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
12 Section 1. For the purposes of this act, the following
13 terms have the following meanings:
14 (1) EMERGENCY WRIT CONDITION. Any time, as determined
15 by the sheriff, when more writs have been issued in the county
16 than the sheriff can execute in a reasonable time after
17 issuance.
18 (2) SPECIAL RESERVE DEPUTY. An individual appointed by
19 the sheriff to serve writs under emergency writ conditions.
20 (3) WRIT. A lawful writ issued by the Circuit or
21 District Clerk of the Tenth Judicial Circuit relating to the
22 seizure or securing of real property located within Jefferson
23 County.
24 Section 2. (a) During an emergency writ condition, the
25 Sheriff of Jefferson County may appoint special reserve
26 deputies to assist in the execution of writs or similar
27 actions involving the seizure or securing of real property.
