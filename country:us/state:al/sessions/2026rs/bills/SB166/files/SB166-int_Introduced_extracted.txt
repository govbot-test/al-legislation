4 SYNOPSIS:
5 The Fair Campaign Practices Act requires
6 political action committees, principal campaign
7 committees, and in certain circumstances municipal
8 candidates to file periodic reports and statements of
9 campaign activity, including details relating to
10 contributions, expenditures, bank accounts, and loans.
11 Prosecutions for violations of the act must be brought
12 within two years from the date of the violation, except
13 prosecutions for violating the law governing how excess
14 campaign contributions may be spent must be brought
15 within four years from the date of the violation.
16 This bill would require any committee or other
17 person required to file a report or statement under the
18 act to preserve all records for a period of four years
19 after the filing date of the report or statement to
20 which the record relates.
