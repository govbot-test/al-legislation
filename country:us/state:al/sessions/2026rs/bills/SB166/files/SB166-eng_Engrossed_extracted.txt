5 A BILL
6 TO BE ENTITLED
7 AN ACT
8
9 Relating to campaign finance; to add Section 17-5-6.1
10 to the Code of Alabama 1975, to allow persons that are
11 required to file any report or statement under the Fair
12 Campaign Practices Act to destroy records after a period of
13 four years.
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
15 Section 1. Section 17-5-6.1 is added to the Code of
16 Alabama 1975, to read as follows:
17 §17-5-6.1
18 Each political action committee, principal campaign
19 committee, or other person required to file any report or
20 statement under this chapter may destroy any records after the
21 expiration of four years. For purposes of this section,
22 records include: (i) any record or account required under this
23 chapter; and (ii) a copy of each report or statement filed
24 under this chapter and all records relevant to the report or
25 statement.
26 Section 2. This act shall become effective on October
27 1, 2026.
