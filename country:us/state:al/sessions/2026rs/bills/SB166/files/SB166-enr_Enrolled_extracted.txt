SB166 Enrolled
1 Enrolled, An Act,
2
3
4 Relating to campaign finance; to add Section 17-5-6.1
5 to the Code of Alabama 1975, to allow persons that are
6 required to file any report or statement under the Fair
7 Campaign Practices Act to destroy records after a period of
8 four years.
9 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
10 Section 1. Section 17-5-6.1 is added to the Code of
11 Alabama 1975, to read as follows:
12 §17-5-6.1
13 Each political action committee, principal campaign
14 committee, or other person required to file any report or
15 statement under this chapter may destroy any records after the
16 expiration of four years. For purposes of this section,
17 records include: (i) any record or account required under this
18 chapter; and (ii) a copy of each report or statement filed
19 under this chapter and all records relevant to the report or
20 statement.
21 Section 2. This act shall become effective on October
22 1, 2026.
