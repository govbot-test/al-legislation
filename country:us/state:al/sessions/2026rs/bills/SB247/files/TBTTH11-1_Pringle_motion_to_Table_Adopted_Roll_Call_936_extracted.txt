49 (a) There is established a permanent Health Care
50 Nonprofit Holding Corporation Oversight Board for the
51 purposes of: (i) ensuring compliance by any health care
52 service corporation that reorganizes pursuant to Section
53 10A-20-6.17 with the requirements of that section; and
54 (ii) monitoring the practices of any nonprofit holding
55 corporation or health care service corporation after
56 reorganization.
57 (b) The membership of the oversight board shall be
58 comprised of all of the following:
59 (1) An individual knowledgeable in the area of health
60 care delivery, health care insurance, or corporate
61 structuring, appointed by the Governor.
62 (2) An individual knowledgeable in the area of health
63 care delivery, health care insurance, or corporate
64 structuring, appointed by the President Pro Tempore of
65 the Senate.
66 (3) An individual knowledgeable in the area of health
67 care delivery, health care insurance, or corporate
68 structuring, appointed by the Speaker of the House of
69 Representatives.
70 (4) A representative of the largest public entity
71 that is insured by a health care service corporation as
72 measured by the total number of individuals covered,
measured by the total number of individuals covered,
