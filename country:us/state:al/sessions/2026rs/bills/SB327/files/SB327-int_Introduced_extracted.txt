4 SYNOPSIS:
5
6 Under existing law, employers subject to the
7 Alabama Workers' Compensation Law are required to meet
8 certain standards relating to substance abuse testing
9 of employees and job applicants, including notice
10 requirements.
11 This bill would require any employer with a drug
12 or alcohol testing policy, including those subject to
13 the Alabama Workers' Compensation Law, to provide
14 verbal and written notice to all employees of the
15 policy and post the policy in a prominent employee
16 access area.
17 This bill would require employers to similarly
18 notify employees of any subsequent changes to the drug
19 and alcohol policy at least 10 days before the change
20 is effective.
21 This bill would also require employers to
22 encourage employees who test positive for drugs or
23 alcohol in violation of policy to seek rehabilitation.
