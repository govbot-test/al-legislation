4 HR____ MOURNING THE DEATH AND CELEBRATING THE LIFE OF EDDIE
5 REED.
6
7 WHEREAS, we deeply regret and are greatly saddened to
8 learn of the death of Eddie Reed, former Mayor of Jemison,
9 Alabama, and we uplift his life and legacy with utmost
10 reverence and respect; and
11 WHEREAS, Eddie Reed, born May 30, 1945, transitioned
12 on March 16, 2026, at the age of 80, following a
13 distinguished and historic career in public service; and
14 WHEREAS, he stands as the longest-serving elected
15 official in Chilton County history, dedicating an
16 extraordinary 49 years to public service, including his
17 tenure as Mayor of Jemison from 2004 until October 2025; and
18 WHEREAS, in 1976, he made history as the first
19 African American elected to public office in Chilton County,
20 marking the beginning of his service on the Jemison City
21 Council and paving the way for future generations of
22 leaders; and
23 WHEREAS, throughout his decades of service, he was
24 widely respected for his steady leadership, integrity, and
25 love for the City of Jemison, consistently working to
26 strengthen the community and improve the lives of its
27 citizens; and
28 WHEREAS, beyond his official duties, he was a pillar
