5 NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL CHEERLEADING CHAMPIONSHIP.
6
7 WHEREAS, commendations and congratulations are herein
8 extended to the Boaz High School Cheer Team on winning the
9 2026 National High School Cheerleading Championship in the
10 Division II Varsity Coed Non-Tumbling Competition; and
11 WHEREAS, the prestigious competition was hosted by
12 the Universal Cheerleaders Association as part of the
13 National School Spirit Championship, and was held on
14 February 1, 2026, at the ESPN Wide World of Sports Complex
15 at Walt Disney World® Resort in Osceola County, Florida; and
16 WHEREAS, under the leadership of Head Coach Tiffany
17 Rhoden and Coach Leah Milam, the Boaz Pirates Varsity Cheer
18 Team delivered a top-notch performance, adding to its
19 impressive resume that already includes 16 State

21 Championships: 1996, 1997, 2002, and 2026; and
22 WHEREAS, Coach Rhoden, who has led the program since
23 2006, has guided the Pirates to nine state titles and now
24 the school's first national cheerleading championship in 24
25 years; and
