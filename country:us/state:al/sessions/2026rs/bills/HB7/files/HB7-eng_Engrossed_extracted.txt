9 Relating to crimes and offenses; to amend Sections
10 13A-10-240, 13A-10-241, 13A-10-242, and 16-1-24.1, Code of
11 Alabama 1975; to provide for the definition of a "credible
12 threat"; to revise the elements of and provide further for the
13 penalties for the crime of making a terrorist threat in the
14 first and second degrees; to require a public school principal
15 to immediately notify law enforcement if the conduct of the
16 student warrants the charge of making a terrorist threat in
17 the first or second degree; to specifically provide that being
18 charged with either crime is a reason for which a student
19 shall immediately be suspended for a minimum of one year; to
20 provide further for the conditions of readmittance; to provide
21 for restitution upon a finding of guilt; and to add Section
22 13A-10-243 to the Code of Alabama 1975; to require any person
23 found guilty of making a terrorist threat in the first or
24 second degree to make restitution.
25 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:

318 §13A-10-243
319 (a) A person convicted or adjudicated of making a
320 terrorist threat in the first degree or second degree shall be

327 course of the investigation or prosecution. Where the
328 terrorist threat results in an emergency response, the
329 defendant shall be ordered to pay restitution for the expenses
330 incurred by any local, state, or federal law enforcement or
331 assisting governmental agency. Expenses include any reasonable
332 costs directly incurred, including the costs of police,
333 firefighting, and emergency medical services, and the
334 personnel costs of those persons who respond to the incident.
