4 SYNOPSIS:
5 This bill would provide that a person commits
6 the crime of bail jumping in the third degree if he or
7 she violates any condition of his or her court ordered
8 electronic monitoring after having been lawfully
9 released from custody with or without bail.
10 This bill would also provide criminal penalties
11 for violations.

14 A BILL
15 TO BE ENTITLED
16 AN ACT
17
18 Relating to crimes and offenses; to add Section
19 13A-10-40.1 to the Code of Alabama 1975, to provide for the
20 crime of bail jumping in the third degree; and to provide
21 criminal penalties for violations.
22 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
23 Section 1. Section 13A-10-40.1 is added to the Code of
24 Alabama 1975, to read as follows:
25 §13A-10-40.1
26 (a) A person commits the crime of bail jumping in the
27 third degree if, having been lawfully released from custody,
28 with or without bail, upon condition that he or she is subject
