4 HR____ MOURNING THE DEATH AND CELEBRATING THE LIFE OF
5 KENNETH THOMPSON.
6
7 WHEREAS, it is with profound sadness that we note the
8 death of Kenneth "Kenny" Thompson of Hartselle, Alabama, on
9 January, 25, 2026, at the age of 78; he was a devoted
10 community member, teacher, leader, and friend to many; and
11 WHEREAS, Kenny was born on July 11, 1947, in
12 Tallapoosa County, Alabama, to Duel Thompson and Johnnie
13 Lucille Wood Thompson; and
14 WHEREAS, as an employee of the Morgan County Board of
15 Education as a teacher, principal, and coach, Kenny helped
16 shape the minds of generations of young people; and
17 WHEREAS, his service to his city was remarkable, he
18 was the longest-serving member of the Hartselle City
19 Council; he served for nearly four decades, first from 1980
20 to 1992 and again from 2004 to 2025, most recently serving
21 as the council president; and
22 WHEREAS, Kenny was known as an intelligent leader who
23 had a keen ability to defuse tense situations; throughout
24 his time serving on the city council, Kenny was able to calm
25 people down and be a voice of reason; he was a tremendous
26 supporter of Hartselle and genuinely cared about all of its
27 residents; and
28 WHEREAS, in addition to his public service, Kenny was
