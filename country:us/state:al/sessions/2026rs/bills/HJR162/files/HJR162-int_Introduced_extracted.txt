8 WHEREAS, obesity is caused by a complex mix of
9 environmental and biological factors, and it puts
10 individuals at risk for other diseases, such as heart
11 disease and diabetes; and
12 WHEREAS, in 2019, the Centers for Disease Control
13 reported that obesity accounted for nearly $173 billion in
14 medical expenditures annually in the United States;
15 GlobalData estimates that obesity has negatively impacted
16 the State of Alabama's GDP by $7.2 billion and annually
17 impacts the state budget by over $750 million; 2024
18 Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System data estimates
19 the prevalence of obesity in Alabama is estimated at 38.9
20 percent for adults and 15.6 percent for children enrolled in
21 the Women, Infants, and Children program in Alabama; and
