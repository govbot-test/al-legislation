5 OF ALABAMA AND DESIGNATING MARCH 10, 2026, AS "JAPAN DAY" IN

8 WHEREAS, the State of Alabama and Japan have worked
9 to develop a strong and enduring relationship grounded in
10 mutual respect, shared values, and decades of cooperation;
11 and

13 robust commercial engagement; two-way trade exceeded $2.8

16 Commerce; and
