8 WHEREAS, it is with great pride and special pleasure

14 postseason run on April 5, 2026, by defeating the University

16 Fieldhouse in Indianapolis, Indiana, thereby securing the
17 first NIT championship in program history; and

23 Pearl, who was named Auburn's 22nd head men's basketball
