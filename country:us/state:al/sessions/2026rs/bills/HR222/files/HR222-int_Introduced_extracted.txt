5 FOUNDATION'S 20TH ANNUAL "HONORING OUR ELDERS DAY" GALA.
6
7 WHEREAS, with great joy and special pleasure, we
8 honor Mr. Willie Smith as the special guest at the Rosetta
9 James Foundation's 20th Annual "Honoring Our Elders Day"
10 Gala at the Jackson Center in Huntsville, Alabama, on
11 Saturday, March 14, 2026; and
12 WHEREAS, a native of Madison County and a New Market
13 resident, Mr. Smith was raised on the family farm, where he
14 learned the value of hard work exemplified by the legacy of
15 his grandfather, Carter Smith, a record-setting Madison
16 County cotton farmer whose strong work ethic has endured
17 through the generations; Mr. Smith utilized the values of
18 diligence, reliability, industriousness, and dedication that
19 he learned on the farm in his professional career; and
20 WHEREAS, Mr. Smith's career includes work at Redstone
21 Arsenal, Alabama A&M University, University Volkswagen of
22 Huntsville, and 28 years with Goodyear Dunlop Tires; a true
23 renaissance man, he is also a gifted actor, writer, and
24 director; he authored and staged Were You There When They
25 Crucified My Lord and performed in Crowns at the Von Braun
26 Civic Center; known as "The Old School Cowboy," Mr. Smith
27 celebrates his heritage as a descendant of a Buffalo Soldier
28 of the 10th Cavalry; he and his wife, Patrice M. Smith,
