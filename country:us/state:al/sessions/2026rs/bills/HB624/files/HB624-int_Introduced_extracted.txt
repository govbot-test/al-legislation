6 AN ACT
7
8
9 Relating to Wilcox County; to amend Section 1 of Act
10 2006-370, 2006 Regular Session, to further provide for the
11 probate recording fee; and to repeal Act 1990-618, 1990
12 Regular Session (Acts 1990, p. 618) and Act 2001-365, 2001
13 Regular Session, relating to probate recording fees.
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
15 Section 1. Section 1 of Act 2006-370, 2006 Regular
16 Session, is amended to read as follows:
17 "Section 1. (a) A special recording fee of five dollars
18 ($5) ten dollars ($10) shall be collected by the Judge of
19 Probate of Wilcox County, with respect to each real property
20 instrument and each personal property instrument that may be
21 filed for record in the office of the judge of probate and for
22 the recording of other instruments and documents in the
23 probate office in the discretion of the governing body of the
24 county, and no.
25 (b) No such instrument shall be received for record in
26 the office of the judge of probate unless the special
27 recording fee of five dollars ($5) ten dollars ($10) is paid
28 thereon.
Page 1
thereon.
HB624 INTRODUCED
29 (c) The special recording fee shall be in addition to
30 all other fees, taxes, and other charges required by law to be
31 paid upon the filing for record of any real property
32 instrument or personal property instrument, and for the
33 recording of other instruments and documents in the probate
34 office in the discretion of the governing body of the county.
35 Section 2. Act 1990-618, 1990 Regular Session (Acts
36 1990, p. 618) and Act 2001-365, 2001 Regular Session, relating
37 to probate recording fees, are repealed.
38 Section 3. This act shall become effective on June 1,
39 2026.
