4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, the violation of any rule of
6 the Department of Conservation and Natural Resources
7 prohibiting the transportation and release of live
8 feral swine is a Class B misdemeanor punishable as
9 provided by law, except the offense is subject to a
10 minimum fine of two thousand five hundred dollars
11 ($2,500).
12 This bill would delete the element of
13 transportation of live feral swine from the specified
14 criminal penalties of this law.
15 This bill would also specify that the
16 transportation of live feral swine is not a criminal
17 offense.
18
19
20
21 A BILL
22 TO BE ENTITLED
23 AN ACT
24
25 Relating to the Department of Conservation and Natural
26 Resources; to amend Section 9-11-72, Code of Alabama 1975, to
27 delete provisions providing criminal penalties for the
28 transportation of live feral swine; and to provide that the
Page 1
transportation of live feral swine; and to provide that the
HB414 INTRODUCED
29 transportation of live feral swine is not a criminal offense.
30 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
31 Section 1. Section 9-11-72 of the Code of Alabama 1975,
32 is amended to read as follows:
33 "§9-11-72
34 (a) A violation of any rule of the Department of
35 Conservation and Natural Resources prohibiting the
36 transportation and release of live feral swine shall be a
37 Class B misdemeanor punishable as provided by law, except the
38 defendant shall be subject to a mandatory minimum fine of not
39 less than two thousand five hundred dollars ($2,500).
40 (b) Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the
41 department may not prohibit the transportation of live feral
42 swine, and the transportation of live feral swine shall not be
43 a criminal offense."
44 Section 2. This act shall become effective immediately.
