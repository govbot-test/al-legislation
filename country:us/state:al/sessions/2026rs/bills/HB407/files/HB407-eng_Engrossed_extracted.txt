9 Relating to state government; to designate the

15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
16 Section 1. (a) For the purposes of this section, the
17 following terms have the following meanings:
18 (1) MISS ALABAMA. The individual selected and
19 recognized by the Miss Alabama Organization to hold the title
20 of Miss Alabama for an annual term.
21 (2) OFFICIAL STATE AMBASSADOR. An honorary
22 representative designated to promote goodwill and civic
23 engagement and to serve as a public representation of the
24 State of Alabama.
25 (3) STATE AGENCY. Any department, board, bureau,
26 commission, or office of the State of Alabama, including the
27 Office of the Governor.

36 (c) An Official State Ambassador, upon invitation or
37 coordination with a state agency, may perform the following
38 duties:
39 (1) Represent the State of Alabama at ceremonial,
40 educational, civic, tourism-related, and economic development
41 events.
42 (2) Promote Alabama's communities, culture, history,
43 workforce, military families, and public-service initiatives.
44 (3) Encourage volunteerism, civic engagement, and
45 charitable service throughout the state.
46 (4) Serve as a goodwill representative of the state at
47 national or international events when appropriate.
48 (d) Participation by an Official State Ambassador in
49 any activity authorized by this section shall be voluntary.
50 (e)(1) Nothing in this section shall be construed to

52 reimbursement of expenses to an Official State Ambassador.
