3 Relating to 911 service; to add Section 11-98-4.3 to
4 the Code of Alabama 1975, to authorize the 911 Board to
5 institute a system to measure the emergency response
6 performance of local communication districts; to require, upon
7 request by the Attorney General, the 911 Board to audit local
8 communication districts and to submit a report on audit
9 findings to the Attorney General to be made public; and to
10 provide for a remediation plan by which to improve the
11 district's emergency response performance.
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
13 Section 1. Section 11-98-4.3 is added to the Code of
14 Alabama 1975, to read as follows:
15 §11-98-4.3
16 (a) For the purposes of this section, the term
17 "readiness" refers to the capability of a district, including
18 a public safety answering point, to expedite the answering,
19 receiving, and transferring of 911 calls and texts to
20 efficiently dispatch the appropriate emergency service
21 provider as soon as possible to a correct location where
22 property or life may be in jeopardy.
23 (b) The 911 Board shall establish a system that is
24 based upon best practices as adopted by nationally recognized
25 associations such as the National Emergency Number Association
26 and the Association of Public Safety Communications Officials
27 to measure the readiness of district public safety services
28 through the collection of data related to a district's
