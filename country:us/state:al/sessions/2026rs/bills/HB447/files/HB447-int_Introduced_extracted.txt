9 Relating to Montgomery County; to provide for the
10 filing of wills with the judge of probate.
11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
12 Section 1. (a) Any individual who is a resident of
13 Montgomery County and who has made a will may file the will in
14 the Office of the Judge of Probate of Montgomery County.
15 (b) The judge of probate shall maintain a docket in
16 which he or she shall register the will, the date on which it
17 was deposited in his or her office, and the date of withdrawal
18 of the will.
19 (c) The files shall be confidential and no individual
20 other than the individual depositing the will, his or her
21 legal representative, or his or her attorney may have access
22 to the file prior to the death of the individual making and
23 depositing the will.
24 (d) The docket shall be available for inspection by the
25 public.
26 Section 2. The judge of probate may charge a reasonable
27 fee for each will filed with his or her office.
