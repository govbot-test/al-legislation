3 SYNOPSIS:
4 Under existing law, the spouses of certain
5 military veterans are provided additional points on
6 open competitive employment tests for positions in the
7 classified state service.
8 This bill would require the spouses of active
9 duty service members to have additional points added to
10 their ratings for these positions.
11 This bill would authorize political subdivisions
12 of the state and private employers to grant veterans
13 and their spouses and spouses of active duty service
14 members preference in hiring and promotion.
15 This bill would also make nonsubstantive,
16 technical revisions to update the existing code
17 language to current style.
