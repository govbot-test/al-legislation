4 SYNOPSIS:
5 This bill would prohibit a franchisor from
6 requiring a franchisee that asserts a sincerely held
7 religious belief to operate on a religious day unless:
8 (i) the original franchise agreement required operation
9 on a religious day; (ii) the franchisee agrees to the
10 operation on a religious day; or (iii) the franchise
11 locations are expanded and the agreement setting forth
12 the expansion requires the operation on a religious
13 day.
14 This bill would also provide for a cause of
15 action if a franchisor violates this act and would
16 provide for penalties.
