4 TASK FORCE.
5
6 WHEREAS, House Joint Resolution 163, enacted as Act
7 2023-260 of the 2023 Regular Session, created the Chronic
8 Weight Management and Type 2 Diabetes Task Force to study
9 the health implications of chronic weight management and
10 type 2 diabetes; and
11 WHEREAS, Act 2023-260 provided that the task force
12 would submit findings and recommendations to the Speaker of
13 the House of Representatives, the President Pro Tempore of
14 the Senate, and the chairs of the appropriate legislative
15 committees no later than the fifteenth legislative day of
16 the 2025 Regular Session, at which time the task force would
17 be dissolved; and
18 WHEREAS, House Joint Resolution 97, enacted as Act
19 2025-79 of the 2025 Regular Session, extended the task
20 force; and
21 WHEREAS, the task force needs an additional year in
22 order to continue to develop recommendations relating to the
23 issue of health implications of chronic weight management
24 and type 2 diabetes; now therefore,
25 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA, BOTH
26 HOUSES THEREOF CONCURRING, That the Chronic Weight
27 Management and Type 2 Diabetes Task Force, created pursuant
28 to Act 2023-260, 2023 Regular Session, is extended.
Page 1
to Act 2023-260, 2023 Regular Session, is extended.
HJR282 Enrolled
29 (a) The task force shall prepare a report of its
30 findings and recommendations to the Speaker of the House of
31 Representatives, the President Pro Tempore of the Senate,
32 and the chairs of the appropriate legislative committees not
33 later than November 1, 2026. Upon filing its report, the
34 task force shall stand dissolved and discharged of any
35 future duties and liabilities.
36 (b) Pursuant to Section 36-14-17.1, Code of Alabama
37 1975, the task force shall provide the Secretary of State a
38 notice of all meetings, the name of each member serving on
39 the task force, and a copy of the final report and any other
40 documents produced by the task force throughout its
41 duration.
