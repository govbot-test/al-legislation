5 DIABETES TASK FORCE.

7 WHEREAS, House Joint Resolution 163, enacted as Act
8 2023-260 of the 2023 Regular Session, created the Chronic
9 Weight Management and Type 2 Diabetes Task Force to study
10 the health implications of chronic weight management and
11 type 2 diabetes; and
12 WHEREAS, Act 2023-260 provided that the task force
13 would submit findings and recommendations to the Speaker of
14 the House of Representatives, the President Pro Tempore of
15 the Senate, and the chairs of the appropriate legislative
16 committees no later than the fifteenth legislative day of
17 the 2025 Regular Session, at which time the task force would
18 be dissolved; and
19 WHEREAS, House Joint Resolution 97, enacted as Act
20 2025-79 of the 2025 Regular Session, extended the task
21 force; and
22 WHEREAS, the task force needs an additional year in
23 order to continue to develop recommendations relating to the
24 issue of health implications of chronic weight management
25 and type 2 diabetes; now therefore,
26 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA, BOTH
27 HOUSES THEREOF CONCURRING, That the Chronic Weight

HOUSES THEREOF CONCURRING, That the Chronic Weight
HJR282 INTRODUCED
28 Management and Type 2 Diabetes Task Force, created pursuant
29 to Act 2023-260, 2023 Regular Session, is extended.
30 (a) The task force shall prepare a report of its
31 findings and recommendations to the Speaker of the House of
32 Representatives, the President Pro Tempore of the Senate,
33 and the chairs of the appropriate legislative committees not
34 later than November 1, 2026. Upon filing its report, the
35 task force shall stand dissolved and discharged of any
36 future duties and liabilities.
37 (b) Pursuant to Section 36-14-17.1, Code of Alabama
38 1975, the task force shall provide the Secretary of State a
39 notice of all meetings, the name of each member serving on
40 the task force, and a copy of the final report and any other
41 documents produced by the task force throughout its
42 duration.
