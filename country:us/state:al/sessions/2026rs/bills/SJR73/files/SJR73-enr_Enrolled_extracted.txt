4 SJR___ ESTABLISHING THE FIRST RESPONDER BENEFITS TASK FORCE.
5
6 WHEREAS, first responders, such as firefighters and
7 law enforcement officers, are invaluable public servants,
8 regularly risking their lives in service to their fellow
9 Alabamians; and
10 WHEREAS, under existing law, local employees,
11 including first responders, who participate in the
12 Retirement Systems of Alabama (RSA) must accrue a certain
13 number of years of certifiable service before their
14 retirement benefits vest; and
15 WHEREAS, in practice, this means that if a first
16 responder sustains an injury in the line of duty and is
17 rendered permanently disabled before his or her retirement
18 benefits have vested, the first responder, whose on-the-job
19 injury prevents his or her return to service, must forfeit
20 certain unvested funds; and
21 WHEREAS, when first responders sustain injuries in
22 the line of duty and are unable to return to work, their
23 injury should not jeopardize their hard-earned benefits;
24 Alabama should ensure that these vital public servants are
25 properly protected and receive the reverence they rightfully
26 deserve; now therefore,
27 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA, BOTH
28 HOUSES THEREOF CONCURRING, That the First Responder Benefits
