5 CHANTE' LATRICE CHATMAN.
6
7 WHEREAS, with profound sorrow, we note the untimely
8 passing of Chante' Latrice Chatman of Oxford, Alabama, on
9 March 10, 2026, while we also celebrate with thanksgiving
10 the 50 years that she brightened the lives of her loving
11 family, many friends, and the community where she was highly
12 regarded; and
13 WHEREAS, born on March 8, 1976, Chante' was the fifth
14 child of Henry and Flossie Chatman; she was a 1994 graduate
15 of Munford High School in Talladega County, followed by her
16 achievement of two degrees from Alabama Agricultural and
17 Mechanical University and an Education Specialist degree
18 from Argosy University-Atlanta; and
19 WHEREAS, Chante' was a proud member of the Order of
20 Eastern Star and a blessed sister of the Iota Mu Omega
21 Chapter of Alpha Kappa Alpha (AKA) Sorority, Incorporated;
22 she also shared her beautiful singing talents with the
23 National Pan-Hellenic Council choir and the Southeastern AKA
24 choir; and
25 WHEREAS, she was a beloved educator, colleague,
26 mentor, and friend for Oxford City Schools, most recently
27 serving as a Reading Specialist at Oxford High School; to
28 her students, Ms. Chatman was far more than a teacher; she
