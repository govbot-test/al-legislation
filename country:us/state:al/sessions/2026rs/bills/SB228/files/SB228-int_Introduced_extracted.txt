4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, the Alabama Space Authority
6 is an office within the Alabama Department of Economic
7 and Community Affairs responsible for promoting
8 research and development of new space exploration and
9 spaceport technology within aerospace, aviation, and
10 related industries.
11 This bill would rename the authority the Alabama
12 Aerospace Authority.
13 This bill would increase the membership of the
14 authority by adding the Finance Director and the
15 Secretary of Workforce as ex officio members, and the
16 Chief Executive Officer of the U.S. Space and Rocket
17 Center Commission as an ex officio nonvoting member.
18 This bill would also provide further for the
19 duties and powers of the authority.
