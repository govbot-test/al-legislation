4 SYNOPSIS:
5 This bill would require both private health
6 benefit plans and Medicaid to pay for influenza
7 vaccinations that are administered by a licensed health
8 care provider.
9 This bill would also recognize that the required
10 vaccination coverage does not affect the right of
11 individuals, parents, or legal guardians to make the
12 decision concerning whether to accept or decline a
13 vaccination.
