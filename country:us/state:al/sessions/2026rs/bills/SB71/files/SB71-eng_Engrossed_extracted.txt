7 AN ACT

9 Relating to administrative law; to prohibit agencies
10 from adopting a new rule, or amending an existing rule, that
11 establishes standards for certain environmental protection
12 subjects that are more stringent than the federal
13 requirements; and to prohibit an agency from adopting a new
14 rule, or amending an existing rule, in the absence of federal
15 requirements, unless the rule is based on the best available
16 science and the weight of scientific evidence.
17 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
18 Section 1. (a) For purposes of this section, the
19 following terms have the following meanings:
20 (1) AGENCY. The term as defined in Section 41-22-3,
21 Code of Alabama 1975.

23 a. Is reliable, unbiased, subject to independent

25 b. Maximizes the quality, objectivity, relevance,

27 statistical information; human, animal, and other relevant
28 scientific studies; and, if applicable, human health
