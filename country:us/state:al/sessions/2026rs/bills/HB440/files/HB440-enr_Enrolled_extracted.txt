HB440 Enrolled
2
3 Relating to Escambia County; to further provide for
4 probate court fees.
5 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
6 Section 1. (a) The Judge of Probate of Escambia County,
7 in lieu of the fees prescribed by general law, shall charge
8 and collect the following fees for the following services:
9 (1) One hundred five dollars ($105) for the probate of
10 a will.
11 (2) One hundred five dollars ($105) for the
12 administration of intestate estate.
13 (3) Sixty-five dollars ($65) for name change.
14 (4) Sixty-five dollars ($65) for legitimation.
15 (5) One hundred forty-five dollars ($145) for
16 guardianship.
17 (6) One hundred forty-five dollars ($145) for
18 conservatorship.
19 (7) One hundred twenty-five dollars ($125) for
20 adoption.
21 (8) Five dollars ($5) for claims against wills and
22 estates.
23 (9) Fifteen dollars ($15) plus five dollars ($5) per
24 page to file a will.
25 (10) Ten dollars ($10) for indexing.
26 (11) Thirty dollars ($30) for notary recording.
27 (12) Twenty-five dollars ($25) for Uniform Commercial
28 Code (UCC) instruments.
Page 1
Code (UCC) instruments.

29 (13) Five dollars ($5) for UCC release.
30 (14) Thirty-five dollars ($35) for plat recording.
31 (b) Except as provided in subsection (a), the judge of
32 probate shall continue to charge and collect the service fees
33 that are prescribed by general law.
34 Section 2. This act shall become effective on October
35 1, 2026.
