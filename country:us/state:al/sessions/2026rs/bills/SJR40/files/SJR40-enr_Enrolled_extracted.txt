5 BAPTIST CHURCH.

7 WHEREAS, highest commendations and congratulations
8 are herein proudly presented to Gardendale First Baptist
9 Church on the momentous occasion of its 150th Anniversary;
10 and
11 WHEREAS, Gardendale First Baptist Church has survived
12 throughout the years by the commitment of its pastors and
13 members and by the loving grace of God; many generations of
14 members have been encouraged and their faith strengthened
15 through the leadership of this church; it has grown into a
16 beacon of unselfish love and compassion for the community;
17 and
18 WHEREAS, in 1876, under the leadership of the
19 church's first pastor of record, Elder James Grimes, a group
20 of believers established a permanent place of worship known
21 as Chalybeate Springs Baptist Church, with an initial
22 membership of 27; and
23 WHEREAS, the congregation moved to Main Street, now
24 the Lighthouse Building, in 1914, and three years later the
25 church changed its name to Gardendale First Baptist Church;
26 and

28 provided leadership that strengthened and improved the
