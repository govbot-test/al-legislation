1 Enrolled, An Act,

3 Relating to exemptions from state income taxation; to
4 amend Section 40-18-19, Code of Alabama 1975; to exempt the
5 first $5,000 of taxable income earned from inactive duty
6 training from state income tax for individuals that serve in
7 the Alabama National Guard; and to make nonsubstantive,
8 technical revisions to update the existing code language to
9 current style.
