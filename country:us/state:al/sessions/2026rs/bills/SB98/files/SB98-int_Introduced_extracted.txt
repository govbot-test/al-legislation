4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under the Alabama G.I. and Dependents'
6 Educational Benefit Act, educational benefits for up to
7 the undergraduate course level are available to
8 eligible children, spouses, and widows of deceased or
9 disabled veterans, veterans who are listed as missing
10 in action, and veterans who were prisoners of war while
11 serving in the United States Armed Forces.
12 This bill would expand these educational
13 benefits to include graduate courses in a medical
14 field.
15 This bill would limit the tuition that may be
16 charged.
17 This bill would also make nonsubstantive,
18 technical revisions to update the existing code
19 language to current style.
