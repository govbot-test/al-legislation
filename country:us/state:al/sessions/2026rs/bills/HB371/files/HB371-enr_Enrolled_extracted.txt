HB371 Enrolled
1 Enrolled, An Act,
2
3 To propose an amendment to the Constitution of Alabama
4 of 2022, relating to Pike County, to authorize the Judge of
5 Probate of Pike County to exercise equity jurisdiction
6 concurrent with certain circuit court cases if the judge of
7 probate is a licensed attorney in the State of Alabama.
8 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
9 Section 1. The following amendment to the Constitution
10 of Alabama of 2022, is proposed:
11 PROPOSED AMENDMENT
12 The Judge of Probate of Pike County may exercise equity
13 jurisdiction concurrent with that of the circuit court in
14 cases originally filed in the Probate Court of Pike County if
15 the judge of probate is a licensed attorney in the State of
16 Alabama. In any case subject to this amendment, the judge of
17 probate shall possess the power and authority of a circuit
18 court judge trying the case, and the case shall be treated in
19 all respects in the same manner as a case filed in circuit
20 court. The Alabama Rules of Civil Procedure shall apply in the
21 cases except as otherwise specifically provided by law. This
22 amendment is self-executing, but the Legislature may enact
23 additional laws to implement this amendment if needed.
24 Upon ratification of this constitutional amendment, the
25 Code Commissioner shall number and place this amendment as
26 appropriate in the constitution omitting this instructional
27 paragraph and may make the following nonsubstantive revisions:
28 change capitalization, hierarchy, spelling, and punctuation
