4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, a person may not transport
6 certain fish in certain numbers outside of this state.
7 This bill would prohibit a person from
8 transporting out of this state a live catfish that is
9 harvested from state waters.
10 This bill would also provide criminal penalties
11 for violations.
