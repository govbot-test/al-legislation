7 WHEREAS, it is with the highest commendation that the
8 Legislature of Alabama recognizes Senior Trooper Hunter
9 Woodruff as a recipient of the 2026 Alabama Legislative
10 Meritorious Service Award for Law Enforcement in recognition
11 of extraordinary courage and decisive action in the line of
12 duty; and
13 WHEREAS, on November 12, 2025, while traveling east
14 on Highway 14 in Autauga County, Trooper Woodruff observed a
15 Prattville Police Department vehicle traveling at a high
16 rate of speed with emergency equipment activated;
17 recognizing the urgency of the situation, he immediately
18 activated his own emergency equipment and followed the
19 officer in order to render assistance if needed; and
20 WHEREAS, Trooper Woodruff followed the Prattville
21 officer to an apartment complex off Covered Bridge Parkway
22 in Prattville, where they were advised of a gunshot victim
23 lying on the ground nearby; upon locating the victim,
24 Trooper Woodruff immediately began rendering lifesaving
25 first aid; and

27 victim's left side, Trooper Woodruff quickly removed the
28 victim's clothing, applied a sterilized clotting agent, and
