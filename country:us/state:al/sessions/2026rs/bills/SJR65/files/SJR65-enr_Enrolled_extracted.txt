8 WHEREAS, highest commendations and congratulations
9 are herein proudly presented to the Maplesville High School
10 Football Team on winning the 2025 Alabama High School
11 Athletic Association (AHSAA) Class 1A State Football
12 Championship in a rematch from last year's championship game
13 against Wadley High School; and
14 WHEREAS, through hard work, diligence, and an
15 unwavering commitment to excellence, the Maplesville Red
16 Devils put up nearly 400 yards of total offense, remained
17 undefeated, and captured the school's fifth state title with
18 a 42-22 win over Wadley; and
19 WHEREAS, the Maplesville Red Devils displayed several
20 standout performances; sophomore Nehemiah McCary ran for 182
21 yards, scoring on runs of 21 and 51 yards in the second
22 half; quarterback Pearce Yeargan ran for a touchdown, caught
23 a touchdown pass, and threw a touchdown pass; Eli Hubbert,
24 championship MVP, threw the touchdown pass to Yeargan,
25 scored on a seven-yard run, and led the Red Devils with
26 seven tackles; and
27 WHEREAS, Auburn University signee, Jacquez Wilkes,
28 ran for 43 yards and a touchdown, adding five total tackles
