4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, the mayor of a municipality
6 with a population of 12,000 or more may not vote with
7 the council or vote in the council's proceedings.
8 This bill would provide that the mayor of a
9 municipality with an even number of council members
10 shall vote as a council member in the case of any tie
11 vote.
