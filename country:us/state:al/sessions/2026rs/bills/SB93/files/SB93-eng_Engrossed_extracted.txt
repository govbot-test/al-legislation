13 Section 1. Section 11-43-2, Code of Alabama 1975, is
14 amended to read as follows:
15 "§11-43-2
16 (a)(1) Except as provided in subsection (c) and
17 subdivision (2), in all cities and towns at the general
18 election to be held at the time provided by law, and
19 quadrennially thereafter, there shall be elected a mayor, who,
20 in cities having a population of 12,000 or more according to
21 the last or any subsequent federal census, shall not sit with
22 the council nor have a vote in its proceedings, and he or she
23 shall have the power and duties conferred in this chapter.
24 (2) In all cities and towns with an even number of
25 council seats, the mayor may vote as a member of the council
26 in the case of any tie vote of the council, provided that the
27 local governing body has adopted a resolution authorizing the
28 mayor to act as such.
