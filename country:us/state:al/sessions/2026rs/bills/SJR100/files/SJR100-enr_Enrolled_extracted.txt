4 SJR___ HONORING MISS PRESTLEY SKELTON FOR WINNING THE 2026
5 AMERICAN LEGION DEPARTMENT OF ALABAMA ORATORICAL
6 COMPETITION.
7
8 WHEREAS, we proudly congratulate Miss Prestley
9 Skelton for winning the 2026 American Legion Department of
10 Alabama Oratorical Contest, held in Montgomery, Alabama, on
11 March 21, 2026; Miss Skelton is a back-to-back winner of
12 this competition, having won in 2025 as well; and
13 WHEREAS, Miss Skelton is a 12th-grade student at
14 Jasper High School, where her speech curriculum, guidance
15 from her teachers, and family support have played a key role
16 in her success; and
17 WHEREAS, the American Legion Oratorical Contest aims
18 to enhance students' knowledge of the U.S. Constitution,
19 offering scholarships that can be applied at colleges
20 nationwide; the competition starts at the post level,
21 advancing through district, division, and state levels,
22 ultimately culminating in a national competition; and
23 WHEREAS, contestants deliver an 8-10 minute prepared
24 oration on an aspect of the Constitution, followed by an
25 assigned topic; the oration emphasizes the duties and
26 obligations of citizens to our government, and the assigned
27 topic must be presented in a 3-5 minute oration; and
28 WHEREAS, Miss Skelton triumphed at the local Post 23

29 contest in Graysville, Alabama; the 21st District contest in
30 Hoover; the 2nd Division contest in Pelham; and at the
31 American Legion Department of Alabama state finals in
32 Montgomery; her prepared oration entitled "From Quill to
33 Code: Regarding AI and the First Amendment" earned her a
34 $2,500 scholarship for her first-place finish; and
35 WHEREAS, Miss Skelton will represent the American
36 Legion Department of Alabama at the 2026 National High
37 School Oratorical Contest Finals at Hillsdale College in
38 Michigan on May 15-17, joining 55 students worldwide for a
39 chance to win a $25,000 scholarship; now therefore,
40 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA, BOTH
41 HOUSES THEREOF CONCURRING, That we extend our highest
42 commendations to Miss Prestley Skelton for her victory in
43 the 2026 American Legion Department of Alabama Oratorical
44 Contest and wish her continued success in all future
45 endeavors.
