3 Relating to Randolph County; to provide for a booking
4 fee to be assessed in certain cases; and to provide for the
5 distribution of the revenue from the fee.
6 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
7 Section 1. (a) In Randolph County, the clerk of court
8 shall assess a booking fee of thirty dollars ($30) against
9 each defendant incarcerated in or booked in the Randolph
10 County Jail if the defendant is convicted of or pleads guilty
11 in cases prosecuted in the circuit or district court in the
12 county.
13 (b) The clerk of the court shall enter the booking fee
14 on the docket sheet and collect the fee in the same manner and
15 at the same time as other court costs are collected. The
16 booking fee assessed pursuant to this section shall be in
17 addition to all other fines, court costs, or other charges
18 provided by law. The court costs provided by this section
19 shall not be waived by any court unless all other fees,
20 assessments, costs, fines, and charges associated with the
21 case are waived.
22 (c) The clerk of court shall remit the booking fees
23 collected pursuant to this section on a monthly basis to the
24 Sheriff of Randolph County to be deposited in the Law
25 Enforcement Fund created pursuant to Section 45-56-232.01,
26 Code of Alabama 1975, and used for law enforcement purposes in
27 the county.
