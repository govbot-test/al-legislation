9 Relating to educational choice; to add Section 16-6J-10
10 to the Code of Alabama 1975, to provide enforcement mechanisms
11 for the CHOOSE Act's prohibition of discrimination against
12 CHOOSE Act participating students with respect to eligibility
13 for K-12 interscholastic athletics; to provide a right of
14 action for CHOOSE Act participants for violations, regardless
15 of when the alleged violation occurred; to permit courts to
16 award reasonable attorney fees; and to provide for legislative
17 findings.
18 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
19 Section 1. Section 16-6J-10 is added to the Code of
20 Alabama 1975, to read as follows:
21 §16-6J-10
22 (a) The Legislature finds and declares the following:
23 (1) The CHOOSE Act was enacted to expand educational
24 opportunity in Alabama and enhance the ability of Alabama
25 parents to choose the K-12 educational setting that best meets
26 the needs of their children.
27 (2) Recognizing the importance of interscholastic
28 athletics, the CHOOSE Act contains an athletic-eligibility

33 (3) Interscholastic athletics are a vital part of a
34 complete education, promoting teamwork, discipline,
35 leadership, personal growth, and community engagement.
36 Participation in interscholastic athletics, like participation

38 academic performance, school engagement, and social outcomes.

42 student undermines the CHOOSE Act's promise of meaningful
43 educational choice.
44 (5) It is essential to provide robust mechanisms to
45 enforce the CHOOSE Act's athletic-eligibility
46 nondiscrimination provision and otherwise ensure that a
47 student's CHOOSE Act status does not limit his or her ability
48 to participate in interscholastic athletics.
