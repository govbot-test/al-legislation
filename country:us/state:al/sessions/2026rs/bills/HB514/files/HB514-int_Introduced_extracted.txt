9 Relating to Tuscaloosa County; to further provide for
10 the compensation of the sheriff.
11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
12 Section 1. (a) Beginning June 1, 2026, the Sheriff of
13 Tuscaloosa County shall receive an additional expense
14 allowance of thirty-six thousand dollars ($36,000) per year,
15 payable in equal monthly installments from the county general
16 fund. This expense allowance shall be in addition to all other
17 expense allowances or other forms of compensation and shall be
18 treated as compensation for retirement contributions.
19 (b) This subsection shall be void upon the next vacancy
20 in the Office of the Sheriff of Tuscaloosa County.
21 Section 2. This act shall become effective on June 1,
22 2026.
