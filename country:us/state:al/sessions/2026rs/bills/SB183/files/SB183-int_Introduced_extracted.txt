4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, hair braiding and hair
6 weaving is considered a practice of cosmetology, and
7 natural hair styling is subject to the licensing and
8 training requirements of the Alabama Board of
9 Cosmetology and Barbering.
10 This bill would remove the hair braiding and
11 hair weaving from the definition of cosmetology.
12 This bill would exempt natural hair styling from
13 regulation by the Alabama Board of Cosmetology and
14 Barbering.
15 This bill would repeal the provisions that
16 provide for applicant qualifications for natural hair
17 stylist examination or licensing.
18 This bill would also make nonsubstantive,
19 technical revisions to update the existing code
20 language to current style.
