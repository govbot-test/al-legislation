12 resilience, highlighting students who have overcome

14 their schools and communities; each student receives a

20 injuries; she was later diagnosed with Juvenile Epilepsy and
21 began a difficult journey marked by monthly seizures,
22 medication side effects, hospitalizations, and ultimately
23 surgery to implant a vagus nerve stimulation device; and
24 WHEREAS, despite ongoing medical challenges, she
25 qualified for the state championship meet in cross country
26 and track, competing among the top runners in the 800-meter
27 run and 4×200-meter relay; and
28 WHEREAS, while managing the effects of her implanted
