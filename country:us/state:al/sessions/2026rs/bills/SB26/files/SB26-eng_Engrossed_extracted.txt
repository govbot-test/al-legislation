7 AN ACT

9 Relating to libraries; to amend Sections 11-90-2 and
10 11-90-4, Code of Alabama 1975; to further provide for the
11 appointment of county and municipal library boards; to provide
12 reporting requirements; and to make nonsubstantive, technical
13 revisions to update the existing code language to current
14 style.
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
16 Section 1. Sections 11-90-2 and 11-90-4, Code of
17 Alabama 1975, are amended to read as follows:
18 "§11-90-2
19 (a) The government and supervision of such All
20 libraries shall be vested in created pursuant to this section
21 shall be supervised by a local library board consisting of
22 five members who shall be appointed by the county commission
23 or the governing body of the municipality.
24 (b) Each library board member shall be appointed by the
25 governing body of the county or municipality that established
26 the library.
27 (c) Each library board member shall be appointed to a
28 four-year term and shall serve at the pleasure of his or her
Page 1
four-year term and shall serve at the pleasure of his or her
SB26 Engrossed
29 respective appointing authority and may be removed at any time
30 by a two-thirds vote of the appointing authority. The terms of
31 membership on the library board, as first appointed, for one
32 member shall be for one year, for the second member shall be
33 for two years, for the third member shall be for three years,
34 and for the remaining two members the terms shall be for four
35 years. After the first term, all appointments shall be for
36 four years.
37 (d) The county commission or respective governing body
38 shall fill all vacancies, including expired and unexpired
39 terms.
40 (e) Members of the library board shall serve without
41 compensation.
42 (f) Each appointing authority shall coordinate its
43 appointments to assure the membership of the local library
44 board is inclusive and reflects the racial, gender,
45 geographic, urban, rural, and economic diversity of the
46 state."
