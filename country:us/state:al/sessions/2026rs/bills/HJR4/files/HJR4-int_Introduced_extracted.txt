4 HJR___ MOURNING THE DEATH AND CELEBRATING THE LIFE OF THE
5 HONORABLE HOWARD SANDERFORD.
6
7 WHEREAS, it is with profound sorrow and deep regret
8 that we record the passing of The Honorable Howard
9 Sanderford on January 7, 2026, at the age of 90; and
10 WHEREAS, an outstanding member of the community,
11 Howard Sanderford selflessly served the state as a military
12 officer, legislator, and public servant; he was known for
13 his commitment to faith, family, and the betterment of his
14 community, and he set a shining example for all who knew
15 him; and
16 WHEREAS, Howard Sanderford was born on October 18,
17 1935, in Meridian, Mississippi; he earned his Bachelor of
18 Science in Accounting from Mississippi State University,
19 where he was active in student government; and
20 WHEREAS, after graduating, he served as an officer in
21 the United States Marine Corps, where he participated in the
22 Beirut, Lebanon, landing of 1958; he was a member of the
23 Marine Corps League, the Military Order of World Wars, the
24 Veterans of Foreign Wars, the American Legion Post 237, and
25 the Military Officers Association of America; and
26 WHEREAS, following his military service, he moved to
27 Alabama and began a business career in the information
28 technology sector, serving as an executive with IBM in

33 WHEREAS, in 1989, Mr. Sanderford won a special
