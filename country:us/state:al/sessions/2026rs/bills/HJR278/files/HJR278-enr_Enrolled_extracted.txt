4 2026 SOUTHERN CONFERENCE TOURNAMENT CHAMPIONSHIP.
5
6 WHEREAS, highest commendations are herein proudly
7 presented to the Samford University Women's Basketball Team
8 on winning the Southern Conference Tournament Championship
9 and securing a spot in the NCAA Tournament; and
10 WHEREAS, the Samford women's basketball team rallied
11 from an early double-digit deficit to defeat top-seeded
12 University of Tennessee at Chattanooga 72-67; and
13 WHEREAS, Chattanooga raced out to a 14-3 lead in the
14 first quarter, but Samford found its footing behind a
15 barrage of three-pointers, closing the opening period
16 trailing by just three; the Samford Bulldogs tightened up
17 defensively in the second quarter, holding Chattanooga to
18 seven points while moving in front 35-27 at halftime; and
19 WHEREAS, in the third quarter, Samford's offense
20 exploded for 19 points, building a 13-point advantage;
21 Chattanooga mounted a late charge, but Samford sank 12 of 13
22 free throws, including two crucial makes from Briana Rivera
23 in the final seconds, to seal the win; and
24 WHEREAS, freshman Kaylee Yarbrough delivered a career
25 performance on the big stage, scoring 25 points off the
26 bench and matching her season high with five made
27 three-pointers; Francie Morris added 22 points and six
28 rebounds in a career-high 35 minutes, securing her the title
