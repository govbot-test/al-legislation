6 CHAMPIONSHIP.
7
8 WHEREAS, highest commendations are herein proudly
9 presented to the Samford University Women's Basketball Team
10 on winning the Southern Conference Tournament Championship
11 and securing a spot in the NCAA Tournament; and
12 WHEREAS, the Samford women's basketball team rallied
13 from an early double-digit deficit to defeat top-seeded
14 University of Tennessee at Chattanooga 72-67; and
15 WHEREAS, Chattanooga raced out to a 14-3 lead in the
16 first quarter, but Samford found its footing behind a
17 barrage of three-pointers, closing the opening period
18 trailing by just three; the Samford Bulldogs tightened up
19 defensively in the second quarter, holding Chattanooga to
20 seven points while moving in front 35-27 at halftime; and
21 WHEREAS, in the third quarter, Samford's offense
22 exploded for 19 points, building a 13-point advantage;
23 Chattanooga mounted a late charge, but Samford sank 12 of 13
24 free throws, including two crucial makes from Briana Rivera
25 in the final seconds, to seal the win; and
26 WHEREAS, freshman Kaylee Yarbrough delivered a career
27 performance on the big stage, scoring 25 points off the
28 bench and matching her season high with five made
