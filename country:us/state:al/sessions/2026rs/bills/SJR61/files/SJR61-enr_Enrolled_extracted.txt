4 SJR___ MOURNING THE DEATH OF MRS. SHIRLEY ANN ROSS.
5
6 WHEREAS, with an overwhelming sense of loss, we mourn
7 the death, yet celebrate the life, of Mrs. Shirley Ann Ross
8 of Montgomery, Alabama, whose passing on February 7, 2026,
9 has left a void in the lives of her family and friends;
10 wife, sister, mother, grandmother, and devoted friend, Mrs.
11 Ross was known for her gentle nature, generosity, and
12 dedication to serving others; and
13 WHEREAS, born on May 23, 1945, in Mauvilla, Alabama,
14 to the late Reverend James and Lettice Rice, Mrs. Shirley
15 Ann Ross accepted Christ at an early age at Mount Olive
16 Baptist Church; and
17 WHEREAS, she graduated as valedictorian of her class
18 from Rosa A. Lott School in Mobile, Alabama, in 1963, and
19 continued her education at Alabama State University, earning
20 a Bachelor of Science degree in Elementary Education, cum
21 laude, in 1968; and
22 WHEREAS, she furthered her studies at Oakland
23 University in Michigan, where she earned a master's degree
24 in Reading, strengthening her preparation for a lifelong
25 career in education; and
26 WHEREAS, Mrs. Ross devoted more than 35 years to the
27 Pontiac School District in Michigan, serving as a teacher,
28 librarian, and media center specialist at Will Rogers,
