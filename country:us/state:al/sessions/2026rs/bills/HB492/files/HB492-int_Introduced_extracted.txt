4 SYNOPSIS:
5 This bill would require the State Department of
6 Human Resources to request a waiver from the United
7 States Department of Agriculture Food and Nutrition
8 Service to exclude candy, soda, prepared desserts, and
9 energy drinks from the definition of eligible food for
10 purposes of SNAP benefits.
11 If the waiver is granted, this bill would
12 require the department to prohibit the purchase of
13 candy, soda, prepared desserts, and energy drinks with
14 SNAP benefits.
15 If the waiver is granted, this bill would
16 require the Department of Revenue to provide guidance
17 on SNAP-eligible purchases to retailers.
18 This bill would also require the department to
19 annually resubmit the request for a waiver if the
20 request is denied.
