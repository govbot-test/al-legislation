3 Relating to Etowah County; to amend Section 45-28-243,

7 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
8 Section 1. Section 45-28-243 of the Code of Alabama
9 1975, is amended to read as follows:
10 "§45-28-243
11 (a) In Etowah County, there is hereby levied and
12 imposed, in addition to all other taxes of every kind now
13 imposed by law, a privilege or license tax upon every person,
14 firm, or corporation engaging in the business of renting or
15 furnishing any room or rooms, lodging, or accommodations to
16 transients in any hotel, motel, inn, tourist camp, tourist
17 cabin, or any other place in which rooms, lodgings, or
18 accommodations are regularly furnished to transients for a
19 consideration, in an amount of two percent of the charge for
20 such room, rooms, lodgings, or accommodations, including the
21 charge for use or rental of personal property and services
22 furnished in such the room. There is exempted from the tax
23 levied under this section any taxes on rentals or services
24 under Division 1, commencing with Section 40-23-1, of Article
25 1, Chapter 23, Title 40. The tax shall not apply to rooms,
26 lodgings, or accommodations supplied for a period of 30 180
27 continuous days or more in any place.
28 (b) Municipal privilege license taxes which are levied
