4 HR____ HONORING CHIEF JERRY TAYLOR FOR 38 YEARS OF
5 DISTINGUISHED SERVICE IN LAW ENFORCEMENT.
6
7 WHEREAS, Chief Jerry Taylor has devoted 38 years of
8 his life to the noble profession of law enforcement,
9 demonstrating unwavering dedication to the safety, security,
10 and well-being of the residents he has faithfully served;
11 and
12 WHEREAS, Chief Taylor began his distinguished career
13 in 1988 with the Mobile Police Department, where he served
14 honorably until 1996; and
15 WHEREAS, he continued his service with the Mobile
16 County Sheriff's Office from 1996 until 2005, further
17 strengthening his commitment to protecting and serving his
18 community; and
19 WHEREAS, in 2005, Chief Taylor assumed leadership
20 roles as Chief of Police for the City of Creola and Public
21 Safety Director for the Town of Mount Vernon, serving with
22 distinction in both capacities until 2016; and
23 WHEREAS, in 2016, Chief Taylor was appointed Chief of
24 Police for the City of Jackson Police Department, where he
25 has continued to lead with integrity, professionalism, and a
26 steadfast commitment to excellence; and
27 WHEREAS, throughout his career, Chief Taylor has
28 served in numerous specialized roles, including the Honor
