4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, the Underground Damage
6 Prevention Program exists to administer the One-Call
7 Notification System to safeguard against injury and
8 loss of life due to excavation or demolition and to
9 protect underground facilities from costly damage and
10 the interruption of utility or other services to the
11 general public.
12 This bill would establish various procedures for
13 excavators to follow prior to commencing complex or
14 large projects and would extend the statutory sunset
15 clause pertaining to the program.
16 This bill would also make nonsubstantive,
17 technical revisions to update the existing code
18 language to current style.
