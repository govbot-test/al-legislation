3 To propose an amendment to the Constitution of Alabama
4 of 2022, relating to Colbert County, to authorize a qualified
5 taxpayer 65 years of age or older to claim a senior property
6 tax exemption for real property owned in the county under
7 certain conditions.
8 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
9 Section 1. The following amendment to the Constitution
10 of Alabama of 2022, is proposed:
11 PROPOSED AMENDMENT
12 (a) In Colbert County, an individual 65 years of age or
13 older may claim a senior property tax exemption for ad valorem
14 tax purposes on real property located in the county which is
15 owned by the individual, classified as single-family
16 owner-occupied residential property, and used as the principal
17 place of residence of the individual for not less than five
18 years immediately prior to the tax year for which the
19 individual first claims the senior property tax exemption. The
20 senior property tax exemption shall freeze the assessed value
21 of the property for the year immediately prior to claiming the
22 exemption.
23 (b) The following provisions shall apply to the
24 individual claiming the senior property tax exemption:
25 (1) The taxpayer shall continue to receive the senior
26 property tax exemption as long as the taxpayer continues to
27 use the property as his or her principal place of residence.
28 (2) The individual shall continue to be eligible to
