4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, termination dates are
6 established for enumerated state agencies. The agencies
7 are periodically reviewed by the Alabama Sunset
8 Committee. After the review process is completed, the
9 committee prepares its recommendation for the agencies
10 to the Legislature in the form of sunset bills which
11 either continue, terminate, or continue with
12 modification each agency reviewed.
13 This bill would provide for the continuance of
14 the Alabama Board of Examiners in Counseling until
15 October 1, 2028.
16
17
18 A BILL
19 TO BE ENTITLED
20 AN ACT
21
22 Relating to the Alabama Sunset Law; to continue the
23 existence and functioning of the Alabama Board of Examiners in
24 Counseling until October 1, 2028.
25 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
26 Section 1. Pursuant to the Alabama Sunset Law, the
27 Sunset Committee recommends the continuance of the Alabama
28 Board of Examiners in Counseling until October 1, 2028.

Board of Examiners in Counseling until October 1, 2028.
SB127 INTRODUCED
29 Section 2. The existence and functioning of the Alabama
30 Board of Examiners in Counseling, created and functioning
31 pursuant to Sections 34-8A-1 to 34-8A-24, inclusive, Code of
32 Alabama 1975, is continued until October 1, 2028, and those
33 code sections are expressly preserved.
34 Section 3. The Legislature concurs in the
35 recommendations of the Sunset Committee as provided in
36 Sections 1 and 2.
37 Section 4. This act shall become effective June 1,
