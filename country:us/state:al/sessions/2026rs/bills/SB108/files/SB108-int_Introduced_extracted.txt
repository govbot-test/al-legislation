4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, certain members of the
6 National Guard may receive tuition reimbursement
7 through the National Guard Educational Assistance
8 Program when seeking a college degree or certain other
9 certifications.
10 This bill would authorize tuition assistance for
11 workforce development programs, in addition to tuition
12 assistance, through the National Guard Educational
13 Assistance Program.
14 This bill would authorize the use of tuition
15 assistance for additional educational expenses, such as
16 on-campus living expenses and meal plans.
17 Under existing law, guard members who achieve a
18 certain grade point average requirement are authorized
19 to receive academic excellence benefits in addition to
20 educational reimbursement through the program.
21 This bill would remove any reference to academic
22 excellence benefits and revises the requirements to
23 qualify for tuition assistance.
24 This bill would repeal a section that provides
25 certain qualifications for the National Guard
26 Educational Assistance Program.
