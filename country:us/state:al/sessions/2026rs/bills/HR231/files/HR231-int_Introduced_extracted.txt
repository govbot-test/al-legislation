6 VEX ROBOTICS COMPETITION.

8 WHEREAS, highest commendations and congratulations

10 Robotics," and Team 31337W, "Womp Womp," from Mountain Brook

12 2026 VEX Robotics Competition held at Auburn University

15 as the largest robotics competition in the world, with more
16 than 20,000 teams from over 60 countries participating in
17 more than 1,700 competitions each year, ranging from local

23 state competitions, and only a limited number of teams
24 advance to the VEX Robotics World Championship; and
