8 WHEREAS, highest commendations are herein proudly

12 nationwide; and
13 WHEREAS, Phi Beta Sigma Fraternity, Incorporated, was
14 founded at Howard University in Washington, D.C., on January
15 9, 1914, by three young African American male students;
16 since the beginning, the fraternity has treated all people
17 as equals and has encouraged others to treat one another as

19 international organization of leaders who proactively serve

21 people's lives; and
