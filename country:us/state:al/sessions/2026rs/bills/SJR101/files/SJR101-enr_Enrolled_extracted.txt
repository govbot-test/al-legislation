7 WHEREAS, with sincere appreciation and utmost
8 respect, we recognize the exemplary work of multiple faculty
9 members and research assistants from the Auburn University
10 School of Architecture, Planning and Landscape Architecture
11 (APLA); the team has created and printed more than 150
12 miniature scale models of the new Alabama State House that
13 will replace the 1963 building and complete the urban
14 complex that includes the Alabama State Capitol Building;
15 and
16 WHEREAS, in 2023, the Alabama Legislature
17 commissioned New York-based Robert A.M. Stern Architects and
18 local architecture and engineering firm Goodwyn Mills Cawood
19 (GMC) to build the new State House, which is set to be
20 completed this year and occupied before the 2027 Legislative
21 Session; and
22 WHEREAS, to commemorate the new building, GMC
23 partnered with APLA to 3D-print phases of the models; in the
24 summer of 2025, Associate Professor Robert Sproull,
25 Assistant Professors Frank Hu and Ernesto Bilbao,
26 Engineering Tech Jon Byler, and a team of student research
27 assistants printed the miniature models as tokens for
28 legislators; the task was completed with the help of APLA's
