69 Section 3. The adoption and incorporation of the
70 supplements specified in this act shall constitute a
71 continuous systematic codification of the entire Code of
72 Alabama 1975, for purposes of Section 85 of the Constitution
73 of Alabama of 2022. This act is a law that adopts a code for
74 the purposes of Section 45 of the Constitution of Alabama of
75 2022. Notwithstanding the foregoing, nothing in this act shall
76 be deemed to codify any provision of law that was adopted in
77 violation of any provision of the Constitution of Alabama of
78 2022, establishing requirements for, or limitations on, the
79 enactment of a law.
80 Section 4. The adoption of this act shall not repeal,
81 supersede, amend, or in any other way affect any statute
82 enacted into law during any 2026 session of the Legislature.
