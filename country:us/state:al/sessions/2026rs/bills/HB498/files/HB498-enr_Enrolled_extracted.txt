3 Relating to the City of Prichard located in Mobile
4 County; to provide for the abatement of repeat overgrown grass
5 and weed nuisances; to provide for abandoned debris on private
6 property to be declared a public nuisance under certain
7 circumstances; to provide for the abatement and removal of
8 inoperable motor vehicles as public nuisances from private
9 property; to provide the procedures for the declaration of a
10 public nuisance; to provide for the collection by the
11 municipality of the costs through the addition of the costs to
12 ad valorem taxes and for enforcement by the county tax
13 collecting official; and to provide for liens on the property
14 under certain conditions.
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
16 Section 1. For the purposes of this act, the following
17 terms have the following meanings:
18 (1) ADMINISTRATIVE OFFICIAL. An individual designated
19 by the city to hear appeals for the purposes of this act. The
20 official may not be the same individual as the enforcing
21 official.
22 (2) ENFORCING OFFICIAL. The municipal official or
23 employee designated by the city to carry out the city's
24 requirements under this act.
25 (3) TAX COLLECTING OFFICIAL. The Mobile County Revenue
26 Commissioner.
