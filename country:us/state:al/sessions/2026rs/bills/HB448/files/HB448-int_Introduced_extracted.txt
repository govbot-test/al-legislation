4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under Section 65 of the Constitution of Alabama
6 of 2022, lotteries and gift enterprises are prohibited.
7 This bill would propose an amendment to the
8 Constitution of Alabama of 2022 to authorize the
9 Legislature to establish an official state lottery under
10 the supervision and control of a public lottery
11 corporation. The lottery would be allowed to consist of
12 draw-based and instant games, would be limited to paper,
13 plastic, or cardboard lottery tickets, would prohibit
14 video lottery terminals, and would include in-state and
15 multi-state lottery games.
16 This bill would also require the Governor to
17 negotiate a compact with the Poarch Band of Creek
18 Indians as contemplated under the federal Indian Gaming
19 Regulatory Act.
