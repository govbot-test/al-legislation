61 "§41-23-131
62 (a) The Alabama Port Infrastructure Grant ProgramInland
63 Ports and Intermodal Development Fund is created within the
64 Alabama Department of Economic and Community Affairs to
65 receive appropriations from the State General Fund for the
66 purpose of financing improvements to existing publicly owned
67 inland ports and intermodal facility infrastructure. The
68 program shall award grants to fund the improvement,
69 maintenance, and construction of ports and intermodal
70 facilities within the state. Funding for the grant
71 programGrants shall be awarded on a competitive bid basis for
72 inland port and intermodal facilityport and intermodal
73 facility projects that will have a significant local or
74 regional impact. Priority of funding shall be given to inland
75 ports and intermodal facilities that have proven performance
76 in reducing congestion on public highways and increasing
77 tonnage at port facilities and that will enhance regional
78 competitiveness for port operators and users.
79 (b) Any project funded from this program shall meet the
80 following requirements:
81 (1) The minimum amount of participation for local
82 matching funds is 20 percent of the project amount.
83 (2) The amount awarded from the program may not exceed
84 50 percent of the total budgeted project costs.
