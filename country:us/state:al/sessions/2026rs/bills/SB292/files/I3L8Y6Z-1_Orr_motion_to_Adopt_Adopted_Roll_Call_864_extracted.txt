4 SYNOPSIS:
5 This bill would create the Alabama Property
6 Protection Act of 2026 which would prevent title fraud
7 and classify the existing crime of fraudulent sale of
8 real property as a Class D felony.
9 This bill would establish the crime of
10 aggravated fraudulent sale of real property and
11 classify it as a Class C felony.
12 This bill would establish the Alabama Title
13 Fraud Recovery Fund and an administrative complaint
14 process through the Alabama Securities Commission;
15 authorize judges of probate to establish a real
16 property owner notification service; and require online
17 real estate platforms to verify ownership prior to
18 publishing a listing and remove listings for properties
19 not for sale.
20 This bill would create an expedited quiet title
21 process for victims of title fraud and provide for the
22 recovery of costs and attorney fees in certain quiet
23 title actions.
24 This bill would prohibit remote notarization of
25 documents conveying interest in real property under
26 certain circumstances and also make nonsubstantive,
27 technical revisions to update the existing code
28 language to current style.
