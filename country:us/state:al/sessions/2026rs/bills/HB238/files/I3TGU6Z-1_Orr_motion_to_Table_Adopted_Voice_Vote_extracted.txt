185 affiliates for personnel providing training to the company's employees, whether
186 those trainers are employees of the company or its vendors or suppliers, including

188 necessary materials, equipment, manuals, or other relevant training materials; (v)

191 training facilities; (vii) travel expenses for prospective and current employees and
192 trainees, including travel, food, lodging, relocation, and other costs necessary and
193 reasonable to enable the company to secure a qualified workforce; and (viii) outside
194 training fees. The above funds appropriated for Workforce Development shall be
