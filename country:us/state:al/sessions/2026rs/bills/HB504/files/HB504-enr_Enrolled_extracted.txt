6 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
7 Section 1. Section 9 of Act 2022-279, 2022 Regular
8 Session, is amended to read as follows:
9 "Section 9. (a) The judge of probate may mail to any
10 person to whom a motor vehicle license has been previously
11 issued an application for renewal of a license required to be
12 returned prior to the expiration date of the license.
13 (b) The application for renewal may be in the form of a
14 postcard and shall contain sufficient information to
15 adequately identify and process the renewal.
16 (c) The signature of the licensee on the application
17 and proper remittance shall constitute sufficient authority
18 for the judge of probate to issue the license and return the
19 license to the licensee by mail.
20 (d)(1) The county commission may establish a fee to be
21 entitled "Mail Order Fee" in an amount not to exceed three
22 dollars ($3) to pay the cost of this mailing procedure.
23 (2) The county commission may establish an alternative
24 fee, not to exceed a total of ten dollars ($10), for a
25 licensee who requests to receive a license plate or decal by
26 priority mail.
27 (e) The fee shall be collected by the judge of probate
28 at the time of issuance and paid over to the general fund of
