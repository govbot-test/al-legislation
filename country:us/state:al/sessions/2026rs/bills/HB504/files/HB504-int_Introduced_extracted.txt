9 Relating to Wilcox County; to amend Section 9 of Act
10 2022-279, 2022 Regular Session; to further provide for mail
11 order fees in certain circumstances.
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
13 Section 1. Section 9 of Act 2022-279, 2022 Regular
14 Session, is amended to read as follows:
15 "Section 9. (a) The judge of probate may mail to any
16 person to whom a motor vehicle license has been previously
17 issued an application for renewal of a license required to be
18 returned prior to the expiration date of the license.
19 (b) The application for renewal may be in the form of a
20 postcard and shall contain sufficient information to
21 adequately identify and process the renewal.
22 (c) The signature of the licensee on the application
23 and proper remittance shall constitute sufficient authority
24 for the judge of probate to issue the license and return the
25 license to the licensee by mail.
26 (d)(1) The county commission may establish a fee to be
27 entitled "Mail Order Fee" in an amount not to exceed three
28 dollars ($3) to pay the cost of this mailing procedure.
Page 1
dollars ($3) to pay the cost of this mailing procedure.

29 (2) The county commission may establish an alternative
30 fee, not to exceed a total of ten dollars ($10), for a
31 licensee who requests to receive a license plate or decal by
32 priority mail.
33 (e) The fee shall be collected by the judge of probate
34 at the time of issuance and paid over to the general fund of
35 the county as are other fees and commissions.
36 (f) The judge of probate may charge up to five dollars
37 ($5) for the cost of mailing any other probate document."
38 Section 2. This act shall become effective on October
39 1, 2026.
