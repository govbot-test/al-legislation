HB372 Enrolled
2
3 Relating to the Department of Insurance; to provide for
4 two exempt personnel positions within the Pharmacy Benefits
5 Manager Division.
6 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
7 Section 1. The Commissioner of Insurance, in
8 conformance with Section 36-26-10, Code of Alabama 1975, may
9 employ personnel to operate the Pharmacy Benefits Manager
10 Division within the Department of Insurance, including two
11 employees who are in the exempt service and not subject to the
12 state Merit System. These positions are in addition to any
13 other exempt position otherwise authorized by law for the
14 department. The compensation of these employees shall be set
15 by the commissioner.
16 Section 2. This act shall become effective on June 1,
17 2026.
