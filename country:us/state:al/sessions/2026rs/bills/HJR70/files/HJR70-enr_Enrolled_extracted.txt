3 COMMENDING THE ALABAMA SCENIC RIVER TRAIL AND WINNERS OF THE
4 2025 GREAT ALABAMA 650 RACE.

6 WHEREAS, the Alabama Scenic River Trail (ASRT) was
7 established in 2008 and subsequently designated as a
8 National Recreational Trail by the United States Secretary
9 of the Interior in 2009 and as a National Water Trail in
10 2012; and
11 WHEREAS, the Alabama Scenic River Trail initially
12 extended for 631 miles and has since expanded to encompass
13 more than 6,300 miles of Alabama's rivers, lakes, streams,
14 and creeks; and
15 WHEREAS, in 2025, the Alabama Scenic River Trail, as
16 the State of Alabama's official paddling resource, partnered
17 with more than 70 canoe and kayak rental shops, outfitters,
18 and community organizations to promote Alabama's exceptional
19 natural resources and contribute to the vitality of the
20 state's outdoor tourism economy; and
21 WHEREAS, with the goal of making Alabama's waterways
22 the safest and most enjoyable in the nation, the Alabama
23 Scenic River Trail continues to foster and expand
24 opportunities for paddling and other freshwater recreational
25 experiences throughout the State of Alabama, including the
26 execution of community-based programming and the
27 installation of safety signage; and
28 WHEREAS, the Great Alabama 650, which is the longest
