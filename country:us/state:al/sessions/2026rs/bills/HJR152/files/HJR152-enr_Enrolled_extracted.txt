4 COMMENDING THOMAS A. HARRIS FOR HIS OUTSTANDING
5 CONTRIBUTIONS TO BUSINESS, CONSERVATION, AND PUBLIC SERVICE
6 IN THE STATE OF ALABAMA.
7
8 WHEREAS, the Alabama Business Hall of Fame, founded
9 in 1973, was established to honor, preserve, and perpetuate
10 the names and accomplishments of business leaders whose
11 achievements have brought lasting distinction and economic
12 advancement to the State of Alabama; and
13 WHEREAS, Thomas A. Harris is a graduate of Auburn
14 University, having earned a degree in business in 1971, and
15 has since distinguished himself as a leader in finance,
16 entrepreneurship, conservation, and public service; and
17 WHEREAS, Mr. Harris founded Merchant Capital, an
18 investment banking firm headquartered in Montgomery with
19 offices throughout the southeastern United States; under his
20 leadership, the firm was recognized as the number-one
21 investment bank in Alabama by Thomson Reuters, a globally
22 respected authority in financial markets; and
23 WHEREAS, Merchant Capital was acquired by Stifel
24 Financial Corp. in 2014, and Mr. Harris continued to serve
25 as Senior Managing Director until his retirement in 2022;
26 and
27 WHEREAS, in 2021, Mr. Harris founded the Birmingham
28 Recovery Center and Longleaf Wellness and Recovery Centers,
