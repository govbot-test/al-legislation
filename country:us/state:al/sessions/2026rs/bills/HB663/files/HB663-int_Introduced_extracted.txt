4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, an excise tax in the amount
6 of $0.10 per milliliter is assessed against consumable
7 vapor products sold in the state.
8 This bill would revise the calculation of the
9 excise tax based on the amount of nicotine in the
10 consumable vapor product.
11 This bill would require each consumable vapor
12 product to be properly labeled with information stating
13 the amount of nicotine in the product and the total
14 liquid content of the product.
15 This bill would also provide civil penalties for
16 violations of the product label requirements.
