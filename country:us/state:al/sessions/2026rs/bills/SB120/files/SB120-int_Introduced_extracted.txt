4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, law enforcement officers may
6 pursue suspects into neighboring jurisdictions and
7 across state lines for certain offenses.
8 This bill would create a standard that state,
9 county, and municipal law enforcement agencies shall
10 follow when developing and implementing vehicular
11 pursuit policies.
12 This bill would also limit vehicular pursuits to
13 individuals suspected of certain violent crimes.
