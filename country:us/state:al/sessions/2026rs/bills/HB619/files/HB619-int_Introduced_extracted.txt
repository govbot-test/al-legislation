52 to make nonsubstantive, technical revisions to update the
53 existing code language to current style.

202 Section 2. Section 34-36-19 is added to the Code of
203 Alabama 1975, to read as follows:
204 §34-36-19
205 The board may adopt and publish minimum installation,
206 repair, and service standards for its licensees, and shall
207 publish the standards to every licensee at least 30 days
208 before the standard becomes effective. Licensees who fail to
209 comply with the minimum standards shall be disciplined as
210 provided under Section 34-36-6. A licensee who is working in a
211 jurisdiction that has adopted and is enforcing an electrical
212 code through its permitting and inspection processes shall be
213 held to the standards adopted and enforced by the local
214 jurisdiction.
