9 Relating to insurance, to amend Sections 27-31B-3,
10 27-31B-6, 27-31B-8, 27-31B-9, and 27-31B-16, Code of Alabama
11 1975; to impose additional requirements on captive insurers;
12 to require notice of any subsequent material change in any of
13 the items required to be submitted; to increase the minimum
14 paid in capital certain captive insurers are required to
15 maintain; to require the commissioner to take into
16 consideration the competence of the captive manager and legal
17 counsel of the captive insurer and to consider the company’s
18 business plan when determining whether the company will
19 promote the general good of the state; and to require licensed
20 captive insurers to annually file an audited financial
21 statement and an actuarial certification of loss reserves and
22 loss expense reserves.
