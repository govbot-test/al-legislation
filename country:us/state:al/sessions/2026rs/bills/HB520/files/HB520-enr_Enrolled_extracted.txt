3 Relating to teacher certification; to add Section
4 16-23-3.2 to the Code of Alabama 1975; to require the State
5 Board of Education to establish an expedited certification
6 pathway for a career and technical teaching certificate; and
7 to provide for qualifications and limitations.
8 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
9 Section 1. The Legislature finds and declares that the
10 state must provide additional career and technical education
11 opportunities for Alabama students to help them prepare to
12 enter the workforce. In order to support these offerings, the
13 state must attract and retain the required number of career
14 and technical education instructors. Therefore, the
15 Legislature finds that the State Department of Education
16 should take steps to increase the number of career and
17 technical education instructors in the state. It is the intent
18 of this act to streamline the process to obtain a teaching
19 certificate for those educators who move to Alabama to teach
20 career and technical education and related courses.
21 Section 2. Section 16-23-3.2 is added to the Code of
22 Alabama 1975, to read as follows:
23 §16-23-3.2
24 (a) The State Board of Education shall establish an
25 expedited certification pathway for the issuance of a teaching
26 certificate by the State Superintendent of Education to a
27 qualified individual who seeks to teach career and technical
28 education (CTE) courses at a public high school in Alabama

29 when that individual has previously taught CTE courses or

31 (b) To be approved for an expedited CTE teaching
32 certificate, the applicant shall satisfy all of the following
33 requirements:
34 (1) Submit to the State Board of Education the required
35 application form and fees to obtain a teaching certificate.
36 (2) Hold a valid, active teaching certificate, or its
37 equivalent, obtained from another state and submit a copy of
38 the certificate with the application.
39 (3) Is recommended for certification by a local
40 superintendent of education.
41 (4) Successfully pass a criminal history background
42 check.
43 (c) Upon verification that an applicant satisfies the
44 requirements of subsection (b), the State Superintendent of
45 Education shall issue an Alabama teaching certificate
46 authorizing the individual to teach CTE courses as soon as
47 practicable and shall not require any additional testing,
48 coursework, or documentation prior to issuance.
49 (d) Any teaching certificate issued in accordance with
50 the requirements of this section shall have the same term and
51 renewal requirements as all other standard teaching
52 certificates issued under this chapter.
53 (e) An individual who is granted a teaching certificate
54 pursuant to this section may only teach CTE courses.
55 (f) A local board of education, in its discretion and
56 subject to available funds, may provide a relocation stipend
