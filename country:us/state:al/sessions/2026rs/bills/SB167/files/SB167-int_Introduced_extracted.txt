4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under the Alabama Administrative Procedure Act,
6 agency rulings in contested cases are required to be
7 taken as prima facie just and reasonable by a reviewing
8 court on questions of fact and the agency's
9 interpretation of laws based on those facts.
10 This bill would revise the standard of judicial
11 review to provide that a court may consider, but not
12 defer to, an agency's interpretation of law and shall
13 favor a reasonable interpretation that limits agency
14 power and maximizes individual liberty.
