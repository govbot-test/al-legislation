20 Schedule; to provide support employees with a two percent pay
21 increase beginning with the fiscal year 2026-2027; to require
22 salary schedules; to provide the employees of certain other
23 public educational institutions and schools with a two percent
24 pay increase for the 2026-2027 fiscal year; to provide for an
25 across the board salary increase on all two-year postsecondary
26 salary schedules, to establish other requirements on the
27 two-year postsecondary salary schedules; and to establish
28 miscellaneous pay provisions relating to public education.

198 equivalent thereof, shall be given a pro rata salary increment

200 c. Local Increment. Any cost-of-living adjustment
201 and/or increase on the State Minimum Salary Schedule for
202 teachers as provided in this act shall be exclusive of any
203 local pay increase granted or due to teachers under provisions
204 of any local salary schedule. Any cost-of-living adjustment

213 Training Corps (JROTC) instructor employed by a local school
214 board.
215 d. Community Education. Each county and city board of
216 education shall have the option to exclude from the provisions
217 of this act any part-time employees of community education or
218 school-sponsored child care or child enrichment program which
219 is supplemental to the state-required educational program.
220 e. Local Chief Executive Officers. The pay increase
221 provisions of this act shall not apply to superintendents of
222 education of any school system or institution. Any pay
223 increase given to the superintendent shall be by majority
224 recorded vote of the governing body or authority.
