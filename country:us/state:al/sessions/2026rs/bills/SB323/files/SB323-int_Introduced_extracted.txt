4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, the court may order a
6 defendant with a mental illness committed to the
7 custody of the Department of Mental Health upon finding
8 that the defendant poses a real and present threat of
9 substantial harm to himself, herself, or to others, and
10 the court may modify a conditional release order upon
11 finding that the defendant's condition has changed.
12 This bill would create a right of appeal for the
13 department and the defendant for orders committing the
14 defendant to the department's custody and orders
15 modifying a conditional release order.
16 This bill would grant the Court of Criminal
17 Appeals the jurisdiction to hear the appeals.
18 This bill would also make nonsubstantive,
19 technical revisions to update the existing code
20 language to current style.
