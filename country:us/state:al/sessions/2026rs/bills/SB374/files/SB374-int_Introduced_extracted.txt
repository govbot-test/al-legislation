16 To propose an amendment to the Constitution of Alabama

18 of three mills south of Sandy Creek for public school purposes
19 in Headland.
20 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
21 Section 1. The following amendment to the Constitution
22 of Alabama of 2022, is proposed:
23 PROPOSED AMENDMENT
24 (a) In addition to all other ad valorem taxes,
25 beginning with the tax year for which taxes become due and
26 payable on October 1, 2027, and continuing through September
27 30, 2034, there is levied an ad valorem tax on all taxable
28 property located in Henry County and south of Sandy Creek at a
Page 1
property located in Henry County and south of Sandy Creek at a

29 rate of three mills per dollar of assessed value.
30 (b) The proceeds of the tax shall be used exclusively
31 for public school purposes for projects in Headland.
32 Upon ratification of this constitutional amendment, the
33 Code Commissioner shall number and place this amendment as
34 appropriate in the constitution omitting this instructional
35 paragraph and may make the following nonsubstantive revisions:
36 change capitalization, hierarchy, spelling, and punctuation
37 for purposes of style and uniformity; correct manifest
38 grammatical, clerical, and typographical errors; revise
39 internal or external citations and cross-references; harmonize
40 language; and translate effective dates.
41 END PROPOSED AMENDMENT
42 Section 2. An election upon the proposed amendment
43 shall be held in accordance with Sections 284, 284.01, and 285
44 of the Constitution of Alabama of 2022, and the election laws
45 of this state. The appropriate election official shall assign
46 a ballot number for the proposed constitutional amendment on
47 the election ballot and shall set forth the following
48 description of the substance or subject matter of the proposed
49 constitutional amendment:
50 "Relating to Henry County, proposing an amendment to
51 the Constitution of Alabama of 2022, to levy a temporary ad
52 valorem tax of three mills on taxable property located south
53 of Sandy Creek to support public school projects in Headland.
54 Proposed by Act ____."
55 This description shall be followed by the following
