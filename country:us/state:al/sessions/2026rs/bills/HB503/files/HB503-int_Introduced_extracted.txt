17 AN ACT

19 Relating to crimes; to prohibit the disclosure or
20 transmission of certain images of deceased individuals taken
21 as part of a coroner or law enforcement investigation; and to
22 provide criminal penalties for a violation.
23 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
24 Section 1. (a) Any photograph or image of a deceased
25 individual or human remains that is taken as part of a coroner
26 death investigation or law enforcement investigation,
27 regardless of whether the investigation is active or not, and
28 regardless of whether the photograph or image was taken by use
Page 1
regardless of whether the photograph or image was taken by use

29 of a personal electronic device or device belonging to a state
30 or local agency, is prohibited from being disclosed,
31 transmitted, or used for any purpose other than an official
32 coroner or law enforcement purpose or as otherwise authorized
33 by law.
34 (b)(1) Any person who violates subsection (a) is guilty
35 of a Class B misdemeanor.
36 (2) Any person who, for a consideration or other gain,
37 including money, goods, or services, discloses or transmits
38 any photograph or image described in subsection (a) is guilty
39 of a Class A misdemeanor.
40 Section 2. This act shall become effective on October
41 1, 2026.
