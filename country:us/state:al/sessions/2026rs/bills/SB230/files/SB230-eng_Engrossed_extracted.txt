6 TO BE ENTITLED
7 AN ACT
8
9 Relating to campaign finance; to add Section 17-5-7.3
10 to the Code of Alabama 1975, to authorize campaign funds to be
11 used for certain security measures to protect the safety of a
12 candidate, an elected official, or his or her immediate family
13 or staff.
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
15 Section 1. Section 17-5-7.3 is added to the Code of
16 Alabama 1975, to read as follows:
17 §17-5-7.3
18 (a) In addition to the use of campaign contributions
19 for the purposes listed in Section 17-5-7, campaign
20 contributions may be used to pay, or reimburse the state or
21 any county or municipality, for security expenses to protect
22 the safety of a candidate, an elected official, or an
23 immediate family or staff member of a candidate or an elected
24 official. For purposes of this section, "security expenses"
25 include all of the following:
26 (1) Reasonable costs of cybersecurity such as data
27 removal services that find and remove personal information of
28 a candidate, elected official, or immediate family or staff
Page 1
a candidate, elected official, or immediate family or staff
SB230 Engrossed
29 member of the candidate or elected official from data brokers
30 and websites, and other online sources.
31 (2) Reasonable costs of installing and monitoring
32 nonstructural security devices, including home security
33 hardware, locks, alarm systems, motion detectors, and security
34 cameras, provided the security devices are intended to provide
35 security and not to improve or increase the value of the
36 property.
37 (3) Reasonable costs of security personnel for a
38 candidate, elected official, or immediate family or staff
39 member of the candidate or elected official.
40 (b) Any device or other item purchased and installed
41 pursuant to subsection (a) which is permanently attached to
42 the property of a candidate, elected official, or immediate
43 family or staff member of the candidate or elected official is
44 exempt from the liquidation provisions of Section 17-5-7.2.
45 Section 2. This act shall become effective immediately.
