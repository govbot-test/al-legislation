HB83 Enrolled
1 Enrolled, An Act,
2 To propose an amendment to the Constitution of Alabama
3 of 2022, relating to Blount County, to authorize the Judge of
4 Probate of Blount County to exercise jurisdiction concurrent
5 with certain circuit court cases if the judge of probate is
6 licensed to practice law in the State of Alabama.
7 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
8 Section 1. The following amendment to the Constitution
9 of Alabama of 2022, is proposed:
10 PROPOSED AMENDMENT
11 The Judge of Probate of Blount County may exercise
12 equity jurisdiction concurrent with that of the circuit court
13 in cases originally filed in the Probate Court of Blount
14 County if the judge of probate is licensed to practice law in
15 the State of Alabama. In any case subject to this amendment,
16 the judge of probate shall possess the power and authority of
17 a circuit court judge trying the case, and the case shall be
18 treated in all respects in the same manner as a case filed in
19 circuit court. The Alabama Rules of Civil Procedure shall
20 apply in the cases except as otherwise specifically provided
21 by law. This amendment is self-executing, but the Legislature
22 may enact additional laws to implement this amendment if
23 needed.
24 Upon ratification of this constitutional amendment, the
25 Code Commissioner shall number and place this amendment as
26 appropriate in the constitution omitting this instructional
27 paragraph and may make the following nonsubstantive revisions:
28 change capitalization, hierarchy, spelling, and punctuation
