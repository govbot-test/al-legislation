5 BREAKING THE STATE RECORD WITH THEIR REPORT CARD SCORE.
6
7 WHEREAS, highest commendations are proudly presented
8 to the Dale County School System for earning a
9 record-breaking score of 95 on the recent Alabama State and
10 Federal Report Card; and
11 WHEREAS, this remarkable accomplishment marks the
12 highest overall score ever recorded by an Alabama county
13 school system in the history of state reporting, setting a
14 new benchmark for educational excellence across the state;
15 and
16 WHEREAS, the score of the schools within a system,
17 individually and collectively, includes various measures
18 such as academic achievement, growth, graduation rates, and
19 student success indicators; and

23 support staff member in Dale County Schools; and
24 WHEREAS, under the visionary leadership of
