9 Relating to Escambia County; to authorize any
10 municipality located, in whole or in part, in Escambia County
11 to operate an automated photographic speeding enforcement
12 system; and to provide for the jurisdiction of civil fines
13 issued pursuant to violations captured by an automated
14 photographic speeding enforcement system.
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
16 Section 1. For the purposes of this act, the following
17 terms have the following meanings:
18 (1) SYSTEM. Technology operated by a law enforcement
19 officer which combines a manually operated camera system that
20 works in conjunction with an electronically operated speed
21 detection system to automatically record digital imaging of a
22 motor vehicle while speeding.
23 (2) TRAINED TECHNICIAN. A law enforcement officer who
24 has been trained by a vendor that sells or maintains the
25 system.
26 (3) ZONE. A reduced speed school zone as described in
27 Section 32-5A-182, Code of Alabama 1975, or a construction
28 zone as described in Section 32-5A-176.1, Code of Alabama
