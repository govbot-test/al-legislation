4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, various programs and
6 organizations receive funds from income tax check-offs.
7 This bill would reinstate the income tax refund
8 check-off for Victims of Crime and Leniency, Inc.,
9 (VOCAL).
10 This bill would also repeal entities whose
11 check-offs have been rescinded and consolidate existing
12 check-offs into a single code section.
13
14
15 A BILL
16 TO BE ENTITLED
17 AN ACT
18
19 Relating to taxation; to amend Section 40-18-140, Code
20 of Alabama 1975, to reinstate the income tax refund check-off
21 for a contribution to Victims of Crime and Leniency, Inc.,
22 (VOCAL); to remove entities whose check-offs have been
23 rescinded; to consolidate check-offs into this section; to
24 repeal Sections 40-18-154, 40-18-155, 40-18-156, and
25 40-18-157, Code of Alabama 1975, regarding check-offs for
26 other entities; and to make nonsubstantive, technical
27 revisions to update the existing code language to current
28 style.
