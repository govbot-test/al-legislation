4 SJR___ HONORING THE LONG-STANDING PARTNERSHIP BETWEEN THE
5 PROVINCE OF QUÉBEC AND THE STATE OF ALABAMA.
6
7 WHEREAS, the Province of Québec has maintained a
8 permanent presence in the Southern United States since 1978,
9 and both Québec and Alabama are active members of the
10 Southeast United States–Canadian Provinces (SEUS-CP)
11 alliance, which fosters economic and diplomatic
12 collaboration; and
13 WHEREAS, Québec is Alabama's second-largest Canadian
14 export destination for automobiles, representing nearly 70
15 percent of Québec's automotive imports from the state; and
16 WHEREAS, Québec is Alabama's ninth-largest supplier
17 of raw aluminum, directly supporting Alabama's automotive
18 and manufacturing sectors; and
19 WHEREAS, Québec is Alabama's largest international
20 supplier of aircraft parts and second-largest for turbojets,
21 each of which plays a vital role in Alabama's economy and is
22 essential to the state's advanced manufacturing and defense
23 industries; and
24 WHEREAS, Québec's contributions to Alabama's
25 transportation, aerospace, and automotive industries reflect
26 a robust and mutually beneficial economic relationship; and
27 WHEREAS, Québec's recognized expertise in emerging
28 technologies, including quantum computing and artificial
Page 1
technologies, including quantum computing and artificial
SJR66 Enrolled
29 intelligence, aligns with Alabama's growing interest in
30 innovation and advanced research; and
31 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that
32 this remarkable partnership be appropriately recognized and
33 celebrated; now therefore,
34 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA, BOTH
35 HOUSES THEREOF CONCURRING, That we do hereby honor the
36 enduring and dynamic relationship between the Province of
37 Québec and the State of Alabama, and express our commitment
38 to strengthening economic, cultural, and diplomatic ties.
39 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That a copy of this
40 resolution be transmitted to the Premier of Québec and the
41 Québec Government Office in Atlanta in recognition of its
42 long-standing efforts to foster collaboration and friendship
43 between Québec and Alabama.
