12 A BILL
13 TO BE ENTITLED
14 AN ACT
15
16 Relating to taxation; to establish a state privilege
17 tax on historical horse racing activities; and to repeal
18 Sections 11-65-29, 11-65-30, 11-65-35, 45-32-150.13,
19 45-44-150.12, and 45-49-151.14, Code of Alabama 1975, levying
20 local pari-mutuel wagering taxes and other fees
21 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
22 Section 1. (a) There is levied in the state a privilege
23 tax of eight percent of the net gambling revenue collected
24 from pari-mutuel wagering on historical horse racing on
25 computerized machines of each historical horse racing
26 licensee. The tax shall be collected by the Department of
27 Revenue from each historical horse racing licensee and
28 distributed as provided by local law.
Page 1
distributed as provided by local law.
HB645 INTRODUCED
29 (b) For purposes of subsection (a), "net gambling
30 revenue" means the total amount of money or value in any form
31 received as a result of any pari-mutuel wagering on historical
32 horse racing activities, excluding free bets, free lays, and
33 promotional credits, less federal excise taxes, voided wagers,
34 and the total money or value in any form paid as prizes or
35 winnings.
36 (c) The state privilege tax collected under this
37 section is exclusive of and supersedes any other local taxes,
38 fees, or commissions now imposed on historical horse racing
39 pari-mutuel activities in the state.
40 (d) Nothing in this section shall change the existing
41 tax structure for live greyhound racing, simulcast live
42 greyhound racing, or live or simulcast horse racing which is
43 not historical horse racing.
44 (e) Nothing in this section shall be construed to
45 authorize, permit, or expand any form of gambling activity
46 beyond that which is permitted by law on the effective date of
47 this act.
48 Section 2. The following sections are repealed:
49 (1) Section 11-65-29, Code of Alabama 1975, imposing
50 the state horse wagering fee in Class 1 municipalities.
51 (2) Section 11-65-30, Code of Alabama 1975, imposing
52 the commission wagering fee in Class 1 municipalities.
53 (3) Section 11-65-35, Code of Alabama 1975, relating to
54 certain racing taxes in Class 1 municipalities.
55 (4) Section 45-44-150.12, Code of Alabama 1975,
56 imposing a tax due on pari-mutuel pools in Macon County,
