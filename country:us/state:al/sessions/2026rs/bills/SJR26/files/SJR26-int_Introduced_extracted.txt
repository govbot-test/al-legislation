4 SJR___ MOURNING THE DEATH AND HONORING THE LIFE OF JOHN
5 DAVID WILLIAMS.
6
7 WHEREAS, it is with profound sadness that we note the
8 death of John "Johnny" David Williams of Tuscaloosa,
9 Alabama, on February 4, 2025, at the age of 68; he touched
10 lives in all parts of the state over a career that took him
11 from the high school and collegiate coaching ranks to
12 athletic director to bowl executive; and
13 WHEREAS, Johnny was born on September 1, 1956, to
14 Julia Pearl Fisher Williams and Huey Williams; Johnny spent
15 his entire life involved in sports, a love instilled in him
16 by his brothers at a young age; he was a star player at Holt
17 High School, which earned him a 4-year scholarship at the
18 University of North Alabama (UNA) where he started every
19 game of his college career; at the conclusion of his playing
20 career he became a coach at UNA, thus beginning his
21 profession in coaching at the college level; and
22 WHEREAS, he progressed to Troy University where he
23 dedicated 20 years, first serving as a graduate assistant
24 and later as Defensive Coordinator; for his last 10 years at
25 Troy, he served as Athletic Director, where he received the
26 2002 General Robert Neyland Athletics Director Award from
27 the All-American Football Foundation and in 2016 was
28 inducted into the Troy Sports Hall of Fame; from there,
