4 HJR___ NAMING THE BRIDGE ON UNITED STATES HIGHWAY 43 THAT
5 SPANS THE B.N. RAILROAD BETWEEN SPORTS PLEX ROAD AND BELL
6 GRAYSON ROAD IN MARENGO COUNTY AS THE FRED ARMSTEAD, SR.
7 MEMORIAL BRIDGE.
8
9 WHEREAS, it is with the highest accolade that this
10 body honors Fred Armstead, Sr. by requesting the Department
11 of Transportation designate the bridge on United States
12 Highway 43 that spans the B.N. Railroad between Sports Plex
13 Road and Bell Grayson Road in the City of Demopolis, Marengo
14 County, Alabama, as the Fred Armstead Memorial Bridge; and
15 WHEREAS, Fred Armstead was a lifelong public servant;
16 he was the first Black police officer in Demopolis and the
17 first Black Marengo County Commissioner for District 1,
18 which he served honorably for 36 years; he served for over
19 30 years on the Alabama Tombigbee Regional Commission
20 Executive Committee; he also served on the boards of The
21 University of Alabama at Birmingham School of Medicine,
22 Dallas County HeadStart, DCH Regional Hospital, and many
23 others; he was widely recognized for his leadership and
24 dedication to supporting the Alabama Black Belt; and
25 WHEREAS, the bridge located on U.S. Highway 43 is a
26 vital piece of public infrastructure that connects
27 communities, supports commerce, and ensures safe travel;
28 designating the bridge in his memory venerates the life,
