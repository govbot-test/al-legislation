9 Relating to crimes and offenses; to amend Section
10 13A-10-52, Code of Alabama 1975; to include various conditions
11 under which the crime of eluding or attempting to elude a law
12 enforcement officer is subject to heightened criminal
13 penalties; and to provide certain mandatory minimum penalties
14 for multiple convictions of eluding or attempting to elude a
15 law enforcement officer.
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
