3 To propose an amendment to the Constitution of Alabama
4 of 2022, relating to Covington County, to authorize the Judge
5 of Probate of Covington County to exercise jurisdiction
6 concurrent with certain circuit court cases if the judge of
7 probate is licensed to practice law in the State of Alabama.
8 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
9 Section 1. The following amendment to the Constitution
10 of Alabama of 2022, is proposed:
11 PROPOSED AMENDMENT
12 The Judge of Probate of Covington County may exercise
13 equity jurisdiction concurrent with that of the circuit court
14 in cases originally filed in the Probate Court of Covington
15 County if the judge of probate is licensed to practice law in
16 the State of Alabama. In any case subject to this amendment,
17 the judge of probate shall possess the power and authority of
18 a circuit court judge trying the case and the case shall be
19 treated in all respects in the same manner as a case filed in
20 circuit court. The Alabama Rules of Civil Procedure shall
21 apply in the cases except as otherwise specifically provided
22 by law. This amendment is self-executing, but the Legislature
23 may enact additional laws to implement this amendment if
24 needed.
25 Upon ratification of this constitutional amendment, the
26 Code Commissioner shall number and place this amendment as
27 appropriate in the constitution omitting this instructional
28 paragraph and may make the following nonsubstantive revisions:
