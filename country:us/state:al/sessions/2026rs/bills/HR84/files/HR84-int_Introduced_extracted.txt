16 ideals of charity, hope, and brotherhood among its members,
17 as well as fostering the growth of leadership within the
18 fraternal system; and

20 14, 18, and 19 serves as an important occasion to gather
21 members, reflect on the mission and purpose of the Order,

23 respect; and
