5 TO BE ENTITLED
6 AN ACT
7
8
9 Relating to Henry County; to authorize the Henry County
10 Commission to pay certain expenses for the operation of the
11 coroner's office.
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
13 Section 1. (a)(1) In addition to any compensation paid
14 to the coroner by law, the Henry County Commission may furnish
15 the county coroner and the deputy coroner with a vehicle to be
16 used for official duties of the office of the coroner, subject
17 to regulation thereof by the county commission. The vehicle
18 shall be equipped with equipment deemed necessary for the
19 coroner to carry out official duties, including, but not
20 limited to, a stretcher and a radio.
21 (2) Fuel, oil, and repairs to the county issued vehicle
22 shall be provided at the county's expense. The county
23 commission may provide the coroner and the deputy coroner with
24 a county issued gas card to cover the cost of fuel used in the
25 performance of the official duties of the coroner and the
26 deputy coroner.
27 (b) In addition, the Henry County Commission may
28 reimburse the coroner and the deputy coroner for expenses
Page 1
reimburse the coroner and the deputy coroner for expenses

29 related to attending state mandated training or continuing
30 education.
31 Section 2. This act shall become effective immediately.
