HB184 Enrolled
2
3 Relating to Henry County; to authorize the Henry County
4 Commission to pay certain expenses for the operation of the
5 coroner's office.
6 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
7 Section 1. (a)(1) In addition to any compensation paid
8 to the coroner by law, the Henry County Commission may furnish
9 the county coroner and the deputy coroner with a vehicle to be
10 used for official duties of the office of the coroner, subject
11 to regulation thereof by the county commission. The vehicle
12 shall be equipped with equipment deemed necessary for the
13 coroner to carry out official duties, including, but not
14 limited to, a stretcher and a radio.
15 (2) Fuel, oil, and repairs to the county issued vehicle
16 shall be provided at the county's expense. The county
17 commission may provide the coroner and the deputy coroner with
18 a county issued gas card to cover the cost of fuel used in the
19 performance of the official duties of the coroner and the
20 deputy coroner.
21 (b) In addition, the Henry County Commission may
22 reimburse the coroner and the deputy coroner for expenses
23 related to attending state mandated training or continuing
24 education.
25 Section 2. This act shall become effective immediately.
