8 WHEREAS, with great pride and special pleasure, we
9 highly honor Brittney Rabens, who was named the 2026 Armed
10 Forces Insurance Maxwell Air Force Base Military Spouse of
11 the Year on February 9, 2026; and
12 WHEREAS, noted for her extensive contributions to the
13 Alabama military community, Mrs. Rabens is deserving of this
14 great honor; she represents Maxwell-Gunter Air Force Base on
15 the Alabama Military Spouse Advisory Council, collaborating
16 with state and installation partners to address quality of
17 life issues affecting military families across the state;
18 she also leads initiatives that support military families,
19 including Operation Cookie Drop for Airmen during the
20 holidays and partnerships with community organizations that
21 provide bicycles and other support to military children and
22 families; and
23 WHEREAS, at Maxwell-Gunter Air Force Base, she serves
24 as the Key Support Liaison Mentor for the 42nd Air Base
25 Wing, working alongside the Base Commander to support
26 military families and advise command teams on family
27 readiness, quality of life concerns, and effective
28 communication; she also serves as Lead Red Cross Volunteer
Page 1
communication; she also serves as Lead Red Cross Volunteer

29 for the Maxwell Medical Group, overseeing more than 30
30 volunteers who support service members and their families,
31 and she leads the Volunteer Crossing Guard Program to
32 address an important child safety need on base; and
33 WHEREAS, Mrs. Rabens has dedicated more than two
34 decades to supporting service members and military families
35 through 10 permanent moves, five deployments, and two
36 command tours alongside her husband, Colonel Clayton J.

41 commended and honored for her extraordinary service to the

43 for receiving the 2026 Armed Forces Insurance Maxwell Air
44 Force Base Military Spouse of the Year.
