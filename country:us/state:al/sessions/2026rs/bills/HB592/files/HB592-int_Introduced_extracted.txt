11 A BILL
12 TO BE ENTITLED
13 AN ACT
14
15 Relating to public K-12 teacher compensation; to
16 provide for the initial placement on the State Minimum Salary
17 Schedule of certificated teachers who were previously employed
18 by accredited private schools.
19 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
20 Section 1.(a) For purposes of this section, "qualified
21 private school employment" means full-time employment of a
22 certificated teacher at an accredited private school.
23 (b) Commencing with the 2026-2027 school year, upon
24 employment by a public K-12 school, a teacher who has a
25 minimum of five years of previous qualified private school
26 employment shall receive half a year salary adjustment on the
27 State Minimum Salary Schedule for each year of that service.
28 The minimum adjustment shall be a two and one-half year
