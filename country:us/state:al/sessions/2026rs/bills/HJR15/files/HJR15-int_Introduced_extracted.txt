4 HJR___ RECOGNIZING MOUNTAIN BROOK POLICE OFFICER RICHARD
5 KNECHT FOR RECEIVING THE SAFE SCHOOLS LEADERSHIP AWARD.
6
7 WHEREAS, it is with profound appreciation and deepest
8 admiration that we recognize Mountain Brook, Alabama, Police
9 Officer Richard Knecht, who is also the School Resource
10 Officer (SRO) for Mountain Brook High School, for receiving
11 the Safe Schools Leadership Award for his outstanding
12 dedication to school safety; and
13 WHEREAS, the award was presented to Officer Knecht at
14 The Alabama Association of School Resource Officers (TAASRO)
15 Conference held June 2-6, 2025, at the Orange Beach
16 Convention Center; the conference, attended by 1,200 school
17 resource officers and school administrators from Alabama and
18 neighboring states, celebrated excellence in school safety;
19 and
20 WHEREAS, Officer Knecht's service as a police officer
21 began in Bessemer in 2000; he transferred to Mountain Brook
22 in 2003, and he has been assigned as the Mountain Brook High
23 School (MBHS) SRO for more than eight years, where he is
24 loved and appreciated by the student body; and
25 WHEREAS, nominated for the Safe Schools Leadership
26 Award by Mountain Brook Municipal Judge K.C. Hairston and
27 former high school AP Chemistry teacher Bryan Rosenstiel,
28 Officer Knecht was honored for his leadership and commitment
