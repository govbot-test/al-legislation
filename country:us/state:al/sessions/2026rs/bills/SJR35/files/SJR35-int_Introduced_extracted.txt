7 WHEREAS, it is with profound appreciation and deepest
8 admiration that we recognize William Riley "Bill" Hawkins,
9 Jr. for more than 20 years of steadfast service to the
10 people of Alabama; we also take this opportunity to heartily
11 congratulate him upon the occasion of his retirement
12 following a distinguished tenure as an Associate State
13 Director with AARP Alabama; and
14 WHEREAS, throughout his career, Bill, a native of
15 Sumter County, Alabama, and longtime resident of Vestavia
16 Hills, has demonstrated unwavering commitment to empowering
17 seniors, fighting fraud, and advancing policies that improve
18 the quality of life for older Alabamians; and
19 WHEREAS, born to Frances and William R. "B.B."
20 Hawkins, he developed a lifelong dedication to service,
21 integrity, and community involvement that defined his life's
22 path; and
23 WHEREAS, his impact and influence extended beyond
24 county borders when he served as President of the
25 Association of County Commissions of Alabama; and
26 WHEREAS, throughout his career, Bill Hawkins has been
27 recognized as a man of character, diligence, and humility,
28 whose leadership has positively impacted the lives of
