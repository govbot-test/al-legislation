1 Enrolled, An Act,

3 Relating to Tuscaloosa County; to authorize the office
4 of the Tuscaloosa County Tax Assessor and Tuscaloosa County
5 Tax Collector to establish procedures for the electronic
6 filing of business personal property tax returns under certain
7 conditions; and to authorize the establishment of procedures
8 for filing of the returns.
9 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
10 Section 1. (a)(1) The Tuscaloosa County Tax Assessor
11 and Tuscaloosa County Tax Collector may establish electronic
12 filing procedures for the reporting, assessment, and payment
13 of business personal property taxes pursuant to Title 40.
14 (2) A person who files a complete business personal
15 property tax return electronically shall format the tax return
16 as prescribed by the tax assessor and tax collector, and the
17 person shall include in the electronic tax return the same
18 information as a business personal property tax return filed
19 on paper.
20 (3) The tax assessor and tax collector shall establish
21 timely filing and electronic signature requirements that
22 generally conform with existing procedures for electronic
23 filing of other tax returns.
24 (b) Beginning on October 1, 2026, the tax assessor and
25 tax collector may require a business personal property tax
26 return to be filed electronically for any of the following
27 returns:
28 (1) Business engaged in the leasing of personal
