9 Relating to food trucks and other mobile food vendors;
10 to exempt mobile food vendors from obtaining individual health
11 and fire inspections in various locations throughout the state
12 in order to operate, so long as the vendors periodically
13 undergo one health and one fire inspection that apply
14 statewide; to require the State Fire Marshal to establish a
15 statewide uniform fire inspection process for mobile food
16 vendors and to maintain a list of certifying fire inspectors;
17 and to provide for criminal penalties for
18 violations.
