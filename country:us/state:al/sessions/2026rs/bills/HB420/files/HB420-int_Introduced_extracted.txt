4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, a person who discharges a
6 firearm into an occupied school bus or school building
7 is guilty of a Class B felony.
8 This bill would increase the penalty for
9 discharging a firearm into an occupied school bus or
10 school building to a Class A felony.
