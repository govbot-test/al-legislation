4 Relating to Choctaw County; to authorize the county
5 commission to increase ad valorem tax; and to provide for the
6 use of proceeds.
7 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
8 Section 1. (a) Pursuant to Section 217(f) of the
9 Constitution of Alabama of 2022, and a resolution adopted by
10 the Choctaw County Commission after a public hearing, the
11 county commission may increase the rate at which the county
12 levies and collects county ad valorem taxes by up to an
13 additional five mills on each dollar of assessed value.
14 (b) The proceeds of the increase shall be used
15 exclusively for county road and bridge improvement.
16 (c) Any increase proposed pursuant to this act shall be
17 limited to five tax years, and the proposal shall state the
18 amount of the levy, the date of the commencement of the levy,
19 and the specific road or bridge projects the increase shall
20 fund, including the projected completion date of each project.
21 (d) The county commission may submit subsequent
22 proposals pursuant to this act in the same manner provided in
23 this section.
24 (e) Choctaw County may become indebted and may issue
25 warrants to finance the construction or improvement of
26 specific county road or bridge projects payable from the
27 proceeds of any tax levied pursuant to this act.
