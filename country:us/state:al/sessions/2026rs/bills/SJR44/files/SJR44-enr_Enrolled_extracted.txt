4 SJR____ CONGRATULATING THE STANHOPE ELMORE BOYS BOWLING TEAM
5 FOR WINNING THE CLASS 6A/7A STATE CHAMPIONSHIP.
6
7 WHEREAS, with great pride and special pleasure,
8 highest commendations are herein conveyed to the Stanhope
9 Elmore Boys Bowling Team on the occasion of capturing the
10 2026 Alabama High School Athletic Association (AHSAA)
11 Division 6A/7A Boys Bowling Championship; and
12 WHEREAS, the championship was held on January 30,
13 2026, at Bowlero Bowling Center in Mobile, Alabama, where
14 the Mustangs toppled an impressive 1,559 pins to earn the
15 Division 6A/7A Blue Map; and
16 WHEREAS, this victory marks an historic achievement,
17 as the Mustangs became the first boys bowling team to win
18 the AHSAA State Championship in the history of the boys'
19 program, cementing their place in local sports history; and
20 WHEREAS, Stanhope Elmore High School has been a
21 strong contender in high school bowling since the program
22 was reinstated during the 2015–2016 school year, and after
23 years of exceptional talent and perseverance, securing the
24 State Championship title represents a victory for every
25 previous team member over the past decade; and
26 WHEREAS, the Mustangs secured the title by defeating
27 the Hoover Buccaneers in a dramatic comeback during the
28 final game of a double-elimination tournament; after
