4 SJR___ EXTENDING THE ALABAMA RURAL ROAD MAP INITIATIVE.
5
6 WHEREAS, Senate Joint Resolution 92, enacted as Act
7 2025-425 in the 2025 Regular Session, strongly encouraged
8 the Alabama Growth Alliance to develop an Alabama Rural
9 Roadmap initiative to serve as the cornerstone of a
10 comprehensive statewide rural development program designed
11 to provide recommendations to revitalize and empower
12 Alabama's rural communities under the direction of a strong
13 leadership team; and
14 WHEREAS, Act 2025-425 provided that the Alabama Rural
15 Roadmap would be completed no later than the fifth
16 legislative day of the 2026 Regular Session, at which point
17 the initiative would be dissolved; and
18 WHEREAS, the Alabama Growth Alliance and the
19 leadership team of the initiative need additional time to
20 develop and implement a robust statewide rural development
21 program to secure a brighter and more prosperous future for
22 all Alabamians, regardless of geographic location; now
23 therefore,
24 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA, BOTH
25 HOUSES THEREOF CONCURRING, That the Alabama Rural Road Map
26 initiative, created pursuant to Act 2025-425, 2025 Regular
27 Session, is extended.
