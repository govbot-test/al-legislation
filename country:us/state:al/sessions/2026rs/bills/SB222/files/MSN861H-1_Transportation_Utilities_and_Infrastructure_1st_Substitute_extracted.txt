4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, vehicles and combinations of
6 vehicles, including any load on the vehicle, may not
7 exceed a total outside width of 102 inches, with an
8 exception for mirrors or other safety equipment.
9 This bill would include within this exception
10 equipment that is an integral part of an automated
11 driving system, including sensors, cameras, and
12 lighting equipment, provided the total outside width
13 does not exceed 122 inches.
14 Under existing law, the Department of
15 Transportation may issue a permit to authorize an
16 applicant to operate a vehicle whose weight, width,
17 length, or height, or combination thereof, exceeds the
18 statutory maximum limits.
19 This bill would authorize the department to
20 issue an oversized vehicle permit for a vehicle
21 equipped with an automated driving system (ADS).
22 This bill would also provide that the statutory
23 authorization for the operation of ADS-equipped
24 vehicles does not impose any legal duty to upgrade any
25 part of the state or local transportation network to
26 accommodate such ADS-equipped vehicles.
