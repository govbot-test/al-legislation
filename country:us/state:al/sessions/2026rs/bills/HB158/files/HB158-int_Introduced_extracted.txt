4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, the crime of promoting
6 prostitution in the third degree is a Class A
7 misdemeanor.
8 This bill would increase the penalty to a Class
9 D felony.
10 Under existing law, the crime of soliciting
11 prostitution is a Class A misdemeanor.
12 This bill would provide that on a second
13 conviction, the person must serve a minimum of five
14 days in the county jail; and that on a third or
15 subsequent conviction, the penalty would be increased
16 to a Class D felony and the person must serve a minimum
17 of 30 days in the county jail.
18 This bill would also increase the penalty for
19 soliciting prostitution to a Class D felony if the
20 person solicited is a victim of human trafficking.
