4 HR____ COMMENDING STEVEN T. COX ON THE OCCASION OF HIS 30TH
5 ANNIVERSARY AS PASTOR OF CHURCH OF LIFE, INC.
6
7 WHEREAS, with great pride and special pleasure, we
8 highly honor Pastor Steven T. Cox for 30 years of ministry
9 to Church of Life, Inc., in Prichard, Alabama; and
10 WHEREAS, Pastor Cox accepted the Lord Jesus in his
11 life on September 21, 1981, and has been walking and working
12 in the service of the Lord for 43 years; he has worked in
13 building up and restoring several churches before becoming a
14 pastor in 1995; and
15 WHEREAS, he is a graduate of The University Of
16 Alabama, where he earned a Bachelor's degree in Public
17 Relations with a minor in Civil Engineering Technology; he
18 earned a Master's degree in Theology Studies from Spring
19 Hill College in Mobile; and
20 WHEREAS, the Lord spoke to Pastor Cox and instructed
21 him to begin the Church of Life, Inc., in Prichard;
22 following the instructions of the Lord, he began building
23 and repairing the site for a place of worship; and
24 WHEREAS, Pastor Cox called on his family and various
25 workers to assist in the preparation and transformation of
26 the building into a local church; the major work of
27 preparing the sanctuary was completed in November 1995; and
