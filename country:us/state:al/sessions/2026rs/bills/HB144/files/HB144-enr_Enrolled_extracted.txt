HB144 Enrolled
2
3 Relating to Jefferson County; to authorize the city
4 council to establish additional entertainment districts in
5 Vestavia Hills.
6 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
7 Section 1. Notwithstanding any other provision of law,
8 the City Council of Vestavia Hills, by ordinance, may
9 establish up to five total entertainment districts within the
10 city permitting consumption of alcoholic beverages purchased
11 from on-premises alcoholic beverage licenses to be consumed by
12 purchasers anywhere within the entertainment districts. The
13 term on-premises as applied to consumption of alcoholic
14 beverages in each entertainment district shall include
15 anywhere within the district.
16 Section 2. This act shall become effective immediately.
