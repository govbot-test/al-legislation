6 TO BE ENTITLED
7 AN ACT
8
9 Relating to bail; to require a pretrial hearing for
10 illegal aliens charged with a violent offense; and to
11 authorize a court to deny bail following a hearing and
12 satisfaction of certain factors.
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
14 Section 1. (a) As used in this section, the following
15 terms have the following meanings:
16 (1) ILLEGAL ALIEN. The term as defined in Section
17 31-13-3, Code of Alabama 1975.
18 (b) If a defendant is an illegal alien and charged with
19 an offense enumerated under Section 15-13-3(b), Code of
20 Alabama 1975, the defendant shall be detained and is not
21 eligible for bail until the court conducts a pretrial
22 detention hearing pursuant to this section.
23 (c) At a pretrial detention hearing, the court shall
24 deny bail for the defendant if the court determines that based
25 on the following considerations, no amount of bail would
26 reasonably assure the defendant's appearance in court or
27 protect the safety of the community and of any person:
28 (1) The nature and circumstances of the offense
Page 1
(1) The nature and circumstances of the offense
TBLBZ19-1 02/18/2026 CMH (H) HSE 2026-294
House Judiciary Reported Substitute for HB348
29 charged.
30 (2) The nature and seriousness of the danger to any
31 person or the community if the defendant is released.
32 (3) The likelihood the defendant will flee from the
33 jurisdiction to escape prosecution. For purposes of this
34 subdivision, there is a presumption that based on the
35 defendant's status as an illegal alien, the defendant is an
36 inherent flight risk. The court shall give considerable weight
37 to this subdivision.
38 (4) The weight of the evidence against the defendant.
39 (5) The history and characteristics of the defendant,
40 including the defendant's character, physical and mental
41 condition, family ties, employment, financial resources,
42 length of residence in the community, community ties, past
43 conduct, history of drug or alcohol abuse, criminal history,
44 and record concerning appearance at court proceedings.
45 Section 2. This act shall become effective on October
46 1, 2026.
