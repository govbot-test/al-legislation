7 AN ACT
8
9 Relating to public K-12 education; to provide
10 definitions; to require Junior Reserve Officers' Training
11 Corps (JROTC) programs to be available to home school and
12 private school students who reside within the attendance zone
13 of the local school system and are the dependents of active
14 duty military parents performing covered military service.
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
16 Section 1. (a) For the purposes of this section, the
17 following terms have the following meanings:
18 (1) BONA FIDE CHANGE IN DUTY STATION OR RESIDENCE. A
19 required relocation pursuant to military orders, whether
20 temporary or permanent, that necessitates that the service
21 member obtain lodging outside the normal commuting area of the
22 member's principal residence.
23 (2) COVERED MILITARY SERVICE. Active duty military
24 service by a member of the Armed Forces of the United States,
25 including a member of the reserve components or the Alabama
26 National Guard, who is:
27 a. Serving on active duty under federal authority; or
28 b. Serving on active duty under state authority

29 pursuant to an order of the Governor or other lawful state

33 board of education shall provide access to Junior Reserve
