4 SYNOPSIS:
5 This bill would apply to parents who are active
6 duty members of the Armed Forces of the United States
7 or members of the National Guard or reserve components
8 of the Armed Forces of the United States, and are on
9 active duty orders that require a temporary or
10 permanent change of station.
11 This bill would require Junior Reserve Officers'
12 Training Corps (JROTC) programs offered by local school
13 systems to be available to home school and private
14 school students who reside within the attendance zone
15 of the local school system and who are the dependents
16 of active duty military parents performing certain
17 covered military service.

30 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
31 Section 1. (a) For the purposes of this section, the
32 following terms have the following meanings:
33 (1) BONA FIDE CHANGE IN DUTY STATION OR RESIDENCE. A
34 required relocation pursuant to military orders, whether
35 temporary or permanent, that necessitates that the service
36 member obtain lodging outside the normal commuting area of the
37 member's principal residence.
38 (2) COVERED MILITARY SERVICE. Active duty military
39 service by a member of the Armed Forces of the United States,
40 including a member of the reserve components or the Alabama
41 National Guard, who is:
42 a. Serving on active duty under federal authority; or
43 b. Serving on active duty under state authority
44 pursuant to an order of the Governor or other lawful state
45 authority, when the service requires a bona fide change in
46 duty station or residence.
47 (b) A local board of education shall provide access to
48 Junior Reserve Officers' Training Corps (JROTC) programs
49 offered in any school within the local school system to any
50 student who resides within the attendance zone of the local
51 school system, who is either home schooled or enrolled in a
52 private school, who is the dependent of an active duty
53 military parent performing covered military service, and who
54 otherwise satisfies all applicable eligibility requirements
55 for participation in the program.
