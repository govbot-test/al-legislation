4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Currently, privately operated hospitals in this
6 state provide funding for the Medicaid Agency through a
7 provider privilege tax. This tax will end on September
8 30, 2028.
9 This bill would remove the sunset clause and
10 make the hospital provider privilege tax permanent.
