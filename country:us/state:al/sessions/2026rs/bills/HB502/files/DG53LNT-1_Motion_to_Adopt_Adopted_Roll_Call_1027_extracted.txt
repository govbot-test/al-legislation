9 Relating to the City of Prichard in Mobile County; to
10 authorize the City of Prichard to file an expedited quiet
11 title and foreclosure action in circuit court to establish
12 clear title to abandoned tax sale properties within the city
13 limits that are acquired from the state land commissioner
14 under Chapter 10 of Title 40, Code of Alabama 1975; and to
15 provide for the procedure and due process for the action in
16 circuit court.
17 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
