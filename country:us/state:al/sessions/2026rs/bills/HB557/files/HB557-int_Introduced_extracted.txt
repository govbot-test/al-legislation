5 TO BE ENTITLED
6 AN ACT
7
8
9 Relating to Wilcox County; to levy an ad valorem tax to
10 replace the additional ad valorem tax expiring in 2027.
11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
12 Section 1. For the purposes of this act, the following
13 terms have the following meanings:
14 (1) BOARD AND COUNTY TAX. The ad valorem tax authorized
15 in Section 66-10.00 of the Constitution of Alabama of 2022,
16 and levied and collected on all taxable property in the county
17 at the rate of 10 mills on each dollar of assessed value.
18 (2) EXPIRING TAX. The ad valorem tax authorized by Act
19 2002-428, 2002 Regular Session (Acts 2002, p. 1107), which is
20 authorized for final levy for the tax year commencing October
21 1, 2026, which will become due and payable, and have a final
22 collection, on October 1, 2027.
23 (3) RENEWAL PROCESS. The proposal made by the Wilcox
24 County Commission to renew and continue the present rate of
25 taxation in the county and the present distribution of ad
26 valorem tax proceeds to the county and the Wilcox County Board
27 of Education by means of increasing the rate at which the
28 board and county tax is authorized to be levied and collected.
Page 1
board and county tax is authorized to be levied and collected.
HB557 INTRODUCED
29 Section 2. (a) Pursuant to Section 217(f) of the
30 Constitution of Alabama of 2022, and a resolution adopted by
31 the Wilcox County Commission after a public hearing, the
32 renewal process is approved and the county commission may
33 increase the rate at which the county levies and collects the
34 board and county tax to a maximum rate, for any tax year of
35 the county commencing with the tax year beginning October 1,
36 2027, equal to 19 mills on each dollar of assessed value, an
37 increase of nine mills on each dollar of assessed value.
38 (b) The increase, if approved, shall become effective
39 simultaneously with the expiration of the expiring tax,
40 leading to no net increase in the total rate of ad valorem tax
41 levied in the county.
42 (c) The proceeds of the increase shall be distributed
43 as follows: (i) two mills to the county general fund to be
44 used for general county purposes; and (ii) seven mills to the
45 Wilcox County Board of Education to be used for public school
46 purposes.
47 Section 3. The increase in the rate of the tax as
48 provided by this act is subject to the approval of a majority
49 of the qualified electors of the county who vote on the
50 proposed increase at the next general, primary,
51 constitutional, or special election held for that purpose.
52 Section 4. This act shall become effective on June 1,
53 2026.
