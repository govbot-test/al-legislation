5 UPON THE OCCASION OF HIS RETIREMENT.

7 WHEREAS, it is with profound appreciation and deepest
8 admiration that we commend Roger David Perkins for 26 years
9 of distinguished public service to the Town of Mount Vernon
10 through his work as the City Inspector; we also take this
11 opportunity to heartily congratulate him upon his
12 retirement; and
13 WHEREAS, throughout his tenure, Mr. Perkins
14 demonstrated professionalism, integrity, and a steadfast
15 commitment to ensuring the safety, compliance, and orderly
16 development of his community; and
17 WHEREAS, his work helped protect the health, safety,
18 and welfare of the residents of Mount Vernon by upholding
19 building codes and standards with fairness and consistency;
20 and
21 WHEREAS, Mr. Perkins earned the respect of town
22 officials, contractors, business owners, and residents alike
23 through his knowledge, dependability, and willingness to
24 serve; and
25 WHEREAS, his dedication and institutional knowledge
26 have left a lasting and positive impact on the growth and
27 stability of the Town of Mt. Vernon; now therefore,

stability of the Town of Mt. Vernon; now therefore,
HJR197 INTRODUCED
28 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA, BOTH
29 HOUSES THEREOF CONCURRING, That we hereby most highly
30 commend Roger David Perkins for his 26 years of outstanding
31 and faithful service as City Inspector for the Town of Mount
32 Vernon, and we extend to him our heartfelt gratitude and
33 sincere best wishes for continued health, happiness, and
34 fulfillment in his future endeavors.
