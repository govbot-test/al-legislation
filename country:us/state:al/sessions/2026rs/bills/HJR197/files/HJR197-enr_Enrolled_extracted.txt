4 THE OCCASION OF HIS RETIREMENT.
5
6 WHEREAS, it is with profound appreciation and deepest
7 admiration that we commend Roger David Perkins for 26 years
8 of distinguished public service to the Town of Mount Vernon
9 through his work as the City Inspector; we also take this
10 opportunity to heartily congratulate him upon his
11 retirement; and
12 WHEREAS, throughout his tenure, Mr. Perkins
13 demonstrated professionalism, integrity, and a steadfast
14 commitment to ensuring the safety, compliance, and orderly
15 development of his community; and
16 WHEREAS, his work helped protect the health, safety,
17 and welfare of the residents of Mount Vernon by upholding
18 building codes and standards with fairness and consistency;
19 and
20 WHEREAS, Mr. Perkins earned the respect of town
21 officials, contractors, business owners, and residents alike
22 through his knowledge, dependability, and willingness to
23 serve; and
24 WHEREAS, his dedication and institutional knowledge
25 have left a lasting and positive impact on the growth and
26 stability of the Town of Mt. Vernon; now therefore,
27 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA, BOTH
28 HOUSES THEREOF CONCURRING, That we hereby most highly
