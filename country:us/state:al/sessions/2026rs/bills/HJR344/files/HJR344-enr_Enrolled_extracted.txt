4 COMMENDING CHRIS RICHARDS AND RECOGNIZING JUNE 12, 2026, AS
5 CHRIS RICHARDS DAY IN ALABAMA.
6
7 WHEREAS, Chris Richards, the 2025 United States
8 Soccer Male Player of the Year, is one of two Alabamians
9 positioned to be on the U.S. roster for this summer's World
10 Cup and is also projected to be the first team captain from
11 Alabama; and
12 WHEREAS, a native of Hoover, Chris Richards played in
13 12 matches for the United States Men's National Team,
14 totaling 1,004 minutes, which is the most of his
15 international career; he scored two goals, including one in
16 the 2025 Concacaf Gold Cup final, in which Mexico prevailed
17 2-1; he was also named to the tournament's Best XI; and
18 WHEREAS, Chris Richards has enjoyed similar success
19 at the club level, making 32 appearances in all
20 competitions, and helping his South London team Crystal
21 Palace win the 2025 Football Association Challenge Cup with
22 a 1-0 win against Manchester City, as well as the 2025
23 Community Shield via a penalty shootout against Liverpool;
24 those were the first major trophies won by Crystal Palace in
25 its 120-year history; and
26 WHEREAS, in just a few short years, Chris Richards
27 has managed to play for teams in three different countries;
28 the 26-year-old was cut from a trial at FC Dallas, then
