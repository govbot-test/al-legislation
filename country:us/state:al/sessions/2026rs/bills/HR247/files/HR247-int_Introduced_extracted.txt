4 HR____ MOURNING THE DEATH OF LEBARRON CHRISTOPHER HORN.
5
6 WHEREAS, it is with deep sorrow and regret that we
7 note the lamentable death of LeBarron Christopher Horn of
8 Mobile, Alabama, on March 7, 2026, at the age of 58; and
9 WHEREAS, LeBarron Christopher Horn, known
10 affectionately as "Barry," was born on July 31, 1967, in
11 Prichard to the late Arnetta Horn Sharp; he was reared with
12 a strong foundation of faith, being christened and later
13 professing his faith in Christ at Macedonia Missionary
14 Baptist Church; and
15 WHEREAS, he was educated in the Mobile County Public
16 School System and graduated from Mattie T. Blount High
17 School in 1985, later continuing his studies at Tuskegee
18 University before answering the call to serve his country in
19 the United States Army as a cannon crewmember with the 11th
20 Field Artillery Regiment; and
21 WHEREAS, following his honorable military service,
22 Mr. Horn served as a forklift operator at Northland
23 Services, Inc., before dedicating nearly 30 years to the
24 United States Postal Service as a mail carrier, earning the
25 admiration of his community through his reliability,
26 kindness, and commitment to service; and
27 WHEREAS, beyond his professional accomplishments, Mr.
28 Horn was a man of warmth and joy known for his infectious
