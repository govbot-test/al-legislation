7 WHEREAS, highest commendations and congratulations
8 are herein proudly presented to the General Federation of
9 Women's Clubs (GFWC) Alabama and its president, Becky
10 Vansant, as she concludes her 2024-2026 administration; and
11 WHEREAS, organized in 1895, GFWC Alabama was
12 chartered into the international organization General
13 Federation of Women's Clubs in 1907; for 131 years, GFWC
14 Alabama has worked to address the most pressing needs of our
15 communities, state, nation, and world; and
16 WHEREAS, The 50 clubs and 1,200 members located in
17 communities throughout Alabama develop outstanding programs
18 and projects to address common issues and concerns; and
19 WHEREAS, some areas addressed through programs and
20 projects include access to and support of the arts,
21 education and libraries, natural resource preservation,
22 veterans, public and individual health and wellness, child
23 advocacy, and citizenship; and
24 WHEREAS, the establishment of scholarships and
25 libraries has been a major focus area for GFWC Alabama and
