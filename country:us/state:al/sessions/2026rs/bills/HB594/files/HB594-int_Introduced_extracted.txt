4 SYNOPSIS:
5 This bill would establish the Rural Industry
6 Reshoring and Reinvestment Act that will stimulate
7 economic growth in rural communities by offering
8 strategic incentives to attract manufacturing and
9 supply chain investments, with a focus on reshoring
10 industries previously located outside the United
11 States.

32 Section 3. For the purpose of this act, the following
33 terms and phrases have the following meanings:
34 (1) DEPARTMENT. The Alabama Department of Commerce.
35 (2) ELIGIBLE BUSINESS. A manufacturing or logistics
36 company that locates and expands in a designated opportunity
37 zone.
38 (3) RURAL OPPORTUNITY ZONE. A county or jurisdiction
39 with a population below a threshold set by the Alabama
40 Department of Commerce and with economic indicators below the
41 state or national average.
42 Section 4. (a) A refundable income tax credit, known as
43 the Rural Reshoring Tax Credit is created.
44 (b) The income tax credit shall be made available to
45 eligible businesses that create at least 15 full-time jobs
46 with a median wage equal to or greater than the county median.
47 (c) The income tax credit is equal to five percent to
48 qualifying payroll for up to 10 years.
49 (d) The income tax credit is nontransferable and may be
50 carried forward for up to five years.
51 (e) The aggregate amount of income tax credits that may
52 be allocated by the department under this section during a
53 calendar year shall not exceed ten million dollars
54 ($10,000,000).
