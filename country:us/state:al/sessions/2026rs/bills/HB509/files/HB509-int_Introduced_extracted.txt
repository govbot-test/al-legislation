22 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
23 Section 1. (a) In a civil action for damages for
24 personal injury, wrongful death, or property damage, a party
25 to the action shall be entitled to an affirmative defense if

27 death, or property damage was sustained by an individual who

29 any public street, highway, bridge, or road by doing any of

34 bridge, or road.
35 (3) Endangering the safe movement of vehicles or
36 pedestrians traveling on the street, highway, bridge, or road.
37 (b) In a civil action in which a defendant raises an
