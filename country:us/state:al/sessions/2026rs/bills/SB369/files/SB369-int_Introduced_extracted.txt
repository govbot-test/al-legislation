9 Relating to Lauderdale County; to create the Lauderdale
10 County Economic Development Authority; to provide for the
11 authority's board; to authorize the county commission to levy
12 an additional sales and use tax; to provide for the
13 administration and proceeds of the tax; and to create the
14 Lauderdale County Economic Development Authority Fund.
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
16 Section 1. (a) There is created the Lauderdale County
17 Economic Development Authority. The authority is created for
18 the purpose and has the responsibility of aiding and assisting
19 current industries an retail establishments and coordinating
20 the efforts of all municipal and county agencies of Lauderdale
21 County as well as aiding organizations in the development of
22 new industries and retail establishments which will provide
23 job opportunities for the residents of Lauderdale County.
24 (b)(1) The authority shall be governed by a board of
25 directors consisting of five members. All five members shall
26 be appointed by the county commission. Vacancies on the board
27 shall be appointed by the same appointing authority that made
28 the initial appointment.

29 (2) Each member shall serve for a term of four years.
30 Before August 1, 2026, the appointing authorities shall submit
31 in writing the name and address of the members of the board of
32 directors to the judge of probate and a record of the
33 appointments shall be filed and maintained in the office of
34 the judge of probate.
35 (3) The members shall elect from among themselves a
36 president, vice president, secretary, and treasurer. Each
37 officer of the board shall serve for a period of one year, or
38 until a successor is elected to serve. The officers of the
39 board shall not be bonded, except the treasurer, who shall
40 obtain a bond in a reasonable amount.
41 (4) The members of the board shall receive the same per
42 diem and mileage for actual attendance of meetings of the
43 board as allowed for state employees for up to 12 meetings in
44 any one year.
45 (c)(1) The authority may employ a qualified executive
46 director who shall be responsible for carrying forth the
47 policies and directives of the board of directors. The
48 executive director shall serve at the pleasure of the board.
49 The board of directors shall set the salary of the executive
50 director and shall provide necessary office space and clerical
51 help.
52 (2) The authority may solicit and receive contributions
53 from other governmental entities and public or private
54 corporations, partnerships, or individuals. The authority
55 shall submit to audits by the Department of Examiners of
56 Public Accounts.

57 (d)(1) There is created the Lauderdale County Economic
58 Development Authority Fund.
59 (2) The Lauderdale County Economic Development
60 Authority Board of Trustees may expend the monies in the fund
61 for any lawful purpose that complies with the purposes of
62 subsection (a).
63 (e)(1) The Lauderdale County Commission may levy an
64 additional sales and use tax of up to three-quarters of one
65 percent on sales, use, storage, consumption, or gross
66 receipts.
67 (2) The gross receipts of any business and the gross
68 proceeds of all sales and use of products or services which
69 are presently exempt under the state sales and use tax
70 statutes are exempt from the taxes authorized by this section.
71 (3) The proceeds of any tax levied pursuant to this
72 subsection shall be deposited in the Lauderdale County
73 Economic Development Authority Fund.
74 (4) Any tax levied pursuant to this subsection shall be
75 administered in the same manner as the tax established in
76 Subpart 2 of Part 6 of Article 24 of Chapter 39 of Title 45,
77 Code of Alabama 1975.
78 Section 2. This act shall become effective on October
79 1, 2026.
