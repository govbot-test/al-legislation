18 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
19 Section 1. (a) For the purposes of this section, the
20 term "board member" means any member of any department,
21 council, board, commission, division, bureau, or other entity
22 subject to the Alabama Sunset Law of 1981, Chapter 20 of Title
23 41, Code of Alabama 1975. The term does not include any
24 cabinet member or statewide elected official.
25 (b) All board members serving on October 1, 2026, shall
26 complete the board governance course provided for in
27 subsection (c) on or before March 1, 2027. Any board member
28 who begins a term of office after October 1, 2026, shall

31 (c) The Department of Examiners of Public Accounts
32 shall provide a board governance course of not less than five
33 hours to all board members. The course's curriculum shall be
34 determined by the Chief Examiner of Public Accounts in
35 consultation with the Attorney General and shall include, but
36 not be limited to, all of the following topics as they relate
37 to board membership:
38 (1) The Alabama Open Meetings Act.
39 (2) The role of the Contract Review Permanent
40 Legislative Oversight Committee.
41 (3) Travel, compensation, and per diem.
42 (4) Reoccurring significant issues determined by the
43 Department of Examiners of Public Accounts during audits and
44 reviews conducted pursuant to Section 41-20-9, Code of Alabama
45 1975.
46 (5) General operating procedures and best practices to
47 ensure the effective discharge of the duties of board members
48 in keeping with the highest standards of stewardship and
49 principles of public service.
50 (d) The board governance course shall be available
51 online and may be conducted online or in person. Evidence of
52 each board member's completion of the course shall be recorded
53 and maintained by the Department of Examiners of Public
54 Accounts.
