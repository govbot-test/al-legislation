6 TO BE ENTITLED
7 AN ACT
8
9 Relating to health insurance; to require that health
10 benefit plans offered in this state which pay or reimburse for
11 breast examinations, including mammography, provide the
12 coverage without imposing a deductible, copayment, or
13 coinsurance charge on the beneficiary.
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
15 Section 1. (a) For purposes of this section, the
16 following terms have the following meanings:
17 (1) COST-SHARING REQUIREMENT. A deductible, copayment,
18 coinsurance, or similar expense borne by the insured for a
19 covered benefit under a health benefit plan.
20 (2) DIAGNOSTIC BREAST EXAMINATION. A medically
21 necessary and appropriate, in accordance with National
22 Comprehensive Cancer Network Guidelines, examination of the
23 breast that includes, but is not limited to, contrast-enhanced
24 mammography, diagnostic mammography, breast magnetic resonance
25 imaging, breast ultrasound, or molecular breast imaging that
26 is used to:
27 a. Evaluate an abnormality seen or suspected from a
28 screening examination for breast cancer; or
Page 1
screening examination for breast cancer; or
U9HZ6XD-1 01/28/2026 JC (H) HSE 2025-3600
House Insurance Reported Substitute for HB300
29 b. Evaluate an abnormality detected by another means of
30 examination.
31 (3) HEALTH BENEFIT PLAN. a. Any plan, policy, or
32 contract issued, delivered, or renewed in this state that
33 provides health coverage that includes payment for
34 hospitalization, physician care, treatment, surgery, therapy,
35 drugs, equipment, and any other medical expense, regardless of
36 the following:
37 1. Whether the plan, policy, or contract is provided by
38 a health care insurer; health maintenance organization
39 established under Chapter 21A of Title 27, Code of Alabama
40 1975; health care services plan established under Chapter 20
41 of Title 10A, Code of Alabama 1975; a nonprofit agricultural
42 organization that provides health benefits to its members
43 under Chapter 33 of Title 2, Code of Alabama 1975; or any
44 other entity that pays for, purchases, or reimburses for
45 health care services.
46 2. Whether the plan is for a group or an individual.
47 b. The term does not include accident-only, specified
48 disease, individual hospital indemnity, credit, dental-only,
49 Medicare supplement, long-term care, disability income, or
50 other limited benefit health insurance policies, or coverage
51 issued as supplemental to liability insurance, workers'
52 compensation, or automobile medical payment insurance.
53 (4) SUPPLEMENTAL BREAST EXAMINATION. A medically
54 necessary and appropriate, in accordance with National
55 Comprehensive Cancer Network Guidelines, examination of the
56 breast that includes, but is not limited to, contrast-enhanced
Page 2
breast that includes, but is not limited to, contrast-enhanced
U9HZ6XD-1 01/28/2026 JC (H) HSE 2025-3600
House Insurance Reported Substitute for HB300
57 mammography, breast magnetic resonance imaging, breast
58 ultrasound, or molecular breast imaging that is used to screen
59 for breast cancer when there is no abnormality seen or
60 suspected, based on personal or family medical history or
61 additional factors that increase the risk of breast cancer,
62 including dense tissue, on the recommendation of a physician.
63 (b) A health benefit plan that pays for, purchases, or
64 furnishes health care services to individuals who reside in
65 this state, and which provides coverage for a supplemental
66 breast examination or a diagnostic breast examination, may not
67 impose any cost-sharing requirement on an enrollee for a
68 supplemental breast examination or diagnostic breast
69 examination.
70 Section 2. This act shall become effective on January
71 1, 2027.
