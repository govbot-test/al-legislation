HB300 Enrolled
1 Enrolled, An Act,
2
3
4 Relating to health insurance; to require that health
5 benefit plans offered in this state which pay or reimburse for
6 breast examinations, including mammography, provide the
7 coverage without imposing a deductible, copayment, or
8 coinsurance charge on the beneficiary.
9 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
10 Section 1. (a) For purposes of this section, the
11 following terms have the following meanings:
12 (1) COST-SHARING REQUIREMENT. A deductible, copayment,
13 coinsurance, or similar expense borne by the insured for a
14 covered benefit under a health benefit plan.
15 (2) DIAGNOSTIC BREAST EXAMINATION. A medically
16 necessary and appropriate examination of the breast that
17 includes, but is not limited to, contrast-enhanced
18 mammography, diagnostic mammography, breast magnetic resonance
19 imaging, breast ultrasound, or molecular breast imaging that
20 is used to:
21 a. Evaluate an abnormality seen or suspected from a
22 screening examination for breast cancer; or
23 b. Evaluate an abnormality detected by another means of
24 examination.
25 (3) HEALTH BENEFIT PLAN. a. Any plan, policy, or
26 contract issued, delivered, or renewed in this state that
27 provides health coverage that includes payment for
28 hospitalization, physician care, treatment, surgery, therapy,
Page 1
hospitalization, physician care, treatment, surgery, therapy,
HB300 Enrolled
29 drugs, equipment, and any other medical expense, regardless of
30 the following:
31 1. Whether the plan, policy, or contract is provided by
32 a health care insurer; health maintenance organization
33 established under Chapter 21A of Title 27, Code of Alabama
34 1975; health care services plan established under Chapter 20
35 of Title 10A, Code of Alabama 1975; a nonprofit agricultural
36 organization that provides health benefits to its members
37 under Chapter 33 of Title 2, Code of Alabama 1975; or any
38 other entity that pays for, purchases, or reimburses for
39 health care services.
40 2. Whether the plan is for a group or an individual.
41 b. The term does not include accident-only, specified
42 disease, individual hospital indemnity, credit, dental-only,
43 Medicare supplement, long-term care, disability income, or
44 other limited benefit health insurance policies, or coverage
45 issued as supplemental to liability insurance, workers'
46 compensation, or automobile medical payment insurance.
47 (4) SUPPLEMENTAL BREAST EXAMINATION. A medically
48 necessary and appropriate examination of the breast that
49 includes, but is not limited to, contrast-enhanced
50 mammography, breast magnetic resonance imaging, breast
51 ultrasound, or molecular breast imaging that is used to screen
52 for breast cancer when there is no abnormality seen or
53 suspected, based on personal or family medical history or
54 additional factors that increase the risk of breast cancer,
55 including dense tissue, on the recommendation of a physician.
56 (b) A health benefit plan that pays for, purchases, or
Page 2
(b) A health benefit plan that pays for, purchases, or

57 furnishes health care services to individuals who reside in
58 this state, and which provides coverage for a supplemental
59 breast examination or a diagnostic breast examination, may not
60 impose any cost-sharing requirement on an enrollee for a
61 supplemental breast examination or diagnostic breast
62 examination.
63 Section 2. This act shall become effective on January
64 1, 2027.
