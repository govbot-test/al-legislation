5 POOLE, JR.
6
7 WHEREAS, it is with profound sadness that we note the
8 death of Frank Poole, Jr. of Montgomery, Alabama, on January
9 31, 2026, at the age of 80; and
10 WHEREAS, his departure leaves a void in the lives of
11 his loving family and friends; he will be remembered as a
12 devoted husband, brother, father, grandfather, and
13 great-grandfather; his legacy lives on through the many
14 lives he touched; and
15 WHEREAS, Frank was born to Frank Poole, Sr. and
16 Fannie Lee Poole in Sprague, Alabama, on July 1, 1946;
17 affectionately known as "BooLee," Frank proudly graduated
18 from Dunbar High School in 1965 with classmates who loved
19 him endlessly; and
20 WHEREAS, he accepted Christ at an early age,
21 professing his faith at White Cloud Baptist Church in Ramer;
22 he subsequently was a member of various congregations, the
23 most recent being First Baptist Church of Greater Washington
24 Park in Montgomery; and
25 WHEREAS, Frank met the love of his life, Carlotter
26 Poole, and they were united in marriage in 1967; he was
27 hardworking and dedicated to caring for his family; and
28 WHEREAS, he worked for Montgomery City Hall as a mail
