HB200 Enrolled
1 Enrolled, An Act,
2
3 Relating to Escambia County; to authorize the county
4 commission to levy an additional motor vehicle license and
5 registration issuance fee; and to provide for distribution of
6 the proceeds.
7 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
8 Section 1. (a)(1) The Escambia County Commission, prior
9 to December 1, 2026, may levy and provide for the collection
10 of an annual additional motor vehicle license and registration
11 issuance fee in the amount of up to five dollars ($5) on each
12 new registration, transfer, or renewal on any vehicle in the
13 county with the motor vehicle license and registration year
14 commencing on January 1, 2027.
15 (2) The issuance fee authorized by this section may
16 only be for one continuing annual fee of up to five dollars
17 ($5).
18 (3) The county's license issuing official shall collect
19 the issuance fee on each new registration, transfer, or
20 renewal on a vehicle in the county.
21 (4) The issuance fee shall be in addition to all other
22 fees, taxes, or other charges provided for the license and
23 registration of a vehicle.
24 (b) The license issuing official, on a monthly basis,
25 shall distribute the proceeds of the issuance fee levied
26 pursuant to this section to the Sheriff's Law Enforcement Fund
27 for law enforcement purposes.
28 Section 2. This act shall become effective on June 1,
