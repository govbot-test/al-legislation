6 AN ACT

9 Relating to Escambia County; to authorize the county
10 commission to levy an additional motor vehicle license and
11 registration issuance fee; and to provide for distribution of
12 the proceeds.
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
14 Section 1. (a)(1) The Escambia County Commission, prior
15 to December 1, 2026, may levy and provide for the collection
16 of an annual additional motor vehicle license and registration
17 issuance fee in the amount of up to five dollars ($5) on each
18 new registration, transfer, or renewal on any vehicle in the
19 county with the motor vehicle license and registration year
20 commencing on January 1, 2027.
21 (2) The issuance fee authorized by this section may
22 only be for one continuing annual fee of up to five dollars
23 ($5).
24 (3) The county's license issuing official shall collect
25 the issuance fee on each new registration, transfer, or
26 renewal on a vehicle in the county.
27 (4) The issuance fee shall be in addition to all other
28 fees, taxes, or other charges provided for the license and
Page 1
fees, taxes, or other charges provided for the license and

29 registration of a vehicle.
30 (b) The license issuing official, on a monthly basis,
31 shall distribute the proceeds of the issuance fee levied
32 pursuant to this section to the Sheriff's Law Enforcement Fund
33 for law enforcement purposes.
34 Section 2. This act shall become effective on June 1,
35 2026.
