HB289 Enrolled
1 Enrolled, An Act,
2
3
4 Relating to vital statistics; to amend Section 22-9A-6,
5 Code of Alabama 1975, to prohibit the inclusion of the date of
6 disposition on state-administered death certificates.
7 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
8 Section 1. Section 22-9A-6, Code of Alabama 1975, is
9 amended to read as follows:
10 "§22-9A-6
11 (a)(1) The board shall by rule determine, by rule, the
12 items or information to be contained on certificates of birth,
13 death, marriage, and divorce and on reports of fetal death and
14 induced termination of pregnancy. Each certificate, report,
15 and other document required by this chapter shall be in a
16 format prescribed by the State Registrar.
17 (2) Certificates of death may not include a date of
18 disposition.
19 (b) Information required in certificates or reports
20 authorized by this chapter may be filed and registered by
21 photographic, electronic, or other means as prescribed by the
22 State Registrar."
23 Section 2. This act shall become effective on October
24 1, 2026.
