HB532 Enrolled
1 Enrolled, An Act,
2
3 Relating to Shelby County; to authorize the county
4 commission to allow the sale of commodities created by solid
5 waste collection and disposal; to provide that contracts for
6 the sale of the commodities shall be competitively bid; and to
7 provide for distribution of the proceeds.
8 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
9 Section 1. (a) The Shelby County Commission, by
10 resolution, may authorize the sale to public or private
11 parties of the gas, minerals, or other commodities created by
12 the operation of its public landfill where solid waste is
13 collected, stored, and disposed.
14 (b) The sale of gas, minerals, or other commodities
15 pursuant to this section shall be made under contractual
16 agreement entered into by free and open competitive bidding,
17 on sealed bids, to the lowest responsible and responsive
18 bidder.
19 (c) Contracts for the sale of gas, minerals, or other
20 commodities shall not be awarded for periods greater than 20
21 years.
22 (d)(1) The proceeds of the sale of gas, minerals, or
23 other commodities under this section shall be deposited into
24 the General Fund of Shelby County.
25 (2) The proceeds shall be used for the collection and
26 disposal of solid waste; maintenance and construction of the
27 landfill, including the landfill gas collection system; and
28 for other legally allowed purposes.
