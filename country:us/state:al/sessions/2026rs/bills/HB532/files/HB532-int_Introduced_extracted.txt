5 TO BE ENTITLED
6 AN ACT
7
8
9 Relating to Shelby County; to authorize the county
10 commission to allow the sale of commodities created by solid
11 waste collection and disposal; to provide that contracts for
12 the sale of the commodities shall be competitively bid; and to
13 provide for distribution of the proceeds.
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
15 Section 1. (a) The Shelby County Commission, by
16 resolution, may authorize the sale to public or private
17 parties of the gas, minerals, or other commodities created by
18 the operation of its public landfill where solid waste is
19 collected, stored, and disposed.
20 (b) The sale of gas, minerals, or other commodities
21 pursuant to this section shall be made under contractual
22 agreement entered into by free and open competitive bidding,
23 on sealed bids, to the lowest responsible and responsive
24 bidder.
25 (c) Contracts for the sale of gas, minerals, or other
26 commodities shall not be awarded for periods greater than 20
27 years.
28 (d)(1) The proceeds of the sale of gas, minerals, or
Page 1
(d)(1) The proceeds of the sale of gas, minerals, or
HB532 INTRODUCED
29 other commodities under this section shall be deposited into
30 the General Fund of Shelby County.
31 (2) The proceeds shall be used for the collection and
32 disposal of solid waste; maintenance and construction of the
33 landfill, including the landfill gas collection system; and
34 for other legally allowed purposes.
35 Section 2. This act shall become effective on October
36 1, 2026.
