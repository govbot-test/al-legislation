5 TO BE ENTITLED
6 AN ACT
7 To alter, rearrange, and extend the boundary lines and
8 corporate limits of the Town of Morris in Jefferson County.
9 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
10 Section 1. The boundary lines and corporate limits of
11 the Town of Morris in Jefferson County are altered,
12 rearranged, and extended to include within the corporate
13 limits of the municipality, in addition to the lands now
14 included, all of the following territory:
15 BEG NW COR OF NW¼ OF SE¼ SEC 3 TP 15 SR 3W THW 235 FT
16 TH N 283.9 FT TO S/L SARDIS RD TH NELY ALG ROW 665.6 FT TH
17 SELY ALG ROW 1247.1 FT TH S 445.7 FT TH W 520 FT TH N 500 FT
18 TO POB SECT 03 TWSP 15S RANGE 3W
19 Section 2. In accordance with Section 11-42-6 of the
20 Code of Alabama 1975, a map showing the proposed territory to
21 be altered, rearranged, and extended from the Town of Morris
22 is on file and open to public inspection in the Office of the
23 Judge of Probate in Jefferson County, Alabama.
24 Section 3. This act shall become effective on October
25 1, 2026.
