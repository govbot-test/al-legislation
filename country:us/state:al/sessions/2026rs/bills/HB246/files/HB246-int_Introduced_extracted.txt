4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, public K-12 schools are
6 required to post adopted classroom curricula online for
7 review by parents and guardians.
8 This bill would require schools to offer an
9 alternative method for the review of copyrighted
10 materials by parents and guardians.
