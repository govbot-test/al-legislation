HB569 Enrolled
1 Enrolled, An Act,
2
3 Relating to Autauga County; to authorize the levy of an
4 additional sales and use tax; and to levy a rental tax.
5 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
6 Section 1. (a) Beginning September 1, 2026, in addition
7 to any other tax provided by law, there is levied a sales and
8 use tax of one-half of one percent for all business activities
9 covered by Section 45-1-243.01(a)(1), Code of Alabama 1975.
10 (b) The proceeds of this tax shall be deposited in the
11 county general fund.
12 (c) This tax shall be administered according to the
13 procedures established in Part 4 of Article 24 of Chapter 1 of
14 Title 45, Code of Alabama 1975.
15 Section 2. (a) In addition to all other taxes,
16 beginning September 1, 2026, there is levied a three percent
17 privilege tax, to be called a rental tax, on each person
18 leasing or renting tangible personal property in Autauga
19 County.
20 (b) The tax levied pursuant to this section shall
21 parallel the tax imposed by Article 4 of Chapter 12 of Title
22 40, Code of Alabama 1975. This tax shall apply to the same
23 persons and transactions to which that tax applies.
24 (c) The county commission shall provide for the
25 collection and administration of this tax by resolution.
26 (d) The proceeds of this tax shall be deposited in the
27 county general fund.
