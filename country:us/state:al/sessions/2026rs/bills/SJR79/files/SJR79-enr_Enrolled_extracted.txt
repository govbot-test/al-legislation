140 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA, BOTH
141 HOUSES THEREOF CONCURRING, That there is established the
142 Alabama Legislative Study Commission on Child Sexual Abuse
143 Laws and Policies.
144 (a) The commission shall consist of the following
145 members:
146 (1) Two members of the Senate appointed by the

150 minority leader.

153 one of whom shall serve as cochair.

155 appointed by the minority leader.

157 (6) The Director of the Department of Human
158 Resources, or his or her designee.
159 (7) The Commissioner of the Department of Public
160 Health, or his or her designee.
161 (8) The Administrative Director of Courts, or his or

164 District Attorneys Association.

166 enforcement association appointed by the Governor.
