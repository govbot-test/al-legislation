4 SYNOPSIS:
5 This bill would require certain county or
6 municipal agencies to present a qualifying officer with
7 his or her agency-issued badge as part of his or her
8 retirement benefits.
9 This bill would authorize certain county or
10 municipal agencies to present a qualifying officer with
11 his or her agency-issued pistol as part of his or her
12 retirement benefits.
13 This bill would also authorize certain county or
14 municipal agencies to sell an agency-issued pistol to a
15 qualified officer at the time of his or her retirement
16 in certain circumstances.
