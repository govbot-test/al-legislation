HB556 Enrolled
1 Enrolled, An Act,
2
3
4 Relating to Montgomery County; to provide for the
5 creation, funding, and operation of the Montgomery County
6 Legislative Delegation Office and Fund.
7 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
8 Section 1. (a)(1) The Montgomery County Commission
9 shall create the Montgomery County Legislative Delegation
10 Fund. The monies in the fund may be expended by the members of
11 the delegation to support the operation of the Montgomery
12 County Legislative Delegation Office.
13 (2) Upon receipt of any monies distributed to
14 Montgomery County pursuant to Section 40-25-82, Code of
15 Alabama 1975, the county commission shall deposit the monies
16 into the fund.
17 (b) The largest municipality in Montgomery County shall
18 provide adequate space for the office, including telephone
19 service, Internet service, electricity, water, and any other
20 necessary utility services.
21 Section 2. This act shall become effective on October
22 1, 2026.
