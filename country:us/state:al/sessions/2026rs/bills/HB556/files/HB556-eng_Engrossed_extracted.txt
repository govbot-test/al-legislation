4 A BILL
5 TO BE ENTITLED
6 AN ACT
7
8
9 Relating to Montgomery County; to provide for the
10 creation, funding, and operation of the Montgomery County
11 Legislative Delegation Office and Fund.
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
13 Section 1. (a)(1) The Montgomery County Commission
14 shall create the Montgomery County Legislative Delegation
15 Fund. The monies in the fund may be expended by the members of
16 the delegation to support the operation of the Montgomery
17 County Legislative Delegation Office.
18 (2) Upon receipt of any monies distributed to
19 Montgomery County pursuant to Section 40-25-82, Code of
20 Alabama 1975, the county commission shall deposit the monies
21 into the fund.
22 (b) The largest municipality in Montgomery County shall
23 provide adequate space for the office, including telephone
24 service, Internet service, electricity, water, and any other
25 necessary utility services.
26 Section 2. This act shall become effective on October
27 1, 2026.
