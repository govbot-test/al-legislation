7 WHEREAS, it is with great honor that we recognize and
8 celebrate the Lauderdale County Future Farmers of America
9 (FFA) String Band, a talented country and bluegrass trio
10 composed of Conner McMeans, Ty McMeans, and Titus Thornton;
11 and
12 WHEREAS, blending classic Southern roots with
13 youthful energy, the group's signature sound features the
14 banjo, acoustic guitar, and bass guitar, performed with a
15 heartfelt authenticity that reflects their agricultural
16 heritage and small-town pride; and
17 WHEREAS, the three musicians first met in an
18 agriculture class in middle school, where their shared
19 passion for music and FFA began to take shape; over the past
20 five years, they have grown together as artists and
21 performers, captivating audiences across Alabama and beyond;
