2 Relating to Randolph County; to provide for the salary
3 of the county revenue commissioner; and to amend Section
4 45-56-83, Code of Alabama 1975, to provide for the salary of
5 the judge of probate.
6 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
7 Section 1. (a) Effective commencing on the next term of
8 office of the county revenue commissioner, the Revenue
9 Commissioner of Randolph County shall receive an annual salary
10 of seventy thousand dollars ($70,000) per year payable in
11 equal bi-weekly installments.
12 (b) The salary provided by this section shall be in
13 lieu of any salary or other compensation provided by law and
14 shall be paid from the same funding sources as the salary of
15 the revenue commissioner is paid on October 1, 2026.
16 (c) The revenue commissioner shall be eligible for any
17 cost-of-living increases granted to other county officers.
18 Section 2. Section 45-56-83, Code of Alabama 1975, is
19 amended to read as follows:

25 other fees, allowances, and percentages heretofore provided by
26 law, and shall be paid in equal in the same manner
