8 Section 1. For purposes of this act, the following
9 terms have the following meanings:
10 (1) AUTHORIZING ORDINANCE. An ordinance adopted by the
11 Prichard City Council that authorizes the formation or renewal
12 of a district and the levying of benefit assessments.
13 (2) BENEFIT ASSESSMENT. A levy for the purpose of
14 funding activities and improvements that will provide benefits
15 to businesses located within a district.
16 (3) BUSINESS. Any business establishment of the types
17 or classes that are described in the district plan and the
18 authorizing ordinance.
19 (4) BUSINESS OWNER. A person recognized by the city as
20 the owner of the business.
21 (5) BUSINESS OWNERS' ASSOCIATION. A new or existing
22 nonprofit corporation, entity, agency, or public corporation
23 that is under contract with the city to administer the
24 district and implement activities and improvements specified
25 in the district plan.
26 (6) CITY. The City of Prichard.
27 (7) CITY COUNCIL. The City Council of the City of
28 Prichard.
