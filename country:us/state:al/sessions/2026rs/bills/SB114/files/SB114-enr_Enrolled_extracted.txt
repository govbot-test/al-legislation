34 §15-9-50
35 (a) For the purposes of this section, the following
36 terms have the following meanings:
37 (1) ARRESTING OFFICER. An officer, employee, or agent

39 required by law to: (i) maintain public order; (ii) make

41 offenses or is limited to specific offenses; and (iii)

46 a. A crime punishable by death.
