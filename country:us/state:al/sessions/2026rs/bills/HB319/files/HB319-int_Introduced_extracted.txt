88 §22-37-7
89 Any rule adopted pursuant to Section 22-37-3 or
90 prohibition provided in Section 22-37-6 regarding the import,
91 distribution, sale, or use of lead-free materials shall not
92 apply to any of the following:
93 (1) A pipe, pipe fitting, plumbing fitting, or fixture,
94 including a backflow preventer, which is used exclusively for
95 nonpotable services such as manufacturing, industrial
96 processing, irrigation, outdoor watering, or any other use
97 where the water is not anticipated to be used for human
98 consumption.
99 (2) A toilet, bidet, urinal, fill valve, flushometer
100 valve, tub filler, shower valve, fire hydrant, service saddle,
101 or water distribution main gate valve that is two inches in
102 diameter or larger.
103 (3) A clothes washing machine, emergency drench shower,
104 emergency face wash equipment, eyewash device, fire
105 suppression sprinkler, steam capable clothes dryer, or sump
106 pump.
