4 Relating to crimes and offenses; to amend Section
5 13A-6-90.1, Code of Alabama 1975, to further provide for the
6 criminal penalties for stalking in the second degree.
7 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
8 Section 1. Section 13A-6-90.1, Code of Alabama 1975, is
9 amended to read as follows:
10 "§13A-6-90.1
11 (a) A person who, acting with an improper purpose,
12 intentionally and repeatedly follows, harasses, telephones, or
13 initiates communication, verbally, electronically, or
14 otherwise, with another personindividual, any member of the
15 other person's individual's immediate family, or any third
16 party with whom the other person individual is acquainted, and
17 causes material harm to the mental or emotional health of the
18 other personindividual, or causes such person the individual
19 to reasonably fear that his or her employment, business, or
20 career is threatened, and the perpetrator was previously
21 informed to cease that conduct is guilty of the crime of
22 stalking in the second degree.
23 (b)(1) The crime of stalking in the second degree is a
24 Class B misdemeanor.
25 (2) A second or subsequent violation is a Class C
26 felony."
27 Section 2. This act shall become effective on October
28 1, 2026.
