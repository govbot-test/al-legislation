8 to change all references to the State Port Authority,
9 the Alabama State Docks Department, and the Alabama
10 State Port Authority to the Alabama Port Authority.
11 This bill would repeal the 1975 laws
12 establishing the Alabama Port Authority because it is

24 Commissioner to change references to the State Port Authority,

Code of Alabama 1975, establishing the Alabama Port Authority.
HB213 INTRODUCED
29 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
30 Section 1. Section 33-1-2, Code of Alabama 1975, is
31 amended to read as follows:
32 "§33-1-2
33 (a) There shall be a state agency known as the Alabama
34 State Port Authority, hereinafter sometimes referred to as the
35 port authority. Notwithstanding any other provision of law,
36 whenever any act, section of this code, or any other provision
37 of law refers to the Alabama State Docks Department, or the
38 Alabama State Port Authority it shall be deemed a reference to
39 the Alabama State Port Authority.
40 (b) The port authority shall promote, supervise,
41 control, manage, and direct the state docks and all other
42 state lands included within the jurisdiction of this chapter
43 and shall maintain and operate all the improvements and
44 facilities authorized by this chapter. The port authority is a
45 body corporate and shall be constituted as provided in this
46 chapter."
47 Section 2. The Code Commissioner, at times determined
48 appropriate, shall change all references to the State Port
49 Authority, the Alabama State Docks Department, and the Alabama
50 State Port Authority in the Code of Alabama 1975, to the
51 Alabama Port Authority.
52 Section 3. Chapter 13 of Title 33, consisting of
53 Sections 33-13-1 through 33-13-13, Code of Alabama 1975,
54 establishing the Alabama Port Authority, is repealed because
55 the Alabama Port Authority is an obsolete entity.
56 Section 4. This act shall become effective on October
