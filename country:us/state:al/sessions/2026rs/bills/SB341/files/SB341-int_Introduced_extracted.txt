42 Section 1. This act shall be known and may be cited as
43 the Alabama Work Zone Safety Act.
44 Section 2. The Legislature finds and declares the
45 following:
46 (1) Highway construction and maintenance creates a high
47 risk environment for highway workers who must work alongside
48 motor vehicles traveling at high rates of speed.
49 (2) Numerous highway workers have been struck and
50 killed in work zones due to motor vehicles traveling at high
51 rates of speed through the work zones.
52 (3) Speed enforcement management within a work zone is
53 one of the most effective measures to improve safety in the
54 work zone.
55 (4) Speed enforcement is highly labor intensive and can
56 even be hazardous to law enforcement officers given the

58 (5) Photographic speed enforcement systems allow law

60 zone in a safe and cost-effective manner.
61 (6) The adoption and implementation of a photographic
62 speed enforcement system for work zones is necessary to reduce
63 severe crashes, reduce speeding, and improve safe traffic flow
64 in work zones in a state where the patrol resources alone are
65 not capable of consistent enforcement of traffic laws at a
66 level necessary to protect human life.
67 (7) The severe lack of safety of highway workers in
68 work zones is an emergency that necessitates the immediate
69 adoption of a photographic speed enforcement system consistent
70 with the terms of this act.
