7 WHEREAS, with utmost admiration and sincere respect,
8 we take great pride in commending Alabama's 31st Lieutenant
9 Governor William "Will" Ainsworth for his many
10 accomplishments toward the betterment of our state,
11 especially in the areas of workforce development, military
12 support, and tax reform; and
13 WHEREAS, Will Ainsworth was born in Birmingham to
14 Billy and Sharon Ainsworth in 1981; he grew up in Boaz and
15 graduated from Westbrook Christian School in 1999; after
16 attaining a Bachelor's degree in marketing from Auburn
17 University in 2004, he returned to Marshall County and
18 served Albertville's Grace Fellowship Church as a youth
19 pastor; and
20 WHEREAS, along with his brother, Austin, Lt. Governor
21 Ainsworth opened Dream Ranch Hunting Lodge in Guntersville

23 Fishing Expo in 2011 and became a real estate investor
24 specializing in residential construction and development; in
25 addition, he operates a commercial cattle operation in
26 Guntersville; and

28 community through elected office, Lt. Governor Ainsworth won
