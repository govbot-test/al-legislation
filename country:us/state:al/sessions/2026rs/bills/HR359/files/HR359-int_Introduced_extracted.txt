5 PAULINE LACY.
6
7 WHEREAS, it is with profound sorrow that we note the
8 death of Pauline Lacy, who transitioned peacefully on April
9 5, 2026, leaving behind a legacy that will continue to
10 impact generations for years to come; and
11 WHEREAS, Pauline Lacy, born July 29, 1941, in Triana,
12 Alabama, lived a life defined by strength, generosity, and
13 unwavering love for her family and community; and
14 WHEREAS, a proud graduate of Council Training,
15 Pauline believed in discipline, resilience, and personal
16 growth, values she carried throughout her life; she
17 dedicated more than 35 years of service to Chrysler in
18 Huntsville, Alabama, where she was respected for her
19 consistency, strong work ethic, and quiet strength; and
20 WHEREAS, Pauline Lacy was a pillar within her
21 community; she had a gift for bringing people together;
22 every Sunday, she prepared meals that became a gathering
23 place for family and friends; Ortega Circle would fill with
24 cars as people arrived in droves to commune, share laughter,
25 and experience the warmth of her presence; what she created
26 was more than a meal, it was a sense of belonging; and
27 WHEREAS, she opened her home to those in need without
28 hesitation, often housing those who needed support,

hesitation, often housing those who needed support,

29 guidance, or simply a place to feel safe; her generosity
30 knew no limits, and her heart was always full of love to
31 give; and
32 WHEREAS, above all, Pauline Lacy was the heart of her
33 family, a devoted mother, loving grandmother, and cherished
34 great-grandmother; her love was deep, intentional, and
35 steadfast; and
36 WHEREAS, she was preceded in death by her son,
37 Charles Lacy (Tarsha Campbell Lacy); left to cherish her

43 WHEREAS, Pauline Lacy's life stands as a testament to
44 faith, service, strength, and the power of love in action;

48 remembered, it is lived daily; now therefore,
49 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE

51 we also celebrate the life and legacy of Pauline Lacy with
52 honor and thanksgiving; this resolution is offered with
53 heartfelt sympathy to her family, whose grief is shared by
54 many.
