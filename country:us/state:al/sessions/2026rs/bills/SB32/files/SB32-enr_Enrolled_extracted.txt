4 Relating to solid waste collection fees; to amend
5 Section 22-27-3, Code of Alabama 1975, to exempt households
6 whose sole source of income is veterans' benefits from paying
7 solid waste collection fees; and to make nonsubstantive,
8 technical revisions to update the existing code language to
9 current style.
10 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
11 Section 1. Section 22-27-3, Code of Alabama 1975, is

14 (a) Generally. (1) The county commission or municipal
15 governing body may, and is hereby authorized to, make
16 available to the general public collection and disposal
17 facilities for solid wastes in a manner acceptable to the
18 department. The county commission or municipal governing body
19 may provide such the collection or disposal services by
20 contract with private or other controlling agencies and may
21 include house-to-house service or the placement of regularly
22 serviced and controlled bulk refuse receptacles within
23 reasonable (generally less than eight miles) distance from the
24 farthest affected household and the wastes managed in a manner
25 acceptable to the department. For purposes of this
26 subdivision, "reasonable distance" means generally less than
27 eight miles.
