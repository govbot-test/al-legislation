4 HR____ MOURNING THE DEATH AND CELEBRATING THE LIFE OF JOHN
5 BARRATT RUDULPH, JR.
6
7 WHEREAS, it is with profound sorrow that we note the
8 passing of John Barratt Rudulph, Jr. in Birmingham, Alabama,
9 on March 20, 2026, at the age of 79; and
10 WHEREAS, his departure leaves a void in the lives of
11 his loving family, many friends, and the community where he
12 was highly regarded; and
13 WHEREAS, born on July 27, 1946, in Birmingham, John
14 was raised in Mountain Brook and spent his summers along the
15 Gulf Coast at his family's home, Perdido, in Orange Beach, a
16 place that remained meaningful to him through the years; and
17 WHEREAS, he graduated from The University of Alabama
18 with a degree in political science before serving four years
19 in the United States Army, including time stationed in
20 Korea; after his military service, he moved to Tampa,
21 Florida, where he began his career in real estate and met
22 his wife; together, they eventually returned to Birmingham
23 where he took over his father's business, Rudulph Real
24 Estate; for three decades, he and his wife built a respected
25 and enduring business that served families across the region
26 until his retirement in 2014; and
27 WHEREAS, he found great joy in time spent with family
28 and close friends; John loved returning to Perdido, sharing
