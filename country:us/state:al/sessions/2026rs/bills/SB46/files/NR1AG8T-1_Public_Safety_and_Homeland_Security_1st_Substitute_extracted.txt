4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Existing law provides for the requirements for
6 the contents of a driver license and nondriver
7 identification card.
8 This bill would require the Alabama State Law
9 Enforcement Agency to establish a method for an
10 individual to voluntarily designate on a driver license
11 or nondriver identification card that the individual
12 has been diagnosed with certain medical conditions.
