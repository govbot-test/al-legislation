2 HJR271 NAMING THE PORTION OF ALABAMA HIGHWAY 21 FROM MILE
3 MARKER 39 TO MILE MARKER 41 IN MONROE COUNTY THE SPECIAL
4 AGENT JUSTIN WILLIAMS MEMORIAL HIGHWAY.
5
6 WHEREAS, it is with the highest accolade that this
7 body honors Special Agent Justin Jemel Williams by
8 requesting the State Department of Transportation designate
9 the portion of Alabama Highway 21 from mile marker 39 to
10 mile marker 41 in Monroe County, Alabama, the Special Agent
11 Justin Williams Memorial Highway; and
12 WHEREAS, Special Agent Williams distinguished himself
13 in the Highway Patrol Division of the Alabama State Law
14 Enforcement Agency (ALEA); after three years with the State
15 Troopers, he was advanced to the Major Crimes Division in
16 the ABI/SBI branch of ALEA, serving five years with that
17 agency and tackling complex criminal investigations; he was
18 assigned to the United States Marshals Fugitive Apprehension
19 Task Force, showcasing his ability to collaborate on a
20 national level; he then served six years in protective
21 services, serving on the protective details of both Governor
22 Robert Bentley and Governor Kay Ivey, where he further
23 distinguished himself in a role that required a high level
24 of skill to manage often high-stress situations; and
25 WHEREAS, Special Agent Williams' dedication to law
26 enforcement is evident in his advancement during his 19
27 years of service to the State of Alabama, particularly by
28 continuing to serve on Governor Ivey's protection detail
