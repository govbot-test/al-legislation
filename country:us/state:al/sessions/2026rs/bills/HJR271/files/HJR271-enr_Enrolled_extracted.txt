3 NAMING THE PORTION OF ALABAMA HIGHWAY 21 FROM MILE MARKER 39
4 TO MILE MARKER 41 IN MONROE COUNTY THE SPECIAL AGENT JUSTIN
5 WILLIAMS MEMORIAL HIGHWAY.
6
7 WHEREAS, it is with the highest accolade that this
8 body honors Special Agent Justin Jemel Williams by
9 requesting the State Department of Transportation designate
10 the portion of Alabama Highway 21 from mile marker 39 to
11 mile marker 41 in Monroe County, Alabama, the Special Agent
12 Justin Williams Memorial Highway; and
13 WHEREAS, Special Agent Williams distinguished himself
14 in the Highway Patrol Division of the Alabama State Law
15 Enforcement Agency (ALEA); after three years with the State
16 Troopers, he was advanced to the Major Crimes Division in
17 the ABI/SBI branch of ALEA, serving five years with that
18 agency and tackling complex criminal investigations; he was
19 assigned to the United States Marshals Fugitive Apprehension
20 Task Force, showcasing his ability to collaborate on a
21 national level; he then served six years in protective
22 services, serving on the protective details of both Governor
23 Robert Bentley and Governor Kay Ivey, where he further
24 distinguished himself in a role that required a high level
25 of skill to manage often high-stress situations; and
26 WHEREAS, Special Agent Williams' dedication to law
27 enforcement is evident in his advancement during his 19
28 years of service to the State of Alabama, particularly by
