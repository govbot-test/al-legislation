4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, there is no provision for
6 retirees and beneficiaries receiving monthly benefits
7 from the Teachers' Retirement System to receive a
8 funded longevity bonus in addition to their retirement
9 allowances.
10 This bill would provide a longevity bonus
11 payment to certain retirees and beneficiaries under the
12 Teachers' Retirement System.
