4 SYNOPSIS:
5 This bill would mandate certain agencies to
6 collect biometric identifiers and provide for their
7 dissemination.
8 This bill would authorize the use of certain
9 approved biometric identification devices by law
10 enforcement officers to identify individuals.
11 This bill would establish and provide for the
12 operation of the Alabama Background Check Service.
13 This bill would provide for approved uses and
14 types of background checks for noncriminal justice
15 purposes.
16 This bill would authorize the Alabama State Law
17 Enforcement Agency (ALEA) to establish and collect fees
18 for background checks for noncriminal justice purposes.
19 This bill would establish the Alabama Rap Back
20 Program.
21 This bill would provide penalties for the misuse
22 of a background check report.
23 This bill would make various changes to update
24 terminology and would make nonsubstantive, technical
25 revisions to update the existing code language to
26 current style.
27 This bill would also repeal provisions of law
28 prohibiting the unauthorized release of criminal
