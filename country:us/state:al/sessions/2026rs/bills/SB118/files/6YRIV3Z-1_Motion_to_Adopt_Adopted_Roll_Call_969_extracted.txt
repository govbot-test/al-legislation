483 (a) An individual commits the crime of misuse of a
484 background check report if he or she does any of the
485 following:
486 (1) Knowingly receives, disseminates, or otherwise uses
487 a background check contrary to the purposes allowed by this
488 article or otherwise allowed in law.

491 subject of the report.

493 Rap Back Program for purposes not authorized in this article
494 with the intent to harass or intimidate another individual.
495 (b) Misuse of a background check report is a Class B
496 misdemeanor.

498 the defendant may be ordered to pay restitution to the victim
499 whose information was misused. Restitution shall include any
