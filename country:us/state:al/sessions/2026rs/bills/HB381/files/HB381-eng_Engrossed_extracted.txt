9 Regarding camp safety; to provide minimum requirements

15 Section 1. This act shall be known and may be cited as
16 the Sarah Marsh Heaven's 27 Camp Safety Act.
17 Section 2. For purposes of this act, the following
18 terms have the following meanings:
19 (1) AGENCY. The Alabama Emergency Management Agency.
20 (2) CABIN. Any structure in which campers sleep,
21 including, but not limited to, a cabin, dormitory, or
22 bunkhouse, regardless of camper capacity.
23 (3) CAMP. Any dedicated site in the State of Alabama
24 where campers temporarily gather for recreational, religious,
25 social, educational, or therapeutic purposes, whether operated
26 for profit or nonprofit purposes, and which provides cabins
27 for campers to stay overnight. The term does not include a
28 dedicated location or structure for hunting or fishing or the

34 the camp operator and staff supervise campers' participation
35 in recreational, religious, social, educational, or
36 therapeutic activities.

38 1. An individual or entity that administers camp

40 any ownership interest in the camp operations, assets,

44 or other facilities, but which otherwise does not administer
45 camp operations and leases the camp to other organizations to
46 conduct camp operations.

49 operator for the purpose of administering camp operatons.

51 or training in which the majority of participants are
52 individuals who are 19 years of age or older.
53 3. An owner of land that leases the land to a camp

55 land.

62 by the agency to a camp operator which certifies that a camp
63 complies with this act.

65 as designated by the Federal Emergency Management Agency
66 pursuant to the National Flood Insurance Act of 1968, 42

174 Section 6. As a condition for receiving an emergency
175 preparedness license, a camp shall operate with all of the
176 following equipment or capabilities:
177 (1) A NOAA Weather Radio that transmits "all-hazards"
178 notifications.
179 (2) A protocol to alert essential staff members of an

181 on text messaging or cellular service.

183 Internet connection and that is capable of providing an alarm
184 and notification of an emergency which is audible both indoors
