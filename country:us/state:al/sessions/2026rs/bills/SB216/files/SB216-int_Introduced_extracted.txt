4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, members of the National
6 Guard may be presented awards for certain
7 accomplishments and service by order of the Governor.
8 This bill would revise the requirements for
9 Alabama National Guard members to receive these awards.
10 This bill would create new awards for Alabama
11 National Guard Members for certain accomplishments and
12 service.
13 This bill would also prescribe that certain
14 awards may be presented by the Governor and that
15 certain awards may be presented by the Adjutant
16 General.
