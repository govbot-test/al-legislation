4 SJR___ EXPRESSING SUPPORT FOR THE UNITED STATES DEPARTMENT
5 OF ENERGY'S NUCLEAR LIFECYCLE INNOVATION ENDEAVOR.
6
7 WHEREAS, the United States Department of Energy
8 issued a Request for Information inviting states to express
9 interest in hosting Nuclear Lifecycle Innovation Campuses in
10 an effort to modernize the nation's full nuclear fuel cycle
11 and strengthen America's leadership in advanced nuclear
12 technology; and
13 WHEREAS, advancing nuclear energy technology will
14 drive innovation, fuel economic growth, and create
15 high-paying jobs; and
16 WHEREAS, American-generated nuclear energy will
17 deliver affordable, reliable, and secure energy needed to
18 power the nation's future; and
19 WHEREAS, Alabama is an emerging national leader in
20 advanced technologies, including, but not limited to,
21 aerospace and defense, advanced manufacturing, large-scale
22 data centers, advanced materials, and biosciences; now
23 therefore,
24 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA, BOTH
25 HOUSES THEREOF CONCURRING, That the members of this body
26 join together in support of President Trump's vision to
27 revitalize America's nuclear base.

revitalize America's nuclear base.

29 encourages the Governor and appropriate state agencies to
30 take necessary measures, including responding to the
31 Department of Energy's Request for Information by April 1,
32 2026, to secure within the state a Nuclear Lifecycle
33 Innovation Campus, and to this end, a copy of this
