4 Relating to Class IV municipalities; to amend Section
5 11-43B-9, Code of Alabama 1975; to remove the restriction that
6 audits for Class IV municipalities may not be conducted by the
7 same accountant for more than three consecutive years.
8 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
9 Section 1. Section 11-43B-9, Code of Alabama 1975, is
10 amended to read as follows:
11 "§11-43B-9
12 The mayor shall, from time to time, shall prepare a
13 detailed statement of all receipts and expenses of the city.
14 At the end of each fiscal year, the council shall cause the
15 books and accounts of the city to be audited by a reputable,
16 disinterested certified public accountant. The same accountant
17 shall not conduct the audit for more than three consecutive
18 fiscal years. A true summary of the annual audit report shall
19 be published once in a newspaper of general circulation in the
20 city. The audit report shall be submitted by the mayor to the
21 council at its first meeting after the completion of such the
22 report."
23 Section 2. This act shall become effective on October
24 1, 2026.
