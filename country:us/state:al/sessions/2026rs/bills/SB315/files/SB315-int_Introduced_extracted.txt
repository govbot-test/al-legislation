4 SYNOPSIS:
5 This bill would provide for a benefit increase
6 for certain eligible state and local retirees and
7 beneficiaries of the Employees' Retirement System and
8 certain eligible retirees and beneficiaries of the
9 Teachers' Retirement System effective October 1, 2026.
10
11
12 A BILL
13 TO BE ENTITLED
14 AN ACT
15
16 Relating to the Employees' Retirement System and the
17 Teachers' Retirement System; to provide for a benefit increase
18 for certain state and local retirees and beneficiaries of the
19 Employees' Retirement System and certain eligible retirees and
20 beneficiaries of the Teachers' Retirement System effective
21 October 1, 2026, subject to the annualized benefit increase
22 procedure for retirees and beneficiaries under the Employees'
23 Retirement System and the Teachers' Retirement System,
24 respectively.
