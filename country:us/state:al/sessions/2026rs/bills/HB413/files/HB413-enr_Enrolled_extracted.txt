HB413 Enrolled
2
3 Relating to Tuscaloosa County; to provide for annual
4 longevity pay to the sheriff, tax assessor, and tax collector
5 as provided to all active full-time regular employees of
6 Tuscaloosa County; and to require the Tuscaloosa County
7 Commission to provide for the amount and date of longevity
8 payments.
9 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
10 Section 1. (a) Beginning June 1, 2026, the Tuscaloosa
11 County Sheriff shall be entitled to receive the same longevity
12 pay as provided to all active full-time regular employees in
13 the service of Tuscaloosa County.
14 (b) The longevity pay available to the sheriff shall be
15 paid from the county general fund in the same manner as all
16 active full-time regular employees of Tuscaloosa County.
17 (c) The Tuscaloosa County Commission shall provide for:
18 (1) The amount of longevity pay that the sheriff
19 receives; and
20 (2) The date on which the longevity payment will be
21 made to the sheriff.
22 Section 2. (a) Beginning June 1, 2026, the Tuscaloosa
23 County Tax Assessor and the Tuscaloosa County Tax Collector
24 shall be entitled to receive the same longevity pay as
25 provided to all active full-time regular employees in the
26 service of Tuscaloosa County.
27 (b) The longevity pay available to the tax assessor and
28 tax collector shall be paid from the county general fund in

34 (2) The date on which the longevity payment will be
