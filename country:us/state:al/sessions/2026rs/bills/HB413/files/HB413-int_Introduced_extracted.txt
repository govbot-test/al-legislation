5 TO BE ENTITLED
6 AN ACT
7
8
9 Relating to Tuscaloosa County; to provide for annual
10 longevity pay to the sheriff, tax assessor, and tax collector
11 as provided to all active full-time regular employees of
12 Tuscaloosa County; and to require the Tuscaloosa County
13 Commission to provide for the amount and date of longevity
14 payments.
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
16 Section 1. (a) Beginning June 1, 2026, the Tuscaloosa
17 County Sheriff shall be entitled to receive the same longevity
18 pay as provided to all active full-time regular employees in
19 the service of Tuscaloosa County.
20 (b) The longevity pay available to the sheriff shall be
21 paid from the county general fund in the same manner as all
22 active full-time regular employees of Tuscaloosa County.
23 (c) The Tuscaloosa County Commission shall provide for:
24 (1) The amount of longevity pay that the sheriff
25 receives; and
26 (2) The date on which the longevity payment will be
27 made to the sheriff.
