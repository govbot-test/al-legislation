4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, certain items are deductible
6 from taxable income for Alabama individual income
7 taxes. There is no current deduction allowed for
8 qualified overtime compensation.
9 This bill would establish an Alabama individual
10 income tax deduction for qualified overtime
11 compensation equal to the amount of qualified overtime
12 compensation received by a taxpayer during the taxable
13 year, not to exceed one thousand dollars ($1,000) per
14 taxpayer, effective for tax years beginning on or after
15 January 1, 2025 through December 31, 2027.
16 This bill would also make nonsubstantive,
17 technical revisions to update the existing code
18 language to current style.
