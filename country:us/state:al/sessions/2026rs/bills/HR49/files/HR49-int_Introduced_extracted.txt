4 HR____ MOURNING THE DEATH AND CELEBRATING THE LIFE OF FORMER
5 EDUCATOR JOHN BRADFORD.
6
7 WHEREAS, it is with profound sorrow that we note the
8 passing of John Bradford in Montgomery, Alabama, on January
9 15, 2026, while we also celebrate the ways he spent his life
10 serving others; and
11 WHEREAS, John Bradford was a man whose life was
12 defined by faith, service, curiosity, and love, leaving a
13 lasting impact on his family, church, students, and
14 community; and
15 WHEREAS, he began his spiritual journey at Morning
16 Pilgrim Missionary Baptist Church; he later became a
17 faithful and devoted member of Stonetank Antioch Baptist
18 Church on Old Selma Road, where he served with dedication
19 and was ordained as a Deacon on December 10, 2000; and
20 WHEREAS, John was among the first students to
21 integrate Sidney Lanier High School, graduating in 1970 and
22 quietly making history through his dignity, intelligence,
23 and determination; and
24 WHEREAS, he dedicated his professional life to
25 education and service, beginning his career at LaGrange Boys
26 Junior High School, where he served as an administrative
27 assistant, team leader, and teacher from 1974 until 1990;
