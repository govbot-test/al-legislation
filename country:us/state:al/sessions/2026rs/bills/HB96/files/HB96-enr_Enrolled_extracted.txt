3 Relating to homestead exemptions; to amend Sections
4 6-10-1 and 6-10-2, Code of Alabama 1975; to increase the
5 homestead exemption to fifty-six thousand four hundred dollars
6 ($56,400) for residents over 62 years of age and individuals
7 with disabilities; to require the exemption amounts in
8 bankruptcy cases be determined by the exemption in effect on
9 the petition date; to revise Sections 6-10-38 and 6-10-40,
10 Code of Alabama 1975, to make conforming changes; and to make
11 nonsubstantive, technical revisions to update the existing
12 code language to current style.
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
14 Section 1. Sections 6-10-1 and 6-10-2, Code of Alabama
15 1975 are amended to read as follows:
16 "§6-10-1
17 (a) Except as provided in subsection (b),The the right
18 of to a homestead or other exemption shall be governed by the
19 law in force effect when the debt or demand was created, but
20 the mode or remedy procedures or remedies for asserting,
21 ascertaining, contesting, and determining claims thereto shall
22 be as prescribed in this chapter.
23 (b) For any case filed under the United States
24 Bankruptcy Code, Title 11 U.S.C., the amount of the debtor's
25 homestead or other exemption, as provided by this chapter,
26 shall be governed by the law in effect as of the date the
27 bankruptcy petition is filed."
