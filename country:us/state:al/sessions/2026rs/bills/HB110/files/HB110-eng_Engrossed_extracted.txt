5 A BILL
6 TO BE ENTITLED
7 AN ACT
8
9 Relating to driver licenses; to authorize the Alabama
10 State Law Enforcement Agency to issue digital driver licenses
11 and nondriver identification cards.
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
13 Section 1. (a)(1) Pursuant to the procedures for
14 issuance and renewal of driver licenses and nondriver
15 identification cards established in Chapter 6 of Title 32,
16 Code of Alabama 1975, the Alabama State Law Enforcement Agency
17 (ALEA), contingent upon the availability of funds, shall issue
18 a digital driver license or nondriver identification card for
19 an additional fee of fifteen dollars ($15), which may be
20 displayed through an ALEA-approved mobile electronic method.
21 (2) The digital driver license or nondriver
22 identification card may be issued in addition to a physical
23 driver license or nondriver identification card.
24 (b) The digital driver license or nondriver
25 identification card shall contain all information present on a
26 physical driver license or nondriver identification card,
27 including a bar code or other mechanism, as utilized to reveal
28 the information.

29 (c) A licensee may produce a valid ALEA-approved
30 digital driver license in lieu of possessing a physical driver
31 license when operating a motor vehicle or vessel pursuant to
32 Section 32-6-9, Code of Alabama 1975.
33 (d)(1) Upon demand of a judge of any court in this
34 state, a peace officer, a state trooper, or any other state or
35 local governmental entity, a licensee may produce an
36 ALEA-approved digital driver license or nondriver
37 identification card, and it shall be recognized as though it
38 were a physical driver license or nondriver identification
39 card.
40 (2) A licensee may not produce an approved digital
41 driver license or nondriver identification card in place of a
42 physical driver license or nondriver identification card as a
43 valid form of voter photo identification pursuant to Section
44 17-9-30, Code of Alabama 1975.
45 (e) If the licensee displays the digital driver license
46 or nondriver identification card through the ALEA-approved
47 mobile method, the individual is not implicitly consenting to
48 allow access to or to search other contents of the wireless
49 communication or electronic device other than provided for by
50 law.
51 (f) ALEA shall apply reasonable standards of security
52 to protect the integrity of the digital driver license or
53 nondriver identification card and privacy of the licensee.
54 (g) ALEA may adopt rules to implement this section.
55 Section 2. This act shall become effective on October
56 1, 2026.
