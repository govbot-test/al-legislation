4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, residents of the state may
6 purchase a physical driver license or nondriver
7 identification card from the Alabama State Law
8 Enforcement Agency.
9 This bill would require the Alabama State Law
10 Enforcement Agency to offer residents the option to
11 obtain a digital driver license or nondriver
12 identification card in tandem with a physical driver
13 license or nondriver identification card.
