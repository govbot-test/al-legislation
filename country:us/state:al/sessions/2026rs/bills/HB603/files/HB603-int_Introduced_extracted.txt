4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, educator preparation
6 providers (EPPs) and Alternative Teacher Preparation
7 Organizations (ATPOs) are state-approved entities that
8 are responsible for preparing educators for
9 certification.
10 This bill would require the State Department of
11 Education, in collaboration with the State Board of
12 Education and the Alabama Commission on Higher
13 Education, to revise and annually update a teacher
14 preparation report card evaluating the performance of
15 each EPP and ATPO.
16 This bill would require the department to
17 prominently publish the teacher preparation report
18 cards on the department's official website.
19 This bill would require EPPs, ATPOs, and other
20 stakeholders to annually provide the department with
21 data for the report cards.
22 This bill would require the department to create
23 a standard survey for program completers and the
24 employers of program completers to assess satisfaction
25 with teacher preparation.
26 This bill would require the board to annually
27 review the report cards to determine if interventions
28 or support is needed for each EPP or ATPO.
