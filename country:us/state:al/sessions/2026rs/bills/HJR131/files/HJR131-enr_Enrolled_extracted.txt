3 COMMEMORATING THE 100TH ANNIVERSARY OF THE ALPHA-DELTA
4 CHAPTER OF SIGMA PI FRATERNITY AT AUBURN UNIVERSITY.
5
6 WHEREAS, the 100th anniversary of the founding of the
7 Alpha-Delta Chapter of the Sigma Pi Fraternity at Auburn
8 University, then Alabama Polytechnic Institute, on February
9 26, 1926, is commemorated and recorded with pride and
10 pleasure; the chapter's founding date echoes the
11 fraternity's inception on February 26, 1897, at Vincennes
12 University; and
13 WHEREAS, the Alabama Polytechnic Institute charter
14 was organized by Alpha-Delta Chapter founders Joe Martin
15 Spencer, Joe William Stone, James B. Leslie, Jr., James W.
16 Baxter, Herbert G. Spencer, Coleman D. McLeod, Clyde L.
17 Brown, Stewart D. McKnight, James M. Jenkins, Wieland B.
18 LaNicca, and Benjamin P. Robinson; and
19 WHEREAS, Sigma Pi has exhorted a positive educational
20 and cultural influence on both the State of Alabama and the
21 national community throughout its 100-year history; during
22 this pivotal century, the chapter has initiated more than
23 2,000 members who have furthered the fraternity's mission,
24 "to inspire, promote, and support the lifelong development
25 of our brothers," and core values, "promote fellowship,
26 develop character and leadership, advance heightened moral
27 awareness, enable academic achievement, and inspire
28 service"; and

29 WHEREAS, the Alpha-Delta Chapter ranks among the top
30 fraternities at Auburn University and boasts among its
31 alumni doctors, lawyers, architects, engineers, educators,
32 researchers, college professors and deans, artists and
33 musicians, and members of the military; just a few among
34 many notable alumni are Jay Jacobs, former Auburn University
35 Athletics Director; James R. Bullington, former United
36 States Ambassador to Burundi and Peace Corps Director,
37 Country of Niger; Dr. Wilbur Davenport, researcher at the
38 Research Laboratory of Electronics and the Lincoln Lab and
39 M.I.T professor; and James E. Livingston, retired Major
40 General in the U.S. Marine Corps and Medal of Honor
41 recipient; now therefore,
42 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA, BOTH
43 HOUSES THEREOF CONCURRING, That the 100th anniversary of the
44 founding of the Alpha-Delta Chapter of Sigma Pi Fraternity
45 at Auburn University is honored and celebrated, and this
46 resolution is offered as evidence of our high regard for the
47 positive impact this chapter has had on the cultural
48 landscape of our state.
