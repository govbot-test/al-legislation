5 TO BE ENTITLED
6 AN ACT
7
8
9 Relating to Montgomery County; to authorize the
10 governing body of the City of Montgomery to impose a municipal
11 occupational tax.
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
13 Section 1. The Legislature hereby finds and declares
14 that it is necessary to provide additional funding to
15 stabilize the health care industry, develop transportation
16 infrastructure, and bolster public safety in the City of
17 Montgomery.
18 Section 2. The governing body of the City of
19 Montgomery, by ordinance or resolution, may levy and impose a
20 municipal license tax or fee upon any individual who engages
21 in or follows any trade, occupation, or profession in the
22 municipality.
23 Section 3. This act shall become effective on June 1,
24 2026.
