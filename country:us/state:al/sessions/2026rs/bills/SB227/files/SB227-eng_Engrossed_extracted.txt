6 TO BE ENTITLED
7 AN ACT
8
9 To establish the Office of Occupational and
10 Professional Licensing within the Alabama Department of
11 Workforce; to add Chapter 2A to Title 25, Code of Alabama
12 1975; to provide for the leadership, support, and oversight of
13 certain occupational and professional licensing boards; to
14 provide for an executive director, deputy directors, and the
15 employment of staff for the boards; to provide uniform
16 standards for fees; to continue existing licenses and rules
17 until amended or repealed by the office; and to amend Section
18 41-20-5, Code of Alabama 1975, to provide for the transfer of
19 an agency to the office by the Sunset Committee pursuant to a
20 recommendation for modification.
21 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
22 Section 1. Chapter 2A is added to Title 25 of the Code
23 of Alabama 1975, to read as follows:
24 CHAPTER 2A. OFFICE OF OCCUPATIONAL AND PROFESSIONAL
25 LICENSING.

34 Workforce.

36 Secretary of Workforce to serve as the executive director of
37 the office.
38 (3) FUND. The Occupational and Professional Licensing

40 (4) LICENSE. The certificate or license issued to an
41 individual that certifies he or she is qualified to perform a
42 particular occupation or profession. The term includes a
43 certificate of registration, temporary license, or similar
44 formal grant of permission.
45 (5) OFFICE. The Office of Occupational and Professional
46 Licensing within the Alabama Department of Workforce,
47 responsible for the oversight and administration of certain
48 occupational and professional licensing boards.

52 Licensing. The mission of the office is to protect the health,
53 safety, and welfare of the public by licensing qualified

55 professional conduct for professions and occupations.
