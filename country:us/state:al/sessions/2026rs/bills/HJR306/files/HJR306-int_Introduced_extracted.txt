4 HJR___ COMMENDING REPRESENTATIVE BRETT EASTERBROOK FOR
5 DISTINGUISHED LEGISLATIVE SERVICE.
6
7 WHEREAS, it is with highest admiration and deepest
8 respect that we commend Brett Easterbrook, currently serving
9 as a Republican member of the Alabama House of
10 Representatives, representing District 65, for his many
11 accomplishments and dedicated service to the people of the
12 State of Alabama; and
13 WHEREAS, since his election in 2018, Representative
14 Easterbrook has been a steadfast advocate for his
15 constituents representing the people of Baldwin, Choctaw,
16 Clarke, and Washington counties through his diligent work;
17 and
18 WHEREAS, Representative Easterbrook was born in
19 Casper, Wyoming; he earned a Bachelor of Science degree in
20 Accounting from Auburn University in 1989; and
21 WHEREAS, before entering politics, he worked as an
22 accountant for Mauldin & Jenkins from 1989 to 1991; he then
23 worked as an Accounting Manager at Evonik Degussa from 1990
24 to 2000, followed by a position as Assistant Controller at
25 the same company from 2002 to 2006; in 2009, he became
26 Director of Finance and Controlling in North and South
27 America for Outokumpu Stainless Steel; he also worked as a
28 contractor for Easterbrook Homes from 2005 to 2009; and
