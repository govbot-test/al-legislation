5 FOUNDATION'S 20TH ANNUAL "HONORING OUR ELDERS DAY" GALA.
6
7 WHEREAS, with great joy and special pleasure, we
8 honor Ms. Lynne Berry as the special guest at the Rosetta
9 James Foundation's 20th Annual "Honoring Our Elders Day"
10 Gala at the Jackson Center in Huntsville, Alabama, on
11 Saturday, March 14, 2026; and
12 WHEREAS, Ms. Berry, a graduate of Vanderbilt
13 University, is a distinguished nonprofit and community
14 leader whose career spans more than 40 years of service
15 across North Alabama; and
16 WHEREAS, Ms. Berry's remarkable career includes
17 service with The Nature Conservancy, Historic Huntsville
18 Foundation, and HudsonAlpha Institute for Biotechnology,
19 where she led transformational fundraising efforts,
20 increased revenue by 400 percent, and expanded the donor
21 base significantly; since 2019, she has served at the
22 Huntsville Hospital Foundation, directing its planned giving
23 program and securing long-term support through estate gifts
24 and charitable instruments; as Founding Executive Director
25 of The Community Foundation of Huntsville/Madison County,
26 she built the organization from inception, growing assets to
27 $9.9 million and distributing $2.2 million in grants; and
28 WHEREAS, Ms. Berry is active on numerous civic

30 Marshall Space Flight Center, Senator Jeff Sessions,

33 received include, but are in no way limited to, the 2024
34 Martin Luther King Unity Award, the Wayne Greenhaw Award

39 is to honor the wisdom, service, and dedication of our

43 their lives to uplifting others and fostering positive

46 us all; and

52 for deserving students who embody the principles of
53 volunteerism, academic excellence, and civic engagement;
54 these outstanding students will also be recognized during
55 the event, connecting their bright futures to the enduring
56 legacies of those who came before them; and
