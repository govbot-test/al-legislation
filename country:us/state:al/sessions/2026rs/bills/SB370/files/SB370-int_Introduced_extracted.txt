4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, a Major 21st Century
6 Manufacturing Zone suitable for the site of certain
7 manufacturing facilities may be established within a
8 tax increment district, and must comprise at least 50
9 percent of the tax increment district within which such
10 zone is located.
11 This bill would allow a Major 21st Century
12 Manufacturing Zone to be located within a tax increment
13 district without regard to the size of such tax
14 increment district, and allow ad valorem tax revenues
15 collected within such tax increment district to be used
16 to reimburse costs incurred by a public entity to
17 acquire land within the Major 21st Century
18 Manufacturing Zone prior to the creation of such tax
19 increment district.
