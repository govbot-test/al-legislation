4 HR____ COMMENDING THE TUSCALOOSA COUNTY HIGH SCHOOL BOYS
5 BASKETBALL TEAM ON WINNING THE 2026 ALABAMA HIGH SCHOOL
6 ATHLETIC ASSOCIATION 7A STATE CHAMPIONSHIP.
7
8 WHEREAS, the Alabama Legislature takes great pride in
9 recognizing outstanding accomplishments by our state's
10 students and athletes; and
11 WHEREAS, the Tuscaloosa County High School Wildcats
12 achieved remarkable success by winning the 2026 Alabama High
13 School Athletic Association (AHSAA) 7A Boys Basketball State
14 Championship on March 7, 2026; the Wildcats conquered the
15 defending champion Hoover Buccaneers 65-54 at Legacy Arena
16 in Birmingham to claim the state title, a significant
17 milestone for the team that captured its first boys'
18 basketball state title since 1960; and
19 WHEREAS, Tuscaloosa County seniors Ford Traweek, who
20 was the tournament MVP, and Tyler Noble led the way for the
21 Wildcats; Ford finished 7-for-15 from the field with 22
22 points, and Tyler went 5-for-11 from the field with 18
23 points; and
24 WHEREAS, the successful team is comprised of Zi
25 Davis, Ford Traweek, Chardarius Hutchins, Kaleb Tillman,
26 Donnavin Turner, Bralen Dove, Kaleb Little, Tyler Noble,
27 Axavian Edwards, Christopher Tunnell, Aiden Edwards, Clark
28 Burrage, and Andarius McClain; and
Page 1
Burrage, and Andarius McClain; and
HR249 INTRODUCED
29 WHEREAS, expert coaching and leadership are provided
30 by Head Coach Curt Weeks and Assistant Coaches Jacob Haas,
31 Aaron Done, Caleb Enfinger, Dylan Robertson, Barry Williams,
32 Nick Gould, and Izaak Parker; Coach Weeks credits the team's
33 championship performance to the "Wildcat Way," the program's
34 core values of maximum effort, complete confidence,
35 composure, and being a great teammate; and
36 WHEREAS, the achievements of the Tuscaloosa County
37 Boys Basketball Team bring great pride to Tuscaloosa County
38 High School, the City of Northport, and the entirety of
39 Tuscaloosa County; now therefore,
40 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE
41 LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA, That the Tuscaloosa County High
42 School Boys Basketball team members and coaches are hereby
43 commended and congratulated on their outstanding victory in
44 the 2026 AHSAA 7A Boys Basketball State Championship, and we
45 offer this resolution in tribute to a remarkable effort and
46 successful finish.
