4 SYNOPSIS:
5 This bill would create the Rural Allied Health
6 Professional Workforce Expansion Act to address
7 shortages in allied health professions across rural
8 communities by expanding training capacity, funding
9 tuition support, and incentivizing rural placements
10 through structured education-to-employment pipelines.
