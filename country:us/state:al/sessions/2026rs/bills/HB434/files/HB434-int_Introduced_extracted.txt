4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, the proceeds from the
6 simplified sellers use tax (SSUT) are distributed to
7 the State General Fund, Education Trust Fund, and the
8 counties and municipalities in this state.
9 This bill would reduce the portion allocated to
10 the Education Trust Fund and reallocate these SSUT
11 proceeds to the local boards of education.
