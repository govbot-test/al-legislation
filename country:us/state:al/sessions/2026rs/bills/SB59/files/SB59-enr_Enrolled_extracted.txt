4 Regarding higher education; to amend Section 41-4-97,
5 Code of Alabama 1975, to remove the Alabama Community College
6 System from federal fund reporting requirements; to make
7 nonsubstantive, technical revisions to update existing code
8 language to current style; and to add a new Section 41-4-98 to
9 the Code of Alabama 1975, to require each public institution
10 of higher education in the state to submit an annual report
11 regarding state and federal fund receipts and expenditures,
12 including plans for operating the institution if state or
13 federal funding is reduced.
