9 Regarding higher education; to amend Section 41-4-97,
10 Code of Alabama 1975, to remove the Alabama Community College
11 System from federal fund reporting requirements; to make
12 nonsubstantive, technical revisions to update existing code
13 language to current style; and to add a new Section 41-4-98 to
14 the Code of Alabama 1975, to require each public institution
15 of higher education in the state to submit an annual report
16 regarding state and federal fund receipts and expenditures,
17 including plans for operating the institution if state or
18 federal funding is reduced.

89 Section 2. A new Section 41-4-98 is added to the Code
90 of Alabama 1975, as follows:
91 §41-4-98
92 (a) For the purposes of this section, the following
93 terms have the following meanings:
94 (1) FEDERAL RECEIPTS. Federal financial assistance
95 received or administered from federal entities in the form of
96 grants, loans, loan guarantees, property, cooperative
97 agreements, interest subsidies, insurance, food commodities,
98 direct appropriations, other assistance, and amounts received
99 as reimbursement for services rendered to individuals, that is
100 reported as part of a single audit.
101 (2) PUBLIC INSTITUTIONS OF HIGHER EDUCATION. Those
102 public educational institutions in Alabama which have been
103 authorized by the Legislature or by the constitution to
104 provide formal education, including vocational, technical,
105 collegiate, professional, or any other form of education,
106 above the secondary school level.
107 (3) STATE RECEIPTS. State financial assistance received
108 from direct appropriations, or other assistance, from the
109 Education Trust Fund, the Education Trust Fund Advancement and
110 Technology Fund, Educational Opportunities Reserve Fund, or
111 from other state sources. The term does not include tuition
112 revenues or other self-generated funds.
