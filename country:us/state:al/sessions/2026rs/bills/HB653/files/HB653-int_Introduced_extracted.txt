4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, human trafficking in the
6 first degree is punishable as a Class A felony. If the
7 offense involves a minor trafficking victim, the
8 defendant is required to be sentenced to a minimum of
9 life imprisonment.
10 This bill would further provide for the criminal
11 penalty by providing that if the victim was subjected
12 to sexual servitude, the defendant must be sentenced to
13 life imprisonment.
