5 BILL PARTRIDGE FOR HIS PROFESSIONAL ACHIEVEMENTS ON THE
6 OCCASION OF HIS RETIREMENT.
7
8 WHEREAS, with great pride and special pleasure, the
9 highest commendations and congratulations are conveyed to
10 the Oxford, Alabama, Chief of Police and Public Safety
11 Director, Bill Partridge, for his many professional
12 achievements and upon the occasion of his retirement after
13 39 years of distinguished service in law enforcement; and
14 WHEREAS, Chief Partridge began his career with the
15 City of Oxford in 1987 and commenced his service as a police
16 officer in 1989; from 2001 to 2007, he served as the Calhoun
17 County Coroner; and, in 2007, he was selected as Chief of
18 Police for the City of Oxford; and
19 WHEREAS, during his tenure as Chief, he exemplified
20 the values of hard work, compassion, and steadfast devotion
21 to the community, which are the hallmarks of a dedicated
22 officer of the law; and
23 WHEREAS, Chief Partridge is credited with modernizing
24 the department and enhancing regional safety through
25 numerous initiatives, including overseeing the addition of
26 advanced crime-fighting technologies; establishing the East
27 Metro Area Crime Center; and leading the department in
28 launching its own aviation unit to assist in local and

30 WHEREAS, throughout his distinguished career, he has
31 earned the respect and admiration of all who have had the
32 privilege to work with him due to his outstanding
33 capability, integrity, and tireless dedication; his many
34 professional contributions include serving as President of
35 the Alabama Association of Chiefs of Police (AACOP) for two
36 terms; Chair of the AACOP Legislative Committee; Vice Chair
37 of the Alabama Peace Officers' Standards and Training
38 Commission; Vice President of the Alabama Chapter of the FBI
39 National Academy Associates; Advisory Board Chair for the
40 Center of Best Practices in Law Enforcement at Jacksonville
41 State University; and as a nationwide lecturer; and
42 WHEREAS, Chief Partridge attributes much of his
43 success to strong mentorship early in his career and to
44 continued training opportunities that helped shape both his

46 he is a 2006 graduate of the prestigious FBI National

53 integrity, earning the enduring respect and admiration of
