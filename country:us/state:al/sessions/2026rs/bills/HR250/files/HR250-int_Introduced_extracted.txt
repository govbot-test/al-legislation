5 OCCASION OF HER 100TH BIRTHDAY.
6
7 WHEREAS, highest commendations and congratulations
8 are offered to Mrs. Evie Mae Watson of Brewton, Alabama, on
9 the momentous occasion of her 100th Birthday; and
10 WHEREAS, Evie was born on March 7, 1926, to John Joe
11 Goneke and Evie Middleton Goneke; she attended school at
12 Center New Grove, Gum Tree, Appleton, and McCall; and
13 WHEREAS, she married Berk Watson on October 31, 1941;
14 she was a supportive wife of her husband, who served as a
15 cannoneer in WWII and was active in the Normandy Campaign in
16 June 1944; she and Berk were married 56 years before he
17 passed away in 1997; Evie enjoyed being a full-time
18 homemaker and caring for their children and many
19 grandchildren, nieces, and nephews throughout the years; and
20 WHEREAS, Evie briefly worked at Hutto's Clothing
21 Store and later at Tot and Teen Clothing Store, owned by her
22 dear friend Doris Pace; although she never had a driver's
23 license or credit card, she has lived a blessed life and has
24 lived in the same home for the last 70 years; and
25 WHEREAS, she and Berk were blessed with four
26 children, Dorothy Kingsland (Harvey), Nellie McNesby
27 (Ronnie), Danny Watson (Nancy), and Terri Rowell (Jack);
28 nine grandchildren, Keith Kingsland, Kelley Moye (Hal), Kam

29 Kingsland, Ron McNesby (Tammy), Misti Colabianchi (Santino),
30 Mandy Kelley (Matt), Jessica Bonner (Christian), Bart Rowell

35 WHEREAS, over the years, Mama Mae has been actively
36 involved at First Assembly of God Church in East Brewton,
37 where she served as a choir member; children's Sunday School
38 teacher with her dear friend, Obera Forsyth; and served on
39 the 55-plus ministry team and as a church greeter for many
40 years; she recently celebrated her birthday at the church
41 fellowship hall; and
42 WHEREAS, Mama Mae has always been passionate about
43 cooking for her family and neighbors; her biscuits, chicken
44 and dressing, pies, and cakes are legendary; her summers
45 have been filled with canning and preserving vegetables,
46 making dewberry jelly, and ensuring the freezer is stocked;
47 and
48 WHEREAS, a 100th Birthday is a significant milestone
49 that represents 10 decades of wisdom earned and memories
50 gained, and it is cause for great rejoicing; now therefore,
51 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE
52 LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA, That Mrs. Evie Mae Watson is
53 deserving of special honor and recognition, and we are
54 pleased to offer this resolution to her as evidence of our
55 high regard for her many life accomplishments, along with
56 sincere wishes for more happy and healthy times to come.
