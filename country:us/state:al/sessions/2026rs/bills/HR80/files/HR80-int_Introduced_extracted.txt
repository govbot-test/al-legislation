4 HR____ MOURNING THE DEATH OF SAMUEL RALPH GARRETT, JR.
5
6 WHEREAS, it is with profound sadness that we note the
7 death of Samuel Ralph Garrett, Jr. of Thomasville, Alabama,
8 on January 14, 2026, at the age of 75; and
9 WHEREAS, Samuel was born on May 6, 1950, to Samuel
10 Ralph Garrett, Sr. and Amanda Johnson Garrett; and
11 WHEREAS, he confessed his hope in Christ at an early
12 age and was a member of various churches over the years, and
13 subsequently joined the St. Mark Missionary Baptist Church
14 in Montgomery, Alabama; and
15 WHEREAS, Samuel was the first Black male student to
16 attend Thomasville High School and was a high achiever, an
17 honor roll student, played clarinet in the band, played
18 football, and graduated high school ahead of his time; and
19 WHEREAS, he attended undergraduate school at Southern
20 University, and later transferred to The University of
21 Alabama (UA) to complete his Bachelor's Degree; while at UA,
22 he pledged Omega Psi Phi Fraternity, Incorporated; and
23 WHEREAS, after his undergraduate studies, he moved to
24 Buffalo, New York, where he attended the University of
25 Buffalo and completed his degree in police science studies;
26 he later earned a Master of Science Degree and concluded his
27 studies at the Institute of Investigative Sciences; he was
28 later employed by Pohlman Foundry; this began a long and
