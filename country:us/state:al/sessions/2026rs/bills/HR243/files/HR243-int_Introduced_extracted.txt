6
7 WHEREAS, the Legislature of Alabama takes great pride
8 in recognizing Sheyann Webb-Christburg, the founder and
9 Executive Director of the Young Ladies of Class (YLOC)
10 Mentorship and Debutante Program; her life's work and
11 dedication as a youth advocate have inspired and impacted
12 generations of young people throughout the state and abroad;
13 and
14 WHEREAS, through her 45-year journey as a youth
15 advocate and humanitarian, she founded YLOC in January 2024
16 with a vision to provide young girls with transformative
17 experiences that foster personal growth, career growth,
18 leadership development, civic engagement, community
19 services, and etiquette training; and

23 class, poise, grace, sophistication, and purpose; and
24 WHEREAS, YLOC's focus is to equip young girls with
