4 HJR___ COMMENDING BARBARA BOYD FOR HER SERVICE TO THE STATE
5 OF ALABAMA.
6
7 WHEREAS, we note with utmost admiration and
8 appreciation the distinguished service and tenure of
9 Representative Barbara Boyd to the Alabama House of
10 Representatives; and
11 WHEREAS, Representative Barbara Boyd has faithfully
12 served the residents of Alabama House District 32,
13 consisting of Calhoun and Talladega counties, since her
14 election in 1994, becoming one of the longest-serving
15 members of the Alabama House of Representatives; and
16 WHEREAS, born on January 31, 1937, in Anniston,
17 Alabama, Representative Boyd devoted more than 45 years to
18 the field of education before entering public office,
19 earning a Bachelor of Arts degree in French and English from
20 Miles College and both a Master's degree and Doctorate in
21 Education from The University of Alabama; and
22 WHEREAS, throughout her tenure in the Legislature,
23 Representative Boyd has been a strong and consistent
24 advocate for education, children, seniors, and local
25 communities, and she has provided thoughtful leadership
26 through her service on several important committees,
27 including the Children and Senior Advocacy Committee, the
28 County and Municipal Government Committee, the Rules
