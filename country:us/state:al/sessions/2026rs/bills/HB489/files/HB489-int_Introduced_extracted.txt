4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, the Alabama Student Grant
6 Program, administered by the Alabama Commission on
7 Higher Education, awards grants to students to attend
8 certain approved independent Alabama institutions of
9 higher education.
10 This bill would add an independent postsecondary
11 online institution located in Orange Beach, Alabama, to
12 the list of institutions approved to participate in the
13 program.
14 This bill would also make nonsubstantive,
15 technical revisions to update the existing code
16 language to current style.
