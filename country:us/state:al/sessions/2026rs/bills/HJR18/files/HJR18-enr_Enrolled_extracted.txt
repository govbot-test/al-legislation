7 WHEREAS, it is fitting and proper that the
8 Legislature of Alabama commend those estimable organizations
9 that diligently strive to promote patriotism, preserve
10 American history, and support excellence in education for
11 our Nation's children; and
12 WHEREAS, among such distinguished service
13 organizations working within local communities throughout
14 this state are the Alabama Society, Daughters of the
15 American Revolution (ASDAR), and the Alabama Society Sons of
16 the American Revolution (ALSSAR); and
17 WHEREAS, the ASDAR was established in 1894 and today
18 consists of more than 4,000 members serving in 71 chapters
19 across the State of Alabama; it is affiliated with the
20 National Society of Daughters of the American Revolution
21 (DAR), a nonprofit, nonpolitical women's service
22 organization comprised of more than 185,000 members
23 nationwide; both the state and national societies are
24 steadfastly committed to the enduring mission of historic
25 preservation, education, and patriotism; and
26 WHEREAS, members of the ASDAR generously volunteer
27 their time and talents in numerous laudable community
28 activities aligned with this mission, including erecting
