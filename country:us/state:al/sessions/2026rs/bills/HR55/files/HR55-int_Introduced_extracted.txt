4 HR____ MOURNING THE DEATH AND CELEBRATING THE LIFE OF BRAZIL
5 SIMON, JR.
6
7 WHEREAS, it is with profound sorrow that we note the
8 passing of Brazil Simon, Jr. of Mobile, Alabama, on January
9 10, 2026, while we also celebrate his long, fulfilling life
10 that ended at the age of 82; and
11 WHEREAS, his departure leaves a void in the lives of
12 his loving family, many friends, and the community where he
13 was highly regarded; and
14 WHEREAS, Brazil "B.S." Simon, Jr. was born on January
15 25, 1943, to Viola and Brazil Simon, Sr.; he accepted Christ
16 at a young age and was a devoted member of Most Pure Heart
17 of Mary Catholic Church; B.S. was a dedicated Catholic who
18 often walked downtown to the cathedral to pray; and
19 WHEREAS, B.S. has always been a hard worker; growing
20 up, his father had so many animals in their yard, that he
21 was known for having the "neighborhood zoo"; it was the
22 responsibility of B.S. to feed the animals daily; and
23 WHEREAS, Brazil was also a well-known entrepreneur
24 and master shoe shiner; he was a sharp dresser and loved
25 fashion; he shined shoes at Abrams Barber Shop for many
26 years, and after the closing of Abrams, he continued shining
27 shoes at his house; B.S. was also a waiter at various
28 restaurants and clubs; he earned a reputation for being one
Page 1
restaurants and clubs; he earned a reputation for being one
HR55 INTRODUCED
29 of the best at both jobs; and
30 WHEREAS, B.S. was preceded in death by his parents
31 and sister, Carol Simon Bradberry; he leaves to cherish his
32 memory his significant other, Barbara Williams; daughters,
33 Pamela Seager and Shannon Bettis; son, Brazil Simon III;
34 loving grandchildren, Antonio Simon and Ayana Simon;
35 stepchildren, Willie Lee Glover, Gloria Glover, Maxine
36 Wilson (Herman), Janie Glover, and Dominick Glover; devoted
37 friend, Jacob Wilson, Sr.; many dedicated church family
38 members, Ed Robinson, Saundra Hubbard Ellis, Josie Gibson,
39 Rosemary Robinson, James Harris, and Russell Williamson; and
40 a host of other relatives and friends; and
41 WHEREAS, B.S. Simon led a life in faith, kindness,
42 and generosity, while leaving a legacy that will be
43 cherished forever by all who knew and loved him; now
44 therefore,
45 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE
46 LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA, That the death of Brazil Simon, Jr.
47 is recorded with deep sadness, and even as we mourn his
48 death and the loss it represents, we also celebrate his
49 life, which will remain a lasting inspiration, carried
50 forward by the family and friends he cherished so dearly.
