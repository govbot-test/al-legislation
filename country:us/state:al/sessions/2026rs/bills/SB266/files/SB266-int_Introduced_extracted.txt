20 Section 1. The following amendment to the Constitution

22 PROPOSED AMENDMENT
23 In Covington County, no person may inject, store, or
24 sequester carbon dioxide in underground wells. This
25 prohibition includes the operation of a Class VI well as
26 defined by the United States Environmental Protection Agency
27 pursuant to the Underground Injection Control Program of the
28 Safe Drinking Water Act, 42 U.S.C. § 300f et seq.
