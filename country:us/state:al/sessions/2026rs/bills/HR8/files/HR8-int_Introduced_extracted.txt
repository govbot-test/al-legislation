5 JONES.
6
7 WHEREAS, it is with profound sorrow that we note the
8 passing of Mr. Donald Jones, while we also celebrate his
9 life as a devoted Christian; a beloved husband, father, and
10 grandfather; and a valuable member of his community; and
11 WHEREAS, the youngest of 10 children, Donald Jones
12 was born to Jesse Jones, Sr. and Vandella Jones; Donald
13 accepted Christ at an early age and became an active member
14 of First Missionary Baptist Church of Hueytown; and
15 WHEREAS, Donald was a 1968 graduate of Westfield High
16 School, where he enjoyed playing the drums in the marching
17 band; following graduation, he attended Bessemer Technical
18 College and later joined the United States Air Force; and
19 WHEREAS, after completing his military service, he
20 worked for 37 years at U.S. Steel Corporation in Fairfield,
21 until his retirement; Donald was also the owner and producer
22 of Jones Upholstery, where he became known for his excellent
23 work; and
24 WHEREAS, Donald married his lovely wife, Minnie
25 Worley, in 1976; they became proud parents and later enjoyed
26 life as grandparents; Donald remained dedicated to his faith
27 throughout his life, serving as a Sunday School teacher,
