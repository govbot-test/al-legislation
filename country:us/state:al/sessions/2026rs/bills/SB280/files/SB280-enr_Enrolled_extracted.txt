4 Relating to the Retirement Systems of Alabama; to add
5 Section 36-27-49.4 to the Code of Alabama 1975, to allow
6 participating members of the Teachers' Retirement System and
7 the Employees' Retirement System to purchase certain federal
8 service credit.
9 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
10 Section 1. Section 36-27-49.4 is added to the Code of
11 Alabama 1975, to read as follows:
12 §36-27-49.4
13 (a)(1) A member of the Teachers' Retirement System or a
14 member of the Employees' Retirement System may receive up to
15 four years of creditable service for federal nonmilitary
16 employment rendered for the United States, provided: (i) the
17 member of the retirement system claiming the credit shall have
18 attained not less than 10 years of contributing membership
19 service credit, exclusive of military service credit, under
20 the retirement system of which he or she is a member; and (ii)
21 the member performs and complies with the conditions
22 prescribed in subdivision (2).
23 (2) Before receiving credit pursuant to subdivision (1)
24 the member shall first:
25 a. Before the date of his or her retirement and for
26 each year of service credit, contribute to his or her
27 respective retirement system the full actuarially determined
28 cost for each year of claimed service as determined by the
