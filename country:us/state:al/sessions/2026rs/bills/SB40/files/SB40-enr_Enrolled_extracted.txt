4 Relating to the Contract Review Permanent Legislative
5 Oversight Committee; to amend Section 29-2-41, Code of Alabama
6 1975, to provide that the committee may recommend that the
7 Governor not sign a contract under review by the committee;
8 and to make nonsubstantive, technical revisions to update the
9 existing code language to current style.
10 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
11 Section 1. Section 29-2-41, Code of Alabama 1975, is
12 amended to read as follows:
13 "§29-2-41
14 (a) Each member of the committee shall be entitled to
15 regular legislative compensation, per diem, and travel
16 expenses for each day he or she attends a meeting of the
17 committee, which shall be paid out of the funds appropriated
18 to the use of the Legislature, on warrants drawn on the state
19 Comptroller upon requisition signed by the committee's chair.
20 Members shall not receive additional compensation or per diem
21 when the Legislature is in session. The Department of
22 Examiners of Public Accounts shall furnish assistance and any
23 relevant information to the committee.
24 (b)(1) The committee shall have the responsibility of
25 reviewing contracts for personal or professional services with
26 private entities or individuals to be paid out of appropriated
27 funds, federal or state, on a state warrant issued as
28 recompense for those services.

29 (2) Each state department entering into a contract to
30 be paid out of appropriated funds, federal or state, on a
31 state warrant which is notified by the committee, shall submit
32 to the committee any proposed contract for personal or
33 professional services. Each contract shall be accompanied by

35 (c) A department, in lieu of the proposed contract, may
36 submit to the committee a letter of intent to contract. A
37 letter of intent to contract shall indicate the contracting
38 parties, the services to be performed, an itemization of the
39 total cost estimate of the contract, and such other
40 information as the department may deem pertinent to the
41 committee review of the contract. If a department elects to
42 submit a letter of intent to contract in lieu of a proposed
43 contract, the department shall be required to submit to the
44 committee for its information the contract described in the
45 letter of intent upon the execution of the contract.
46 (d) The committee shall hold a hearing to review and
47 comment where necessary on any contract or letter of intent to
48 contract, including a recommendation that the Governor not
49 sign a contract, within a reasonable time not to exceed 45
50 days after a department has submitted the contract or letter
51 of intent to contract to the committee. If the committee fails
52 to hold a hearing to review a contract or letter of intent to
53 contract within the 45-day time period, the contract shall be
54 deemed to have been reviewed in compliance with this section.
