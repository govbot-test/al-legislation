4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, the Contract Review
6 Permanent Legislative Oversight Committee reviews and
7 comments on contracts entered into by state departments
8 for personal or professional services with private
9 entities or individuals to be paid out of appropriated
10 funds.
11 This bill would provide that the committee, when
12 reviewing a contract, may recommend that the Governor
13 not sign the contract.
14 This bill would also make nonsubstantive,
15 technical revisions to update the existing code
16 language to current style.
