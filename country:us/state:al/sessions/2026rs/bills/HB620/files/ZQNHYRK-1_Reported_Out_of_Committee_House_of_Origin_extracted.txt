6 TO BE ENTITLED
7 AN ACT
8
9 Relating to contractors; to authorize a swimming pool
10 contractor to request any inspection required by a local
11 permit for the construction, repair, or maintenance of a
12 swimming pool, under certain conditions.
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
14 Section 1. When an inspection is required by a local
15 governing entity as a condition of a permit for the
16 construction, repair, or maintenance of a residential or
17 commercial swimming pool, the contractor may request the local
18 governing entity to perform the inspection if the contractor
19 is licensed pursuant to Chapter 8 of Title 34, Code of Alabama
20 1975 and the construction, repair, or maintenance is performed
21 by the contractor, the contractor's employee, or a
22 subcontractor under the supervision of the contractor.
23 Section 2. This act shall become effective on October
24 1, 2026.
