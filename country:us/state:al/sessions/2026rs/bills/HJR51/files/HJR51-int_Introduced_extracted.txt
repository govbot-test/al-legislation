7 WHEREAS, artificial intelligence (AI), social media,
8 and the Internet are widely used by children with minimal
9 safeguards in place; and
10 WHEREAS, the rapid advancement of AI technology is
11 changing how children learn and develop socially; and
12 WHEREAS, AI continues to be deployed in new
13 capacities across social media and the Internet; and
14 WHEREAS, there are valid concerns about potential
15 misuse or unintended consequences of children's unfettered
16 access to AI, social media, and the Internet, especially
17 without the development and implementation of safeguards to
18 protect children; and
19 WHEREAS, in 2025, all 50 states, Puerto Rico, the
20 U.S. Virgin Islands, and Washington D.C. introduced AI
21 legislation, and 38 states adopted or enacted AI
22 legislation; and
23 WHEREAS, it is in the best interest of the State of
24 Alabama and its residents to examine how AI technologies,
25 social media, and the Internet can be made safer for
26 children; now therefore,
27 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA, BOTH
28 HOUSES THEREOF CONCURRING, That there is created the Study
