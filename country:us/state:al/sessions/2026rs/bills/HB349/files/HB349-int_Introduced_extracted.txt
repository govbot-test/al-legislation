4 SYNOPSIS:
5 This bill would propose an amendment to the
6 Constitution of Alabama of 2022, relating to Etowah
7 County, to create the Whorton Bend Agricultural
8 Protection Act.
9 This bill would propose to define the boundaries
10 of the protected Whorton Bend Community area, to
11 specify the permitted and prohibited activities within
12 the protected area, and to provide exemptions.
