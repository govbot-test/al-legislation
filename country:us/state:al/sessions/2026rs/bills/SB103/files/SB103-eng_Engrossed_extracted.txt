6 A BILL
7 TO BE ENTITLED
8 AN ACT
9
10 Relating to the Alabama Sunset Law; to continue the
11 existence and functioning of the Alabama Construction
12 Recruitment Institute until October 1, 2028, with certain
13 modifications; to require the Alabama Construction Recruitment
14 Institute, the Alabama Department of Workforce, and other
15 interested parties to determine options for the institute to
16 better utilize the administrative and operational services of
17 the department.
18 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
19 Section 1. Pursuant to the Alabama Sunset Law, the
20 Sunset Committee recommends the continuance of the Alabama
21 Construction Recruitment Institute until October 1, 2028, with
22 additional recommendation for statutory change as set out in
23 Section 3.
24 Section 2. The existence and functioning of the
25 Alabama Construction Recruitment Institute, created and
26 functioning pursuant to Sections 41-10-720 to 41-10-732,
27 inclusive, Code of Alabama 1975, is continued until October 1
28 2028, and those code sections are expressly preserved.
Page 1
2028, and those code sections are expressly preserved.
SB103 Engrossed
29 Section 3. The Alabama Construction Recruitment
30 Institute, the Alabama Department of Workforce, and other
31 interested parties shall convene to evaluate and determine
32 options to enable the institute to utilize the administrative
33 and operational resources of the department. A report shall be
34 prepared detailing these findings and submitted to the Chair
35 of the Sunset Committee and other members of the Legislature
36 as deemed appropriate not later than the tenth legislative day
37 of the 2027 Regular Session, at which point all parties shall
38 be absolved of their duties and shall cease meeting.
39 Section 4. The Legislature concurs in the
40 recommendations of the Sunset Committee as provided in
41 Sections 1, 2, and 3.
42 Section 5. This act shall become effective on June 1,
43 2026.
