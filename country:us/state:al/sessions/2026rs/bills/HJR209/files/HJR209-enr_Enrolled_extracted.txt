3 COMMENDING PAUL H. REYNOLDS FOR HIS LONG-STANDING PUBLIC
4 SERVICE.
5
6 WHEREAS, highest commendations are herein proudly
7 presented to Paul H. Reynolds for outstanding public
8 service; he has devoted many decades to political and civic
9 engagement, playing a significant role in the development
10 and growth of the Republican Party in Alabama; and
11 WHEREAS, his involvement in politics began in 1964
12 while attending college, when he worked for a conservative
13 radio station in Hattiesburg, Mississippi, and participated
14 in the presidential campaign of United States Senator Barry
15 Goldwater; and
16 WHEREAS, over the years, Mr. Reynolds has been
17 actively involved in numerous political campaigns and party
18 leadership roles, including supporting Congressman Bill
19 Dickinson's campaigns, directing the Gerald Ford
20 presidential campaign in several Alabama counties in 1976,
21 and assisting with regional efforts supporting President
22 Ronald Reagan and United States Senator Jeremiah Denton in
23 1980; and
24 WHEREAS, in 1974, he was elected to represent Butler
25 County on the Alabama Republican Party State Executive
26 Committee, where he served faithfully until 1998, providing
27 leadership and helping guide the party through decades of
28 growth and development; and
Page 1
growth and development; and
HJR209 Enrolled
29 WHEREAS, Mr. Reynolds later served as Chair of
30 Alabama's Second Congressional District for the Alabama
31 Republican Party from 1998 to 2008 and, in 2008, was elected
32 Alabama's National Committeeman to the Republican National
33 Committee, where he contributed to national policy
34 discussions and party leadership; and
35 WHEREAS, among his notable initiatives, Mr. Reynolds
36 helped lay the groundwork for the national "Keep Nine"
37 effort, advocating for a constitutional amendment to
38 maintain the traditional nine-justice structure of the
39 United States Supreme Court; and
40 WHEREAS, Paul H. Reynolds' decades of leadership,
41 dedication, and steadfast commitment to public service have
42 had a lasting and meaningful impact on Alabama's political
43 community and on the many individuals with whom he has
44 worked over the years; now therefore,
45 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA, BOTH
46 HOUSES THEREOF CONCURRING, That Paul H. Reynolds is highly
47 honored and commended for his distinguished service to the
48 State of Alabama, and this resolution is offered to him in
49 deepest admiration and appreciation for his immeasurable
50 service, as well as with sincere best wishes for continued
51 success in all future endeavors.
