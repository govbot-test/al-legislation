10 Relating to ad valorem tax; to amend Section 40-7-2.1
11 and Section 40-9-21.1, Code of Alabama 1975, to provide that
12 permanently and totally disabled veterans of the United States
13 Armed Forces are released from the annual verification
14 requirement for homestead exemptions.
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
16 Section 1. Section 40-7-2.1, Code of Alabama 1975, is
17 amended as follows:
18 "§40-7-2.1
19 (a) This amendatory act shall be known as the 1983
20 Taxpayer Convenience Act.
21 (b) Notwithstanding any other law to the contrary, the
22 county tax assessor may assess property for tax purposes and
23 perform related tax assessing functions and requirements,
24 including the acceptance of applications for homestead
25 exemptions, from January 1 to September 30 of each taxable
26 year and the assessment, including the homestead exemption,
27 shall become effective on the following October 1. Provided,
28 however, nothing herein contained shall be construed to

33 the exemption will be effective as required by law. Any person
34 who has qualified for thea homestead exemption because of age
