6 classification in the law governing the licensing and
7 operation of pharmacy benefits managers.
8 The current term of "independent pharmacy,"
9 referring to a pharmacy classified by the Alabama State
10 Board of Pharmacy as a community pharmacy, would be
11 deleted. In its place, a new term, "unaffiliated
12 pharmacy," would be introduced to refer to a pharmacy
13 that is not affiliated with a pharmacy benefits
