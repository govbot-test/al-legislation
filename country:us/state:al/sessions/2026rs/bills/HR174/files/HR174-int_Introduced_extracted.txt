8 WHEREAS, it is a great pleasure to commemorate the
9 50th Anniversary "Deltaversary" of the Intensified
10 Twenty-Five Delta Delta Chapter, Delta Sigma Theta Sorority,
11 Incorporated; and
12 WHEREAS, on March 6, 1974, 25 young women became
13 members of Delta Sigma Theta Sorority, Incorporated, during
14 the Spring intake on the campus of Alabama A&M University;
15 these women were selected for membership based on high
16 scholastic achievement, demonstrated leadership ability, and
17 a proven commitment to the ideals and public service mission
18 of Delta Sigma Theta Sorority, Incorporated; and
19 WHEREAS, the Delta Delta Chapter proudly introduced

24 communities, the nation, and the world; and
25 WHEREAS, the 25 members have shown exemplary

28 these women have served with distinction as educators,
