9 Relating to school lands; to add Section 16-20-10 to
10 the Code of Alabama 1975, to require the Department of
11 Conservation and Natural Resource, upon request of the
12 relevant local board of education, to issue a deed to certain
13 school indemnity lands to the board.
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
15 Section 1. Section 16-20-10 is added to the Code of
16 Alabama 1975, to read as follows:
17 §16-20-10
18 (a) It is the intent of the Legislature that this
19 section effectuates and carries out its intent in passing
20 Section 30-5.20 of the Constitution of 2022 during the 2024
21 Regular Session which was ratified in November of 2024
22 pursuant to a statewide election.
23 (b) Upon the request of the Franklin County Board of
24 Education, the Department of Conservation and Natural
25 Resources shall issue to the board a deed to all indemnity
26 lands located in Walker and Fayette counties for which schools
27 in Franklin County are the sole beneficiary.
