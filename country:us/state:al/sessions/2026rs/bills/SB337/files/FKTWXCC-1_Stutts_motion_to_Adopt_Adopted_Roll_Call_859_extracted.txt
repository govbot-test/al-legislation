EDUCATION POLICY SUBSTITUTE TO SB337
OFFERED BY SENATOR STUTTS
1
2
3
4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, certain school lands have
6 been transferred to local school boards and local
7 school boards may request school lands be sold.
8 This bill would require the Department of
9 Conservation and Natural Resources to, upon request of
10 the relevant school board, issue a deed to certain
11 school indemnity lands to the board.
12
13
14 A BILL
15 TO BE ENTITLED
16 AN ACT
17
18 Relating to school lands; to add Section 16-20-10 to
19 the Code of Alabama 1975, to require the Department of
20 Conservation and Natural Resource, upon request of the
21 relevant local board of education, to issue a deed to certain
22 school indemnity lands to the board.
23 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
24 Section 1. Section 16-20-10 is added to the Code of
25 Alabama 1975, to read as follows:
26 §16-20-10
27 (a) It is the intent of the Legislature that this
28 section effectuates and carries out its intent in passing
