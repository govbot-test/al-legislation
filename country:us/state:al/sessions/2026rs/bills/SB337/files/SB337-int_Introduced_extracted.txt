4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, the proceeds from the sale
6 of certain school lands must be used for educational
7 purposes where the land is located.
8 This bill would require the Department of
9 Conservation and Natural Resources to sell these lands
10 upon the request of the relevant local board of
11 education and provide for the disposition of the
12 proceeds of the sale.
