4 HR____ MOURNING THE DEATH AND CELEBRATING THE LIFE OF HENRY
5 LEE GOSHAY.
6
7 WHEREAS, with great sadness, we note the peaceful
8 passing of Henry Lee "Chuck" Goshay on February 24, 2026,
9 yet we also celebrate his abundant life that ended at the
10 age of 78; and
11 WHEREAS, born to Tommie Lee and Clara Bell Goshay on
12 February 14, 1948, in Russell County, Alabama, Chuck
13 accepted Christ at an early age and became a devoted member
14 of Bethel A.M.E. Church in Phenix City; and
15 WHEREAS, Chuck was a proud graduate of Mount Olive
16 High School, Class of 1966; following graduation, he
17 received a four-year baseball scholarship to Fort Valley
18 State College in Georgia, where he set numerous records and
19 earned many honors; among his most notable achievements were
20 being named Most Outstanding College Athlete in America and
21 becoming inducted into the inaugural class of the Phenix
22 City Sports Hall of Fame; and
23 WHEREAS, after college, Chuck enjoyed a successful
24 30-year career at Mead Coated Board, where he was known for
25 his strong work ethic and steady leadership; and
26 WHEREAS, Chuck had a passion for golf and a lifelong
27 love for baseball; he enjoyed singing in the choir and
28 playing the bass guitar, which he taught himself; he also
