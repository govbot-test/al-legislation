5 TO BE ENTITLED
6 AN ACT
7
8
9 Relating to Henry County; to further provide for the
10 compensation of the sheriff; and to repeal Section 45-34-230,
11 Code of Alabama 1975, relating to the compensation of the
12 sheriff.
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
14 Section 1. (a) Beginning June 1, 2026, the Sheriff of
15 Henry County shall receive an additional expense allowance in
16 an amount that brings his or her total compensation to
17 ninety-four thousand dollars ($94,000) per year.
18 (b) Beginning January 18, 2027, the base annual salary
19 for the sheriff shall be ninety-four thousand dollars
20 ($94,000) per year, in lieu of all other expense allowances or
21 other forms of compensation.
22 (c)(1) Notwithstanding subsection (b), beginning
23 January 18, 2027, any individual serving as sheriff shall
24 receive an increase in his or her then-existing salary of
25 eight percent for each second or subsequent successive term to
26 which he or she is elected sheriff.
27 (2) An individual who returns to the office of sheriff
28 for any non-successive term shall be compensated as if he or
Page 1
for any non-successive term shall be compensated as if he or
HB536 INTRODUCED
29 she has obtained the office of sheriff for the first time, and
30 shall be eligible for increases pursuant to subdivision (1)
31 only for currently successive service.
32 (d) The sheriff shall receive all benefits provided to
33 employees of the Henry County Commission, but he or she shall
34 not be eligible for any increases in compensation pursuant to
35 Chapter 2A of Subtitle 1 of Title 11, Code of Alabama 1975.
36 Section 2. Section 45-34-230, Code of Alabama 1975,
37 relating to the compensation of the Sheriff of Henry County,
38 is repealed.
39 Section 3. This act shall become effective on June 1,
40 2026.
