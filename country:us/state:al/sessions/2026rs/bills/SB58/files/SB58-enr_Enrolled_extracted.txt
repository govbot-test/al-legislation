4 Relating to public corruption convictions; to amend
5 Section 36-27D-1, Code of Alabama 1975, to use retirement
6 contributions made by a public servant found guilty of a
7 criminal offense involving his or her government position to
8 be used for restitution; to require a public servant to pay
9 back compensation received while on leave with pay in certain
10 circumstances; and to make nonsubstantive, technical revisions
11 to update the existing code language to current style.
