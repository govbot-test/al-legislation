4 HR____ CELEBRATING THE 65TH ANNIVERSARY OF THE MONTGOMERY
5 ALUMNAE CHAPTER OF ZETA TAU ALPHA FRATERNITY.
6
7 WHEREAS, it is a great pleasure to commemorate the
8 65th Anniversary of the Montgomery Alumnae Chapter of Zeta
9 Tau Alpha Fraternity, which originated on July 26, 1961; and
10 WHEREAS, the Montgomery Alumnae Chapter of Zeta Tau
11 Alpha is comprised of a small but mighty group of women
12 dedicated to serving and improving their community; the
13 chapter channels its efforts into local community
14 volunteerism and philanthropy focused on Zeta Tau Alpha's
15 national initiative, breast cancer research and awareness;
16 and
17 WHEREAS, over the years, the alumnae chapter members
18 have demonstrated excellence in service, particularly
19 through involvement in various efforts to support the
20 community of Montgomery; from food drives for local food
21 pantries and coat and sock drives for local homeless
22 shelters to adopting animals at the Montgomery Zoo and many
23 more incentives, the chapter has charged its members to
24 serve the area, thus upholding the national motto "Seek the
25 Noblest"; and
26 WHEREAS, the same commitment to friendship that bound
27 together the nine young women who formed Zeta Tau Alpha in
28 1898 is carried on today through the women of the Montgomery
