4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under current law, the Sweet Home Alabama
6 Tourism Investment Act provides tax rebates to
8 amounts equal to the company's tax liability for state
9 and local sales and use tax, lodgings tax, and other
10 transactional taxes associated with the project.
11 This bill clarifies that the annual one-time two
12 million five hundred thousand dollar ($2,500,000) award
13 is supplemental to the initial award.
14 This bill would also require approved companies
15 to document actual cost of projects through
16 certification by an independent certified public
17 accountant.
18 Finally, this bill would require certification
19 of project costs.
