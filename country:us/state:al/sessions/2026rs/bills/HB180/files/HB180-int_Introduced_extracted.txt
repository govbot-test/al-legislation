5 TO BE ENTITLED
6 AN ACT
7
8
9 Relating to Conecuh County; to change the selection of
10 the county superintendent of education from elected to
11 appointed commencing with the next term of office; and to
12 repeal Acts 1923, No. 163 (Local Acts 1923, p. 79) and Acts
13 1959, No. 524 (Acts 1959, p. 1300).
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
15 Section 1. Commencing after the expiration of the term
16 of office of the Conecuh County Superintendent of Education
17 holding office on June 1, 2026, the Conecuh County Board of
18 Education shall appoint the Superintendent of Education of
19 Conecuh County pursuant to the general law of the state. The
20 county superintendent of education shall possess all the
21 qualifications prescribed for a county superintendent of
22 education by the general law, shall possess all the powers
23 granted by the general law, and shall perform and discharge
24 all the duties of a county superintendent of education
25 prescribed by the general law. The Conecuh County
26 Superintendent of Education shall devote his or her entire
27 time to the public school business of the county and shall
28 receive as compensation an annual salary in an amount set by
Page 1
receive as compensation an annual salary in an amount set by
HB180 INTRODUCED
29 the county board of education. The board shall have discretion
30 to grant expenses from time to time during the term of the
31 superintendent. The compensation shall be payable from the
32 public school funds of the county and shall be the total
33 compensation.
34 Section 2. Acts 1923, No. 163 (Local Acts 1923, p. 79)
35 and Acts 1959, No. 524 (Acts 1959, p. 1300), relating to the
36 election, qualifications, powers, and duties of the Conecuh
37 County Superintendent of Education, are repealed.
38 Section 3. This act shall become effective on June 1,
39 2026.
