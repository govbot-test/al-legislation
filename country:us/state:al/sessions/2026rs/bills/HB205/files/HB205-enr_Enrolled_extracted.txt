HB205 Enrolled
2 Relating to Henry County; to abolish the Southeast
3 Alabama Human Development Council; and to provide for the
4 transfer of the duties of the council.
5 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
6 Section 1. (a) The Southeast Alabama Human Development
7 Council, established pursuant to Section 45-34-83, Code of
8 Alabama 1975, is abolished.
9 (b) Pursuant to the provisions of Chapter 1 of Title
10 44, Code of Alabama 1975, the Alabama Department of Youth
11 Services shall assume the duties of the council.
12 Section 2. Section 45-34-83, Code of Alabama 1975,
13 establishing the Southeast Alabama Human Development Council,
14 is repealed.
15 Section 3. This act shall become effective on June 1,
16 2026.
