1 Enrolled, An Act,

3 To propose an amendment to the Constitution of Alabama
4 of 2022, relating to Macon County, to increase the age limit
5 for being elected or appointed as judge of probate of the
6 county to 75 years of age at the time of qualifying for
7 election or date of appointment.
8 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
9 Section 1. The following amendment to the Constitution
10 of Alabama of 2022, is proposed:
11 PROPOSED AMENDMENT
12 Notwithstanding Section 155 of the Constitution of
13 Alabama of 2022, a person who is 75 years of age or younger at
14 the beginning time of qualifying for election, or at the time
15 of his or her appointment, may be elected or appointed to the
16 office of Judge of Probate of Macon County.
17 Upon ratification of this constitutional amendment, the
18 Code Commissioner shall number and place this amendment as
19 appropriate in the constitution omitting this instructional
20 paragraph and may make the following nonsubstantive revisions:
21 change capitalization, hierarchy, spelling, and punctuation
22 for purposes of style and uniformity; correct manifest
23 grammatical, clerical, and typographical errors; revise
24 internal or external citations and cross-references; harmonize
25 language; and translate effective dates.
26 END PROPOSED AMENDMENT
27 Section 2. An election upon the proposed amendment
28 shall be held in accordance with Sections 284, 284.01, and 285
