4 ALABAMA FARMERS FEDERATION.
5
6 WHEREAS, we extend our sincerest commendation and
7 gratitude to Wilcox County cattleman and timber landowner
8 Jake Harper, who has served as Southwest Area Vice President
9 of the Alabama Farmers Federation and Alfa Insurance since
10 2003; and
11 WHEREAS, Mr. Harper, known for his logical mind,
12 plain speech, and strong accountability to fellow farmers,
13 was recognized for his commitment to excellence with the
14 Service to Agriculture Award by Federation President Jimmy
15 Parnell during the organization's 104th annual meeting in
16 Montgomery on December 7, 2025; the Service to Agriculture
17 Award is the Federation's highest honor; and
18 WHEREAS, Mr. Harper joined the Wilcox County Farmers
19 Federation (WCFF) board in 1981, filling the seat of his
20 late father; he was soon elected WCFF president, a role he
21 continues to hold; by 1987, Jake Harper became chair of the
22 State Young Farmers Committee, followed by nine years on the
23 state board serving District 9, comprised of 17 counties in
24 central Alabama's Black Belt; in 2003, peers from these
25 counties elected him Southwest Area vice president; and
26 WHEREAS, in addition to his Federation service, Mr.
27 Harper is a longtime local Soil & Water Conservation
28 District board member, has served multiple terms on the

30 Wild Land Trust board; he is also past president of the
31 Alabama Association of Conservation Districts and Alabama
32 TREASURE Forest Association; he and his family attend Camden
33 Associated Reformed Presbyterian Church; and
34 WHEREAS, Mr. Harper has become known throughout his
35 various leadership roles for his high integrity and honesty,
36 his stalwart, steady, and straightforward way of conducting
37 business, and his intense review of board minutes,
38 financials, publications, and Federation proposals; and
39 WHEREAS, Jake Harper and his loving wife, Pam, a
40 pharmacist, have instilled the value of service in their
41 family; in 32 cumulative years of service, Mr. Harper has
42 missed only one Federation board meeting; and
43 WHEREAS, Jake Harper's undeniable influence and
44 steady source of insight to incoming leaders will be sorely
45 missed; he and Pam look forward to adventures ahead,
46 including greater freedom as Papa and Gigi to grandsons Cal,
47 Banks, and Wil, as well as time with their son Jacob and
48 daughter-in-law Landon, son Morgan and daughter-in-law
49 Patricia, and daughter Claudia and son-in-law Bannock
50 Farrens; now therefore,
51 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA, BOTH
52 HOUSES THEREOF CONCURRING, That we offer our highest regards
53 and deepest appreciation to Jake Harper for his invaluable
54 service to the State of Alabama and the field of
55 agriculture; the legacy of his hard work and professionalism
