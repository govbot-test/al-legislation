7 WHEREAS, we extend our sincerest commendation and
8 gratitude to Wilcox County cattleman and timber landowner
9 Jake Harper, who has served as Southwest Area Vice President
10 of the Alabama Farmers Federation and Alfa Insurance since
11 2003; and
12 WHEREAS, Mr. Harper, known for his logical mind,
13 plain speech, and strong accountability to fellow farmers,
14 was recognized for his commitment to excellence with the
15 Service to Agriculture Award by Federation President Jimmy
16 Parnell during the organization's 104th annual meeting in
17 Montgomery on December 7, 2025; the Service to Agriculture
18 Award is the Federation's highest honor; and
19 WHEREAS, Mr. Harper joined the Wilcox County Farmers
20 Federation (WCFF) board in 1981, filling the seat of his
21 late father; he was soon elected WCFF president, a role he
22 continues to hold; by 1987, Jake Harper became chair of the
23 State Young Farmers Committee, followed by nine years on the
24 state board serving District 9, comprised of 17 counties in
25 central Alabama's Black Belt; in 2003, peers from these
26 counties elected him Southwest Area vice president; and
27 WHEREAS, in addition to his Federation service, Mr.
28 Harper is a longtime local Soil & Water Conservation
