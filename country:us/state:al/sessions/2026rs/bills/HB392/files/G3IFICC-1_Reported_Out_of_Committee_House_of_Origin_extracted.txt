7 AN ACT

9 Relating to the Public Service Commission; to amend
10 Sections 37-1-3, 37-1-5, 37-1-6, and 37-1-7, Code of Alabama
11 1975; to provide for appointment of commissioners to the
12 Public Service Commission; to further provide for
13 qualifications of commissioners; to extend conflict of
14 interest provisions imposed on commissioners to include
15 actions with certain nonutility entities participating in
16 matters before the commission; to require the commission to
17 hold certain periodic public informational meetings with
18 utility representatives; to add Section 37-1-54.1 to the Code
19 of Alabama 1975, to require the commission to ensure lobbying
20 expenses are not charged to ratepayers; to amend Sections
21 17-14-1, 17-14-2, and 17-14-3, and 36-3-1, Code of Alabama
22 1975, to make conforming changes; and to make nonsubstantive,
23 technical revisions to update the existing code language to
24 current style.
