5 STATE REPRESENTATIVE MICKY RAY HAMMON.
6
7 WHEREAS, with profound sorrow, we note the passing of
8 Micky Ray Hammon of Morgan County, Alabama, on March 8,
9 2026, while we also celebrate an accomplished life that
10 ended at the age of 68; and
11 WHEREAS, Mr. Hammon proudly represented District 4 in
12 the Alabama House of Representatives and served as Majority
13 Leader for six years; his departure leaves a void in the
14 lives of his loving family, many friends, and the community
15 where he was highly regarded; and
16 WHEREAS, born on August 25, 1957, Mr. Hammon was a
17 lifelong resident of Morgan County; a faithful Christian, he
18 was a devoted father and the loving husband of 44 years to
19 his wife, Pam, who preceded him in death; he loved the
20 outdoors and spent many of his happiest moments hunting and
21 fishing with his two sons and daughter; and
22 WHEREAS, Mr. Hammon received an electrical degree
23 from Calhoun Community College and worked as an electrical
24 contractor; he was a member of Associated Builders and
25 Contractors, Business Council of Alabama, the Decatur
26 Chamber of Commerce, the Morgan County Economic Development
27 Association, the National Federation of Independent
28 Business, the Tennessee Valley Training Center, and the
Page 1
Business, the Tennessee Valley Training Center, and the
HJR240 INTRODUCED
29 United States Chamber of Commerce; and
30 WHEREAS, first elected to the Alabama House of
31 Representatives in 2002, Mr. Hammon was proud to represent
32 the people of Limestone and Morgan counties, and to serve
33 the people of Alabama; after Republicans took control of the
34 House following the 2010 elections, he was elected Majority
35 Leader, a position he held until February 2017; and
36 WHEREAS, Mr. Hammon served as Chair of the Public
37 Safety and Homeland Security Committee, as well as the
38 Subcommittees on Economic Development and Incentives, and
39 Homeland Security and Immigration; he served as Vice Chair
40 of the Economic Development and Tourism Committee; he
41 announced his retirement from public service in 2017 and
42 did not seek reelection; and
43 WHEREAS, known for never meeting a stranger, Mr.
44 Hammon was loved by many for his kindness and welcoming
45 spirit; he leaves a legacy of faith, service, and love for
46 his family and community; and
47 WHEREAS, although his presence will be greatly
48 missed, the memory of Micky Hammon will be cherished in the
49 hearts of all who knew and loved him; now therefore,
50 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA, BOTH
51 HOUSES THEREOF CONCURRING, That the death of Micky Ray
52 Hammon is recorded with deep sadness, and this resolution is
53 offered in tribute to his life and service to others, along
54 with heartfelt sympathy to his family, whose grief is shared
55 by many.
