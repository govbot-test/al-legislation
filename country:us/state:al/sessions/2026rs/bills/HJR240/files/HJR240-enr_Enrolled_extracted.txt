3 MOURNING THE DEATH AND CELEBRATING THE LIFE OF FORMER STATE
4 REPRESENTATIVE MICKY RAY HAMMON.
5
6 WHEREAS, with profound sorrow, we note the passing of
7 Micky Ray Hammon of Morgan County, Alabama, on March 8,
8 2026, while we also celebrate an accomplished life that
9 ended at the age of 68; and
10 WHEREAS, Mr. Hammon proudly represented District 4 in
11 the Alabama House of Representatives and served as Majority
12 Leader for six years; his departure leaves a void in the
13 lives of his loving family, many friends, and the community
14 where he was highly regarded; and
15 WHEREAS, born on August 25, 1957, Mr. Hammon was a
16 lifelong resident of Morgan County; a faithful Christian, he
17 was a devoted father and the loving husband of 44 years to
18 his wife, Pam, who preceded him in death; he loved the
19 outdoors and spent many of his happiest moments hunting and
20 fishing with his two sons and daughter; and
21 WHEREAS, Mr. Hammon received an electrical degree
22 from Calhoun Community College and worked as an electrical
23 contractor; he was a member of Associated Builders and
24 Contractors, Business Council of Alabama, the Decatur
25 Chamber of Commerce, the Morgan County Economic Development
26 Association, the National Federation of Independent
27 Business, the Tennessee Valley Training Center, and the
28 United States Chamber of Commerce; and
Page 1
United States Chamber of Commerce; and
HJR240 Enrolled
29 WHEREAS, first elected to the Alabama House of
30 Representatives in 2002, Mr. Hammon was proud to represent
31 the people of Limestone and Morgan counties, and to serve
32 the people of Alabama; after Republicans took control of the
33 House following the 2010 elections, he was elected Majority
34 Leader, a position he held until February 2017; and
35 WHEREAS, Mr. Hammon served as Chair of the Public
36 Safety and Homeland Security Committee, as well as the
37 Subcommittees on Economic Development and Incentives, and
38 Homeland Security and Immigration; he served as Vice Chair
39 of the Economic Development and Tourism Committee; he
40 announced his retirement from public service in 2017 and
41 did not seek reelection; and
42 WHEREAS, known for never meeting a stranger, Mr.
43 Hammon was loved by many for his kindness and welcoming
44 spirit; he leaves a legacy of faith, service, and love for
45 his family and community; and
46 WHEREAS, although his presence will be greatly
47 missed, the memory of Micky Hammon will be cherished in the
48 hearts of all who knew and loved him; now therefore,
49 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA, BOTH
50 HOUSES THEREOF CONCURRING, That the death of Micky Ray
51 Hammon is recorded with deep sadness, and this resolution is
52 offered in tribute to his life and service to others, along
53 with heartfelt sympathy to his family, whose grief is shared
54 by many.
