HB383 Enrolled
2
3 Relating to the Tuscaloosa County Civil Service System;
4 to provide for a probationary period for new hires; to provide
5 for certain county employees to work less than full-time; and
6 to repeal Section 9 of Act 49-357 (1949 Acts, p. 524),
7 relating to temporary employees.
8 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
9 Section 1. All newly hired employees subject to the
10 Tuscaloosa County Civil Service System shall serve a
11 probationary period of 12 months.
12 Section 2. The Tuscaloosa County Civil Service Board,
13 as necessary for the efficient operation of county operations,
14 may adopt rules for the hiring of additional employees who may
15 work less than full-time.
16 Section 3. Section 9 of Act 49-357 (Local Acts 1949, p.
17 524), relating to temporary employees, is repealed.
18 Section 4. This act shall become effective on October
19 1, 2026.
