13 Section 1. Section 45-27-234, Code of Alabama 1975, is
14 amended to read as follows:
15 "§45-27-234
16 (a) On or after May 18, 2020Beginning June 1, 2026, a
17 person an individual qualifying for election as Sheriff of
18 Escambia County or any person individual appointed to serve as
19 a sheriff shall meet all of the following minimum
20 qualifications, in addition to any other qualifications
21 required by law:
22 (1) The person individual is a citizen of the United
23 States.
24 (2) The person individual has been a resident of the
25 county for at least one year immediately prior to the
26 qualification date.
27 (3) The person individual has the qualifications of an
28 elector pursuant to state and federal law and the person has

33 (5) The person individual is 25 years of age or older

35 (6) The person individual has three or more years of
36 full-time prior service as a law enforcement officer having
37 the power of arrest, which service is certified by the Alabama

40 law enforcement certified through the Alabama Peace Officers'

50 from active law enforcement, he or she has three or more years
51 of full-time post-retirement law enforcement service prior to
52 the date of qualifying.
53 (7) The person individual has never been convicted of a
