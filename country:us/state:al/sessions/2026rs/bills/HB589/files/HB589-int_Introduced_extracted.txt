4 SYNOPSIS:
5 This bill would allow active and participating
6 judges, clerks, and district attorneys to purchase
7 withdrawn service from the District Attorneys Plan and
8 the Judges' and Clerks' Plan in the same manner that
9 members of the Employees' Retirement System and the
10 Teachers' Retirement System are allowed under their
11 plans.
