3 COMMENDING COACH TIM CARTER FOR HIS MANY CONTRIBUTIONS TO
4 ALABAMA SCHOOLS AS A FOOTBALL COACH ON THE OCCASION OF HIS
5 RETIREMENT.
6
7 WHEREAS, with very special pleasure, longtime
8 Fairhope High School head football coach and athletic
9 director Tim Carter is heartily commended for his 35-year
10 career of service to the State of Alabama as a high school
11 football coach, and we find it necessary and proper to honor
12 him for his many accomplishments and efforts for the
13 betterment of Alabama students both on and off the field;
14 and
15 WHEREAS, Coach Carter, the longest-tenured coach in
16 Fairhope High School's history, announced his retirement in
17 January 2026 following 11 seasons leading the school's
18 football team; during his tenure, he compiled an impressive
19 70-49 record, reached the playoffs eight times, and secured
20 the school's first Class 7A playoff win; and
21 WHEREAS, the son of a coach, Tim Carter graduated
22 from Monroe Academy in Monroeville, Alabama; he received his
23 bachelor's degree from Auburn University and master's degree
24 from Troy University; following graduation, he served as an
25 assistant coach in several counties across the state; and
26 WHEREAS, in 1994, he returned to his alma mater,
27 Monroe Academy, to serve as head coach; after his time at
28 Monroe Academy, he progressed to Auburn High School, where

51 contributions to the youth in the State of Alabama, and we
52 dedicate this resolution to him with utmost appreciation and
53 respect, along with sincere best wishes for continued good
54 health, happiness, and success in all his endeavors.
