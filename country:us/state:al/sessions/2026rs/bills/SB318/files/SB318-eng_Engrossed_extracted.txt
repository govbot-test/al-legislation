9 Relating to crimes and offenses; to amend Section
10 13A-6-65.1, Code of Alabama 1975; to revise the crime of
11 sexual torture.
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
13 Section 1. Section 13A-6-65.1, Code of Alabama 1975, is
14 amended to read as follows:
15 "§13A-6-65.1
16 (a) A person commits the crime of sexual torture if he
17 or she does any of the following:
18 (1) Penetrates the vagina, penile opening, anus, or
19 mouth of another person with an inanimate object, by forcible
20 compulsion, with the intent to sexually torture, sexually
21 abuse, or to gratify the sexual desire of either party.
22 (2) Penetrates the vagina, penile opening, anus, or
23 mouth of a person who is incapable of consent by reason of
24 being incapacitated, with an inanimate object, with the intent
25 to sexually torture, sexually abuse, or to gratify the sexual
26 desire of either party.
27 (3) Penetrates the vagina, penile opening, anus, or
28 mouth of a person who is less than 12 years old of age, with
Page 1
mouth of a person who is less than 12 years old of age, with

29 an inanimate object, by a person who is 16 years old of age or
30 older with the intent to sexually torture, sexually abuse, or
31 to gratify the sexual desire of either party.
32 (4) By inflicting physical injury, including, but not
33 limited to, burning, crushing, wounding, mutilating, or
34 assaulting the sex organs or intimate parts of another person,
35 with the intent to sexually torture, sexually abuse, or to
36 gratify the sexual desire of either party.
37 (b) The crime of sexual torture is a Class A felony."
38 Section 2. This act shall become effective on October
39 1, 2026.
