5 BRIGADIER GENERAL VANDIVER H. CARTER ON BEING SELECTED AS
6 THE 2026 MARION MILITARY INSTITUTE ALUMNUS OF THE YEAR.
7
8 WHEREAS, it is an honor to extend highest
9 commendations to United States Army Reserve Retired
10 Brigadier General (BG) Vandiver H. Carter on being selected
11 as the 2026 Marion Military Institute (MMI) Alumnus of the
12 Year; and
13 WHEREAS, this honor reflects not only BG Carter's
14 accomplishments as an alumnus but also his extraordinary and
15 sustained commitment to the Institute; over the past 15
16 years of service on the MMI Board of Trustees, he has
17 provided steady leadership, wise counsel, and unwavering
18 advocacy for MMI's mission; his dedication to ensuring the
19 continued strength and relevance of MMI has made a lasting
20 impact on the institution and the Corps of Cadets it serves;
21 and

23 was born in Selma, Alabama, and graduated from Albert G.
24 Parrish High School in May 1961; after joining the Alabama
25 Army National Guard in November, he entered basic training
26 in March 1962; upon completion of his six-month tour of
27 duty, he entered Marion Military Institute in September; and
