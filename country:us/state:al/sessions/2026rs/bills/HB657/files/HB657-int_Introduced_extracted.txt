15 Relating to income taxes; to add Section 40-18-513 to

18 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
19 Section 1. Section 40-18-513 is added to the Code of
20 Alabama 1975, to read as follows:
21 §40-18-513
22 (a) As used in this section, the term "qualified
23 taxpayer" means an individual taxpayer who filed an Alabama
24 individual income tax return pursuant to the tax levied in
25 Section 40-18-5, for both the 2024 and 2025 tax years,
26 including any extensions that have been granted.
27 (1) The term shall not include:
28 a. A nonresident;
