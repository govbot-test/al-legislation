5 UPON THE OCCASION OF ITS 60TH ANNIVERSARY.
6
7 WHEREAS, we are pleased to recognize the Muscle
8 Shoals High School Band Program as it celebrates its 60th
9 Anniversary, marking six decades of excellence, achievement,
10 and service through music; and
11 WHEREAS, the Muscle Shoals High School Band Program
12 was organized in 1965 under the leadership of Director
13 Charles R. Stratford, establishing a tradition of
14 outstanding musicianship and performance that continues to
15 thrive today; and
16 WHEREAS, remarkably, in the 60-year history of the
17 program, there have been only five Head Directors including
18 Charles Stratford, Dexter Greenhaw, Pat Stegall, David
19 Waters, and Jessica Hood; each director has contributed to
20 the sustained success and prestige of the program; and
21 WHEREAS, the Muscle Shoals High School Band has long
22 represented a high degree of musicianship and continues to
23 serve as a model for band programs throughout the State of
24 Alabama; and
25 WHEREAS, the band has earned recognition locally,
26 statewide, regionally, nationally, and internationally for
27 its musical achievements, demonstrating the highest
28 standards of excellence; and
Page 1
standards of excellence; and

29 WHEREAS, the band has consistently produced student
30 musicians of exceptional caliber, many of whom have earned
31 positions in local, state, and national honor ensembles; and
32 WHEREAS, the band has been invited to perform at
33 numerous state conferences and workshops, further
34 establishing its reputation as a premier program and a
35 standard-bearer for others; and
36 WHEREAS, for 60 years, the Muscle Shoals High School
37 Band Program has provided entertaining and inspiring
38 halftime performances and concerts at home and throughout
39 Alabama and the surrounding region; and
40 WHEREAS, while the band's many honors are too
41 numerous to list, it has received acclaim in competitive
42 events and festivals throughout its history and continues to
43 earn accolades each year, including over 30 consecutive
44 years of superior ratings in concert band, 60 years of
45 consistent superior ratings in marching band, four
46 back-to-back Bands of America Class Championships, three
47 Grand Champion Awards at the Vanderbilt Marching
48 Invitational, numerous other Grand Champion awards, and
49 recognition as a four-time Alabama Marching Band Champion;
50 and
51 WHEREAS, the Muscle Shoals High School Band Program
52 has inspired generations of student musicians, many of whom
53 have pursued careers as music educators and professional
54 performers, including the current Director, Jessica Hood;
55 now therefore,

now therefore,

56 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE
57 LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA, That we do hereby honor and commend
58 the Muscle Shoals High School Band Program for 60 years of
59 extraordinary excellence and achievement, and we extend
60 sincere congratulations upon the occasion of its 60th
61 Anniversary.
