4 SYNOPSIS:
5 This bill would propose an amendment to the
6 Constitution of Alabama of 2022, relating to Escambia
7 County, to authorize the district court to hold court
8 in the county courthouse for cases arising under the
9 ordinances of any municipality in the county in which
10 there is no municipal court.

17 To propose an amendment to the Constitution of Alabama
18 of 2022, relating to Escambia County, to further provide for
19 where the district court may hold court in certain cases.
20 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
21 Section 1. The following amendment to the Constitution
22 of Alabama of 2022, is proposed:
23 PROPOSED AMENDMENT
24 In Escambia County, the district court may hold court
25 in the county courthouse for cases arising under the
26 ordinances of any municipality in the county in which there is
27 no municipal court.
