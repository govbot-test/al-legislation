4 HR____ COMMENDING CHRISTOPHER HOOD ON HIS ACADEMIC
5 ACHIEVEMENTS.
6
7 WHEREAS, highest commendations are herein proudly
8 conveyed to Christopher "Chris" Hood for his outstanding
9 academic achievements and distinguished record of leadership
10 and service at Troy University; and
11 WHEREAS, Christopher Hood is a junior majoring in
12 exercise science and has demonstrated a steadfast commitment
13 to excellence both in the classroom and throughout campus
14 life; and
15 WHEREAS, Chris graduated with honors from Helena High
16 School in 2023, where he distinguished himself as an officer
17 in four honor societies and a member of three additional
18 societies, and he served as a Peer Helper dedicated to
19 supporting and uplifting his fellow students; and
20 WHEREAS, since his freshman year at Troy University,
21 Chris has been actively involved in the Student Government
22 Association, beginning his service as Treasurer for the
23 Freshman Forum; and
24 WHEREAS, during his sophomore year, he continued his
25 service as a Senator, where he was a dedicated member of the
26 Governing Laws Committee, contributing to the drafting and
27 presentation of legislation, and served as Housing Seat
28 Leader; and

33 his leadership and his unwavering dedication to Troy
34 University; and
35 WHEREAS, his leadership philosophy is reflected in
36 his campaign slogan, "Do Good with Hood," wherein "GOOD"
37 represents the core values of Genuine, Objective, Ownership,
38 and Development, which guide his service and vision for the
39 student body; and
40 WHEREAS, beyond his service in student government, he
