197 the amount appropriated to defray the expenses of operating
198 the department for each fiscal year. After the distributions
199 provided herein and the distributions of use tax on
200 automobiles to the General Fund as provided in Section
201 40-23-61(c), the balance of the tax collected under and
202 pursuant to Section 40-23-61 shall be distributed as follows:
203 (1) remote use tax amounts, seventy-five percent (75%) to the
204 General Fund and twenty-five percent (25%) to the Education
205 Trust Fund, and (2) any remaining amounts, forty-seven percent
206 (47%) to the Education Trust Fund and fifty-three percent
207 (53%) to the General Fund. An amount sufficient to fund the
208 Children's Health Insurance Program (CHIP) shall be
209 distributed annually as a first charge against the amounts
210 allocated to the General Fund under this section. It is the
211 legislative intent that all amounts collected on transactions
212 involving a seller located outside the State of Alabama when
213 the property is shipped or transported from outside the state
214 shall be considered use tax for the purpose of this
215 distribution, regardless of whether the taxes may be
216 considered sales taxes for other purposes."
