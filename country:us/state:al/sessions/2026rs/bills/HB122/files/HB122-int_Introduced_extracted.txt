4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, the Teacher Excellence and
6 Accountability for Mathematics and Science (TEAMS)
7 Salary Schedule Program provides for additional
8 compensation to be paid to K-12 qualified teachers of
9 mathematics and science who elect to participate in the
10 program. Under TEAMS, a participating teacher with over
11 20 years of service that satisfies the advanced
12 contract criteria is required to be offered a five-year
13 contract.
14 This bill would provide that a contract may be
15 offered to the participating teacher for a period of up
16 to five years.
17 This bill would also make conforming changes to
18 hierarchy units.
