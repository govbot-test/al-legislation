3 COMMENDING REPRESENTATIVE TRACY ESTES FOR HIS SERVICE TO THE
4 STATE OF ALABAMA.
5
6 WHEREAS, it is with great admiration and deep respect
7 that we commend Representative Tracy Estes, currently
8 serving his second term as a Republican member of the
9 Alabama House of Representatives, for his many
10 accomplishments and tireless devotion to our state,
11 particularly in matters involving education, public safety,
12 and advocacy for children and seniors; and
13 WHEREAS, since his first election in 2018,
14 Representative Estes has served as a positive and principled
15 advocate for the people of Alabama's 17th District, which
16 includes Lamar, Marion, and Winston Counties; and
17 WHEREAS, John Tracy Estes, born on June 20, 1967,
18 graduated from The University of Alabama in 1989 with a
19 Bachelor's degree in News Editorial Journalism; following
20 graduation, Representative Estes worked for The Tuscaloosa
21 News and The Montgomery Advertiser sports department before
22 taking a news editor position at the Journal-Record
23 Newspaper in Marion County; and
24 WHEREAS, during his time as an editor, Representative
25 Estes became a member of the Marion County Republican Party,
26 the Alabama Association of School Boards Board of Directors,
27 and the Winfield Chamber of Commerce; he worked as an editor
28 for 27 years before retiring to run for public office; and
