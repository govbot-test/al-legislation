4 SYNOPSIS:
5 The State Pilotage Commission was established by
6 law in 1931 to regulate the piloting of ships and all
7 steam or sail vessels crossing into and out of any
8 harbor or seaport in this state. These laws set
9 requirements for the apprenticeship and licensing of
10 bar pilots in this state.
11 In 2019, the Legislature enacted laws
12 establishing a board of pilotage commissioners for each
13 Class 2 municipality, also by the name of the State
14 Pilotage Commission, along with requirements for the
15 apprenticeship and licensing of bar pilots.
16 This bill would repeal the laws establishing the
17 State Pilotage Commission and requirements for bar
18 pilots.
19 However, this bill would not repeal the laws
20 providing for a State Pilotage Commission in Class 2
21 municipalities.
