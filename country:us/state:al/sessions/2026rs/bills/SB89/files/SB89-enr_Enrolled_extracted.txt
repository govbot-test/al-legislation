4 Relating to the State Pilotage Commission and bar
5 pilots; to repeal Article 1 of Chapter 4 of Title 33,
6 consisting of Sections 33-4-1 through 33-4-14, Code of Alabama
7 1975, establishing the State Pilotage Commission; and to
8 repeal Article 2 of Chapter 4 of Title 33, consisting of
9 Sections 33-4-30 through 33-4-57, Code of Alabama 1975,
10 establishing requirements for bar pilots in this state.
11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
12 Section 1. Article 1 of Chapter 4 of Title 33,
13 consisting of Sections 33-4-1 through 33-4-14, Code of Alabama
14 1975, establishing the State Pilotage Commission, and Article
15 2 of Chapter 4 of Title 33, consisting of Sections 33-4-30
16 through 33-4-57, Code of Alabama 1975, establishing
17 requirements for bar pilots in this state, are repealed.
18 Section 2. This act shall become effective on June 1,
19 2026.
