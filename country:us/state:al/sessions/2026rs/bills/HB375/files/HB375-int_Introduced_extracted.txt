4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, the Department of Human
6 Resources arranges protective services for adults in
7 need of care and protection.
8 This bill would require the department to
9 develop a dementia training program about how to
10 identify individuals with Alzheimer's or other
11 dementias, common safety risks, and best practices for
12 servicing those individuals.
13 This bill would require individuals who are
14 employed by or contracted with the department to
15 investigate complaints about adults in need of care and
16 protection to complete a dementia training program upon
17 the start of employment or contract assignment, and
18 would require continuing education relating to dementia
19 for those individuals every two years.
20 This bill would also authorize the department to
21 adopt rules.

44 (2) Common safety risks, including wandering, falling,

46 (3) Communication impairments and behavioral changes.
47 (4) Best practices for interacting with and providing
48 services to individuals with dementia, including
49 considerations for preventing abuse and neglect.

52 a. General information about Alzheimer's disease and
