4 SYNOPSIS:
5 This bill would establish the Steel and Advanced
6 Manufacturing Competitiveness Act that will support
7 steel and heavy manufacturing sectors by providing
8 financial assistance, procurement preferences, and
9 innovation incentives to counteract global trade
10 disruptions and maintain industrial competitiveness.
