6 A BILL
7 TO BE ENTITLED
8 AN ACT
9
10 Relating to the Alabama Sunset Law; to continue the
11 existence and functioning of the Alabama Behavior Analyst
12 Licensing Board operating within the Department of Mental
13 Health with certain modifications; to amend Sections 34-5A-1,
14 34-5A-2, 34-5A-3, 34-5A-4, 34-5A-5, 34-5A-6, and 34-5A-7, Code
15 of Alabama 1975, to rename the board as the Alabama Behavior
16 Analyst Advisory Council; to provide the Department of Mental
17 Health with regulatory authority over licensed behavior
18 analysts and licensed assistant behavior analysts practicing
19 behavior analysis under the former board; to require the
20 advisory council to advise the department on matters relating
21 to the practice of behavior analysis; to provide for the
22 duties of the department and the Mental Health Commissioner
23 relating to the advisory council; to require the department to
24 provide all necessary administrative services for the advisory
25 council; to transfer all property of the advisory council to
26 the department; and to repeal Section 34-5A-8, Code of Alabama
27 1975, to remove the former board from review by the Sunset
28 Committee.
