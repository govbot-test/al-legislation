4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, termination dates are
6 established for enumerated state agencies. The agencies
7 are periodically reviewed by the Alabama Sunset
8 Committee. After the review process is completed, the
9 committee prepares its recommendation for the agencies
10 to the Legislature in the form of sunset bills which
11 either continue, terminate, or continue with
12 modification each agency reviewed.
13 This bill would provide for the continuance of
14 the Alabama Behavior Analyst Licensing Board operating
15 within the Department of Mental Health with the
16 following changes: To rename the board as the Alabama
17 Behavior Analyst Advisory Council; to provide the
18 Department of Mental Health with regulatory authority
19 over licensed behavior analysts and licensed assistant
20 behavior analysts practicing behavior analysis under
21 the former board; to require the advisory council to
22 advise the department on matters relating to the
23 practice of behavior analysis; to provide for the
24 duties of the department and the Mental Health
25 Commissioner relating to the advisory council; to
26 require the department to provide all necessary
27 administrative services for the advisory council; to
28 transfer all property of the advisory council to the
