34 Section 1. Section 41-9-623.2 is added to the Code of
35 Alabama 1975, to read as follows:
36 §41-9-623.2
37 (a) This section shall be known as the Sergeant WyTasha
38 Carter Truth and Transparency Act.
39 (b) Not later than July 1, 2026, and February 1,
40 annually thereafter, each criminal justice agency shall report
41 all of the following on a form prescribed by the Alabama State
42 Law Enforcement Agency:
43 (1) The number of sworn law enforcement officers
44 employed by the criminal justice agency.
45 (2) Whether the employed law enforcement officers are
46 certified by the Alabama Peace Officers' Standards and
47 Training Commission, or otherwise recognized in state law as a
48 law enforcement officer with the authority to arrest an
49 individual for the commission of a criminal offense.
50 (c) Each criminal justice agency shall submit the
51 report to the Alabama Criminal Justice Information Commission
52 who shall share that information with the Alabama State Law
53 Enforcement Agency and the Attorney General.
54 (d) The Alabama State Law Enforcement Agency shall
55 compile the reports and annually publish the reports or the
56 results of the reports on the agency's official website.
