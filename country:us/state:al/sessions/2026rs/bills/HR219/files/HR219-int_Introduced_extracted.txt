5 FOUNDATION'S 20TH ANNUAL "HONORING OUR ELDERS DAY" GALA.
6
7 WHEREAS, with great joy and special pleasure, we
8 honor Dr. Evelyn Penn as the special guest at the Rosetta
9 James Foundation's 20th Annual "Honoring Our Elders Day"
10 Gala at the Jackson Center in Huntsville, Alabama, on
11 Saturday, March 14, 2026; and
12 WHEREAS, Dr. Penn is a native of Hillsboro, Alabama;
13 she graduated salutatorian and matriculated to Alabama A&M
14 University where she earned a Bachelor of Science in
15 Mathematics and a Master of Science in Computer Science; she
16 later received a Doctor of Theology degree from the
17 Minnesota Graduate School of Theology, focusing on
18 Pneumatology; and
19 WHEREAS, Dr. Penn's life uniquely bridges faith and
20 science; for more than 37 years, Dr. Penn served as an
21 Aerospace Engineer at NASA's Marshall Space Flight Center;
22 her work places her among the pioneering African American
23 women whose brilliance strengthened America's space program;
24 simultaneously, she has devoted more than four decades to
25 ministry, traveling nationally as a worship leader and
26 teacher; she has reached many more students of theology
27 through radio and television; she currently serves as an
28 instructor at O.L. Meadows Bible College and is an active

30 WHEREAS, Dr. Penn is a member of Alpha Kappa Alpha

34 WHEREAS, the mission of the Rosetta James Foundation

52 legacies of those who came before them; and
