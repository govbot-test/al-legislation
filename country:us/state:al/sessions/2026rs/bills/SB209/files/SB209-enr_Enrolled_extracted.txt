4 Relating to sex education in public K-12 schools; to
5 amend Section 16-40A-2, Code of Alabama 1975, to require sex
6 education and human reproductive process curricula and
7 programs to teach sexual risk avoidance and encourage
8 abstinence from all sexual activity; to prohibit the promotion
9 of abortions or any sexual activity in sex education or human
10 reproductive process curricula; to prohibit demonstrations of
11 contraceptives in sex education or human reproductive process
12 curricula; to authorize parents or guardians to opt their
13 child out of sex education or human reproductive process
14 curricula or programs; and to authorize the Attorney General
15 to enforce the act.
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
17 Section 1. Section 16-40A-2, Code of Alabama 1975, is
18 amended to read as follows:
19 "§16-40A-2
20 (a) Any program or curriculum in the public schools in
21 Alabama that includes sex education or the human reproductive
22 process shall teach sexual risk avoidance content and, at a
23 minimum, include and emphasize each of the following:
24 (1) Abstinence from sex all sexual activity is the only
25 completely100 percent effective protection against way to
26 prevent unintended pregnancy, sexually transmitted diseases
27 and infections, and human immunodeficiency virus (HIV) when
28 transmitted sexually. For the purposes of this section,
