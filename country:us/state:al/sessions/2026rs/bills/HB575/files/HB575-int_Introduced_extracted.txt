4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, ballots are required to have
6 a mechanism for straight political party voting.
7 This bill would prohibit ballots from including
8 a mechanism for straight political party voting.
9 This bill would also repeal code sections that
10 provide for certain procedures necessary due to
11 straight political party voting.
