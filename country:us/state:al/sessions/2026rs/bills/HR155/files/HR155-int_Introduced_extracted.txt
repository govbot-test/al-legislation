5 HIS EIGHTH PASTORAL ANNIVERSARY AT AGAPE MISSIONARY BAPTIST
6 CHURCH.
7
8 WHEREAS, it is both a privilege and an honor to
9 recognize Pastor Chauncey Ballard for his exemplary and
10 steadfast service to Agape Missionary Baptist Church in
11 Montgomery, Alabama; and
12 WHEREAS, Pastor Ballard has faithfully served in the
13 ministry for more than 27 years, including the past eight
14 years as Pastor of Agape Missionary Baptist Church, during
15 which time he has labored tirelessly to proclaim the Word of
16 God and provide comfort, counsel, and spiritual guidance to
17 all who seek it; and
18 WHEREAS, in addition to his pastoral leadership,
19 Pastor Ballard serves as a chaplain with Ross-Clayton
20 Funeral Home, offering compassion and solace to families
21 during times of profound loss and grief; and
22 WHEREAS, under Pastor Ballard's visionary and
23 steadfast leadership, Agape Missionary Baptist Church has
24 flourished as a beacon of faith, worship, discipleship,
25 outreach, and service throughout Montgomery and the
26 surrounding communities; and
27 WHEREAS, Pastor Ballard exemplifies servant
28 leadership, humility, perseverance, and Christlike
Page 1
leadership, humility, perseverance, and Christlike
HR155 INTRODUCED
29 compassion, and his tenure as Pastor stands as a testament
30 to his unwavering devotion to his calling, his congregation,
31 and the broader community he so faithfully serves; and
32 WHEREAS, the celebration of Pastor Ballard's eighth
33 pastoral anniversary marks a distinguished milestone that
34 reflects his dedication, spiritual fortitude, and the deep
35 trust and affection bestowed upon him by the membership of
36 Agape Missionary Baptist Church; now therefore,
37 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE
38 LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA, That Pastor Chauncey Ballard is
39 hereby highly honored and commended upon the occasion of his
40 eighth pastoral anniversary at Agape Missionary Baptist
41 Church, and this resolution is presented to him with
42 profound admiration and gratitude for his faithful ministry
43 and exemplary community service, together with sincere best
44 wishes for continued success, strength, and abundant
45 blessings in the years to come.
