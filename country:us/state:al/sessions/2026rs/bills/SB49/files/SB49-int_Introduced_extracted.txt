21 Section 1. (a) For the purposes of this section, the
22 following terms have the following meanings:
23 (1) LAW ENFORCEMENT OFFICER. As defined in Section
24 36-21-40, Code of Alabama 1975.
25 (2) TASER. Any mechanism that is designed to emit or
26 project an electronic, magnetic, or other type of charge or
27 shock for the purpose of temporarily incapacitating an
28 individual.
