1 Enrolled, An Act,

3 Relating to the Administrative Office of Courts; to
4 amend Section 12-5-11, Code of Alabama 1975; to authorize the
5 Administrative Office of Courts to form and use a nonprofit
6 entity for judicial education expenses; to authorize the
7 entity to maintain accounts outside the State Treasury which
8 would be audited or reviewed annually; to authorize the
9 Administrative Office of Courts and other state agencies to
10 transfer funds to the entity to support judicial education; to
11 provide that this act is remedial and curative; to exempt the
12 Alabama Judicial College Education Fund from paying sales and
13 use taxes; and to make nonsubstantive, technical revisions to
14 update the existing code language to current style.
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:

62 Section 2. (a) The Alabama Judicial College Education
63 Fund is exempt from paying state sales and use taxes.
64 (b) Any county or municipality may exempt this entity
65 from the payment of county or municipal sales and use taxes in

68 September 1, 2026, through August 31, 2031.
