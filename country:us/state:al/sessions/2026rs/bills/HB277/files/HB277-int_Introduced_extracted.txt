4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Current rules prohibit possessing oysters for a
6 commercial purpose that have been shucked or otherwise
7 processed by an establishment without a permit from the
8 Alabama Department of Public Health.
9 This bill would authorize oyster aquaculture
10 licensees to harvest, shuck, and serve oysters from the
11 aquaculture facility for product tasting.

22 Section 1. (a) For purposes of this section, the term
23 "product tasting" means providing oysters at no cost to the
24 consumer for sampling to determine the qualities of the
25 oysters such as taste and texture.

27 including a permit or licensure requirement, an oyster
28 aquaculture licensee may harvest, shuck, and serve oysters
