4 SYNOPSIS:
5 This bill would enact the College and Higher
6 Education Excellence and Results (CHEER) Act to
7 establish a program to provide bonus funding to higher
8 education institutions that meet identified student and
9 institution performance goals and objectives.
10 This bill would also establish the
11 Outcomes-Based Higher Education Funding Coordinating
12 Committee and provide for its duties and
13 responsibilities.
