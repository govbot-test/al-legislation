10 Proposing an amendment to Section 127 of the
11 Constitution of Alabama of 2022, relating to the Office of
12 Lieutenant Governor; to require the office to be filled by
13 election rather than remain vacant in certain circumstances;
14 and to require any expenditure of legislative funds for travel
15 to be approved by a legislative officer.

PART II.
SB271 Engrossed
85 The following provision is added to the Constitution of
86 Alabama of 2022 to read as follows:
87 Notwithstanding any other provision of the
88 constitution, any expenditure of funds appropriated for use by
89 members of the Legislature for travel shall be approved by an
90 officer of the Legislature elected from the full membership of
91 the respective chamber.
92 Upon ratification of this constitutional amendment, the
93 Code Commissioner shall number and place this amendment as
94 appropriate in the constitution, which may include inserting a
95 provision into an existing section, omitting this
96 instructional paragraph and may make the following
97 nonsubstantive revisions: change capitalization, hierarchy,
98 spelling, and punctuation for purposes of style and
99 uniformity; correct manifest grammatical, clerical, and
100 typographical errors; revise internal or external citations
101 and cross-references; harmonize language; and translate
102 effective dates.
103 END OF PROPOSED AMENDMENT
104 Section 2. An election upon the proposed amendment
105 shall be held in accordance with Sections 284 and 285 of the
106 Constitution of Alabama of 2022, and the election laws of this
107 state. The appropriate election official shall assign a ballot
108 number for the proposed constitutional amendment on the
109 election ballot and shall set forth the following description
110 of the substance or subject matter of the proposed
111 constitutional amendment:
112 "Proposing an amendment to the Constitution of Alabama
