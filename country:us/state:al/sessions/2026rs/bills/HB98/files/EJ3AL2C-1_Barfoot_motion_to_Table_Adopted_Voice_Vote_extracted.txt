4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Existing law provides that in certain
6 circumstances a child or a spouse of a law enforcement
7 officer who is killed or totally disabled in the line
8 of duty may receive financial assistance to pay college
9 tuition.
10 This bill would establish a program to pay
11 tuition and mandatory fees on behalf of children and
12 spouses of long-term city, county, and state law
13 enforcement officers. This bill would provide that the
14 scholarship money may be used for an undergraduate
15 course of study offered by a public technical school,
16 college, or university in the State of Alabama.
17 This bill would further provide for the
18 administration of this scholarship by the Alabama
19 Commission on Higher Education; require annual
20 reporting on the program to the Legislature; and
21 establish the Alabama Law Enforcement Officers' Family
22 Scholarship Fund.
23 This bill would also authorize creation by the
24 Department of Revenue of a blackout license plate,
25 proceeds for which would be used to fund the
26 scholarship.
