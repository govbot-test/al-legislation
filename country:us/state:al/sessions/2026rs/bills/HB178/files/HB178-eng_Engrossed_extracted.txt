9 Relating to public K-12 education; to provide a process
10 for the creation of a consolidated city school system by
11 merging two or more city boards of education; to provide a
12 procedure for protesting a proposed consolidation of two or
13 more city school systems; and to require the State Board of
14 Education to adopt rules.
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
16 Section 1. (a) Whenever two or more contiguous city
17 boards of education deem it advisable to consolidate the
18 administration of their respective city school systems under
19 one remaining controlling city board of education, each city
20 board of education shall adopt and record within the minutes
21 of each board an agreement providing for the consolidation. A
22 formal resolution adopted by all members of the agreeing
23 boards shall detail and provide for the payment of their
24 respective indebtedness, specify how the consolidation will
25 take place, identify which city board of education will be the
26 remaining controlling city board of education and which city
27 board or boards of education will be abolished, and designate
28 the time frame during which the consolidation will be
