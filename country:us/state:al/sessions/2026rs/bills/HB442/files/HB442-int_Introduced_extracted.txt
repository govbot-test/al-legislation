9 Relating to Escambia County; to amend Section
10 45-27-247.07, Code of Alabama 1975, to further provide for the
11 administration of the cigarette and beer tax; and to repeal
12 Act 575, 1953 Regular Session (Acts 1953, p. 818), relating to
13 the beer tax.
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
15 Section 1. Section 45-27-247.07, Code of Alabama 1975,
16 is amended to read as follows:
17 "§45-27-247.07
18 (a) The governing body of the county is hereby given
19 the right, power, and authority to promulgate and may adopt
20 rules and regulations governing the collection of the tax
21 hereby imposed by this subpart, if it is necessary so to do in
22 order to more effectually carry out the terms and provisions
23 of this subpart, but it shall not have the right to alter or
24 change the distribution of the tax as herein provided for.
25 (b) The county governing body shall make available to
26 supply the judge of probate with ample funds from the county
27 general fund for the to purchase of stamps and other materials
28 and or supplies needed by the judge of probate for carrying

33 hundred fifty dollars ($450) per month from the funds derived

49 including salaries shall not exceed five hundred dollars
50 ($500) per month and shall be paid from the county general
51 fund. However, the county general fund shall be reimbursed for
52 all monies expended in connection with the administration and
53 enforcement of the beer tax as provided by general law.
54 Section 3. Act 575, 1953 Regular Session (Acts 1953, p.
