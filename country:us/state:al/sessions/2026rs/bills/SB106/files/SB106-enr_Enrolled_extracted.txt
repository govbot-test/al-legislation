1 Enrolled, An Act,
2
3
4 Relating to jury service; to amend Section 12-16-62,
5 Code of Alabama 1975; to allow individuals 80 years of age or
6 older to request a permanent exemption from jury service.
7 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
8 Section 1. Section 12-16-62, Code of Alabama 1975, is
9 amended to read as follows:
10 "§12-16-62
11 No qualified prospective juror is exempt from jury
12 service, except as provided in subsection (b).
13 (b) An individual who is 80 years of age or older may
14 submit a written statement to the court requesting that he or
15 she be permanently exempt from jury service. On receipt of the
16 request, the court shall remove the prospective juror from the
17 master jury list."
18 Section 2. This act shall become effective on October
19 1, 2026.
