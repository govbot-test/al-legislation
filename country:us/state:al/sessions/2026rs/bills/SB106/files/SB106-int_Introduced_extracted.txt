4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, an individual may be excused
6 from jury service for a 24 month period only in certain
7 situations.
8 This bill would allow an individual 80 years of
9 age or older, upon the individual's request, to be
10 permanently exempt from jury service.

13 A BILL
14 TO BE ENTITLED
15 AN ACT
16
17 Relating to jury service; to amend Section 12-16-62,
18 Code of Alabama 1975; to allow individuals 80 years of age or
19 older to request a permanent exemption from jury service.
20 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
21 Section 1. Section 12-16-62, Code of Alabama 1975, is
22 amended to read as follows:
23 "§12-16-62
24 No qualified prospective juror is exempt from jury
25 service, except as provided in subsection (b).
26 (b) An individual who is 80 years of age or older may
27 submit a written statement to the court requesting that he or
28 she be permanently exempt from jury service. On receipt of the
