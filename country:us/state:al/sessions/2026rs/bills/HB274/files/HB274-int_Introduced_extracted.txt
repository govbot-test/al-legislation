4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, persons are prohibited from
6 operating unmanned aircraft systems within a certain
7 distance of a correctional facility, with certain
8 specified exemptions.
9 This bill would authorize the Department of
10 Corrections to take reasonable and necessary measures
11 against unmanned aircraft systems operating in
12 violation of the prohibition against operating unmanned
13 aircraft systems near a correctional facility.

20 Relating to crimes and offenses; to add Section
21 13A-7-94.1 to the Code of Alabama 1975; to authorize the
22 Department of Corrections to take certain actions regarding
23 unmanned aircraft systems.
