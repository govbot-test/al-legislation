6 WHEREAS, the Alabama Legislature takes great pride in
7 recognizing outstanding accomplishments by our state's
8 students and athletes; and
9 WHEREAS, the 2024–2025 season marked a historic
10 milestone for the Chattahoochee Valley Community College
11 Lady Pirates Basketball Team, as the team captured its
12 first-ever Alabama Community College Conference (ACCC) State
13 Championship with a hard-fought victory over Snead State;
14 the Lady Pirates dominated the ACCC Division II conference,
15 completing a perfect 12–0 regular season and securing the
16 Regular Season Championship; and
17 WHEREAS, this remarkable run continued as the team
18 claimed the program's first Gulf South District Championship
19 with a win over Palm Beach State College, earning a berth in
20 the National Junior College Athletic Association (NJCAA)
21 Division II National Tournament in Joplin, Missouri; at the
22 national tournament, the Lady Pirates secured their
23 first-ever tournament victory with a 19-point win over
24 Bryant & Stratton Community College, finishing the season
25 with an impressive 22–8 record; and
26 WHEREAS, individual honors highlighted the team's
27 success; freshman standout Zy Thompson earned NJCAA Third
28 Team All-American honors and First Team All-Conference
