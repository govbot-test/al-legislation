HB465 Enrolled
1 Enrolled, An Act,
2
3 Relating to motor vehicle registration and license
4 plates; to authorize the issuance of a distinctive license
5 plate decal to individuals who have certain medical conditions
6 and to the primary caregivers of those individuals.
7 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
8 Section 1. (a) For purposes of this section, the term
9 "invisible disability" means a physical or mental condition
10 that may interfere with an individual's ability to communicate
11 with a first responder, including:
12 (1) A communication impediment;
13 (2) Hearing loss;
14 (3) Blindness or a visual impairment;
15 (4) Autism spectrum disorder;
16 (5) Traumatic brain injury;
17 (6) Schizophrenia;
18 (7) Alzheimer's disease or dementia;
19 (8) Post traumatic stress disorder;
20 (9) Epilepsy;
21 (10) A developmental disability;
22 (11) Down syndrome;
23 (12) Diabetes;
24 (13) A heart condition; or
25 (14) A cognitive disability.
26 (b) An owner of a motor vehicle who is a resident of
27 Alabama and who has been diagnosed with an invisible
28 disability or an individual who is the primary caretaker of an
Page 1
disability or an individual who is the primary caretaker of an
HB465 Enrolled
29 individual who has been diagnosed with an invisible disability
30 shall be issued a license plate of the individual's choosing
31 and a "Invisible Disability" license plate decal upon:
32 (1) Application to the judge of probate, license
33 commissioner, or other issuing official;
34 (2) Compliance with motor vehicle registration and
35 licensing laws;
36 (3) Payment of regular fees required by law for license
37 plates for private passenger or pleasure motor vehicles.
38 (4) Payment of any additional fees required by law for
39 distinctive license plates, should the individual seek to be
40 issued a distinctive license plate; and
41 (5) Presentation of a diagnosis from a physician
42 licensed to practice medicine in this state as proof of
43 eligibility.
44 (c) The validity of the "Invisible Disability" license
45 plate decals shall coincide with the reissuance of the
46 registrant's license plate as provided in Section 32-6-62,
47 Code of Alabama 1975.
48 (d) Payment of required license fees and taxes for the
49 years during which a new plate is not issued shall be
50 evidenced as provided in Section 32-6-63, Code of Alabama
51 1975.
52 (e) Kulturecity, Incorporated shall be responsible for
53 the design of the license plate decal subject to approval by
54 the Commissioner of Revenue and compliance with all laws and
55 rules.
56 (f) The "Invisible Disability" decal provided by this
Page 2
(f) The "Invisible Disability" decal provided by this
HB465 Enrolled
57 section shall not qualify the individual to be parked in
58 special access parking as provided in Sections 40-12-300 and
59 32-6-231, Code of Alabama 1975, unless the individual has been
60 issued a disability distinctive special access plate or
61 placard as provided for in those sections.
62 (g) The Department of Revenue may adopt rules to
63 implement this section.
64 Section 2. This act shall become effective on January
65 1, 2027.
