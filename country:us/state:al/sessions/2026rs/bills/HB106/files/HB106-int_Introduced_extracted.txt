4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, a person commits the crime
6 of doxing if he or she intentionally electronically
7 shares personal identifying information of another
8 individual with the intent that others will use that
9 information to harass or harm the other individual, and
10 the other individual is actually harassed or harmed.
11 This bill would further provide that a person
12 commits the crime of doxing if he or she intentionally
13 electronically shares personal identifying information
14 of another individual with the intent that others will
15 use that information to harass or harm the other
16 individual, regardless of whether the individual is
17 actually harmed.
18 This bill would also further provide for
19 penalties of initial and subsequent violations.
