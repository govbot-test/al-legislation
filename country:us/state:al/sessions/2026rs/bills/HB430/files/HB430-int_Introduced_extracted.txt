4 A BILL
5 TO BE ENTITLED
6 AN ACT
7
8
9 Relating to Montgomery County; to authorize the judge
10 of probate to conduct fundraising activities; to provide for
11 the use of the proceeds; and to provide that this act is
12 remedial and curative.
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
14 Section 1. (a) The Judge of Probate of Montgomery
15 County may conduct fundraising events, including, but not
16 limited to, rodeos and may use the proceeds and profits from
17 the events for any lawful purpose relating to the operation of
18 the office of the judge of probate.
19 (b) This section is remedial and curative and any
20 actions taken by the judge of probate in conformance with
21 subsection (a) are ratified and confirmed.
22 Section 2. This act shall become effective immediately.
