HB430 Enrolled
2
3 Relating to Montgomery County; to authorize the judge
4 of probate to conduct fundraising activities; to provide for
5 the use of the proceeds; and to provide that this act is
6 remedial and curative.
7 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
8 Section 1. (a) The Judge of Probate of Montgomery
9 County may conduct fundraising events, including, but not
10 limited to, rodeos and may use the proceeds and profits from
11 the events for any lawful purpose relating to the operation of
12 the office of the judge of probate.
13 (b) This section is remedial and curative and any
14 actions taken by the judge of probate in conformance with
15 subsection (a) are ratified and confirmed.
16 Section 2. This act shall become effective immediately.
