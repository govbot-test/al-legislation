5 HALEYVILLE HIGH SCHOOL.
6
7 WHEREAS, highest commendations are herein proudly
8 extended to Coach Jamie White for his outstanding service
9 and many contributions to Haleyville High School; and
10 WHEREAS, Coach White has served as an educator for
11 more than 13 years, during which time he has positively
12 influenced countless students through his commitment to
13 leadership, mentorship, and character development; and
14 WHEREAS, while Coach White has coached multiple
15 sports during his tenure, his true passion and greatest
16 impact have been realized through his leadership of the
17 Haleyville High School golf program; and
18 WHEREAS, Coach White began coaching golf in 2020 and
19 swiftly transformed the program into a championship-caliber
20 powerhouse, now widely recognized as one of the premier high
21 school golf programs in the State of Alabama; and
22 WHEREAS, under his exceptional leadership, the
23 Haleyville High School golf team has compiled an outstanding
24 overall record of 70–8, has remained undefeated in
25 regular-season competition throughout his tenure, and has
26 suffered only one loss to a Class 4A opponent in six years;
27 and
28 WHEREAS, all tournament losses during this period
