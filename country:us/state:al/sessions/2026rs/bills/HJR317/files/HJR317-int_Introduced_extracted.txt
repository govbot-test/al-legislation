5 ALABAMA STATE HOUSE.
6
7 WHEREAS, the Legislature of Alabama, pursuant to
8 Section 48.02 of the Constitution of Alabama of 2022, is
9 authorized to designate by resolution a suitable place for
10 the meeting of the Legislature, which place shall be known
11 as the Alabama State House; and
12 WHEREAS, the Legislature will be relocating its
13 principal operations to the newly constructed legislative
14 building located within the Capitol Complex of Montgomery,
15 Alabama; and
16 WHEREAS, it is necessary and appropriate for purposes
17 of public identification, clarity in official records, and
18 continuity of legislative operations that the new
19 legislative building be formally designated as the Alabama
20 State House; now therefore,
21 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA, BOTH
22 HOUSES THEREOF CONCURRING, That certain State of Alabama
23 property located in the Capitol Complex of Montgomery,
24 Alabama, bounded by state property formerly fronting Pelham
25 Street on the North, Jackson Street on the East, Ripley
26 Street on the West, and Washington Avenue on the South is
27 hereby designated as the place of meeting for the Senate and
28 House of Representatives of the Alabama Legislature for
Page 1
House of Representatives of the Alabama Legislature for

29 official legislative business and duties beginning January
30 1, 2027.
