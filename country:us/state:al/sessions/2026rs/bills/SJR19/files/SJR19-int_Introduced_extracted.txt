4 SJR___ HONORING THE LEGACY AND EXCEPTIONAL LIFE OF ANNA
5 SMITH BEDSOLE HOLMES.
6
7 WHEREAS, it is fitting and proper that the
8 Legislature of Alabama honor the life of The Honorable Anna
9 "Ann" Smith Bedsole Holmes, a trailblazing public servant,
10 conservationist, philanthropist, and champion for Alabama's
11 children and communities, whose passing on December 1, 2025,
12 marked the loss of one of the state's most influential
13 leaders; and
14 WHEREAS, born in Selma and reared in Jackson,
15 Alabama, Ann Bedsole Holmes was educated at The University
16 of Alabama and the University of Denver; she devoted her
17 life to public service, breaking historic barriers as the
18 first Republican woman elected to the Alabama House of
19 Representatives and the first woman elected to the Alabama
20 Senate; and
21 WHEREAS, during her legislative career, she advanced
22 transformative initiatives, including modernized inheritance
23 rights for widows, the creation of Alabama's 911 emergency
24 system, and major conservation efforts, later serving as
25 founding president of the Alabama Forest Resources Center
26 and preserving thousands of acres of green space across the
27 state; and
28 WHEREAS, her passion for education led to the
