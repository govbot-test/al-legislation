4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, an individual seeking
6 compensation from the Alabama Crime Victims
7 Compensation Commission must file a claim within one
8 year of the injury or death upon which the claim is
9 based.
10 This bill would provide that an individual
11 seeking compensation from the Alabama Crime Victims
12 Compensation Commission may file a claim within two
13 years of the injury or death upon which the claim is
14 based.
