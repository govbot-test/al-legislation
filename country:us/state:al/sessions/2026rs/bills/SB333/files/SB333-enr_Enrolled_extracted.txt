4 Relating to Class 2 municipalities; to further provide
5 for the powers of certain industrial development boards.
6 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
7 Section 1. (a) For the purposes of this section, the
8 following terms have the following meanings:
9 (1) AUTHORIZING MUNICIPALITY. A Class 2 municipality.
10 (2) DEVELOPMENT PROJECTS. Any interests in land,
11 buildings, structures, facilities, or other improvements and
12 any fixtures, machinery, equipment, furniture, or other
13 property of any nature whatsoever used on, in, or in
14 connection with any such land, interest in land, building,
15 structure, facility, or other improvement, all for the
16 essential public purpose of the development of trade,
17 commerce, industry, and employment opportunities in the
18 corporate limits of the authorizing municipality for any
19 industrial, commercial, business, office, parking, utility,
20 residential, including without limitation homes, apartments,
21 town houses, condominiums, hotels, and motels, or other use,
22 provided that a majority of the members of the board
23 determine, by a resolution duly adopted, that the development
24 project and the use thereof would further the public purpose
25 of this section. Any such resolution shall include findings
26 that the primary purpose of the development project is to
27 advance one or more public purposes of this section,
28 including, without limitation: (i) the creation or retention

41 (4) INDUSTRIAL DEVELOPMENT BOARD. Any public
42 corporation now in existence or hereafter organized pursuant
43 to the provisions of the Industrial Development Board Act in a
44 Class 2 municipality.
45 (b) The purpose of this section is to grant industrial
46 development boards organized in a Class 2 municipality,
47 whether existing or formed after June 1, 2026, in addition to
48 those powers conferred on them, the power to issue and sell
49 bonds and to acquire, construct, expand, improve, replace,
50 equip, maintain, operate, lease, and dispose of development
51 projects. This section shall be liberally construed to carry
52 out its powers.
53 (c) In addition to any powers that are conferred on
54 industrial development boards by the Industrial Development
55 Board Act, each industrial development board within a Class 2
56 municipality shall have the following powers with respect to
