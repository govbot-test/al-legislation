4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Existing law authorizes municipalities to levy a
6 business license on certain businesses operating within
7 their jurisdictions.
8 This bill would provide for the circumstances
9 when a municipality may levy a business license on
10 certain general contractors, subcontractors, engineers,
11 and land surveyors.

15 TO BE ENTITLED
16 AN ACT

18 Relating to municipalities; to add Section 11-51-133 to
19 the Code of Alabama 1975, to limit municipalities from levying

21 subcontractors, engineers, and land surveyors.
