6 WHEREAS, it is with great pride and special pleasure
7 that Mitchie Clegg Neel is highly congratulated and
8 commended for her dedication and devotion to the Blount
9 County School System over the last 48 years; and
10 WHEREAS, Mitchie began her career in 1978 at
11 Blountsville Elementary School; she was an exemplary
12 elementary teacher and taught there for 20 years; she
13 transitioned to Curriculum Coordinator for Blount County
14 Schools in 1998 and served to promote instruction,
15 curriculum development, and professional development for
16 teachers for 11 years; she retired from this position in
17 2009; and
18 WHEREAS, her influence and impact reached even
19 further beyond the classroom when she began working on the

22 years and has served as its Executive Director for the last
23 17 years; and
