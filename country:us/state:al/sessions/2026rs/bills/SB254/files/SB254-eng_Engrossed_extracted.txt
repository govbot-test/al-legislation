9 Relating to parole; to amend Section 15-22-32, Code of
10 Alabama 1975, as last amended by Act 2025-273, 2025 Regular
11 Session, to further provide for the authority of the Board of
12 Pardons and Paroles relating to parole; to provide various
13 conditions where the board would be required to revoke parole;
14 to provide various conditions where the board would have
15 discretion as to the imposed punishment for a parole
16 violation; and to provide various conditions where the board
17 would be authorized to require a parolee to serve a sentence
18 for a parole violation in a residential transition center or
19 consenting county jail.

185 parolee and the supervising parole officer, with approval of a
186 supervisor, the parolee may be treated, monitored, or confined
187 for the period recommended in the violation report and
188 designated on the waiver. The parolee may not request a review
189 if he or she has signed a written waiver of rights as provided
190 in this subsection.
191 (g) The board shall adopt guidelines and procedures to
192 implement the requirements of this section, which shall
193 include the requirement of a supervisor's approval prior to
194 exercise of the delegation of authority authorized by
195 subsection (e)."
