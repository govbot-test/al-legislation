5 BASKETBALL TEAM FOR WINNING THE 2026 ALABAMA HIGH SCHOOL
6 ATHLETIC ASSOCIATION 1A STATE BASKETBALL CHAMPIONSHIP.
7
8 WHEREAS, commendations and congratulations are herein
9 proudly extended to the Winterboro High School Girls
10 Basketball Team on winning the Alabama High School Athletic
11 Association (AHSAA) 1A State Championship on March 5, 2026,
12 at BJCC Legacy Arena in Birmingham, Alabama; and
13 WHEREAS, the Winterboro Bulldogs, through teamwork,
14 diligence, and an unwavering commitment to excellence,
15 defeated the Marion County Raiders 67–60, closing out the
16 most historic year in the program's history; it was not only
17 the first girls' state championship but also the first state
18 basketball championship for any school in the Talladega
19 County School System; and
20 WHEREAS, the Bulldogs secured the Class 1A title
21 thanks in large part to their "Special K" lineup: Class 1A
22 State Tournament MVP Katelyn Brown scored 17 points in the
23 finals and added five rebounds, three assists, and two
24 steals; Kaleria Massingale and Kailynn Williams earned Class
25 1A All-Tournament honors with 13 and 15 points,
26 respectively; Katelyn Jones added 10 points; and Kaniya
27 Duncan had eight points and a team-high nine rebounds; and
28 WHEREAS, the Winterboro High School Girls Basketball

29 Program has been superbly coached and inspired by Head Coach
30 Becca Williams and Assistant Coach Len Wilson; under their
31 leadership, the players have broadened their knowledge of

33 strengthened the school's tradition of excellence and true

45 therefore,
46 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA, BOTH
47 HOUSES THEREOF CONCURRING, That the 2025–2026 Winterboro
48 High School Lady Bulldogs Basketball Team is congratulated
49 for its athletic achievements, including being named the
50 Alabama High School Athletic Association 1A State Champions,
51 and we hereby dedicate this resolution to the team members,
52 coaches, and fans with the highest admiration, along with
53 sincere best wishes for success in all future endeavors.
