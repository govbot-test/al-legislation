6 AN ACT

9 Relating to Etowah County; to amend Section 45-28-121,
10 Code of Alabama 1975, to provide further for the membership of
11 the personnel board.
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
13 Section 1. Section 45-28-121 of the Code of Alabama
14 1975, is amended to read as follows:
15 "§45-28-121
16 (a) In Etowah County, there is hereby created and
17 established a personnel department for the government and
18 control of all employees and appointees holding positions in
19 the classified service, as defined in Section 45-28-121.03.
20 (b)(1) The personnel department shall consist of a
21 personnel board and a personnel director. The personnel board
22 shall consist of five members. Three members shall be
23 appointed for a term of six years each as follows: One to be
24 appointed by
25 a. Upon the term of the member who is a joint appointee
26 of the judge of probate judge and the sheriff of the county;
27 one to be expiring on September 30, 2028, one member appointed
28 by the judge of probate.
Page 1
by the judge of probate.

29 b. Upon the term of the member appointed by the county
30 commission; and one to be appointed as expiring on September
31 30, 2030, one member appointed by the county commission upon
32 the recommendation of the county engineer.
33 c. Upon the term of the member who is a joint appointee
34 of the county tax assessor and the county tax collector;
35 however, the terms for the above three named initial
36 appointees shall be staggered. For the first appointments the
37 joint appointee of the probate judge and the sheriff of the
38 county shall be for two years; the first appointee by the
39 county commission shall be for a term of four years; and the
40 first joint appointee by the county tax assessor and county
41 tax collector shall be for six years. The other two members of
42 the board shall be appointed for one-year terms. One of the
43 additional members shall be appointed annually expiring on
44 September 30, 2026, one member appointed by the revenue
45 commissioner.
46 d. Upon the term of the member appointed by the
47 President of the Etowah County Chamber of Commerce; and one
48 member shall be appointed annually expiring on September 30,
49 2026, one member appointed by the county commission upon the
50 recommendation of the chief administrative officer.
51 e. Upon the term of the member appointed by the
52 President of the Etowah County Labor Council expiring on
53 September 30, 2026, one member appointed by the President of
54 the Northeast Alabama Labor Council.
55 (2) Subsequent terms on the board shall be for four
56 years and shall begin on October 1.
Page 2
years and shall begin on October 1.
HB633 INTRODUCED
57 (c) The members of the board shall be qualified
58 electors of the county. No person shall be appointed to the
59 board who holds any salaried public office or employment with
60 the county, nor shall any member, while a member of the board
61 or for a period of one year after he or she has ceased to be a
62 member, be eligible for appointment to any salaried office or
63 employment in the service of the county or any county elective
64 office.
65 (d) For regular meetings of the board or special
66 hearings or meetings of the board relating to a pending
67 disciplinary action, each member shall receive sixty dollars
68 ($60) per diem, not to exceed one hundred eighty dollars
69 ($180) per month paid from the county treasury, and the chair
70 shall receive seventy-five dollars ($75) per diem, not to
71 exceed two hundred twenty-five dollars ($225) per month paid
72 from the county treasury."
73 Section 2. This act shall become effective on July 1,
74 2026.
