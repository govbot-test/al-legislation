4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, public schools are required
6 to grant access to military recruiters in the same
7 manner as provided to prospective employers.
8 This bill would require public high schools to
9 designate a school day for military recruiters to
10 administer the Armed Services Vocational Aptitude
11 Battery (ASVAB), on a voluntary basis, on campus to
12 students.
