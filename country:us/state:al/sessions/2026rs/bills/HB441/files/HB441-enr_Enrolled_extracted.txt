1 Enrolled, An Act,
2
3 Relating to Escambia County; to authorize the sheriff,
4 county commission, and a municipality to contract for the
5 sheriff to provide police services for the municipality; to
6 provide for the expenditure of funds; and to authorize the
7 county commission to provide a supplemental salary to the
8 sheriff for the performance of additional duties relating to
9 the contracting of police services for a municipality.
10 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
11 Section 1. (a) Any municipality within Escambia County
12 may contract with the Sheriff of Escambia County for police
13 services. The contract may include enforcement by the sheriff,
14 or his or her duly commissioned deputies, of all laws within
15 the corporate limits and police jurisdiction of the
16 municipality, including municipal ordinances, and the arrest
17 or citation of any offenders as if the sheriff, or his or her
18 duly commissioned deputies, were duly constituted police
19 officers of the municipality.
20 (b) A municipality entering into a contract pursuant to
21 subsection (a) shall pay to the county treasury a sum
22 sufficient to provide the necessary manpower and equipment as
23 mutually agreed upon by the municipal governing body, the
24 sheriff, and the county commission.
25 (c) The county commission shall receive from the
26 municipality the funds paid under the contract for police
27 services and may expend funds for police services in the
28 municipality, including the police jurisdiction of the
Page 1

municipality, including the police jurisdiction of the

29 municipality.
30 (d) Each contract between the sheriff and a
31 municipality must have the consent and approval of the sheriff
32 and the county commission.
33 (e) The sheriff and his or her duly commissioned
34 deputies, in the performance of any duties carried out
35 pursuant to a contract with a municipality, shall retain the
36 legal and constitutional immunity afforded to each of them by
37 law.
38 (f)(1) The Escambia County Commission may by resolution
39 of the commission provide a supplemental salary to the sheriff
40 for the performance of additional duties relating to the
41 contracting of police services for a municipality. The
42 performance of additional duties relating to the contracting
43 of police services for a municipality by the sheriff shall be
44 pursuant to an agreement between the sheriff and the county
45 commission subject to approval of the county commission.
46 (2) The supplemental salary shall be paid by the county
47 for the performance of additional duties by the sheriff and
48 shall not be considered a part of the regular salary of the
49 sheriff. In the event the county commission or sheriff
50 withdraws from the agreement, the supplemental salary shall be
51 discontinued. The supplemental salary provided by this
52 subsection shall not affect or increase the compensation of
53 any supernumerary sheriff in the county.
54 Section 2. This act shall become effective on June 1,
55 2026.
