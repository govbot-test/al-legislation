4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, following an acquittal or
6 dismissal of a felony, misdemeanor, violation, traffic
7 violation, boating violation, or municipal ordinance
8 violation, the defendant may petition the circuit court
9 in which the charges were filed to have the records
10 expunged.
11 This bill would revise the conditions under
12 which a person is eligible to apply for the expungement
13 of his or her criminal records, including lowering the
14 number of days a person must wait following a dismissal
15 of the charge with prejudice or a finding of not
16 guilty.
