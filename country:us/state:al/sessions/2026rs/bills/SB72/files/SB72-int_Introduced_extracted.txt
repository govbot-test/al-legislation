4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, the ReEngage Alabama Grant
6 Program administered by the Alabama Commission on
7 Higher Education provides grant award payments to adult
8 learners pursuing a first associate or baccalaureate
9 degree to assist them in earning short-term
10 credentials.
11 This bill would decrease the minimum age of an
12 adult learner from 25 to 22 years of age.
13 This bill would also include as an eligible
14 student an individual who, in addition to other
15 specific criteria, has not previously received a grant
16 award and is pursuing a first baccalaureate degree
17 following a two-year stop-out period after receiving
18 his or her first associate degree.
