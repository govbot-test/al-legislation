4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Existing law provides that firefighters may be
6 paid benefits for disability or death caused by a
7 firefighter's occupational disease.
8 This bill would add Parkinson's disease to the
9 list of firefighter's occupational diseases. This bill
10 would also provide a standard for demonstrating that
11 Parkinson's disease is occupational in origin in order
12 to qualify for benefits.
13 This bill would also delete duplicative language
14 and would make nonsubstantive, technical revisions to
15 update the existing code language to current style.
