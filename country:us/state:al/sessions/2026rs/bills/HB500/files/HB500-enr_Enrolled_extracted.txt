HB500 Enrolled
1 Enrolled, An Act,
2
3 Relating to Mobile County; to further provide for the
4 office of constable.
5 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
6 Section 1. (a) In Mobile County, beginning with the
7 terms commencing in 2029, there shall be three constables for
8 each Alabama House of Representatives district with territory
9 in the county, to be elected as provided by law. Each
10 constable shall hold office for four years from the first
11 Monday after the second Tuesday next after his or her election
12 and until his or her successor is elected and qualified.
13 (b) Beginning with the terms commencing in 2029, each
14 constable must be a qualified elector of the district he or
15 she represents to be eligible to perform the duties of the
16 office.
17 Section 2. This act shall become effective January 1,
18 2028, contingent on the enactment of a general law authorizing
19 the alteration of the office of constable by local
20 legislation.
