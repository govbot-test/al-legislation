3 COMMENDING ROBERTA SWANN ON HER RETIREMENT AS DIRECTOR OF
4 THE MOBILE BAY NATIONAL ESTUARY PROGRAM.
5
6 WHEREAS, it is with great pride and deep appreciation
7 that we recognize individuals who have devoted their lives
8 to public service and environmental stewardship, improving
9 the quality of life for the people and natural resources of
10 Alabama; and
11 WHEREAS, Roberta Swann has served the Mobile Bay
12 National Estuary Program since 2004, first as Deputy
13 Director, and since 2009, as Director, providing more than
14 two decades of dedicated leadership and guidance through a
15 period of historic growth and impact; and
16 WHEREAS, throughout her tenure, Roberta Swann has
17 demonstrated exceptional vision, determination, and
18 commitment in advancing a community-based approach to
19 watershed management that significantly improved and
20 protected Alabama's coastal and estuarine resources; and
21 WHEREAS, under her leadership, more than $100 million
22 in federal, state, local, and private funding was invested
23 in Alabama's estuaries and coastal watersheds, supporting
24 large-scale restoration, conservation, and resilience
25 initiatives; and
26 WHEREAS, Roberta Swann has been widely recognized for
27 her ability to convene and coordinate diverse stakeholders
28 from government, industry, academia, nonprofit
