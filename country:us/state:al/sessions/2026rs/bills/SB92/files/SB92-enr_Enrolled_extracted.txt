SB92 Enrolled
2

4 Relating to campaign contributions; to prohibit
5 principal campaign committees, political action committees,
6 and political parties from accepting contributions made with a
7 credit card if the billing address associated with the credit
8 card is outside the United States, with certain exceptions for
9 contributions from U.S. citizens living outside the United
10 States.
11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
12 Section 1. (a) No principal campaign committee,
13 political action committee, or political party, as those terms
14 are defined in Section 17-5-2, Code of Alabama 1975, may
15 accept any contribution, as defined in Section 17-5-2, Code of
16 Alabama 1975, made with a credit card via the Internet unless,
17 at the time of making the contribution: (i) the person making
18 the contribution provides to the principal campaign committee,
19 political action committee, or political party the credit card
20 verification value or code; and (ii) except as provided in
21 subsection (b), the billing address associated with the credit
22 card is located in the United States.
23 (b) If the contribution under subsection (a) is made by
24 a U.S. citizen living outside of the United States and the
25 credit card billing address is not a location in the United
26 States, the individual shall provide to the principal campaign
27 committee, political action committee, or political party, at
28 the time of making the contribution, the mailing address of
Page 1
the time of making the contribution, the mailing address of
SB92 Enrolled
29 the location in the United States which the individual uses
30 for voter registration purposes.
31 (c) The treasurer of each principal campaign committee,
32 political action committee, and political party shall maintain
33 the names and addresses of each person making a contribution
34 under this subsection for purposes of making reports under
35 Section 17-5-8, Code of Alabama 1975.
36 (d) Any credit card processor that processes credit
37 card contributions via the Internet to a principal campaign
38 committee, political action committee, or political party
39 without requiring the credit card verification value or code
40 and a zip code located in the United States or a billing
41 address located outside the United States is subject to a
42 forfeiture in an amount equal to the cumulative amount of the
43 contributions.
44 Section 2. This act shall become effective on October
45 1, 2026.
