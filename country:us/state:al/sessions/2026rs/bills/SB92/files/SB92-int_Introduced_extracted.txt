5 This bill would prohibit a principal campaign
6 committee, political action committee, or political
7 party from accepting campaign contributions made with a
8 credit card via the Internet unless the contributor
9 provides the credit card verification value or code and
10 the billing address associated with the credit card is
11 located in the United States, with exceptions made for
12 contributions from U.S. citizens located outside the
13 United States in certain circumstances.
