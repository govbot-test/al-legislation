4 SJR___ MOURNING THE DEATH AND CELEBRATING THE LIFE AND
5 LEGACY OF CLIFFORD RAY MILLER.
6
7 WHEREAS, it is with profound sorrow that we note the
8 peaceful passing of Mr. Clifford "Ray" Miller of Talladega,
9 Alabama, on December 15, 2025, while we also celebrate his
10 long, beautiful life that ended at the age of 90; and
11 WHEREAS, his life was a blessing, and he will be
12 lovingly remembered by the countless lives he touched; and
13 WHEREAS, Ray Miller was born in Ball Play, Alabama,
14 but lived most of his life in Talladega; he was a member of
15 First Methodist Church of Talladega for more than 60 years,
16 where he served as the head usher; and
17 WHEREAS, early in his career, he worked as a manager
18 for B.F. Goodrich in Talladega, Tuscaloosa, and Selma; in
19 1967, he became Executive Director of the Alabama Industries
20 for the Blind (AIB), from where he retired in 1996; during
21 his tenure there, he tripled AIB's workforce, making it the
22 largest employer of visually impaired people in the state
23 and second in the entire country; Ray was honored with the
24 Lifetime Achievement Award from the National Industries of
25 the Blind for his dedication and exemplary service; and
26 WHEREAS, Ray was the embodiment of a selfless public
27 servant, which he demonstrated through his membership on
28 various boards and organizations throughout Talladega; he
