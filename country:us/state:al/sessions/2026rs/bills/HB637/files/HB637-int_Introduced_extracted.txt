4 SYNOPSIS:
5 Under existing law, certain organizations and
6 vendors are exempt from payment of state, county, and
7 municipal sales and use taxes.
8 Under existing law, the Alabama Gulf Coast Zoo
9 was exempt from the payment of state, county, and
10 municipal sales and use taxes for capital expenditures;
11 however, this exemption expired on September 30, 2022.
12 This bill would reinstate the state portion of
13 the exemption for all state sales and use taxes through
14 September 30, 2028, and allow counties and
15 municipalities to adopt local exemptions for the
16 entity.

24 40-23-5, Code of Alabama 1975, to reinstate the exemption for
25 the Alabama Gulf Coast Zoo from the payment of state sales and
26 use taxes for capital expenditures, and to allow counties and
27 municipalities to adopt local exemptions for the entity.
